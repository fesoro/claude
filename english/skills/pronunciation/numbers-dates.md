# Numbers, Dates & Time — Rəqəmlər, Tarixlər və Vaxt

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

Müsahibədə "I have 5 years of experience", "I started in 2019", "My salary is $85,000" demək çox vacibdir. Amma rəqəmləri, illəri, tarixləri səhv tələffüz etmək çox geniş yayılıb.

Bu fayl **A2-B1 üçün praktik** — hər növ rəqəm tipini düzgün ifadə etməyi öyrədir.

---

## 1. Əsas Rəqəmlər (0-100)

### 0-20
| # | Söz |
|---|-----|
| 0 | zero / oh (telefon nömrəsində) / nought (British) |
| 1-10 | one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten |
| 11-12 | eleven, twelve |
| 13-19 | thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen |
| 20 | twenty |

### 20-100 (onluqlar)
20 twenty, 30 thirty, 40 forty *(⚠️ "u" yoxdur — "forty", "fourty" deyil)*, 50 fifty, 60 sixty, 70 seventy, 80 eighty, 90 ninety

### Birləşmə
- 21 = **twenty-one** (defis! "twentyone" yox)
- 35 = thirty-five
- 99 = ninety-nine

### ⚠️ 13 vs 30, 14 vs 40 — Vurğu Fərqi
| Teens (vurğu SONA) | Tens (vurğu ƏVVƏLƏ) |
|---------------------|----------------------|
| thir**TEEN** /θɜːrˈtiːn/ | **THIR**ty /ˈθɜːrti/ |
| four**TEEN** | **FOUR**ty |
| fif**TEEN** | **FIF**ty |
| six**TEEN** | **SIX**ty |

**Qayda:** teens son hecada vurğu, tens birinci hecada. Yanlış vurğu "15-i 50" kimi səsləndirir.

## 2. Böyük Rəqəmlər

| Yazılış | Söz |
|---------|-----|
| 100 | one hundred / a hundred |
| 200 | two hundred |
| 250 | two hundred and fifty *(British: "and" olur; American: əksər halda olmur)* |
| 1,000 | one thousand / a thousand |
| 10,000 | ten thousand |
| 100,000 | one hundred thousand |
| 1,000,000 | one million / a million |
| 1,000,000,000 | one billion / a billion |

### ⚠️ hundred/thousand/million — CƏM DEYİL
Rəqəmdən sonra **tək formada**:
- ❌ "five hundreds"
- ✅ "**five hundred**"
- ❌ "two thousands dollars"
- ✅ "**two thousand** dollars"

Yalnız **qeyri-dəqiq** sayda cəm olur:
- "Hundreds of people came." (yüzlərlə)
- "Thousands of emails." (minlərlə)

### İngilis vs Amerika
- 1,200 = "**one thousand two hundred**" / "**twelve hundred**" (hər iki forma ok)
- 2,500 = "**two thousand five hundred**" / "**twenty-five hundred**"

## 3. İllər (Years)

### 2 cür oxunur — hansı halda hansı?

#### Forma 1: İki cüt (1990-1999)
- **1990** = "nineteen ninety"
- **1999** = "nineteen ninety-nine"
- **1850** = "eighteen fifty"
- **1776** = "seventeen seventy-six"

#### Forma 2: 2000-2009 — xüsusi (thousand-la)
- **2000** = "two thousand" (or "the year two thousand")
- **2001** = "two thousand and one" / "twenty oh one"
- **2005** = "two thousand and five"
- **2009** = "two thousand and nine"

#### Forma 3: 2010+ — iki cür ola bilər
- **2010** = "twenty ten" / "two thousand and ten" (ikisi də ok)
- **2019** = "twenty nineteen" / "two thousand and nineteen"
- **2026** = "twenty twenty-six"

**Hal-hazırda:** 2010+ illəri üçün "twenty [two digits]" daha təbii səslənir.

### Müsahibədə nümunə:
- "I graduated in **twenty eighteen** (2018)."
- "I started this job in **twenty twenty-three** (2023)."
- "In **nineteen ninety-five** (1995), I was born."

---

## 4. Tarixlər (Dates)

### Formal yazılış — 2 sistem

#### British
- **15 March 2026** (day/month/year)
- Oxunuş: "the fifteenth of March, twenty twenty-six"

#### American
- **March 15, 2026** (month/day/year)
- Oxunuş: "March fifteenth, twenty twenty-six"

### ⚠️ Vacib: ORDINAL (sıra) rəqəmlər
Gün üçün **"th/st/nd/rd"** işlədilir:

| # | Ordinal |
|---|---------|
| 1st | first |
| 2nd | second |
| 3rd | third |
| 4th | fourth |
| 5th | fifth *(⚠️ "fifeth" yox)* |
| 8th | eighth *(⚠️ "eightth" yox, bir "t")* |
| 9th | ninth *(⚠️ "e" yox: ninth, nineth deyil)* |
| 12th | twelfth *(⚠️ f + th)* |
| 20th | twentieth |
| 21st | twenty-first |
| 30th | thirtieth |

### Ayların yazılışı (böyük hərf!)
January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December

### ⚠️ Ay + gün səhvləri:
- ❌ "March three" → ✅ "**March third**" / "the third of March"
- ❌ "15 March" oxuyanda "fifteen march" → ✅ "the **fifteenth** of March"

## 5. Vaxt (Time)

### 12 saatlıq sistem
| Yazılış | Oxunuş |
|---------|--------|
| 9:00 | nine o'clock / nine AM |
| 9:15 | nine fifteen / a quarter past nine |
| 9:30 | nine thirty / half past nine |
| 9:45 | nine forty-five / a quarter to ten |
| 10:05 | ten oh five / five past ten |
| 10:55 | ten fifty-five / five to eleven |

### AM / PM
- AM = səhər (12:00 gecə - 11:59 səhər)
- PM = günorta / axşam (12:00 günorta - 11:59 gecə)

### 24 saatlıq sistem (rəsmi)
- 14:00 = "fourteen hundred hours" (hərbi / aviasiya) / "two pm" (gündəlik)
- 20:30 = "twenty thirty" / "half past eight in the evening"

### Müsahibədə:
- "The meeting is at **3 pm**." / "at **three in the afternoon**."
- "I can talk at **10 am** your time."
- "Let's sync at **half past two**."

### Vaxt ifadə edərkən:
- **at** 3 pm (at saat üçün)
- **on** Monday (on gün üçün)
- **in** March / in 2026 (in ay / il üçün)
- **in** the morning / afternoon / evening
- **at** night (at — night üçün!)

## 6. Telefon Nömrələri

### Qayda: Rəqəmləri **tək-tək** oxu
- 555-1234 = "**five five five, one two three four**"
- 0 = əksəriyyət **"oh"** (O kimi), bəzən "zero"
- İki eyni rəqəm = "**double** [rəqəm]"
  - 77 = "seven seven" / "**double seven**"
  - 000 = "**triple oh**" / "zero zero zero"

### Nümunə:
- (055) 123 45 67 = "**oh five five, one two three, four five, six seven**"
- +994 50 555 00 12 = "**plus nine nine four, five oh, five five five, double oh, one two**"

---

## 7. Qiymətlər və Pul

### Dollar və Cent
- $5 = "five dollars"
- $5.50 = "five dollars fifty" / "five fifty" / "five dollars and fifty cents"
- $1,250 = "one thousand two hundred fifty dollars" / "twelve hundred fifty dollars"
- $85,000 = "eighty-five thousand dollars"

### Maaş (müsahibədə vacib!)
- "My current salary is $**60K**." = "sixty thousand" / "sixty K"
- "I'm looking for **70 to 80K**." = "seventy to eighty thousand"
- "The range is **$80-100K**." = "eighty to one hundred K"

**K** = thousand (min), **M** = million

### Nümunələr:
- "The company has **$2M** in revenue." = "two million"
- "I saved **$15K** last year." = "fifteen thousand / fifteen K"

## 8. Faizlər və Hissələr (Percentages & Fractions)

### Faiz
- 25% = "**twenty-five percent**"
- 50% = "fifty percent" / "**half**"
- 100% = "one hundred percent" / "completely"
- 0.5% = "**half a percent**" / "**zero point five percent**"

### Kəsr (Fraction)
- 1/2 = **a half** / one half
- 1/3 = **a third** / one third
- 1/4 = **a quarter** / one fourth
- 3/4 = **three quarters**
- 2/3 = **two thirds**

### Onluq kəsrlər (Decimals)
- 3.14 = "**three point one four**" (vergül yox, nöqtə!)
- 2.5 = "**two point five**"
- 0.8 = "**zero point eight**" / "oh point eight" / "**point eight**"

### ⚠️ Azərbaycan vs İngilis
- Azərbaycan: 3,14 (vergül) → İngilis: 3.14 (nöqtə)
- Azərbaycan: 1.000.000 → İngilis: 1,000,000 (əksi!)

---

## 9. Müddət (Duration)

### "for" ilə
- "I worked there **for 3 years**."
- "The meeting lasted **for an hour**."

### "since" ilə
- "I have lived here **since 2019**."
- "She has worked here **since March**."

### Təqribi müddət
- "**About** 5 years." (təqribən)
- "**Around** 2 hours."
- "**Roughly** 10 people."
- "**More or less** 3 months."

---

## 10. Yaş və Təcrübə

### Yaş
- "I am **25 years old**." / "I am 25."
- ❌ "I have 25 years." (yanlış — Azərbaycan calque)
- "She's **in her thirties**." = 30-40 arası
- "He's **in his early twenties**." = 20-24 arası
- "He's **in his late fifties**." = 55-59 arası

### Təcrübə
- "I have **5 years of experience** in backend development."
- "I've been working in IT for **over 3 years**."
- "I've spent **nearly a decade** in this field." (10 il)

---

## 11. Sürət, Məsafə, Temperatur

### Məsafə
- km vs miles (US): "**5 kilometers**" / "**3 miles**"
- "About **10 kilometers** away."

### Sürət
- "60 km/h" = "sixty kilometers per hour" / "sixty kilometers an hour"
- "70 mph" = "seventy miles per hour"

### Temperatur
- Celsius: "25°C" = "twenty-five degrees Celsius" / "twenty-five degrees"
- Fahrenheit: "77°F" = "seventy-seven degrees Fahrenheit"

## 12. Müsahibədə Tez-tez İşlədilən Rəqəmlər — Dialoq Nümunəsi

```
Interviewer: "How many years of experience do you have?"
You: "I have about **five years** of experience in backend engineering."

Interviewer: "When did you join your current company?"
You: "In **twenty twenty-one** (2021), so almost three years ago."

Interviewer: "What's your expected salary?"
You: "I'm looking at a range of **sixty to seventy thousand** euros per year."

Interviewer: "How big was the team?"
You: "We had around **twelve engineers**, plus **two product managers** and **one designer**."

Interviewer: "What was the user base?"
You: "We had about **fifty thousand daily active users**, and around **two million** total registered users."
```
