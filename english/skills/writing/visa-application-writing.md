# Visa Application Writing

Visa üçün yazılı sənədlər — cover letter, purpose statement, supporting letter. Bunlar formal, dəqiq və inandırıcı olmalıdır.

---

## 1. Cover Letter (Müşayiət məktubu)

Bu məktub vizanı niyə istədiyini izah edir. Əksər ölkələr tələb edir.

### Struktur

```
1. Greeting
2. Purpose — niyə müraciət edirsən
3. Who you are — qısa peşəkar tanıtım
4. Employment details — iş yeri, vəzifə, maaş
5. Ties to home country — ev ölkəyə bağlılıq
6. Intention — planların
7. Closing
```

### Tam nümunə (Work Visa Cover Letter)

```
[Your full name]
[Your address]
[Date]

To the Visa Section,
[Embassy/Consulate name]

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to apply for a Skilled Worker Visa to the United Kingdom. 
I have received a formal job offer from [Company Name], a technology 
company based in London, registered as a licensed sponsor under the 
UK Home Office scheme.

**About me**
I am a Senior Backend Software Engineer with 7 years of professional 
experience, specialising in Go, Java, and distributed systems. I am 
currently employed at [Current Company] in Baku, Azerbaijan.

**Job offer details**
Position: Senior Backend Engineer  
Employer: [Company Name]  
Salary: £[X] per annum  
Start date: [Date]  
Certificate of Sponsorship reference: [CoS number]

**Ties to Azerbaijan**
I maintain strong ties to my home country. My parents and siblings 
reside in Baku. I own/rent property at [address]. I have been a 
resident and taxpayer in Azerbaijan for my entire working life.

**My intentions**
I intend to relocate to the United Kingdom to take up this employment 
opportunity. I am committed to complying with all visa conditions and 
maintaining legal status throughout my stay.

I have enclosed all supporting documents as required. Please do not 
hesitate to contact me if you require any further information.

Yours faithfully,
[Your full name]
[Phone number]
[Email address]
```

## 2. Personal Statement / Purpose of Visit

Bəzi ölkələr (xüsusən tələbə vizası üçün) ayrıca personal statement tələb edir.

### Nümunə (Short version — tech job):

```
I am applying for a [visa type] to the [country] to take up 
employment as a Senior Backend Developer at [Company], effective 
[date].

I have [X] years of experience in software engineering, with 
expertise in [technologies]. I was offered this position following 
a competitive interview process. The role aligns closely with 
my career development in distributed systems engineering.

I have sufficient financial resources to support myself during the 
transition period. My employer will provide relocation assistance 
and my salary of £[X] per annum is sufficient for living expenses 
in [city].

I intend to fully comply with the conditions of my visa and 
contribute productively to my employer and the wider tech community.
```

## 3. Employer Support Letter (İşəgötürənin məktubu)

HR-dan istəmən lazım olan məktub. Nə yazmalı olduğunu onlara bildirə bilərsən.

```
To Whom It May Concern,

This letter confirms that [Your Name] has been offered the position 
of Senior Backend Engineer at [Company Name], commencing [date].

[Your Name] will be employed on a permanent, full-time basis at 
a salary of £[X] per annum. [Company Name] is a licensed sponsor 
under the UK Skilled Worker scheme (Sponsor Licence Number: [X]).

A Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) reference [number] has been 
assigned to [Your Name].

Should you require any further information, please contact:

[HR contact name]
[HR email]
[Company phone]

Yours sincerely,
[HR Manager name]
[Title]
[Company Name]
```

## 4. Bank statement cover note

Bəzən bank çıxarışına əlavə olaraq qısa izahat yazılır:

```
I hereby confirm that the attached bank statements belong to me, 
[Full Name], holder of account [account number/last 4 digits] 
at [Bank Name].

The statements cover a period of [3/6] months and demonstrate 
sufficient funds to support my visa application and initial 
relocation costs.

All statements are authentic and unaltered.

Signed: [Name]
Date: [Date]
```

## 5. Tez-tez istifadə olunan frazalar

### Başlanğıc frazaları:
```
"I am writing to apply for..."
"I hereby submit my application for..."
"I wish to formally apply for..."
```

### Sənəd istinadları:
```
"Please find enclosed the following supporting documents:"
"As evidenced by the attached [document]..."
"Further details are provided in the enclosed [document]."
```

### Bağlayıcı frazalar:
```
"I trust you will find my application in order."
"I welcome any opportunity to provide further information."
"I look forward to a favourable outcome."
"Should you require any clarification, please do not hesitate to contact me."
```

## 6. Ümumi səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün |
|------|--------|
| "I want to go to UK" | "I am applying for..." |
| "My boss says I can work there" | "I have received a formal job offer from..." |
| Çox şəxsi məlumat (ailə problemləri, etc.) | Yalnız faktlar: mövqe, maaş, tarix |
| Qeyri-müəyyən niyyət | Konkret: vəzifə adı, şirkət, başlama tarixi |
| Copy-paste templatei dəyişdirməmək | Hər məktub özünə uyğun fərdiləşdirilmiş olmalıdır |
