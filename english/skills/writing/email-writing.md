# Email Writing — Tech & Relocation Focus

Remote işdə, job search-də, visa prosesindən tutmuş landlord-a qədər email ən çox işlətdiyin komunikasiya alətidir. Bu fayl real həyatdan şablonlar verir.

---

## Email Structure

```
Subject line     — Qısa, aydın, informativ
Greeting         — Dear / Hi
Opening          — Məktubun məqsədi (1-2 cümlə)
Body             — Ətraflı məlumat (qısa paraqraflar)
Closing          — Növbəti addım / xahiş
Sign-off         — Regards / Sincerely
Signature        — Ad, vəzifə, kontakt
```

## Sample Professional Email

> **Subject:** Meeting Request — Project Update
>
> Dear Ms Chen,
>
> I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to request a brief meeting to discuss the progress of the current project.
>
> As we are approaching the deadline, I would like to review the key milestones and address any outstanding issues. Would you be available for a 30-minute call sometime this week?
>
> Please let me know a time that suits you, and I will send a calendar invitation accordingly.
>
> Thank you for your time, and I look forward to hearing from you.
>
> Kind regards,
> [Your Name]
> Senior Backend Engineer | [Company]

## Job Search Emails

### Recruiter-ə ilk müraciət (Cold Outreach)

```
Subject: Senior Backend Engineer — Open to Opportunities in [City/UK/EU]

Hi [Name],

I came across your profile on LinkedIn and wanted to reach out.
I'm a Senior Backend Engineer with 7 years of experience in Go, 
Java, and distributed systems. I'm actively looking to relocate 
to [city/country] and explore senior engineering roles.

My background includes [2-3 specific things: e.g., 
"building high-throughput APIs at scale, leading small teams, 
microservices on Kubernetes"].

I'd love to hear if you're working with companies that might be 
a good fit — particularly those open to visa sponsorship.

Happy to share my CV if useful.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn URL]
```

---

### Job offer aldıqdan sonra — Təsdiq etmək

```
Subject: Re: Offer Letter — Senior Backend Engineer

Hi [Hiring Manager name],

Thank you so much for the offer — I'm genuinely excited 
about the opportunity to join [Company].

I've reviewed the offer letter and I'm happy to accept.
Could you confirm the next steps — onboarding paperwork, 
start date confirmation, and the Certificate of Sponsorship 
process?

Looking forward to working with the team.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

### Müsahibə sonrası follow-up

```
Subject: Thank You — [Role] Interview on [Date]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today 
about the [role] position.

I enjoyed our conversation, particularly the discussion 
about [specific topic from interview]. It confirmed my 
enthusiasm for the role and the team's approach to [X].

Please let me know if you need anything further from my side.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## Relocation Emails

### Landlord-a ilk müraciət

```
Subject: Enquiry — [Property address/listing]

Hi,

I'm interested in the property advertised at [address/link].
I'm relocating to [city] for work, starting [date], 
and I'm looking for accommodation from [move-in date].

A few quick questions:
- Is the property still available?
- Are bills included in the rent?
- Would it be possible to arrange a viewing this week?

I'm a software engineer relocating with [company sponsorship / 
my own funds], so I can provide an employment letter and 
references if needed.

Many thanks,
[Your Name]
[Phone]
```

---

### HR-a relocation support soruşmaq

```
Subject: Relocation Support — [Your Name] / Start Date [Date]

Hi [HR Name],

I'm writing to understand the relocation support available 
as part of my offer.

My main questions:
1. Is there a relocation budget, and if so, what does it cover?
2. Can the company provide a reference letter for my visa 
   application?
3. Is there HR support for the Certificate of Sponsorship 
   process?
4. Is temporary accommodation assistance available for 
   the first few weeks?

I want to plan the move efficiently — any guidance on 
the typical timeline would be very helpful.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
```

## İş Yerindəki Emaillər

### Status update — ongoing task

```
Subject: Update: [Task/Project Name]

Hi [Name / team],

Quick update on [task]:

✅ Done: [what's complete]
🔄 In progress: [what's ongoing — ETA: X]
⚠️ Blocker: [if any — what you need]

Let me know if you need more detail on any of these.

[Your Name]
```

---

### Blocker bildirmək

```
Subject: Blocker — [Brief description]

Hi [Name],

I've hit a blocker on [task] and wanted to flag it early.

The issue: [one sentence description]
What I've tried: [what you already attempted]
What I need: [specific ask — a decision, access, info]

Happy to jump on a quick call if that's easier.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

---

### Deadline-ı dəyişdirmək istəmək

```
Subject: Re: [Task] — Timeline Update

Hi [Name],

I wanted to flag a potential delay on [task].

Original estimate: [date]
New estimate: [date]
Reason: [honest, brief explanation]

I'll prioritise this and keep you updated. 
Let me know if the new timeline doesn't work — 
I can discuss options.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

---

### İzin / Qeyri-işdə olmaq

```
Subject: Out of Office — [Date(s)]

Hi [Manager],

I'd like to request [X days] leave from [date] to [date].

I'll make sure all current tasks are handed over or 
in a good state before I leave. Happy to discuss timing 
if it conflicts with anything.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

## Formal vs Informal Tone

| Situation | Tone | Sign-off |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Recruiter (first contact) | Semi-formal | Best regards |
| Hiring manager | Semi-formal | Best regards |
| HR / admin | Formal | Kind regards |
| Landlord | Semi-formal | Many thanks |
| Bank / visa | Formal | Yours sincerely |
| Close colleague | Informal | Thanks / Best |
| Manager (regular) | Semi-formal | Thanks |

## Ümumi Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün |
|------|--------|
| "Dear Sir/Madam, I want to..." | "I am writing to..." |
| Çox uzun açılış paraqrafı | 1-2 cümlə — məqsədi birinci yaz |
| Emoji işlətmək (formal email-də) | Heç vaxt formal email-də emoji |
| "ASAP" işlətmək | "at your earliest convenience" |
| Cavab verməmişdən əvvəl "Sorry for the late reply" | Yalnız həqiqətən gecikirsənsə |
