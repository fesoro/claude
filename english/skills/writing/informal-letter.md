# Informal Letter / Email Writing

Dostu, ailə üzvü və ya tanışa yazılan məktub.

---

## Structure (Quruluş)

```
1. Greeting (Salamlama)
2. Opening line (Açılış cümləsi)
3. Main body (Əsas hissə — 2-3 abzas)
4. Closing line (Bağlayıcı cümlə)
5. Sign-off (Vida)
6. Name (Ad)
```

---

## 1. Greetings

| Expression | Usage |
|------------|-------|
| Dear John, | Standart, hər zaman uyğun |
| Hi Sarah, | Çox yaxın dost |
| Hey Mike, | Çox qeyri-rəsmi |
| Dear Mum/Dad, | Ailə |

## 2. Opening Lines

| Expression | Azerbaijani |
|------------|-------------|

| It was great to hear from you! | Səndən xəbər almaq gözəl idi! |

## 3. Main Body Phrases

### Xəbər vermək:
| Expression | Azerbaijani |
|------------|-------------|

| I'm writing to let you know that... | Bildirmək istəyirəm ki... |

## Sample Letter

> Dear Emma,
>
> How are you? I hope everything's going well with you and your family!
>
> I'm writing because I have some exciting news — I've just been offered a new job in London! I can't believe it, I've been waiting for this opportunity for so long. The company seems amazing and the salary is much better than my current one.
>
> The only problem is that I'll have to move away from home. I'm a bit nervous about living alone in a big city. Have you got any advice? You've lived in London before, so you'd know better than anyone!
>
> Anyway, write back soon and let me know how things are going with you. I miss you lots!
>
> Love,
> Orkhan

## Informal vs Formal — Fərq

| Informal | Formal |
|----------|--------|
| Hi / Hey | Dear Mr/Ms Smith |
| Thanks | I am grateful for |
| I'm writing to tell you | I am writing to inform you |
| I can't | I am unable to |
| Lots of love | Yours sincerely |
| Contractions (I'm, don't) | No contractions (I am, do not) |
