# Relocation Job Interview English

Beynəlxalq şirkətlərdə müsahibə — texniki biliklə yanaşı, relocation-a hazır olduğunu sübut etmək lazımdır. Bu fayl sənin başqa ölkədə iş axtararkən lazım olan xüsusi frazaları əhatə edir.

---

## 1. Relocation niyyəti — açıq danışmaq

İşəgötürən bu sualı mütləq verəcək. Qısa, inamlı, faktlarla cavab ver.

**"Are you willing to relocate?"**
```
"Yes, absolutely. I'm actively looking to relocate to [city/country]. 
I've already started the process — I have contacts there and I'm 
familiar with the visa requirements."
```

**"When could you start?"**
```
"My current notice period is [1 month]. After that, I'd need 
approximately [2-4 weeks] to sort out the relocation logistics. 
Realistically, I could start on [date]."
```

**"Have you lived abroad before?"**
```
"Not long-term, but I've visited [country] and I've been working 
in an international team remotely for [X] years. I'm very 
comfortable with the cultural shift."
```

---

## 2. Work authorisation

İşəgötürən sponsorluq etmək istəyib-istəmədiyini bilmək istəyir.

**"Do you have the right to work in [country]?"**
```
"Not yet — I would need visa sponsorship. I understand this is 
a requirement and I've looked into the [Skilled Worker / EU Blue Card] 
process. I'm eligible based on my background and salary expectations."
```

```
"I'm in the process of applying for a [visa type]. The expected 
processing time is [X weeks]. I can share the current status 
if helpful."
```

**"We don't sponsor visas. Is that an issue?"**
```
"I understand. Could you let me know if that policy might change, 
or whether there are contractors or agencies you work through? 
Otherwise, I'll keep it in mind for future opportunities."
```

---

## 3. Relocation package müzakirəsi

**"We offer a relocation package. What would you need?"**
```
"Thank you — that's very helpful. My main costs would be:
- One-way flight and shipping of belongings
- Temporary accommodation for the first [2-4 weeks]
- Potentially some support with the visa application costs

I'm flexible and happy to discuss what's standard for your company."
```

**"We offer a lump sum of £X for relocation."**
```
"That works for me. Could you clarify what it covers — 
is it paid upfront or after I start?"
```

---

## 4. Maaş müzakirəsi (yeni ölkə kontekstindən)

Maaş gözləntiləri müəyyən edərkən yaşayış xərclərini nəzərə al.

**"What are your salary expectations?"**
```
"Based on my research into [city] market rates for senior backend 
engineers and the cost of living, I'm looking at [£X–£Y] per year 
gross. I'm open to discussing the full package including benefits."
```

**Faydalı frazalar:**
```
"What's the typical range for this role?"
"Is the salary reviewed annually?"
"What does the benefits package include? (health insurance, pension, etc.)"
"Is remote work partially possible after settling in?"
"What is the standard working hours and overtime policy?"
```

---

## 5. Müqavilə oxumaq

Offer letter aldıqdan sonra aşağıdakı şərtlərə diqqət et:

| Termin | Nə soruşmalısan |
|--------|-----------------|
| Probation period | "What is the performance review process during probation?" |
| Notice period | "Is the notice period the same both ways — for me and for you?" |
| Non-compete clause | "How broad is the non-compete — does it restrict freelance work?" |
| Stock options / RSUs | "What is the vesting schedule?" |
| Remote work policy | "What is the current policy on working from home?" |
| Holiday allowance | "How many days annual leave is included?" |

---

## 6. İnterviewda məlum olmayan şey

```
"That's a great question. I haven't worked with [tech] directly, 
but I've worked with [similar thing] and I'm confident I could 
pick it up quickly."
```

```
"Could you tell me more about the tech stack? I want to make 
sure I can contribute from day one."
```

---

## 7. Müsahibənin sonunda soruşmaq

Beynəlxalq müsahibədə bunları soruş — güclü, hazırlıqlı görünürsən:

```
"How international is the team — are there other people who relocated?"
"What support does the company offer for new arrivals settling in?"
"What does the onboarding process look like for someone joining from abroad?"
"How distributed is the team — different time zones?"
"Is there a relocation buddy or HR contact I can work with on the move?"
```

---

## 8. Reject cavabı (peşəkarcasına)

Offer almadıqda:

```
"Thank you for letting me know. Could you share any feedback on 
my application? I'd appreciate it for future opportunities."
```

Gözleme dövründe:
```
"I wanted to follow up on my application — is there an update 
on the timeline?"
```
