# Small Talk — Gündəlik Söhbət

Small talk — ciddi mövzulardan əvvəl olan qısa, dostluq söhbəti. İş müsahibələrində, iş yoldaşlarıyla, görüşlərdə çox vacibdir.

---

## 1. Interview-dan Əvvəl (Before the Interview)

Müsahibəçi sizi qarşıladıqda:

| Onlar deyir | Siz deyirsiniz |
|---|---|
| "Did you find us okay?" | "Yes, the directions were very clear, thank you." |
| "How was your journey?" | "It was fine, thanks. The traffic wasn't too bad." |
| "Can I get you a coffee or water?" | "A glass of water would be great, thank you." |
| "Have you been waiting long?" | "No, not at all. I arrived a little early." |

---

## 2. Ümumi Small Talk Mövzuları

### Hava (Safe in any culture)
- "The weather's been lovely this week, hasn't it?"
- "I heard it might rain later — typical!"
- "Finally some sunshine!"

### Yolculuq / Ofis
- "Is this your main office, or do you have other locations?"
- "How long have you been in this building?"
- "It's a great area — lots of nice places nearby."

### Həftəsonu / Tətil
- "Did you have a good weekend?"
- "Are you doing anything nice for the holidays?"
- "Have you had a busy week?"

---

## 3. Small Talk-u Necə Bitirmək

Müsahibəyə keçid üçün:
- "Anyway, I'm really looking forward to finding out more about the role."
- "I'm excited to be here — it's a great company."

---

## 4. Nədən Çəkinmək Lazımdır

| Çəkin | Bunun əvəzinə |
|---|---|
| Siyasət, din haqqında danışmaq | Neytral mövzular seçin |
| Şikayət etmək ("terrible traffic", "I'm exhausted") | Müsbət qalın |
| Çox uzun cavab vermək | 1-2 cümlə kifayət edir |
| Sükut içində qalmaq | Kiçik bir sual verin: "How long have you been with the company?" |

---

## 5. Faydalı Reaksiyalar

| İfadə | İstifadə |
|---|---|
| "Oh, really?" | Maraq göstərmək |
| "That's interesting." | Dinlədiyini göstərmək |
| "I know what you mean." | Razılaşmaq |
| "Absolutely." | Güclü razılaşma |
| "Right, of course." | Anladığını göstərmək |
| "That makes sense." | Anladığını ifadə etmək |
| "Tell me more." | Söhbəti uzatmaq |
| "I can imagine." | Empatiya |
| "No way!" | Təəccüb |
| "You don't say!" | Təəccüb (informal) |
| "Sounds great!" | Razılaşma, təsdiq |
| "That's good to hear." | Müsbət cavab |
| "I'm sorry to hear that." | Pis xəbərə cavab |
| "Good for you!" | Təbrik |

---

## 6. Ofis və İş Yerində Small Talk

### Səhər gəlişi
| Situasiya | İfadə |
|---|---|
| Günaydın | "Morning! How was your evening?" |
| Qəhvə maşını yanında | "Did you have a good weekend?" |
| Liftdə | "Busy day ahead?" |
| İş yoldaşını görəndə | "How's it going? / How are things?" |

### Görüşdən əvvəl
- "Thanks for making the time."
- "How's your week going so far?"
- "Have you been busy lately?"
- "Anything exciting happening on your side?"

### Görüşdən sonra
- "That was a productive meeting."
- "Nice chatting with you."
- "Let's catch up soon."
- "I'll follow up with an email."

### Nahar fasiləsində
- "Where are you getting lunch today?"
- "Have you tried the new place around the corner?"
- "What are you having?"
- "Mind if I join you?"

---

## 7. Virtual Görüşlərdə (Zoom / Teams)

### Başlanğıcda
- "Hi everyone, can you hear me okay?"
- "Let me know if you can see my screen."
- "Let's give it another minute for others to join."
- "How's everyone doing today?"
- "Where are you dialing in from?"

### Texniki problem
- "Sorry, I was on mute."
- "You're cutting out a bit — could you repeat that?"
- "I think we lost ___ for a second."
- "My connection is a bit unstable today."
- "Let me turn my camera on."

### Bitərkən
- "Great, I think we can wrap up here."
- "Thanks everyone, have a good rest of your day."
- "Let's pick this up next week."

---

## 8. Həftəsonu və Tətil Mövzuları (Daha geniş)

### Sual vermək
- "Any plans for the weekend?"
- "Doing anything fun this weekend?"
- "Got anything lined up?"
- "How was your weekend?"
- "Did you do anything nice?"

### Cavablar
- "Nothing special, just relaxing at home."
- "I'm catching up on some chores."
- "I'm going out with friends on Saturday."
- "I'm visiting family."
- "I had a really quiet weekend — much needed!"
- "I went hiking — it was great to be outdoors."

### Tətil
- "Do you have any holiday plans coming up?"
- "Are you taking any time off soon?"
- "Have you been anywhere nice recently?"
- "Where did you go on your last trip?"

---

## 9. Texnologiya və Mövzuya Uyğun (Software Engineers üçün)

### Tech/industry-related
- "Have you been following the news about ___?"
- "Did you see what happened with ___?"
- "Are you using ___ these days?"
- "What's your team working on?"
- "Are you more on the backend or frontend side?"
- "How long have you been in tech?"

### Konfrans / hadisə
- "Did you attend the last conference?"
- "Which talks did you find interesting?"
- "Any good podcasts you'd recommend?"
- "What's your setup like? (desk, tools)"

---

## 10. Sağlamlıq və Ruhiyyə Mövzusu

Nəzakətli və qısa saxla — detallara getmə.

| Onlar deyir | Siz deyirsiniz |
|---|---|
| "How are you?" | "Doing well, thanks. You?" |
| "You look tired." | "Yeah, it's been a long week, but I'm okay." |
| "Are you feeling better?" | "Much better, thanks for asking." |
| "How's your family?" | "They're good, thanks." |

---

## 11. Small Talk Axını (Flow) — 3 Addım

**1. Başlat (Opener):** neytral mövzu  
→ "Did you have a good weekend?"

**2. Davam et (Follow-up):** detal sual ver  
→ "Oh, you went hiking? Where did you go?"

**3. Bitir (Bridge / Close):** əsas mövzuya keç və ya dostcasına bitir  
→ "Sounds amazing. Anyway, should we get started?"

---

## 12. Mövzudan Mövzuya Keçid (Transitions)

- "Speaking of ___, ___."
- "That reminds me of ___."
- "By the way, ___."
- "On a different note, ___."
- "Before I forget, ___."
- "Anyway, I was going to ask you ___."

---

## 13. Small Talk Bitirmək — Nəzakətli

Söhbəti uzatmadan bitirməli olsan:
- "Well, I should get back to my desk."
- "Anyway, let me let you go."
- "It was nice catching up."
- "I've got to run — catch you later!"
- "I don't want to keep you."
- "Let's talk again soon."

---

## 14. Praktik Ssenariləri

### Ssenari A: Müsahibədən əvvəl qəbul zalında
```
Resepsiyonist: "Good morning, how can I help you?"
Sən: "Good morning, I have an interview with Sarah at 10."
R: "Great, please take a seat. Can I get you a coffee or water?"
Sən: "Water would be lovely, thank you."
R: "Here you go. Sarah will be with you shortly."
Sən: "Thanks. This is a beautiful office, by the way."
R: "Thank you! We just moved here last year."
```

### Ssenari B: Ofis mətbəxində
```
İş yoldaşı: "Morning! Coffee?"
Sən: "Yeah, just what I need."
İş yoldaşı: "Rough morning?"
Sən: "Nothing too bad, just running a bit late. How about you?"
İş yoldaşı: "Same — Mondays, right?"
Sən: "Always the hardest. Anyway, have a good one!"
```

### Ssenari C: Virtual görüşdən əvvəl
```
Menecer: "Hey, how's it going?"
Sən: "Pretty good, thanks. Busy week so far?"
Menecer: "Back-to-back meetings, but can't complain."
Sən: "Yeah, I know the feeling. Everything okay on your side?"
Menecer: "All good. Shall we jump in?"
Sən: "Sounds good."
```
