# Common Interviewer Follow-Up Questions

Müsahiblər ilk cavabınızdan sonra tez-tez əlavə suallar verir. Bu sualları tanımaq və onlara hazır olmaq intervyuda rahatlığınızı artırır.

---

## 1. Dərinləşdirmə Sualları (Probing Deeper)

Müsahib daha çox detal istəyir:

| Sual | Məna (Az) | Nə etməli |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| "Can you elaborate on that?" | Bunu daha ətraflı izah edə bilərsiniz? | Daha çox detal və konkret nümunə verin |
| "Could you give me a specific example?" | Konkret nümunə verə bilərsiniz? | Real bir hadisə danışın |
| "What do you mean by that?" | Bununla nəyi nəzərdə tutursunuz? | Sadə sözlərlə yenidən izah edin |
| "Can you walk me through that?" | Addım-addım izah edə bilərsiniz? | Prosesi ardıcıl izah edin |
| "Tell me more about that." | Bu barədə daha çox danışın. | Kontekst, detallar, nəticə əlavə edin |

### Necə cavab vermək:

> **Müsahib:** "You mentioned you improved the deployment process. Can you elaborate on that?"
>
> **Siz:** "Of course. Before my changes, deployments were done manually — someone had to SSH into the server, pull the latest code, and restart the service. This took about twenty minutes and was error-prone. I set up a CI/CD pipeline using GitHub Actions that automatically ran tests, built a Docker image, and deployed to the staging environment. Once approved, it would deploy to production with a single click. This reduced deployment time from twenty minutes to about three minutes, and eliminated human errors."

## 2. Alternativ Ssenari Sualları (What-If Questions)

Müsahib fərqli bir vəziyyətdə nə edəcəyinizi soruşur:

| Sual | Məna (Az) |
|------|-----------|
| "What would you do differently if you could do it again?" | Yenidən etsəniz, nəyi dəyişərdiniz? |
| "What if the deadline couldn't be extended?" | Deadline uzadıla bilməsəydi nə olardı? |
| "How would you handle it if your colleague disagreed?" | Həmkarınız razı olmasaydı nə edərdiniz? |
| "What would you do if you didn't have enough resources?" | Kifayət qədər resurs olmasaydı nə edərdiniz? |
| "What if the client changed the requirements again?" | Müştəri tələbləri yenə dəyişsəydi nə olardı? |

### Cavab şablonları:

**"What would you do differently?"**
> "Looking back, I would probably **[change]**. At the time, I chose to **[original action]** because **[reason]**, but knowing what I know now, **[alternative]** would have been more effective because **[reason]**."

**"What if the deadline couldn't be extended?"**
> "If the deadline was fixed, I would focus on **[priority]** first and communicate clearly with stakeholders about what could realistically be delivered. I'd rather deliver a smaller set of features that work well than try to rush everything and compromise quality."

## 3. Özünü Qiymətləndirmə Sualları (Self-Assessment)

| Sual | Məna (Az) |
|------|-----------|
| "What was your specific contribution to the team?" | Komandaya sizin şəxsi töhfəniz nə idi? |
| "What was the most challenging part for you?" | Sizin üçün ən çətin hissə nə idi? |
| "How did that experience change your approach?" | Bu təcrübə yanaşmanızı necə dəyişdi? |
| "What did you learn from that?" | Bundan nə öyrəndiniz? |
| "Are you satisfied with the outcome?" | Nəticədən razısınız? |

### Cavab şablonları:

**"What did you learn from that?"**
> "The biggest takeaway for me was **[lesson]**. Before this experience, I used to **[old approach]**. Now I always **[new approach]** because **[reason]**."

**"What was the most challenging part?"**
> "The most challenging part was **[challenge]**. It was difficult because **[reason]**. I overcame it by **[action]**, and it taught me **[lesson]**."

## 4. Motivasiya Sualları (Motivation Follow-Ups)

| Sual | Məna (Az) |
|------|-----------|
| "Why is that important to you?" | Bu niyə sizin üçün vacibdir? |
| "What specifically interests you about this?" | Sizi konkret olaraq nə maraqlandırır? |
| "Where do you see this leading?" | Bunun hara aparacağını görürsünüz? |
| "What drives you in your work?" | İşinizdə sizi nə motivasiya edir? |
| "How does this role fit into your long-term goals?" | Bu rol uzunmüddətli məqsədlərinizə necə uyğun gəlir? |

### Cavab şablonu:

> "What specifically interests me about this role is **[aspect]**. In my career, I've always been drawn to **[interest]**, and this position offers the opportunity to **[opportunity]**. I believe it aligns well with my goal of **[long-term goal]**."

---

## 5. Gözlənilməz Suallar (Unexpected Questions)

Bəzən müsahib gözlənilməz suallar verir. Panika etməyin — düşünmək üçün vaxt istəyin:

| Sual | Nə yoxlayır |
|------|-------------|
| "What's something you're not good at?" | Özünü tanıma, dürüstlük |
| "How would your colleagues describe you?" | Özünə tənqidi baxış |
| "What would you do in your first 30 days?" | Planlaşdırma, təşəbbüs |
| "Why should we hire you over other candidates?" | Özünə inam, dəyər təklifi |
| "Do you have any questions for us?" | Maraq, hazırlıq |

### Vaxt almaq üçün ifadələr:

- "That's a great question. Let me think about that for a moment."
- "That's an interesting one. I'd say..."
- "I haven't been asked that before, but off the top of my head..."
- "Let me consider that briefly..."

---

## 6. "Do you have any questions for us?" — Soruşmalı Suallar

Bu sual həmişə gəlir. **Heç vaxt "No, I think you've covered everything" deməyin.**

### Yaxşı suallar:

**Rol haqqında:**
- "What does a typical day or week look like in this role?"
- "What are the biggest challenges someone in this position would face in the first six months?"
- "How is success measured in this role?"

**Komanda haqqında:**
- "Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with?"
- "How does the team handle code reviews and knowledge sharing?"
- "What's the team's approach to technical debt?"

**Şirkət haqqında:**
- "What's the company's approach to professional development?"
- "How would you describe the engineering culture here?"
- "What are the company's biggest priorities for the next year?"

**Proses haqqında:**
- "What does the onboarding process look like?"
- "What are the next steps in the interview process?"

## Məşq

Hər sual üçün 30 saniyəlik cavab hazırlayın:

1. "Can you elaborate on your experience with databases?"
2. "What would you do differently in that project?"
3. "What did you learn from that mistake?"
4. "What specifically interests you about our company?"
5. "What's something you want to improve about yourself?"

Cavablarınızı səsli deyin və vaxtı ölçün. 30 saniyədən çox olmamalıdır.
