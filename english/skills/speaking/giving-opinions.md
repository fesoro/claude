| Expression | Azerbaijani |
|------------|-------------|

| In my view, ... | Mənim baxışımca, ... |

---

## 7. IELTS Speaking Part 3 — Fikir bildirmə

IELTS Speaking Part 3-də fikrinizi ətraflı bildirməli olursunuz:

**Formula:** Opinion → Reason → Example → Restate

> **"I think** social media has a negative impact on mental health. **This is because** people constantly compare themselves to others online. **For example**, studies show that heavy Instagram use is linked to increased anxiety among teenagers. **For this reason, I believe** stricter age limits should be introduced."

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| In my opinion I think... | In my opinion, ... OR I think... (ikisi birlikdə olmaz) |
| I am thinking that... | I think that... |
| My opinion is that I believe... | In my opinion, ... OR I believe... |
| According to me, ... | In my opinion, ... / From my perspective, ... |
