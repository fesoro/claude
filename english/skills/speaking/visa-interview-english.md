# Visa Interview English

Konsulat müsahibəsi — relocate üçün ən kritik addımlardan biri. Bu fayl sənə ən çox verilən sualları, güclü cavab strukturlarını və siqnal sözləri öyrədir.

---

## 1. Ümumi məlumat

Visa müsahibəsi qısa (5-15 dəqiqə) amma intensivdir. Məmur:
- Sənin həqiqi niyyətini yoxlayır
- Ev ölkənə bağlılığını (ties to home country) qiymətləndirir
- Maliyyə vəziyyətini yoxlayır

**Əsas prinsip:** Qısa, aydın, konkret cavablar ver. Uzun izahat şübhə yaradır.

---

## 2. Ən çox verilən suallar + nümunə cavablar

### İş / Məqsəd

**"What is the purpose of your visit?"**
```
"I have accepted a job offer as a Senior Backend Developer at [Company], 
based in [City]. I'm applying for a work visa to relocate permanently."
```

**"Who is sponsoring your visa?"**
```
"My employer, [Company name], is sponsoring my visa application. 
They are a registered sponsor under the [Skilled Worker / Tech Talent] scheme."
```

**"What kind of work will you be doing?"**
```
"I'll be working as a backend software engineer, primarily with Go and 
Java, building distributed systems and APIs. The role is at senior level."
```

---

### Bağlılıq / Ev ölkəsi (Ties to home country)

**"Do you have family here in [home country]?"**
```
"Yes, my parents and my brother live here. I maintain strong ties to 
my home country."
```

**"Do you own property here?"**
```
"Yes, I own an apartment here in [city]."
```
```
"Not at the moment, but I have been renting here for several years."
```

**"What are your plans after the visa expires?" (tourist/temp visa)**
```
"I intend to return before my visa expires. I have flights booked for 
[date] and my return is confirmed."
```

---

### Maliyyə

**"How much do you earn currently?"**
```
"My current salary is [amount] per year. I also have savings of 
approximately [amount] to cover initial relocation costs."
```

**"Who will cover your living expenses?"**
```
"My employer will cover relocation costs, and my salary will cover 
all living expenses. I have sufficient savings as well."
```

---

### Plan / Niyyət

**"Where will you live when you arrive?"**
```
"For the first month, I have temporary accommodation arranged. 
I plan to find a permanent rental once I settle in."
```

**"Have you been to [country] before?"**
```
"Yes, I visited [city] for a tech conference in [year]."
```
```
"No, this will be my first time. I've been preparing by researching 
the city and connecting with the local tech community."
```

---

## 3. Çətin suallar

**"Why did you choose [country] specifically?"**
```
"The tech industry there is very strong, particularly in [city]. 
I received a competitive offer from a company with good values and 
growth opportunities. The quality of life is also attractive."
```

**"Why are you leaving your current job?"**
```
"I've reached the ceiling at my current position. This new role offers 
senior-level challenges, a better team, and international experience 
which is important for my career growth."
```

**"Are you applying to any other countries?"**
```
"I have focused my search on [country] because [specific reason — 
tech hub, language, personal preference]. This is my first choice."
```

---

## 4. Sığınacaq / Nasazlıq vəziyyətləri

```
"I'm sorry, could you repeat the question please?"
"Could you rephrase that? I want to make sure I understand correctly."
"Do you mean [X]? Just to clarify."
```

---

## 5. Müsahibə başlanğıcı / sonu

**Başlanğıc:**
```
"Good morning. My name is [name]. I have an appointment for [visa type] at [time]."
"Here is my passport and supporting documents."
"Of course." / "Certainly." (sənəd istədikdə)
```

**Son:**
```
"Thank you for your time."
"Is there anything else you need from me?"
"How long does the decision typically take?"
"Will I be notified by email or post?"
```

---

## 6. Əlavə hazırlıq: Bilmən lazım olan terminlər

| Term | Məna |
|------|------|
| Sponsor / sponsoring employer | İş yeri visa üçün sponsor olur |
| Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) | UK-də lazım olan sponsor sənədi |
| Ties to home country | Ev ölkənə bağlılıq (ailə, mülk, maliyyə) |
| Right of abode | Daimi qalma hüququ |
| Leave to remain | Ölkədə qalma icazəsi |
| Biometric residence permit (BRP) | Biometrik qalma icazəsi kartı |
| ILR (Indefinite Leave to Remain) | Daimi qalma (Permanent Residence) |
| Skilled Worker Visa | UK-də texniki mütəxəssis vizası |
| EU Blue Card | Avropa İttifaqında yüksək ixtisaslı işçi icazəsi |
