# Making Excuses & Apologizing — Bəhanə və Üzr İstəmək

## Səviyyə
B1 (iş kontekstində hər gün)

---

## Niyə Vacibdir?

İş həyatında:
- Gec qalırsan
- Bug buraxırsan
- Deadline-ı itirirsən
- Səhv edirsən

**Yaxşı üzr = peşəkar imza**

---

## Apologizing — Üzr İstəmək

### Format (qızıl qayda)

1. **Acknowledge** (tanı)
2. **Apologize** (üzr iste)
3. **Explain** (qısa izah — bəhanə yox!)
4. **Fix** (nə edəcəm)
5. **Prevent** (gələcək üçün)

---

## 1. Simple Apology

### Minimum

- "Sorry."
- "My apologies."
- "I apologize."

### Kiçik səhv

- "Sorry about that."
- "My bad." (informal)
- "Oops, sorry."

### Orta səhv

- "I'm really sorry."
- "I apologize for the mistake."
- "Sorry for the confusion."

### Böyük səhv

- "I sincerely apologize."
- "I take full responsibility."
- "Please accept my apologies."

---

## 2. Specific Apologies

### Gec qalmaq

- "Sorry I'm late."
- "Apologies for the delay."
- "Sorry for keeping you waiting."

### Gecikmiş cavab

- "Sorry for the late reply."
- "Apologies for the delayed response."
- "Sorry it took so long to get back to you."

### Bug / Səhv kod

- "Sorry about that bug — I'll fix it."
- "My mistake on the PR — rolling back now."
- "I introduced a regression — fixing."

### Yanlış anlayış

- "Sorry, I misunderstood."
- "I got that wrong — let me clarify."
- "My apologies, I misread the ticket."

### Unutmaq

- "Sorry, I forgot."
- "Apologies, I missed your email."
- "My bad, it slipped my mind."

---

## Sorry vs Apologize vs My Apologies

| Forma | Tonu |
|-------|------|
| **Sorry** | neytral, gündəlik |
| **I'm sorry** | daha güclü |
| **I apologize** | rəsmi |
| **My apologies** | rəsmi, yazılı |
| **Sincerely apologize** | çox rəsmi |

### Work context

- Slack: "Sorry, my bad!" ✓
- Email: "Apologies for the delay." ✓
- Formal: "I sincerely apologize." ✓

---

## 3. Nə Edəcəyini Söylə

Sadəcə "sorry" bəs deyil. Fix təklif et!

### ✓ Good

- "Sorry about that — **I'll fix it by EOD.**"
- "My apologies. **I'm rolling back the change now.**"
- "Sorry — **I'll add a test to prevent this.**"

### ✗ Weak

- "Sorry." (nə olur?)
- "My bad." (nə edirsən?)

---

## 4. Prevent — Gələcəyə

Böyük problemlər üçün:

- "**I'll make sure this doesn't happen again** by..."
- "**Going forward**, I'll..."
- "**To prevent this**, I'll..."

### Nümunə

- "Sorry for the outage. I'll add monitoring so we catch this earlier next time."

---

## ✗ Common Mistakes

### Too many "sorry"-lər

- ✗ "Sorry, sorry, sorry!" (panik)
- ✓ "Sorry." (bir dəfə, amma güclü)

### Blame shifting

- ✗ "Sorry, but it's not my fault — X did it." (kobud)
- ✓ "Sorry, I'll look into it." (ownership)

### No action

- ✗ "Sorry." (sonra?)
- ✓ "Sorry — here's what I'll do..."

### Fake apology

- ✗ "I'm sorry you feel that way." (passive aggressive)
- ✓ "I'm sorry for what I did."

---

## Making Excuses — Bəhanə

### İki növ:

1. **Legitimate** (real səbəb, professional)
2. **Weak** (bəhanə kimi səslənir, pis)

---

## ✓ Good Excuses (Legitimate)

### Texniki səbəb

- "**My laptop had an issue**, so I'm running late."
- "**The VPN was down**, I couldn't access the code."
- "**I was blocked by infra issues**, waiting on them."

### Vaxt səbəb

- "**I had back-to-back meetings**, couldn't get to your email."
- "**Prod incident took priority**, couldn't finish this today."
- "**I underestimated the scope**, needs another day."

### Təbii fəlakət

- "**Power outage at home**, just got back online."
- "**Internet was down**, using mobile hotspot now."

---

## ✗ Weak Excuses (Avoid!)

### Personal (too much TMI)

- ~~"My cat was sick..."~~
- ~~"I had a bad morning..."~~

### Blame

- ~~"It's not my fault..."~~
- ~~"Nobody told me..."~~

### Vague

- ~~"Stuff came up..."~~
- ~~"I've been busy..."~~

---

## Owning Your Mistake

### Ownership > Excuses

- ✓ "I should have caught that in review. I'll add a checklist."
- ✗ "I didn't know the code had that bug."

### Professional tone

- "I dropped the ball here."
- "I take full responsibility."
- "This was my oversight."

## Email Templates

### Running late

```
Subject: Running late to 3 PM meeting

Hi team,

Sorry — I'm stuck on a call and will be 10-15 min late.
Please start without me.

Thanks,
Orkhan
```

### Missed deadline

```
Subject: Update on API project

Hi [Manager],

I need to push back the delivery by 2 days. I underestimated
the complexity of the auth integration.

New ETA: Friday EOD.

To prevent this, I'll do better scoping for future estimates.

Let me know if this is a blocker.

Thanks,
Orkhan
```

### Introduced a bug

```
Subject: Hotfix deployed for login bug

Hi all,

My apologies — PR #456 introduced a regression in the login
flow. It's been rolled back, and I'm deploying a proper fix.

To prevent recurrence, I'm adding an integration test.

ETA for fix: 1 hour.

Sorry for the inconvenience.

Thanks,
Orkhan
```

## Interview Kontekstində

### Past mistakes

Interviewer soruşa bilər: "Tell me about a time you failed."

### Good answer struktur

1. **Situation**: context
2. **Task**: nə idi edilməli
3. **Action**: nə etdin (mistake)
4. **Result**: nə oldu
5. **Learning**: nə öyrəndin

### Nümunə

"Last year, I pushed a change to production without full testing. It caused a 30-minute outage.

I apologized to the team and took ownership in the post-mortem. We added an automated staging deployment step to prevent this.

I learned the importance of rigorous staging testing, even for 'simple' changes."

### Ciddi səhv

- Email / formal channel

### Kiçik səhv

- Slack / daily
- Quick apology
- Fix immediately

### Ultra səhv (data loss, security breach)

- Manager-ə dərhal
- Formal email
- Post-mortem

- Direct apology
- Specific fix
- Move on quickly

### Britaniyalı

- "Sorry" çox (hər yerdə!)
- "Terribly sorry"
- "Awfully sorry"

### Avropa (Alman / Holland)

- Less "sorry"
- More direct
- "I was wrong"

### Azərbaycan context

## Accepting Apology

Başqaları da sənə "sorry" deyəcək. Cavab:

### Graceful

- "No worries."
- "It's fine."
- "No problem."
- "Don't worry about it."
- "It happens."

### Warm

- "Thanks for letting me know."
- "I appreciate your honesty."
- "We all make mistakes."

### Professional

- "Thank you for the update."
- "Let's move forward."

---

## Xatırlatma

**Apology formulu:**
1. **Acknowledge** (I did X)
2. **Apologize** (Sorry)
3. **Explain** (brief, no excuses)
4. **Fix** (what I'll do)
5. **Prevent** (won't happen again)

**Interview qızıl:**
- Ownership > excuses
- Learning > regret
