# Spoken Connectors — Danışıq Üçün Bağlayıcı Sözlər

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

Yazı dilindəki "however", "moreover" danışıqda çox formal səslənir. Gündəlik söhbətdə native speakers fərqli, daha təbii bağlayıcılar istifadə edir: **well, you know, actually, I mean, like, anyway**.

Bu bağlayıcıları öyrənmək səni **A2-B1 kimi danışan** olmaqdan **təbii B1-B2 danışan** olmağa keçirir — çünki danışığın ritmi, axarı düzəlir.

---

## 1. Başlanğıc Sözlər (Fikir Başlatmaq)

### "Well" — ən çox işlədilən başlanğıc
- **"Well,** let me think about that."
- **"Well,** it depends."
- **"Well,** to be honest, I don't know."

Funksiya: Cavabdan əvvəl qısa pauza. Hazırlıqsız bir cavabdan əvvəl vaxt qazanmağa kömək edir.

### "So" — kontekst verə və keçid edə
- **"So,** what I'm trying to say is..."
- **"So,** long story short,..."
- **"So,** as I was saying,..."

### "Now" — diqqəti yenidən yönəltmək
- **"Now,** the interesting part is..."
- **"Now,** let me explain why."

### "Right" — diqqət cəlb etmək, təsdiq
- **"Right,** let's get started."
- **"Right,** where were we?"

---

## 2. Əlavə Etmək və Genişləndirmək (Adding / Expanding)

### "And also / Plus / On top of that"
- "I love the job. **Plus,** the team is great."
- "The salary is good. **On top of that,** I get flexible hours."

### "What's more" — əlavə fikir
- "It was a great event. **What's more,** I got to meet the CEO."

### "Not to mention" — unutmamaq
- "He's smart, hardworking, **not to mention** a great team player."

### "As well as"
- "I work on backend **as well as** DevOps."

---

## 3. Zidd Fikir (Contrast)

### "But / However" (However formaldır danışıqda, amma işlədirik)
- "I like the idea, **but** I'm not sure it will work."
- "It's a good plan. **However,** we need more time."

### "Still" — buna baxmayaraq
- "It was hard, but **still,** I enjoyed it."

### "Although / Though" — "though" sonda da işlədilir
- "**Although** it was late, I kept working."
- "It was late. I kept working, **though**."

### "On the other hand"
- "I could take the job. **On the other hand,** the pay is lower."

### "Actually" — fikrin əksini bildirmək
- "I thought it was boring, but **actually,** it was quite fun."
- "**Actually,** I disagree with that."

---

## 4. Səbəb-Nəticə (Cause & Effect)

### "Because / 'Cause" *("'cause" tamamilə qeyri-rəsmi)*
- "I left early **because** I wasn't feeling well."
- "I quit my job **'cause** it was too stressful." *(informal)*

### "So" — nəticə bildirmək
- "It was raining, **so** we stayed home."

### "That's why"
- "I was tired. **That's why** I skipped the gym."

### "As a result"
- "We lost the client. **As a result,** we had to lay off two people."

### "For that reason" *(daha formal)*
- "**For that reason,** we decided to postpone."

---

## 5. Nümunə Vermək (Giving Examples)

### "For example / for instance"
- "I love outdoor sports. **For example,** hiking and kayaking."

### "Like" — danışıqda ən çox işlənir
- "I enjoy challenges, **like** debugging complex issues."

### "Such as" *(daha formal)*
- "Tools **such as** Git and Docker are essential."

### "Let's say / Let me give you an example"
- "**Let's say** you have 100 users..."
- "**Let me give you an example** from my last project."

---

## 6. Dəqiqləşdirmə (Clarifying)

### "I mean" — ən çox işlədilən
- "It was amazing. **I mean,** really incredible."
- "**I mean,** what I'm trying to say is..."

### "In other words"
- "**In other words,** we're running out of time."

### "That is to say" *(daha formal)*
- "We need to act fast; **that is to say,** by Friday."

### "What I'm saying is"
- "**What I'm saying is** we need more developers."

### "Basically"
- "**Basically,** it's a simple API call."

---

## 7. Ardıcıllıq (Sequence)

### "First of all / Firstly"
- "**First of all,** thanks for coming."
- "**Firstly,** let's review the numbers."

### "Then / After that / Next"
- "I graduated in 2019. **Then** I worked at ___. **After that,** I moved to ___."

### "Finally / In the end / Eventually"
- "**Finally,** we fixed the bug."
- "**In the end,** it was worth the effort."
- "**Eventually,** the server came back up."

### "Meanwhile / In the meantime"
- "The team is reviewing the design. **In the meantime,** I'm writing tests."

---

## 8. Fikrini Sona Yetirmək (Concluding)

### "So, in short / To sum up / Basically"
- "**So, in short,** it's a great opportunity."
- "**To sum up,** we need more data."
- "**Basically,** that's my main point."

### "At the end of the day" — ən populyar
- "**At the end of the day,** it's your decision."

### "All in all"
- "**All in all,** it was a productive meeting."

### "The bottom line is"
- "**The bottom line is** we need to ship by Q2."

---

## 9. Həqiqəti Vurğulamaq (Emphasizing)

### "Actually / In fact"
- "**Actually,** I'm quite proud of it."
- "**In fact,** we exceeded the target."

### "To be honest / Honestly / To tell you the truth"
- "**To be honest,** I didn't enjoy it."
- "**Honestly,** I was surprised."
- "**To tell you the truth,** I don't remember."

### "Obviously / Clearly"
- "**Obviously,** we can't do that."
- "**Clearly,** there's a misunderstanding."

### "Of course"
- "**Of course** I'll help you."

---

## 10. Şübhə və Qeyri-müəyyənlik (Uncertainty)

### "Maybe / Perhaps / Possibly"
- "**Maybe** we should try again."
- "**Perhaps** next week."

### "I guess / I suppose"
- "**I guess** we have no choice."
- "**I suppose** you're right."

### "Probably / Most likely"
- "I'll **probably** be late."
- "**Most likely,** they'll agree."

### "Kind of / Sort of"
- "It's **kind of** tricky."
- "I'm **sort of** tired."

---

## 11. Fillər (Filler / Thinking Time)

### Ən çox işlədilən
- **"you know"** — "It was, **you know,** quite challenging."
- **"I mean"** — "**I mean,** it wasn't perfect."
- **"like"** — "It was **like** a million different things at once."
- **"sort of / kind of"** — "It's **sort of** hard to explain."
- **"basically"** — "**Basically,** it's a web app."

**Xəbərdarlıq:** Bu sözləri həddindən artıq istifadə etmə — təbii azlıqda yaxşıdır.

### Hesitation pauseları
- "Hmm..."
- "Umm..."
- "Err..."
- "Let me see..."
- "Give me a second..."
- "How can I put this..."

---

## 12. Diqqət Cəlb Etmək (Getting Attention)

- **"Look,"** — "**Look,** I understand your concern."
- **"Listen,"** — "**Listen,** we need to talk."
- **"Here's the thing:"** — "**Here's the thing:** we can't afford it."
- **"The point is"** — "**The point is** we ran out of time."

---

## 13. Söhbət Axını — Keçidlər (Topic Shifts)

### Yeni mövzuya keçmək
- **"By the way,"** — "**By the way,** did you see the email?"
- **"Speaking of,"** — "**Speaking of** meetings, is there one tomorrow?"
- **"That reminds me,"** — "**That reminds me,** I need to call John."
- **"Before I forget,"** — "**Before I forget,** thanks for the help."

### Köhnə mövzuya qayıtmaq
- **"Going back to what you said,"**
- **"As I was saying,"**
- **"Coming back to the point,"**
- **"Where were we?"**

### Söhbət bağlamaq
- **"Anyway,"** — "**Anyway,** we should get started."
- **"Long story short,"**
- **"To make a long story short,"**

---

## 14. Danışıq Dilində Hansıları İşlətməli

### ✅ Çox işlət (natural sounds)
well, so, actually, basically, anyway, I mean, you know, like, by the way, to be honest, at the end of the day

### ⚠️ Az işlət (formal, danışıqda süni)
moreover, furthermore, nevertheless, consequently, hence, thus, albeit, notwithstanding

### ⚠️ Ehtiyatla işlət (həddindən çox olsa qıcıqlandırıcı)
like (çox təkrarlarsan, qeyri-peşəkar)
you know (həddindən artıq boş)
literally (hərfən deməkdir, ancaq çoxu yanlış işlədir)

---

## 15. Müsahibə Üçün Mükəmməl 20 Connector

Müsahibədə danışığını təbii göstərmək üçün:

| Funksiya | İfadə |
|----------|-------|
| Başlamaq | "Well, ..." / "So, ..." |
| Vaxt qazanmaq | "That's a great question." / "Let me think for a moment." |
| Nümunə | "Let me give you an example." / "For instance, ..." |
| Dəqiqləşdirmə | "What I mean is..." / "In other words..." |
| Əlavə fikir | "On top of that, ..." / "What's more, ..." |
| Zidd fikir | "However, ..." / "On the other hand, ..." |
| Səbəb | "The reason being..." / "That's because..." |
| Nəticə | "As a result, ..." / "That's why..." |
| Dürüst | "To be honest, ..." / "Frankly, ..." |
| Yekun | "At the end of the day, ..." / "All in all, ..." |

---

## 16. Praktik Məşq

### Səviyyə 1: Tək cümlə
Hər connector üçün 1 öz cümləni yaz:
- "Well, ___"
- "By the way, ___"
- "At the end of the day, ___"

### Səviyyə 2: Qısa cavab
Müsahibə sualına 3-4 connector işlət:
> **Sual:** "Tell me about a challenge you faced."  
> **Cavab:** "**Well,** one example that comes to mind is from my previous role. **Basically,** we had a critical bug in production. **So,** I stayed late and debugged it. **At the end of the day,** we fixed it together as a team."

### Səviyyə 3: 1 dəqiqəlik monoloq
Bir mövzu seç, 1 dəqiqə danış, minimum 5 fərqli connector işlət.

---

## 17. Səhvləri Yoxla

### Over-use hazardı:
- "**You know,** like, I was **you know,** going to, like, say something." ❌  
  → Təmizlə: "I was going to say something."

### Formal/informal qarışıq:
- "**Moreover,** bro..." ❌  
  → "**What's more,** bro..." ✅

### İngilis dilindən kənar direct translation:
- "From one side... from the other side..." ❌ *(Slavyan calque)*  
  → "**On the one hand... on the other hand...**" ✅
