# Common Mistakes by Azerbaijani Speakers (Azərbaycanlıların Ən Çox Etdiyi Xətalar)

---

## 1. Articles (a, an, the)

Azərbaycan dilində artikel yoxdur — bu ən çox səhv edilən sahədir.

| Wrong | Correct | Rule |
|-------|---------|------|
| I have ~~the~~ dog. | I have **a** dog. | Birinci dəfə qeyri-müəyyən |
| She is ~~a~~ teacher of ~~the~~ year. | She is the teacher of the year. | Yeganə, xüsusi |
| I go to ~~the~~ school. | I go to **school**. | Institusional — article yoxdur |
| ~~The~~ life is beautiful. | Life is beautiful. | Ümumi anlayış — article yoxdur |

---

## 2. Subject-Verb Agreement

| Wrong | Correct | Azerbaijani problem |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| She **go** to work. | She **goes** to work. | 3rd person -s unutulur |
| The team **are** ready. | The team **is** ready. | Qrup = tək |
| Everyone **have** a phone. | Everyone **has** a phone. | Everyone = tək |
| There **is** many problems. | There **are** many problems. | Çoxluqla "are" |

---

## 3. Prepositions

Azərbaycanda şəkilçi olanlar ingilis dilindəki prepozisiyalara uyğun gəlmir:

| Wrong | Correct | Azerbaijani |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| I am **in** home. | I am **at** home. | Evdəyəm |
| He arrived **to** the airport. | He arrived **at** the airport. | Hava limanına çatdı |
| I will call you **in** Monday. | I will call you **on** Monday. | Bazar ertəsi |
| She is married **with** him. | She is married **to** him. | Onunla evlidir |
| I am interested **for** it. | I am interested **in** it. | Buna marağım var |
| We discussed **about** it. | We discussed it. | Discuss prepozisiya istəmir |
| She **entered to** the room. | She **entered** the room. | Enter prepozisiya istəmir |

---

## 4. Tenses

| Wrong | Correct | Azerbaijani problem |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| I am knowing you. | I know you. | Stative verb davamlı formada olmur |
| I am having a car. | I have a car. | "have" (sahib olmaq) — stative |
| I am understanding. | I understand. | Understand — stative |
| She has went. | She **has gone**. | Irregular past participle |
| I didn't **went**. | I didn't **go**. | did + base verb (went yox) |
| When I will come... | When I **come**... | Time clause-da future yoxdur |

---

## 5. Word Order

Azərbaycanda Subyekt-Obyekt-Fel (SOF) sırası var, İngilis dilindəki S-V-O (SVF) fərqlidir:

| Wrong (Azerbaijani order) | Correct (English order) |
|--------------------------|------------------------|
| I yesterday went. | I went **yesterday**. |
| She always coffee drinks. | She **always drinks** coffee. |
| He very fast runs. | He runs **very fast**. |

---

## 6. Double Negatives

Azərbaycan dilindəki çoxlu mənfi ingilis dilinə keçmir:

| Wrong | Correct | Azerbaijani düşüncəsi |
|-------|---------|----------------------|
| I don't know nothing. | I don't know **anything**. | Heç nə bilmirəm |
| She didn't say nothing. | She didn't say **anything**. | O heç nə demədi |
| Nobody didn't come. | **Nobody** came. | Heç kim gəlmədi |

---

## 7. Say vs Tell

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| She **told** "goodbye". | She **said** "goodbye". |
| He **said** me a secret. | He **told** me a secret. |
| They **told** that... | They **said** that... |

**Qayda:** tell + person / say + (that) + clause

---

## 8. Make vs Do

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| **Do** a mistake | **Make** a mistake |
| **Make** homework | **Do** homework |
| **Do** a decision | **Make** a decision |
| **Make** exercise | **Do** exercise |

---

## 9. Adjective Order

İngilis dilindəki sifət sırası: Opinion → Size → Age → Shape → Color → Origin → Material

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| a Italian beautiful car | a beautiful Italian car |
| a old big house | a big old house |
| a wooden round table | a round wooden table |

---

## 10. Countable / Uncountable

| Wrong | Correct | Azerbaijani problem |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| informations | information | Sayılmaz isim |
| advices | advice | Sayılmaz |
| furnitures | furniture | Sayılmaz |
| I need a money | I need money / some money | Sayılmaz |
| How many luggages? | How much luggage? | Sayılmaz |

---

## 11. "There is" vs "It is"

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| It is a book on the table. | **There is** a book on the table. |
| There is cold today. | **It is** cold today. |
| There is 5 o'clock. | **It is** 5 o'clock. |

---

## 12. Gerund vs Infinitive

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| I enjoy to swim. | I enjoy **swimming**. |
| She avoids to go out. | She avoids **going** out. |
| I want going home. | I want **to go** home. |
| He decided leaving. | He decided **to leave**. |

---

## Quick Reference Table

| Category | Remember |
|----------|---------|
| Articles | a (first time), the (known), — (general) |
| 3rd person | She go**es**, He work**s** |
| Prepositions | at home, on Monday, in the morning |
| Tenses | didn't + base verb; no future in time clauses |
| Double negative | anything, anyone (not nothing, nobody + didn't) |
| Uncountable | information, advice, furniture, money |
