# Tech Conversation Topics — Söhbət Mövzuları (IT üçün)

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

100+ söhbət mövzusu və sual — networking events, konfranslar, iş yoldaşları ilə söhbət, müsahibə sonrası söhbət üçün. Hər mövzu üçün:
- Söhbət başlatmaq sualları
- Dərinləşdirmə sualları
- Nümunə cavablar

**A2-B1 səviyyə:** hər sualın cavabını hazırla, 30-60 saniyəlik cavab ver bacar.

---

## 1. İş və Karyera Haqqında

### Söhbət başlatma sualları:
- "What do you do?"
- "Where do you work?"
- "How long have you been in tech?"
- "Are you more backend or frontend?"
- "What's your current project?"

### Dərinləşdirmə sualları:
- "How did you get into programming?"
- "What's the most interesting project you've worked on?"
- "What's the hardest bug you ever fixed?"
- "How's the team culture at your company?"
- "Do you work remotely or in the office?"

### Nümunə cavablar:
> "I'm a backend developer. I work at a fintech startup in Baku — we build payment APIs. I've been in tech for about 4 years now. Got into it during university — started with Python and never looked back."

### Follow-up sualları:
- "Why Python?"
- "What was the switch like from X to Y?"
- "Would you recommend someone else get into this field?"

## 2. Texnologiya Trendləri

### Sualları:
- "What do you think about AI coding assistants?"
- "Are you using Copilot or similar tools?"
- "Do you think [framework] is overhyped?"
- "What technology are you most excited about right now?"
- "Any tech you're tired of hearing about?"

### Nümunə cavablar:

**Müsbət:**
> "I've been using Copilot for about 6 months. Honestly, it's saved me a lot of time — especially for boilerplate code. I still write the hard logic myself, but it handles the boring parts well."

**Tənqidi:**
> "I'm a bit skeptical of all the AI hype. It's useful, but some people talk about it like it's going to replace developers. I think we'll use it as a tool, not a replacement."

**Neytral:**
> "I haven't formed a strong opinion yet. I'm waiting to see how things play out."

## 5. Remote Work

### Sualları:
- "Do you prefer remote or office?"
- "How do you stay focused at home?"
- "Any tips for working from home?"
- "Do you miss the office?"
- "Have you considered being a digital nomad?"

### Nümunə cavablar:

**Remote-ı sevən:**
> "I love remote. I save 90 minutes a day in commute, I can focus better, and I live close to my family. The downside is less spontaneous conversation with colleagues, but we have regular syncs."

**Office-i sevən:**
> "Honestly, I prefer the office. I find it easier to separate work from home life. Plus, I'm energized by being around people — at home I feel isolated sometimes."

**Hybrid:**
> "Hybrid works best for me. Two days in the office for team things, three days at home for focus work. Best of both worlds."

## 31. 3-Pointli Söhbət Strukturu

Hər sualı cavablandıranda 3 hissə:

### 1. Birbaşa cavab
"Yes." / "I think so." / "Not really."

### 2. Səbəb / Misal
"...because in my last project..."

### 3. Sual qaytarma
"What about you?" / "Do you agree?"

### Nümunə:
**Sual:** "Do you prefer working from home?"

**Cavab:**
> "**(1)** Yes, mostly. **(2)** I'm more productive without the office distractions, and I save the commute time for exercise. That said, I do go to the office once a week for team lunch — helps with connection. **(3)** How about you?"

## 35. 60 Saniyəlik Response Şablonu

### Formula:
> "Yes/No. I think ___ because ___. For example, ___. In short, ___. What about you?"

### Praktik nümunə:
**Sual:** "Do you think AI will replace developers?"

> "**(Yes/No)** I don't think so.  
> **(Səbəb)** I believe AI will transform the role — we'll use it as a tool, not fight it.  
> **(Misal)** For example, AI now handles boilerplate code fast, but when our system went down last month, AI didn't fix it. It needed a human who understood the context.  
> **(Yekun)** So short answer: AI changes what developers do, but doesn't replace us.  
> **(Sual qaytar)** What's your take?"

## 36. Azərbaycan Danışanlar üçün Xüsusi Tövsiyyələr

### ❌ Edici səhvlər:
1. **Çox qısa cavab** — "I think yes." (cansıxıcı)
2. **Misal yoxdur** — konkret olmalısan
3. **Düşünmədən cavab** — pauza alma normaldır
4. **"I am agree"** — "I agree"
5. **Sual qaytarmamaq** — söhbət birtərəfli olur

### ✅ Et:
- 60 saniyə cavab standardıdır
- Konkret nümunə hər cavabda
- "What about you?" ilə bitir
- Pauza al — "That's a good question, let me think"

## 37. 1-saatlıq Səhnə — Full Networking Simulation

### Gəliş (0-5 dəq):
- "Hi! I'm Orkhan."
- "How do you know this group?"
- "Have you been before?"

### Söhbət (5-20 dəq):
Əvvəlki mövzulardan 2-3 sual və cavab. Dinlə. Öz təcrübəndən nümunə ver.

### Keçid (20-25 dəq):
- "I want to grab some water — good talking!"
- "Let's connect on LinkedIn — what's your handle?"

### Başqası ilə (25-50 dəq):
Başqa qrupa keç. Prosesi təkrar et.

### Çıxış (50-60 dəq):
- "Have to head out, great meeting you all."
- "Let's grab coffee sometime."
