# Opinion & Discussion Topics (B1-B2)

Bu mövzular fikir bildirmək, mübahisə etmək və arqument qurmaq üçün nəzərdə tutulub.

---

## 1. Education (Təhsil)

1. Should university education be free for everyone?
2. Is homework necessary, or does it cause more harm than good?
3. Should schools teach practical life skills (cooking, finance) instead of academic subjects?
4. Is online learning better or worse than traditional classroom learning?
5. Should students be able to choose all their own subjects?

**Your opinion should include:** a clear stance + reason + example + possible counter-argument

---

## 2. Social Media (Sosial Media)

1. Do you think social media does more harm than good?
2. Should there be age limits on social media platforms?
3. Is social media making people more lonely or more connected?
4. Should influencers be regulated?
5. How much should employers look at employees' social media profiles?

---

## 3. Environment (Ətraf Mühit)

1. Should governments ban single-use plastics?
2. Is recycling enough to save the environment?
3. Should people eat less meat to protect the planet?
4. Who is more responsible for climate change — individuals or corporations?
5. Would you pay higher taxes if the money went to environmental projects?

---

## 4. Health & Lifestyle (Sağlamlıq)

1. Should junk food be taxed more heavily?
2. Is it the government's responsibility to make people healthier?
3. Should physical education be compulsory throughout school?
4. Are people today more or less healthy than 50 years ago?
5. Should healthcare be completely free for everyone?

---

## 5. Technology & Society

1. Are smartphones making us less intelligent?
2. Will robots take most jobs in the future?
3. Should AI-generated content be labelled?
4. Is technology making us more or less productive?
5. Should children under 12 be banned from using smartphones?

---

## 6. Work & Economy

1. Should the working week be reduced to 4 days?
2. Is working from home good or bad for productivity?
3. Should the minimum wage be significantly higher?
4. Is it better to have a secure job or follow your passion?
5. Should very wealthy people pay more taxes?

---

## 7. Culture & Society

1. Is it important for immigrants to adopt the culture of their new country?
2. Are traditional values becoming less important?
3. Should famous celebrities be held to higher moral standards?
4. Has globalisation been mostly positive or negative?
5. Is gender equality fully achieved in your society?

---

## Discussion Phrases to Use

| Starting your view | Supporting | Conceding |
|--------------------|------------|-----------|
| In my opinion, ... | This is because... | Admittedly, ... |
| I strongly believe... | A good example is... | Having said that,... |
| From my perspective,... | Research shows that... | On the other hand,... |
| I tend to think... | This means that... | It could be argued that... |
