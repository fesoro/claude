# Everyday Conversation Topics (A2-B1)

---

## 1. Family (Ailə)

1. How many people are in your family?
2. Describe your family members. What do they look like?
3. What do you usually do together as a family?
4. Who are you closest to in your family? Why?
5. How often do you see your extended family (grandparents, cousins)?

**Useful vocabulary:** only child, sibling, relative, get along with, close-knit family

---

## 2. Daily Routine (Gündəlik Rutin)

1. Describe your typical day from morning to night.
2. What time do you usually wake up? Are you a morning person?
3. What do you do in your free time?
4. What's the best part of your day? Why?
5. Have your daily habits changed recently?

**Useful vocabulary:** commute, habit, routine, tend to, squeeze in time for

---

## 3. Hobbies & Free Time (Hobbilər)

1. What do you enjoy doing in your free time?
2. When did you start this hobby? How did you get into it?
3. Why do you enjoy it?
4. Is it something you do alone or with others?
5. Would you recommend it to others? Why?

**Useful vocabulary:** take up, be into, keen on, pass the time, unwind

---

## 4. Food & Eating (Yemək)

1. What's your favourite food? Why?
2. Do you prefer eating at home or in restaurants?
3. Can you cook? What's your signature dish?
4. Are there any foods you dislike? Why?
5. How important is food in your culture?

**Useful vocabulary:** cuisine, ingredient, recipe, flavour, nutritious, home-cooked

---

## 5. Travel & Places (Səyahət)

1. Where have you travelled to?
2. What was your most memorable trip? Why?
3. Do you prefer the city or the countryside?
4. Where would you most like to visit in the future?
5. What do you think tourists should see in your city?

**Useful vocabulary:** sightseeing, local culture, off the beaten track, must-see, landmark

---

## 6. Work & Study (İş & Təhsil)

1. What do you do? / What are you studying?
2. Why did you choose this job/subject?
3. What do you enjoy most about your work/studies?
4. What are your career goals?
5. How do you manage work-life balance?

**Useful vocabulary:** demanding, rewarding, deadlines, ambition, career path

---

## 7. Weather & Seasons (Hava & Mövsümlər)

1. What's your favourite season? Why?
2. What's the weather like in your city?
3. How does the weather affect your mood?
4. What do you usually do in summer/winter?
5. Have you noticed changes in weather patterns recently?

**Useful vocabulary:** scorching, freezing, mild, unpredictable, weather forecast

---

## 8. Technology (Texnologiya)

1. How many hours a day do you spend on your phone?
2. What apps do you use most often?
3. How has technology changed daily life?
4. Can you live without internet for a week?
5. What technology do you wish existed?

**Useful vocabulary:** gadget, social media, screen time, digital, connectivity
