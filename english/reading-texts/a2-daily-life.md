# A2 Reading: A Typical Day (Gündəlik Həyat)

---

## Text

**My Name is Sara**

My name is Sara and I live in a small flat in the city centre. I'm a teacher at a primary school. I usually wake up at 6:30 in the morning. First, I have a shower and get dressed. Then I make breakfast — usually toast with eggs and a cup of coffee.

I walk to school because it's only ten minutes away. My lessons start at 8 o'clock. I teach children between the ages of six and ten. I really enjoy my job because the children are funny and enthusiastic.

I finish work at four o'clock in the afternoon. On the way home, I sometimes stop at the supermarket to buy food. In the evening, I usually cook dinner and watch TV. On weekdays, I go to bed at about ten o'clock. But at the weekends, I like to stay up late and meet friends.

My favourite day is Saturday. I often go to the market in the morning and then meet my friends for lunch. We usually go to a café in the park. In the summer, we sit outside and enjoy the sunshine.

---

## Comprehension Questions (Anlama sualları)

1. Where does Sara live?
2. What is her job?
3. What time does she wake up?
4. How does she travel to work?
5. What does she do on the way home from work?
6. What does she do in the evenings on weekdays?
7. What is her favourite day? Why?
8. What does she do on Saturday morning?

---

## True or False?

1. Sara lives in a big house. (T / F)
2. She walks to school. (T / F)
3. She teaches teenagers. (T / F)
4. She finishes work at 5 o'clock. (T / F)
5. She stays up late on weekdays. (T / F)
6. On Saturdays, she meets friends for lunch. (T / F)

## Answers (Cavablar)

### Comprehension:
1. In a small flat in the city centre.
2. She is a teacher at a primary school.
3. At 6:30 in the morning.
4. She walks.
5. She sometimes stops at the supermarket.
6. She cooks dinner and watches TV.
7. Saturday — she goes to the market and meets friends.
8. She goes to the market.

### True or False:
1. False (small flat)
2. True
3. False (6-10 year olds)
4. False (4 o'clock)
5. False (only at weekends)
6. True
