# That / Which / Who / Whose — Relative Pronouns

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Pronoun | Kim/Nə üçün | İşləndiyi yer |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **who** | insanlar | subject və ya object |
| **whom** | insanlar (object, formal) | object |
| **which** | əşyalar, heyvanlar, ideyalar | subject və object |
| **that** | insanlar və ya əşyalar | defining clauses |
| **whose** | mülkiyyət (kimin?) | possessive |
| **where** | yer | yer clause |
| **when** | vaxt | vaxt clause |
| **why** | səbəb | reason clause |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - İnsan → **who / that**
> - Əşya → **which / that**
> - Mülkiyyət → **whose**
> - Yer → **where**
> - Vaxt → **when**

---

## 1. Who — İnsanlar üçün

### Subject kimi

- The man **who** called me is my brother.
- People **who** exercise live longer.
- She is the one **who** helped me.

### Object kimi

- The woman **who** I met yesterday. (informal)
- The person (**who**) I talked to. (parentheses: who silinə bilər)

### Nümunələr — iş konteksti

- The developer **who** wrote this code is excellent.
- A manager **who** listens is a good manager.
- Engineers **who** document their work save time.

---

## 2. Which — Əşyalar, Heyvanlar üçün

### Subject və object kimi

- The book **which** I bought is interesting.
- The dog **which** barked is mine.
- The software **which** we use is outdated.

### "which" — əlavə məlumat verir (non-defining)

Non-defining clauses-da which istifadə olunur (vergül ilə):

- My laptop, **which** I bought last year, is broken.
- The book, **which** was written in 1950, is a classic.

> **Diqqət:** Non-defining clauses-da `that` YANLIŞDIR.
> - ✓ My laptop, **which** is new, works fine.
> - ✗ My laptop, that is new, works fine.

---

## 3. That — Həm İnsan Həm Əşya üçün

`that` həm insan, həm əşya üçün işlənə bilər — **yalnız defining clauses-da**.

### Nümunələr

- The person **that** called me. (= who)
- The book **that** I read. (= which)
- The car **that** he bought. (= which)

### That vs Who/Which — Fərq

| Kontekst | Məsləhət |
|----------|----------|
| İnsan (defining) | who / that — ikisi OK, who daha rəsmi |
| Əşya (defining) | which / that — that daha yaygındır |
| Non-defining (vergül ilə) | yalnız who / which, **that YOXDUR** |

### Non-defining ilə that — YANLIŞ

- ✗ My car, **that** is red, is fast.
- ✓ My car, **which** is red, is fast.

---

## 4. Defining vs Non-Defining Clauses

### Defining — vacib məlumat (vergülsüz)

Cümləni şərh etmək üçün lazımdır. Olmasa, məna dəyişir.

- The book **that I bought** is interesting. (hansı kitab?)
- The woman **who called** is my sister. (hansı qadın?)

### Non-defining — əlavə məlumat (vergüllü)

Əlavə məlumat. Olmasa, əsas məna dəyişmir.

- My father, **who lives in Ganja**, is a teacher. (əlavə məlumat)
- Laravel, **which is a PHP framework**, is popular.

---

## 5. Whose — Mülkiyyət

"Kimin?" — Həm insan, həm əşya üçün mülkiyyət.

### Nümunələr

- The man **whose** car is stolen.
- A writer **whose** books I love.
- The company **whose** CEO resigned.
- A developer **whose** code is clean.

---

## 6. Where / When / Why — Vaxt, Yer, Səbəb

### Where — yer

- The office **where** I work.
- The city **where** I was born.

### When — vaxt

- The day **when** we met.
- The year **when** I graduated.

### Why — səbəb

- The reason **why** I came.
- This is why I'm here. (why işlənir, amma "why" silinə bilər: the reason I came)

---

## 7. Relative Pronoun Omission — Silmə Qaydası

Object kimi işlənəndə relative pronoun silinə bilər (defining clauses-da):

### Silinə bilər

- The book (**that**) I bought.
- The woman (**who**) I met.

### Silinə bilməz (subject kimi işlənəndə)

- The book **that** is on the table. ✓ (silinməz)
- ✗ The book is on the table.

### Sadə qayda

- Pronoun + subject + verb → silinə bilər
- Pronoun + verb → silinməz

| Silinəbilən | Silinməyən |
|-------------|------------|
| The book (that) I read. | The book **that** arrived today. |
| The person (who) she met. | The person **who** called. |

---

## 8. Cümlə Quruluşu — Nümunələr

### Sadə relative clause

- The man [**who** lives next door] is friendly.
- This is the book [**which** I recommend].

### Which ilə cümlə başlamağı

- Tomorrow is Monday, **which** I don't like. (bütün cümləyə istinad)
- She was late, **which** made me angry. (bütün hadisəyə)

> `which` bütün cümləyə də istinad edə bilər!

---

## 9. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Öz-özünü təqdim etmə

- I'm someone **who** thrives under pressure.
- I'm a developer **who** enjoys learning new technologies.
- I'm looking for a role **where** I can grow.

### Layihələr

- I worked on a project **which** handled 1M requests/day.
- The team **that** I led had 5 engineers.
- This is a feature **that** I designed from scratch.
- The system **whose** performance I optimized...

### Past experience

- At my previous company, **where** I worked for 3 years, I...
- In 2020, **when** I joined the team, we had 2 microservices.

---

## 10. Xüsusi Strukturlar

### With prepositions (formal)

Formal yazıda preposition + whom/which:

| Informal | Formal |
|----------|--------|
| The person **who** I talked to. | The person **to whom** I talked. |
| The book **which** I read about. | The book **about which** I read. |
| The friend **that** I work with. | The friend **with whom** I work. |

### Whom — formal object

- The person **whom** I met. (formal)
- = The person **who** I met. (informal, more common)
- = The person I met. (ən natural)

---

## 11. Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| The book what I read. | The book **that / which** I read. | "what" relative pronoun kimi işlənmir |
| The man which lives here. | The man **who / that** lives here. | insan → who |
| The dog who is barking. | The dog **which / that** is barking. | heyvan → which |
| My car, that is red, is fast. | My car, **which** is red, is fast. | non-defining → which |
| This is John, who his wife is a doctor. | This is John, **whose** wife is a doctor. | mülkiyyət → whose |
| The book I read it yesterday. | The book **(that) I read** yesterday. | obyekt təkrar yox |
| The place which I work. | The place **where** I work. | yer → where |
| The reason which I came. | The reason **why** I came. | səbəb → why |
| The person who I talked. | The person **who I talked to**. (informal) / **to whom I talked**. (formal) | preposition lazım |

---

## 12. Qısa Seçim Cədvəli

```
İNSAN → who (or that for defining)
ƏŞYA → which (or that for defining)
MÜLKİYYƏT → whose
YER → where
VAXT → when
SƏBƏB → why
NON-DEFINING → who / which (NEVER that)
```

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Düzgün pronoun seçin

1. The man ___ lives next door is a doctor.
2. The book ___ I bought yesterday is great.
3. My mother, ___ is 65, is still working.
4. The company ___ hired me is in London.
5. The place ___ we met is beautiful.
6. A person ___ car was stolen reported it.
7. I don't know the reason ___ he left.
8. The dog ___ is barking is loud.

**Cavablar:** 1-who/that, 2-that/which, 3-who, 4-which/that, 5-where, 6-whose, 7-why, 8-which/that

### 2. Pronoun silinə bilərmi? (Yes/No)

1. The book (that) I read. → Yes
2. The man (who) lives here. → No (subject)
3. The woman (that) I met. → Yes
4. The song (which) is playing. → No
5. The house (that) he built. → Yes

### 3. Defining və ya non-defining?

1. My brother, who lives in Ganja, is a teacher. → Non-defining
2. The student who finishes first will win. → Defining
3. Python, which was created in 1991, is popular. → Non-defining
4. The book that I recommended is great. → Defining

### 4. Tərcümə edin

1. Yanımda yaşayan adam həkimdir. → The man **who / that** lives next door is a doctor.
2. Satın aldığım kitab maraqlıdır. → The book (**that / which**) I bought is interesting.
3. Laravel, PHP framework olan, populyardır. → Laravel, **which** is a PHP framework, is popular.
4. Çalışdığım yer Bakıdadır. → The place **where** I work is in Baku.
