# Either / Neither / Both / None — İkili və Çoxlu Seçimlər

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Söz | Məna | Sayı | Fel |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| **both** | hər ikisi | 2 (ikisi də) | plural |
| **either** | biri / ya biri ya digəri | 2 | singular (formal) |
| **neither** | nə biri nə o biri | 2 (inkar) | singular (formal) |
| **none** | heç biri | 3+ | single or plural |
| **all** | hamısı | 3+ | plural |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - 2 obyekt üçün: **both** (ikisi də), **either** (biri), **neither** (heç biri)
> - 3+ obyekt üçün: **all** (hamısı), **any** (biri), **none** (heç biri)

---

## 1. Both — Hər İkisi

İki obyektin **ikisi də**.

### Strukturlar

| Forma | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **both** + noun | **Both** books are interesting. |
| **both of** + (the/my/us/them) | **Both of** my sisters are teachers. |
| **both** (pronoun) | **Both** are fine. |
| Subject + verb + **both** | They **both** like coffee. |
| Auxiliary + **both** + verb | They are **both** happy. |

### Nümunələr

- **Both** cars are new.
- I like **both** of them.
- They **both** came to the party.
- We are **both** tired.
- **Both of** us agreed.

### Diqqət: "both... and..."

- I speak **both** English **and** Russian.
- She is **both** smart **and** kind.

---

## 2. Either — Biri (İkidən)

"Biri və ya o biri" — iki variantdan biri.

### Strukturlar

| Forma | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **either** + singular noun | **Either** book is fine. |
| **either of** + plural | **Either of** the books is fine. (formal) |
| **either** (pronoun) | **Either** will do. |
| **either... or** | Take **either** tea **or** coffee. |
| Müsbət cümlələrdə razılıq | I want **either**. |
| İnkar cavab (me neither / I don't either) | "I don't like it." — "I don't **either**." |

### Nümunələr

- You can sit at **either** end of the table.
- **Either** date works for me.
- I don't want **either**. (heç biri — either + inkar)
- You can have **either** pizza **or** pasta.

### "Either + of" + cəm

`either of` sonrası cəm, amma fel **tək** (formal) və ya cəm (informal):

- **Either of** the options is acceptable. (formal — is)
- **Either of** my parents are coming. (informal — are)

---

## 3. Neither — Heç Biri (İkidən)

"Nə biri, nə o biri". Müsbət fel, amma inkar məna.

### Strukturlar

| Forma | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **neither** + singular noun | **Neither** option is good. |
| **neither of** + plural | **Neither of** us is ready. (formal) |
| **neither... nor** | **Neither** John **nor** Mary came. |

### Nümunələr

- **Neither** answer is correct.
- **Neither of** my parents speaks English.
- I like **neither** of them.
- **Neither** John **nor** Mary knew.

### Verb agreement — neither

- **Neither** + singular noun → tək fel:
  - **Neither** book **is** here.
- **Neither of** + plural → tək fel (formal), cəm (informal):
  - **Neither of** them **is/are** coming.
- **Neither... nor** → son subyektə uyğun:
  - **Neither** the students **nor** the teacher **was** aware.
  - **Neither** the teacher **nor** the students **were** aware.

---

## 4. None — Heç Biri (3+)

3 və ya daha çox obyektin **heç biri**.

### Strukturlar

- **None of** + plural noun
- **None of** + uncountable noun

### Nümunələr

- **None of** the students passed. (cəm)
- **None of** my friends came. (cəm)
- **None of** the water was clean. (sayılmayan)
- I have **none**. (qalmayıb)

### Verb agreement — none

- Formal: tək fel
- Informal: cəm olur

- **None of** them **is / are** here. (hər ikisi qəbul edilir)
- **None of** the water **is** drinkable. (uncountable → tək)

---

## 5. Müqayisə — 2 vs 3+

| Say | "hamısı" | "hər hansı biri" | "heç biri" |
|-----|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| 2 | **both** | **either** | **neither** |
| 3+ | **all** | **any** | **none** |

### Nümunə

- I have 2 brothers. **Both** are doctors. (hər ikisi)
- I have 3 brothers. **All** are doctors. (hamısı)

- Do you want tea or coffee? **Either** is fine. (biri)
- Do you want tea, coffee or juice? **Any** is fine. (hər hansı)

- I don't like tea or coffee. I like **neither**. (heç biri)
- I don't like tea, coffee or juice. I like **none**. (heç biri)

---

## 6. "Me Too", "Me Neither" — Razılıq / İnkar

### Müsbət razılıq — "me too" / "so do I"

- "I like coffee." — "**Me too**." / "**So do I**."
- "I'm tired." — "**Me too**." / "**So am I**."

### Mənfi razılıq — "me neither" / "neither do I"

- "I don't like tea." — "**Me neither**." / "**Neither do I**."
- "I'm not hungry." — "**Me neither**." / "**Neither am I**."

### "So" və "Neither" qaydaları

- Müsbət → **so** + auxiliary + subject
  - I am happy. **So am I**.
  - He works hard. **So do I**.
- Mənfi → **neither** + auxiliary + subject
  - I don't smoke. **Neither do I**.
  - She isn't tired. **Neither am I**.

## 7. Sintaksis Testi

Fel ilə uyğunluq yoxla:

| Subyekt | Fel |
|---------|-----|
| Both books | are |
| Either book | is |
| Neither book | is |
| Either of them | is (formal) |
| Neither of them | is (formal) |
| Both of us | are |
| None of them | is/are |
| All of them | are |

---

## 8. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Both

- I have experience in **both** Java **and** Python.
- We can **both** contribute to this project.
- **Both** approaches are valid.

### Either / Neither

- **Either** option works for me.
- **Neither** candidate was a perfect fit.
- I'd be happy with **either** of those roles.

### None / All

- **None of** the tests failed.
- **All of** our sprints are documented.
- I had **no** feedback. (=**no feedback** — daha natural)

---

## 9. Xüsusi İfadələr

### Both

- both **at once** / **at the same time**
- have the **best of both worlds**
- on **both sides**

### Either

- in **either case**
- on **either side**

### Neither

- on **neither side**
- **Neither** is **fine**.

### None

- second to **none** — ən yaxşı
- **none** whatsoever — heç bir

---

## 10. Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| Both books is new. | Both books **are** new. | both → plural |
| Either of them are right. | Either of them **is** right. (formal) | formal: singular |
| Neither answers is correct. | **Neither answer** is correct. | neither + singular |
| None of the students was came. | None of the students **came**. (fel past simple yalnız) | tense agreement |
| I like both of it. | I like **both of them**. | of + plural/pronoun |
| Either John or Mary are coming. | Either John or Mary **is** coming. | son subyektə (tək) |
| Me too. (negative context: "I don't like...") | **Me neither.** / **Neither do I.** | neqativdə |
| So do I. (negative context) | **Neither do I.** | neqativ → neither |
| I don't have neither. | I don't have **either**. / I have **neither**. | ikiqat inkar yox |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. both / either / neither / all / none seçin

1. ___ of my parents speak English — only one.
2. ___ of my parents speak English — neither does.
3. ___ of my parents speak English — they both do.
4. I have 3 cats. ___ of them are friendly.
5. ___ of us like this movie — zero people liked it.
6. Take ___ seat — any of the two.

**Cavablar:** 1-One (or Only one of...), 2-Neither, 3-Both, 4-All, 5-None, 6-either

### 2. so / neither cavablar

1. "I love sushi." → "So ___ I."
2. "I don't eat meat." → "Neither ___ I."
3. "She is tired." → "So ___ I."
4. "We aren't going." → "Neither ___ they."
5. "He works from home." → "So ___ I."

**Cavablar:** 1-do, 2-do, 3-am, 4-are, 5-do

### 3. Düzgün forma seçin

1. Neither my sister nor my brother (is / are) at home.
2. Both of us (is / are) ready.
3. Either option (is / are) fine.
4. None of the students (has / have) arrived yet.
5. All of the tickets (was / were) sold.

**Cavablar:** 1-is (son subyektə — brother), 2-are, 3-is, 4-has/have (formal: has), 5-were

### 4. Tərcümə edin

1. İkimiz də yorğunuq. → We are **both** tired. / **Both of us** are tired.
2. Heç birini bəyənmirəm. → I like **neither** of them.
3. Biri yaxşıdır, digəri deyil. → **One** is good, **the other** isn't. / (2 idi → the other)
4. Həm kofe həm çay içirəm. → I drink **both** coffee **and** tea.
5. Mən də yorğunam. (pozitiv) → **Me too.** / **So am I.**
6. Mən də yorulmadım. (neqativ) → **Me neither.** / **Neither am I.**
