# A Few vs Few / A Little vs Little — "A"nın Gizli Gücü

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Fərq — "A" varmı?

"A few / a little" və "few / little" formaları demək olar eyni görünür, amma **mənaları əkisidir**.

| Forma | Məna | Tonu |
|-------|------|------|
| **a few** / **a little** | bir az, var | **pozitiv** (yetər) |
| **few** / **little** | çox az, yoxa yaxın | **neqativ** (çatışmır) |

> **Qayda:**
> - **a few / a little** = "bir neçə, bir az" (var — OK)
> - **few / little** = "demək olar heç" (yoxdur — problem)

---

## 1. Sayıla Bilən / Bilməyən Fərqi

| Forma | Ne ilə? |
|-------|---------|
| **a few** / **few** | sayıla bilən cəm (books, friends, people) |
| **a little** / **little** | sayılmayan (water, time, money) |

### Nümunələr

| Söz | Nümunə |
|-----|--------|
| a few **friends** | several friends (bir neçə dost) |
| few **friends** | almost no friends (çox az dost) |
| a little **water** | some water (bir az su) |
| little **water** | almost no water (çox az su) |

---

## 2. A Few — "bir neçə" (pozitiv)

Bir neçə var — kifayətdir, problem deyil.

### Nümunələr

- I have **a few** friends in London. (bir neçə dost — yaxşıdır)
- She has **a few** books to recommend.
- We have **a few** minutes left.
- I speak **a few** languages.
- There are **a few** things to discuss.

**Ton:** "Var, yaxşıdır" (positive).

---

## 3. Few — "çox az, demək olar heç" (neqativ)

Çox az — lazım olandan azdır, problem var.

### Nümunələr

- I have **few** friends in London. (çox az dostum var — təklik)
- **Few** people attended the meeting. (çox az adam gəldi — qayda pozuldu)
- She has **few** options. (az variant — çətin)
- **Few** students passed the exam. (az keçdi — pis)

**Ton:** "Yoxdur, pisdir" (negative).

### Fərq nümunəsi

| A few (pozitiv) | Few (neqativ) |
|-----------------|---------------|
| I have **a few** friends. (bir neçə dost var, yaxşıdır) | I have **few** friends. (çox az dost var, üzüntülü) |
| **A few** people came. (bir neçə adam gəldi) | **Few** people came. (çox az gəldi) |

---

## 4. A Little — "bir az" (pozitiv)

Bir az var — kifayətdir.

### Nümunələr

- I have **a little** time for a coffee.
- She knows **a little** Spanish.
- There is **a little** sugar left.
- I've got **a little** money for lunch.
- Can you give me **a little** help?

**Ton:** "Var, yaxşıdır".

---

## 5. Little — "çox az, demək olar heç" (neqativ)

Çox az — problemdir.

### Nümunələr

- I have **little** time today. (vaxtım yoxdur, sıxışığam)
- She has **little** experience. (təcrübəsizdir)
- There is **little** hope. (ümid yoxdur)
- He paid **little** attention. (diqqət etmədi)

**Ton:** "Yoxdur, çatışmır" (negative).

### Fərq nümunəsi

| A little (pozitiv) | Little (neqativ) |
|--------------------|------------------|
| I have **a little** money. (bir az pulum var, alıcəm) | I have **little** money. (çox az pulum var, çətinlik) |
| He has **a little** experience. (bir az təcrübəli) | He has **little** experience. (təcrübəsizdir) |

---

## 6. Praktik Cədvəl

| Nə? | Pozitiv "var" | Neqativ "çatışmır" |
|-----|---------------|-------------------|
| Sayıla bilən cəm | **a few** books | **few** books |
| Sayılmayan | **a little** water | **little** water |

### Nümunə cütləri

- I have **a few** good ideas. (yaxşı) ↔ I have **few** good ideas. (çox az)
- She has **a little** patience. (bir az səbri var) ↔ She has **little** patience. (səbirsizdir)
- There are **a few** things to do. (bir neçə iş var) ↔ There are **few** things to do. (az iş)

---

## 7. Azərbaycanca Uyğunluq

| Azərbaycanca | İngiliscə |
|--------------|-----------|
| Bir neçə dost | **A few** friends |
| Az dost (dostum yoxdur kimi) | **Few** friends |
| Bir az su | **A little** water |
| Az su (yoxdur kimi) | **Little** water |
| Bir az vaxt var | I have **a little** time |
| Az vaxt var (yoxa yaxın) | I have **little** time |

> **Azərbaycanca "az"** bəzən "a few/a little" (bir az var) və bəzən "few/little" (çox az, yoxdur) mənasında olur. İngiliscədə bu fərq vacibdir.

---

## 8. Alternativləri — Daha da natural

Sorğu üçün sadə alternativlər:

### Pozitiv (bir az)

- **a few** → some, a couple of, several
- **a little** → some, a bit of, a small amount of

### Neqativ (çox az)

- **few** → not many, hardly any, very few
- **little** → not much, hardly any, very little

### Nümunələr

- I have **a few** / **some** ideas.
- We have **little** / **hardly any** time left.

---

## 9. Only / Very ilə güclənməsi

### Only a few / Only a little = "yalnız bir neçə" (məhdud, amma neqativ deyil)

- **Only a few** people came. (az, amma göstərilir)
- I have **only a little** time.

### Very few / Very little = "çox az" (güclü neqativ)

- **Very few** students attended.
- There is **very little** left.

---

## 10. Quite a few / Quite a little = "xeyli" (pozitiv)

Bu ifadələr "xeyli, olduqca çox" mənasındadır (əksinə!):

- **Quite a few** people came. (xeyli adam gəldi)
- I've been to Istanbul **quite a few** times.
- ⚠ "quite a little" nadir — adətən **quite a lot of** işlənir.

---

## 11. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Pozitiv təqdimat

- I have **a few** years of experience in backend. (təcrübəm var)
- I speak **a little** German. (bir az bilirəm)
- We have **a few** strong candidates.
- I've got **a little** experience with Docker.

### Neqativ (çətinlik, zəiflik)

- We had **little** time to prepare.
- There were **few** resources available.
- He had **little** experience in that area.

### Müsahibə sualları / cavablar

- Q: "How much JavaScript do you know?"
- A: "I know **a little** — enough to read code." (pozitiv, özünü azaltma)

- Q: "Have you had many bugs in production?"
- A: "Very **few** — we test carefully." (pozitiv, az olduğunu vurğulayır)

---

## 12. Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| I have little friends. (bir neçə) | I have **a few** friends. | pozitiv var → a few |
| She has a little money, so she can't buy it. | She has **little** money, so she can't buy it. | yoxdur → little |
| A few people came, but it was great! (düzgün?) | ✓ | pozitiv: bir neçə var |
| Few of water is left. | **Little** water is left. / **A little** water is left. | water sayılmayan → little / a little |
| I have a few times for you. | I have **a little** time for you. | time sayılmayan → a little |
| Little friends of mine come here. | **A few friends** of mine come here. / **Few friends** (yox, üzüntülü) | kontekst → a few |

---

## 13. Qısa Test

| Cümlə | Hansı? |
|-------|--------|
| Only ___ people support this idea. (çox az, problemdir) | **few** |
| I have ___ tickets for Saturday. Want one? | **a few** |
| She has ___ patience with slow people. (yoxdur) | **little** |
| Can you spare ___ minutes? (bir az) | **a few** |
| There's ___ milk in the fridge. Don't worry, we have some. | **a little** |
| There's ___ milk. We need to buy more. | **little** |

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. a few / few / a little / little seçin

1. I have ___ time, so let's hurry.
2. She has ___ friends — only 2 or 3.
3. ___ people know this secret. (çox az)
4. There is ___ water in the bottle — enough for a glass.
5. Can you give me ___ minutes?
6. We had ___ hope of success — it was a dark moment.
7. I speak ___ English. (bir az)
8. ___ students passed the difficult exam.

**Cavablar:** 1-little, 2-a few, 3-Few, 4-a little, 5-a few, 6-little, 7-a little, 8-Few

### 2. Cümlələri tonuna görə sıralayın (pozitiv / neqativ)

1. I have **few** opportunities. → neqativ
2. I have **a few** opportunities. → pozitiv
3. She showed **little** interest. → neqativ
4. She showed **a little** interest. → pozitiv

### 3. Tərcümə edin

1. Bir neçə sualım var. → I have **a few** questions.
2. Az vaxtım var. (yoxa yaxın) → I have **little** time.
3. Bir az kömək lazımdır. → I need **a little** help.
4. Çox az adam bilir. → **Few** people know.
5. Həftəsonu üçün bir neçə planım var. → I have **a few** plans for the weekend.
