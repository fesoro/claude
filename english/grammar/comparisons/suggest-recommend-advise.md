# Suggest / Recommend / Advise — Tövsiyə Fellər

## Səviyyə
B1 (interview və workplace kritik)

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Fel | Tonu | Həm də isim formasında? |
|-----|------|-------------------------|
| **suggest** | təklif edirəm | ✓ suggestion (noun) |
| **recommend** | tövsiyə edirəm (güclü) | ✓ recommendation |
| **advise** | məsləhət verirəm | ✓ advice (noun) |
| **propose** | formal təklif | ✓ proposal |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **suggest** = sadə təklif
> - **recommend** = güclü / təsdiq olunmuş tövsiyə
> - **advise** = səlahiyyətli / peşəkar məsləhət
> - **propose** = rəsmi / formal təklif (toy, biznes)

---

## 1. Suggest — Sadə Təklif

"Təklif edirəm" — ən neytral variant.

### Struktur

| Forma | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **suggest + -ing** | I **suggest going** to the park. |
| **suggest + that + clause** | I **suggest (that) we go** to the park. |
| **suggest + noun** | I **suggest a different approach**. |

### ⚠ Diqqət — Struktur Səhvi

**suggest + obj + to + V** STRUKTURU YOXDUR:

- ✗ I suggest **you to go**. (ən çox edilən səhv!)
- ✓ I suggest **going**. (gerund)
- ✓ I suggest **that you go**. (clause)
- ✓ I suggest **you should go**. (that silinə bilər)

### Nümunələr

- I **suggest** we start with the design.
- Can you **suggest** a good restaurant?
- I **suggested** that we hire more engineers.
- She **suggested** **meeting** next week.

---

## 2. Recommend — Güclü Tövsiyə

"Tövsiyə edirəm" — təcrübəyə / keyfiyyətə əsaslanan.

### Struktur

| Forma | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **recommend + -ing** | I **recommend reading** this book. |
| **recommend + that + clause** | I **recommend (that) you try** it. |
| **recommend + noun** | I **recommend this restaurant**. |
| **recommend + sb to + noun** | I was **recommended to the manager**. (passive) |

### ⚠ Eyni səhv

- ✗ I recommend **you to go**. (yanlış)
- ✓ I recommend **going**. (gerund)
- ✓ I recommend **that you go**. (clause)

### Nümunələr

- I **recommend** this course — it's excellent.
- Can you **recommend** a good book on Go?
- Doctors **recommend** exercising daily.
- I **recommended** him for the job.
- I'd **recommend** **trying** Docker for this.

### Formal recommend — sifət + subjunctive

Formal yazıda:
- I recommend that he **be** promoted. (formal subjunctive)
- I recommend that she **attend**. (that + base verb — formal)

---

## 3. Advise — Səlahiyyətli Məsləhət

"Məsləhət verirəm" — adətən ekspert və ya səlahiyyətli şəxsdən.

### Struktur

| Forma | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **advise + sb + to + V1** | I **advise you to read** the contract. ✓ (sb + to işlənir!) |
| **advise + -ing** | I **advise waiting** before deciding. |
| **advise + against + -ing** | I **advise against investing** now. |
| **advise + on/about** | I'll **advise on** the matter. |

### ⚠ advise — **sb + to** ilə işlənir! (fərq!)

- ✓ I **advise you to** see a doctor. (yalnız advise ilə + to işlənir)
- ✓ I **advise seeing** a doctor. (gerund)
- ✗ I suggest / recommend you to see a doctor.

### Nümunələr

- The doctor **advised** me **to rest**.
- I **advise you to take** the offer.
- She **advised against spending** so much.
- Legal teams **advise on** contracts.

### Advise vs Advice (noun)

- **advise** = verb (fel)
- **advice** = noun (isim, uncountable)

- Can you give me some **advice**? (isim)
- I **advise** you to wait. (fel)

> **Diqqət:** `advice` uncountable! Çoxul yoxdur.
> - ✓ some advice, a piece of advice
> - ✗ advices, an advice

---

## Üç Fellin Müqayisəsi — Struktur

| Struktur | suggest | recommend | advise |
|----------|---------|-----------|--------|
| + gerund (-ing) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| + that + clause | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| + noun | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| + sb + to + V | **✗** | **✗** | **✓** |

### Eyni məna — üç fərqli forma

| Fel | Nümunə |
|-----|--------|
| suggest | I **suggest going** to the park. / I **suggest (that) we go** to the park. |
| recommend | I **recommend going** to the park. / I **recommend (that) we go** to the park. |
| advise | I **advise going** to the park. / I **advise you to go** to the park. |

---

## 4. Propose — Formal / Rəsmi Təklif

Çox formal, biznes və ya rəsmi situasiyalarda.

### Nümunələr

- I **propose** a new meeting time.
- The committee **proposed** changes.
- He **proposed** to her. (evlilik təklifi)

---

## 5. Əlaqəli Fellər

### Offer — təklif etmək (vermək)

- I **offered** him a job. (vermək)
- He **offered** to help. (kömək təklif)

### Insist — israr etmək

- I **insist** that you stay.
- She **insisted on** paying.

### Urge — təhrik etmək (güclü)

- I **urge** you to reconsider.

---

## 6. Formal vs Informal

### Informal / Günlük

- "Why don't we try it?"
- "Let's go for Plan B."
- "Maybe we could..."

### Semi-formal

- "I **suggest** we try it."
- "I **recommend** we go with Plan B."

### Formal

- "I would **propose** Plan B."
- "I'd **advise** against this approach."
- "My **recommendation** is..."

---

## 7. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Müsahibədə fikir bildirmə

- "I **would suggest** a phased rollout." (təklif)
- "I **recommend** using event-driven architecture here."
- "I **would advise** caution with this library — it's outdated."
- "My **recommendation** is to start with the MVP."

### Komanda içi

- "Let me **suggest** an alternative."
- "Can I **recommend** we take a different approach?"
- "I'd **advise against** deploying on a Friday."

### Email / Yazılı

- "I'd like to **recommend** John for the role."
- "**I advise you to** review the latest policy."
- "**We suggest** scheduling a follow-up call."

## 8. Yaygın İfadələr

### Suggest ilə

- Any **suggestions**?
- I have a **suggestion**.
- To **suggest** otherwise would be...
- **Suggestion** box (təklif qutusu)

### Recommend ilə

- I highly **recommend**.
- **Recommend** with confidence.
- Would you **recommend** this?
- **Recommendation** letter.

### Advise ilə

- Be **advised** that... (formal, bilginizə olsun)
- **Advice** column (məsləhət kolunu, qəzetdə)
- Financial **advisor** (maliyyə məsləhətçisi)
- Get **advice** from an expert.

## 9. Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| I suggest you to go. | I **suggest you go**. / I **suggest going**. | suggest + sb + to yox |
| I recommend you to read it. | I **recommend reading** it. / I **recommend that you read** it. | recommend + sb + to yox |
| Can you give me an advice? | Can you give me **some advice**? / **a piece of advice**. | advice uncountable |
| I advised him going home. | I **advised him to go** home. / I **advised going** home. | advise + sb + to |
| She suggested me to try. | She **suggested (that) I try**. | suggest + sb + to yox |
| I will advice you. | I will **advise** you. | verb = advise |
| Good advise! | Good **advice**! | noun = advice |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Düzgün formaya qoyun

1. I suggest ___ (go / going / to go) early.
2. She recommended ___ (read / reading / to read) this book.
3. My doctor advised me ___ (take / taking / to take) vitamins.
4. Can you suggest ___ (us / to us / ∅) a good restaurant?
5. I advise ___ (against / on / for) investing in that company.

**Cavablar:** 1-going, 2-reading, 3-to take, 4-∅ (direct: suggest a restaurant to me), 5-against

### 2. Səhvi düzəldin

1. I suggest you to try it. → I **suggest trying** it. / I **suggest you try** it.
2. She advised me trying. → She **advised me to try**. / She **advised trying**.
3. Can I have advices? → Can I have **some advice**? / **a piece of advice**.
4. I will advice you. → I will **advise** you.
5. Doctor recommended me drinking water. → The doctor **recommended drinking** water. / **recommended that I drink** water.

### 3. Tərcümə edin

1. Sizə bu kitabı tövsiyə edirəm. → I **recommend this book** (to you).
2. Sizə bu kitabı oxumağı məsləhət görürəm. → I **advise you to read this book**.
3. Sabah görüşməyi təklif edirəm. → I **suggest meeting tomorrow**.
4. Sizə bir məsləhətim var. → I have some **advice** for you. / I have **a piece of advice**.
5. Həkim mənə istirahət etməyi məsləhət gördü. → The doctor **advised me to rest**.
