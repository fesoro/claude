## 1. Hair (Saç/Tük) — VACİB: Uncountable!

### ⚠ Uncountable!

Ümumi halda "hair" sayılmaz.

- My **hair** is long. ✓ (is, not are!)
- Her **hair** is beautiful. ✓

### ✗ Səhv

- ✗ My hairs are long.
- ✓ My **hair** is long.

### Exception

Fərqli tüklər üçün:
- "There's a **hair** in my soup." (bir tək tük)
- "Grey **hairs** are appearing." (grey tüklər)

### Struktur

- **hair** + adj: long hair, short hair, curly hair, straight hair.

## 4. Mustache — Bığ

### Yazılış

- AmE: **mustache**
- BrE: **moustache**

Hər ikisi düzgün.

### Nümunələr

- Thin **mustache**.
- Handlebar **mustache**.
- Trim your **mustache**.

### Beard vs Mustache

- **beard** = bütün
- **mustache** = yalnız bığ (üst dodaq)

Bəzən birlikdə:
- "**Beard and mustache**."
- "A full **beard and mustache**."

## Descriptions (Physical Appearance)

### Template

"He has [length] [color] [texture] hair."

- "She has **long, curly, blonde** hair."
- "He has **short, straight, black** hair with a **beard**."
- "She has **shoulder-length, wavy, brown** hair."

### Complete description

- "**Brunette** with **green eyes**, about 5'8"."
- "**Clean-shaven** with **short black** hair."
- "**Bearded**, **bald** with **glasses**."

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ My hairs are long. (uncountable!)
- ✓ My **hair** is long.

- ✗ His bigs are big. (bıq → mustache)
- ✓ His **mustache** is thick.

- ✗ He has beards. (bütün saqqal — uncountable)
- ✓ He has a **beard**.
