# Turn Down / Reject / Refuse / Decline / Deny — Rədd Etmək Fellər

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Söz | Tonu | Kontekst |
|-----|------|----------|
| **turn down** | informal rədd | təklif / iş |
| **reject** | rəsmi rədd | güclü |
| **refuse** | qəti rədd | stubborn |
| **decline** | nəzakətli rədd | formal |
| **deny** | təkzib et | ittiham |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **turn down** = rədd et (informal)
> - **reject** = rəsmi rədd (strong)
> - **refuse** = qəti / stubborn rədd
> - **decline** = polite rədd
> - **deny** = inkar (ittiham)

---

## 1. Turn Down — İnformal Rədd

Təklifi / işi / insanı rədd.

### Nümunələr

- I **turned down** the offer.
- **Turn down** the job.
- She **turned him down**. (romantic context)
- Don't **turn this down**!

### Struktur

- **turn down** + N

### İkinci məna — səs

- **Turn down** the volume.
- **Turn down** the music.

### Reject vs Turn down

- **reject** = rəsmi / güclü
- **turn down** = informal

- "I **rejected** the proposal." (formal)
- "I **turned down** the offer." (informal)

---

## 2. Reject — Rəsmi Rədd

Güclü. Rəsmi kontekst.

### Nümunələr

- They **rejected** my proposal.
- **Reject** the application.
- The PR was **rejected**.
- **Reject** the settlement.

### Tech kontekst

- **Reject** a PR.
- **Reject** the request.
- HTTP **reject** connection.
- Malformed JSON **rejected**.

### Reject vs Turn down

- **reject** = formal, written
- **turn down** = informal, spoken

- Code review: "I **rejected** the PR." ✓
- Casual: "I **turned down** the offer." ✓

---

## 3. Refuse — Qəti Rədd

"Etməyəcəm!" Stubborn.

### Struktur

- **refuse** + to + V
- **refuse** + N

### Nümunələr

- I **refuse to** accept this.
- He **refused to** help.
- She **refused** the offer.
- **Refuse** entry.

### Refuse vs Reject

- **reject** = evaluate + say no
- **refuse** = decide not to do

- "I **rejected** his idea." (değerlendirip rədd)
- "I **refused to** follow." (qəti "etməyəcəm")

### Tonu

Refuse daha stubborn. Polite yox.
- "He **refused** to pay." (ittiham)
- "He **declined** to pay." (nəzakətli)

---

## 4. Decline — Nəzakətli Rədd

Polite, formal rədd.

### Struktur

- **decline** + N
- **decline** + to + V

### Nümunələr

- I **declined** the invitation.
- She **declined to** comment.
- Polite to **decline** gracefully.

### Refuse vs Decline

- **refuse** = strong, sometimes rude
- **decline** = polite, professional

- "I **refused**." (qəti)
- "I **declined**." (nəzakətli)

**İnterview / business:** decline daha yaxşıdır.

### Decline abstract

- **decline** = azalmaq
- Sales are **declining**.
- Health is **declining**.

Bu başqa məna. Kontekst fərqlidir.

---

## 5. Deny — İnkar

Ittiham / iddianın əksini iddia.

### Nümunələr

- He **denied** the allegation.
- I **deny** everything.
- **Deny** access.
- **Deny** the request.

### Struktur

- **deny** + N
- **deny** + V-ing

- **Deny** any wrongdoing.
- **Deny** knowing about it.

### Deny vs Reject

- **deny** = inkar (həqiqət deyil deyirsən)
- **reject** = rədd (qəbul etmirəm)

- "I **deny** stealing." (etmədim)
- "I **reject** the accusation." (ittihamı rədd)

### Tech kontekst

- **Deny** access (permission).
- HTTP 403 = access **denied**.

## Müqayisə — Eyni Situasiya

**Job offer scenario:**

### Turn down (informal)

"I **turned down** the offer because..." ✓ (daily)

### Decline (formal)

"I respectfully **declined** the offer." ✓ (polite letter)

### Reject

"They **rejected** my application." ✓ (formal, strong — usually "they rejected me")

### Refuse

"I **refuse** to work weekends." ✓ (stubborn commitment)

### Deny

"I **deny** being involved." ✗ (different context — accusation)

## Tech / İş Kontekstində

- "I'll **turn down** the music." ✓ (volume)
- "**Turned down** the offer." ✓ (informal)

### Reject (tech!)

- "PR **rejected** due to tests failing." ✓
- "Request **rejected** — invalid token." ✓
- "**Reject** malformed input." ✓

### Refuse

- "The server **refuses** connections." ✓
- "I **refuse to** push untested code." ✓

### Decline

- "I **declined** the position." ✓ (polite)
- "She **declined to** comment." ✓ (legal)

### Deny

- "Access **denied**." ✓ (permission)
- "**Deny** the request." ✓ (RBAC)

## Email / Communication

### Politely declining

```
Subject: Re: [Offer]

Thank you for considering me for this role.

After careful consideration, I've decided to **decline**
the offer. I appreciate the opportunity and the time
your team invested.

Best wishes,
[Your name]
```

### Rejecting firmly

"I'm unable to accept this."

### Firm refusal

"I won't do that."

## Interview Kontekstində

### Saying "no" to project

- ✓ "I'll have to **decline** this additional work." (polite)
- ✗ "I **refuse**." (too stubborn)

### Declining offer

- "I appreciate the offer, but I must **decline**." ✓

### Rejecting approach (code)

- "I'd **reject** this approach because..." ✓ (strong reasoning)

## Phrasal Verbs

### Turn away

- **Turn away** = geri göndər (bina).
- Many people were **turned away** at the door.

### Shoot down

- **Shoot down** = informal reject (idea).
- He **shot down** my proposal.

### Veto

- **Veto** = rəsmi rədd (power).
- The CEO **vetoed** the plan.

### Pass on

- **Pass on** = rədd (informal).
- I'll **pass on** the dessert.

İgnor / rədd.

### Knock back (BrE)

Rədd.
- **Knocked back** the offer.

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ I refused his offer. (very strong → declined)
- ✓ I **declined** his offer. (polite)
- ✓ I **turned down** his offer. (informal)

- ✗ Deny the offer. (offer ≠ accusation → reject)
- ✓ **Reject** / **decline** the offer.

- ✗ Reject to work weekends. (to infinitive → refuse)
- ✓ **Refuse to** work weekends.
