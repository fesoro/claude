# Surprised / Shocked / Amazed / Astonished / Stunned — Təəccüb Sifətləri

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Cədvəl (İntensivlik)

| Söz | Dərəcə | Kontekst |
|-----|--------|----------|
| **surprised** | təəccüb | daily |
| **amazed** | heyran | pozitiv |
| **astonished** | çox təəccüb | formal güclü |
| **stunned** | dondurmuş | çox güclü |
| **shocked** | şokda | negativ |
| **flabbergasted** | inanılmaz şok | informal güclü |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **surprised** = təəccüb (neytral)
> - **amazed** = heyran (pozitiv)
> - **astonished** = çox təəccüb (formal)
> - **stunned** = dondurulmuş
> - **shocked** = şokda (adətən negativ)

---

## İntensivlik

```
surprised < amazed ≈ astonished < stunned ≈ shocked < flabbergasted
  neytral    pozitiv                   negativ      informal strong
```

---

## 1. Surprised — Təəccüb (Daily)

Ən çox istifadə. Neytral.

### Nümunələr

- I'm **surprised**!
- What a **surprise**!
- A **surprise** party.
- I was **surprised** to see her.

### Struktur

- **surprised** by + N
- **surprised** to + V
- **surprised** that + clause

- **Surprised by** the result.
- **Surprised to** hear.
- **Surprised that** he came.

### Tonu

Neytral — pozitiv və neqativ hər ikisi.

---

## 2. Amazed — Heyran (Pozitiv)

Pozitiv. "Vay!"

### Nümunələr

- I'm **amazed** by her skill.
- **Amazing** performance!
- Truly **amazing**.

### Struktur

- **amazed** by / at + N
- **amazing** (positive adj)

### Surprised vs Amazed

- **surprised** = neytral
- **amazed** = pozitiv heyranlıq

- "**Surprised** by the news." (neytral)
- "**Amazed** by her talent." (pozitiv)

### Tech kontekst

- "**Amazing** optimization!" ✓ (tərif)
- "**Amazed** by the performance gain." ✓

---

## 3. Astonished — Çox Təəccüb (Formal)

Rəsmi / yazılı. Surprised-dən güclü.

### Nümunələr

- I was **astonished**.
- **Astonishing** result!
- To my **astonishment**...

### Struktur

- **astonished** by + N
- **astonished** to + V

### Surprised vs Astonished

- **surprised** = daily
- **astonished** = formal + stronger

- "**Surprised** by the news." (informal)
- "**Astonished** by the breakthrough." (formal)

---

## 4. Stunned — Dondurulmuş

Fiziki reaksiya. Tam "donmuş".

### Nümunələr

- I was **stunned**.
- **Stunning** news!
- **Stunned** silence.
- Absolutely **stunned**.

### Tonu

Güclü. Pozitiv və ya neqativ.

- "**Stunned** by her beauty." (pozitiv — gözəl)
- "**Stunned** by the loss." (neqativ)

### Tech

- "**Stunned** by the 10x performance improvement." ✓

---

## 5. Shocked — Şokda (Adətən Neqativ)

Güclü emosional reaksiya.

### Nümunələr

- I'm **shocked**!
- **Shocking** news.
- I was **shocked** to hear.

### Struktur

- **shocked** by + N
- **shocked** to + V
- **shocked** that + clause

### Tonu

Adətən neqativ:
- **Shocked** by the crime.
- **Shocked** at the behavior.

Bəzən pozitiv sürpriz:
- **Shocked** (in a good way).

### Electric shock

- **Shock** = elektrik şoku (isim).
- **Shocked** by electricity.

---

## 6. Flabbergasted — İnanılmaz Şok (İnformal)

Çox güclü. İnformal.

### Nümunələr

- Absolutely **flabbergasted**!
- **Flabbergasted** by the news.

Gündəlikdə maraqlı söz. Formal yazıda nadir.

---

## 7. Other Related

### Taken aback

Yanlış istiqamətə gəlmiş. Temporary surprise.
- I was **taken aback**.

### Thrown off

Gözlənilməzlikdən.
- That question **threw me off**.

### Speechless

Sözsüz qalmaq.
- I'm **speechless**.

### Gobsmacked (BrE)

Çox təəccüb (informal).
- **Gobsmacked** by the result.

### Dumbfounded

Sözsüz təəccüb.
- **Dumbfounded** by the answer.

## Spectrum Cədvəli

| Intensity | Pozitiv | Neqativ |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Light | surprised | surprised |
| Medium | amazed | shocked |
| Strong | astonished | shocked / stunned |
| Extreme | flabbergasted | horrified |

## Test

Hansı söz uyğundur?

1. I was ______ by her kindness — not expected. (pozitiv)
2. ______ by the sudden loss. (neqativ güclü)
3. I'm a bit ______ you forgot. (yüngül)
4. **Astonished** / surprised that he finished so fast. (formal güclü)
5. Completely ______ by the 1000x speedup! (dondurulmuş pozitiv)

**Cavablar:**
1. amazed / surprised
2. shocked / stunned
3. surprised
4. astonished
5. stunned

- "**Amazing** performance improvements." ✓

### Astonished (formal)

- "**Astonishing** level of complexity." ✓

### Stunned (strong)

- "**Stunned** by the 10x scale up." ✓

### Shocked

- "**Shocked** by the security breach." ✓ (negative)

---

## Interview Nümunələri

### Describing achievements

- "The team was **amazed** by the launch success." ✓
- "**Surprised** by the user adoption rate." ✓
- "**Astonishing** growth numbers." ✓

### ✗ Avoid

- ~~I was shocked by bad code~~ (too emotional)
- ~~Flabbergasted by feedback~~ (too informal)

### Safer alternatives

- "I was **surprised** by..."
- "It was **remarkable** that..."
- "It was **impressive**..."

## Positive vs Negative

| Pozitiv | Neqativ |
|---------|---------|
| amazed | shocked |
| astonished | horrified |
| stunned (good news) | stunned (bad news) |
| impressed | dismayed |

### Contextə görə

- "I'm **stunned**!" (pozitiv və ya neqativ olar — kontekst)

---

## Common Expressions

### Surprise

- **in for a surprise** — təəccüb gözləyir
- **take by surprise** — gözlənilmədən yaxala
- **surprise** visit / party

### Shock

- **in shock** — şokda
- **culture shock** — kültürəl şok
- **electric shock** — elektrik

### Stun

- **stun gun** — şok tabancası
- **stunning** visuals

### Amaze

- **amazing** (cooler than "good")
- "Absolutely **amazing**!"
