## Bölmə 1: Fewer vs Less

### Əsas qayda

| Söz | Nə ilə | Nümunə |
|-----|--------|--------|
| **fewer** | sayıla bilən cəm (countable plural) | **fewer** books, **fewer** people |
| **less** | sayılmayan (uncountable) | **less** water, **less** money |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - Sayla bilirsənmi? 1 alma, 2 alma... → **fewer**
> - Sayıla bilmir (su, pul, vaxt)? → **less**

### Nümunələr

#### Fewer — sayıla bilən

- There are **fewer** cars on the road today.
- I have **fewer** friends than I used to.
- We need **fewer** meetings and more work.
- **Fewer** people came than expected.

#### Less — sayılmayan

- I drink **less** coffee now.
- We have **less** time than I thought.
- She earns **less** money than her husband.
- This recipe needs **less** sugar.

### İstisnalar (native speakers da qarışdırır!)

**Less** aşağıdakılarla da işlənir:

1. **Rəqəm + vaxt/məsafə/pul** (vahid kimi sayılır):
   - I have **less than** 10 minutes.
   - The house is **less than** 5 km away.
   - It costs **less than** $100.

2. **Percentage, statistics:**
   - **Less than** 10% of users.

3. **Comparative formal:**
   - Rəsmi yazıda: "no fewer than" preferred for count nouns.

> Ümumi qayda: gündəlik danışıqda bəzən `less` sayıla biləndə də işlənir (less people), amma formal yazıda `fewer` daha doğrudur.

### Collocations — Less ilə

- **less** time, **less** money, **less** work
- **less** than ever, **less** than before
- much **less**, far **less**
- more or **less** (təxminən)

### Collocations — Fewer ilə

- **fewer** people, **fewer** problems, **fewer** opportunities
- **fewer** than ever, **fewer** and **fewer**

## Bölmə 2: Between vs Among

### Əsas qayda

| Söz | İstifadə | Nümunə |
|-----|---------|--------|
| **between** | 2 və ya konkret obyektlər arasında | **between** you and me |
| **among** | 3+ qrup arasında (ümumi, konkret deyil) | **among** the students |

> **Qayda:**
> - İki obyekt? → **between**
> - İki ayrı (müəyyən) obyekt 3+ sayda? → hər halda **between**
> - Qrup / kütlə (müəyyən olmayan hissə) → **among**

### Between — 2 və ya konkret say

#### İki arasında

- The ball is **between** the two chairs.
- **Between** 8 and 10 AM.
- Choose **between** coffee and tea.
- **Between** you and me, I don't like him. (aramızda)

#### 3+ konkret obyekt (adları aydındır)

- The treaty was signed **between** France, Germany, and Italy. (3 ölkə adı var)
- **Between** my parents, my sister, and me, we raised $500.
- The profit was divided **between** the three partners.

> Əgər **konkret obyektlər adlandırılıbsa** (hətta 3+), `between` işlənir.

### Among — qrup, kütlə arasında

Müəyyən olmayan, kütlə, qrup üçün.

- He walked **among** the trees. (ağaclar çox, konkret deyil)
- She is **among** the best students. (tələbələr qrupu)
- The news spread **among** the team.
- **Among** the options, Python is my favourite.
- He is popular **among** his colleagues.

### Müqayisə

| Between (konkret) | Among (ümumi qrup) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Between** Monday and Friday. (2 konkret gün) | **Among** the days of the week... (qrup) |
| **Between** the 3 candidates, he's the best. (konkret 3 nəfər) | **Among** the candidates, he stands out. (ümumi qrup) |
| The money was split **between** Anna, Ben, and me. | The money was distributed **among** the employees. |

## Xüsusi İfadələr

### Between ilə

- **between** you and me (aramızda) — **Between you and me**, I'm leaving the job.
- **between** us (aramızda)
- read **between** the lines (sətirlər arasında oxumaq)
- **between** a rock and a hard place (ikili çıxılmaz vəziyyət)
- fall **between** the cracks (unudulmaq, atılmaq)
- in **between** (arasında)

### Among ilə

- **among** other things (başqa şeylər ilə birlikdə)
- **among** the best — ən yaxşılar arasında
- popular **among** — ...arasında məşhur
- divided **among** — bölüşdürülmüş

---

## İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Fewer / Less

- "We had **fewer** bugs this sprint." (bug = sayıla bilən)
- "We spent **less** time on meetings." (vaxt = sayılmayan)
- "**Fewer** errors, **less** downtime."

### Between / Among

- "The decision was made **between** the team lead and me." (2 nəfər)
- "This project was divided **among** the developers." (qrup, konkret adlar yox)
- "I had to choose **between** two offers." (2 təklif)
- "Among the team, I was the backend lead." (qrup)

## Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| Less people came today. | **Fewer** people came today. | people = sayıla bilən |
| Fewer water is needed. | **Less** water is needed. | water = sayılmayan |
| Between all the students, he is the best. | **Among** all the students, he is the best. | kütlə → among |
| Between the three of us (konkret 3 nəfər var) | ✓ **Between** the three of us | konkret 3 kəs — OK |
| Among you and me | **Between** you and me | 2 nəfər → between |
| I have less friends than her. | I have **fewer** friends than her. | friends = countable |
| We spent less hours on it. | We spent **fewer** hours on it. / We spent **less time** on it. | sayıla bilən → fewer |

---

## Tez Baxış

```
SAYILA BİLƏN → fewer, between (2 və ya konkret)
SAYILMAYAN → less, among (qrup)

fewer books → less water
between two options → among many options
```

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. fewer / less seçin

1. I have ___ time today.
2. ___ people signed up for the class.
3. We need ___ bugs in production.
4. The house has ___ rooms than I expected.
5. Drink ___ coffee in the evening.
6. There are ___ cars on the street now.
7. She makes ___ mistakes than before.
8. I have ___ money than I thought.

**Cavablar:** 1-less, 2-Fewer, 3-fewer, 4-fewer, 5-less, 6-fewer, 7-fewer, 8-less

### 2. between / among seçin

1. The meeting is ___ 10 and 11 AM.
2. He is very popular ___ his friends.
3. Choose ___ these three options.
4. She walked ___ the crowd.
5. The secret is ___ you and me.
6. The profit was divided ___ the partners.
7. ___ all my teachers, she was the best.
8. The ball fell ___ the two teams.

**Cavablar:** 1-between, 2-among, 3-between (konkret 3 options), 4-among, 5-between, 6-between / among (if partners listed → between), 7-Among, 8-between

### 3. Tərcümə edin

1. Bu həftə daha az bug var. → We have **fewer** bugs this week.
2. Bizim daha az vaxtımız var. → We have **less** time.
3. Bu qərar komanda ilə menecer arasında verildi. → This decision was made **between** the team and the manager.
4. Tələbələr arasında məşhurdur. → He is popular **among** the students.
