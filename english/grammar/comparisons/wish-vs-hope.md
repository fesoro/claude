# Wish vs Hope — Arzu və Ümid

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Fərq

| Söz | Məna | Mümkünlük |
|-----|------|-----------|
| **wish** | arzu etmək | **qeyri-real** və ya mümkünsüz |
| **hope** | ümid etmək | **real** və mümkün gələcək |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **hope** = mümkün olacaq (gələcəkdə real)
> - **wish** = kaş ki... (indi və ya keçmiş, mümkünsüz)

---

## 1. Hope — real, mümkün ümid

Mümkün olan bir şey üçün istifadə edilir. Gələcəkdə ola bilər.

### Struktur: hope + that + positive clause

- I **hope** (that) you pass the exam.
- I **hope** he gets the job.
- We **hope** it doesn't rain.
- She **hopes** to finish the project soon.

### Tense: Present / Future (real, pozitiv)

| Struktur | Nümunə |
|----------|--------|
| hope + Present Simple | I **hope** he **comes**. |
| hope + will + V1 | I **hope** he **will come**. |
| hope + to + V1 | I **hope to see** you soon. |
| hope + for + noun | I'm **hoping for** good news. |

### Nümunələr

- I **hope** you're doing well.
- I **hope** to hear from you soon.
- I **hope** it goes well!
- Let's **hope** the meeting finishes early.
- We **hope** you enjoy your stay.

---

## 2. Wish — qeyri-real, mümkünsüz arzu

Real olmayan, olmasını istədiyimiz şey üçün. "Kaş ki..." mənası.

### Struktur 1: wish + past simple — **indi** qeyri-real

Indiki vaxta istinad edir, amma past simple istifadə olunur.

- I **wish** I **had** more money. (indi pulum yoxdur, kaş olsaydı)
- I **wish** you **were** here. (indi burada deyilsən)
- She **wishes** she **knew** the answer. (bilmir)
- I **wish** I **could** speak French. (bacarmıram)

### Struktur 2: wish + past perfect — **keçmiş** qeyri-real

Keçmişdə baş verməyən bir şey üçün peşmanlıq.

- I **wish** I **had studied** harder. (oxumadım, indi peşmanam)
- She **wishes** she **hadn't said** that. (dedi, peşmandır)
- I **wish** I **had listened** to you.

### Struktur 3: wish + would — başqasının davranışı haqqında şikayət

- I **wish** you **would stop** smoking.
- I **wish** it **would rain**.
- I **wish** they **would be** quiet.

> ⚠ `wish + would` özünüz üçün işlənmir.
> - ✗ I wish I would stop smoking.
> - ✓ I wish I **could** stop smoking.

---

## Müqayisə Cədvəli

| Forma | Struktur | Vaxt | Nümunə |
|-------|----------|------|--------|
| **hope** + present / future | real gələcək | + / gələcək | I hope you're well. |
| **wish** + past simple | indi qeyri-real | indi | I wish I had time. |
| **wish** + past perfect | keçmiş peşmanlıq | keçmiş | I wish I had studied. |
| **wish** + would | başqasına şikayət | davranış | I wish you'd listen. |

---

## Əsas Fərq — Cümlə-cümlə

| Hope (real) | Wish (qeyri-real) |
|-------------|-------------------|
| I **hope** I **get** the job. (mümkündür) | I **wish** I **had** that job. (indi yoxdur) |
| I **hope** it **doesn't rain**. | I **wish** it **wouldn't rain**. (amma yağır) |
| I **hope** you **feel** better soon. | I **wish** you **felt** better. (indi pis hiss edirsən) |
| I **hope** I **can** help. | I **wish** I **could** help. (amma bacarmıram) |

---

## Xüsusi İstifadə

### Wish — nəzakətli istək (formal)

- I **wish** to speak with the manager. (çox formal)
- We **wish** to inform you that... (rəsmi yazı)

Bu "want" sözünün çox formal variantıdır.

### I wish you... — yaxşı arzu

- **I wish you** a Happy New Year.
- **I wish you** the best of luck.
- **I wish you** a safe journey.

### Hope — ümumi yaxşı ümidlər

- I **hope** you're well.
- **Hope** to see you soon.
- **Hope** all is well.

---

## İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Müsahibə sonunda

- I **hope** to hear from you soon. (real ümid)
- I **wish** you all the best with the project. (yaxşı arzu)

### Peşmanlıq ifadə edərkən (STAR metodu)

- I **wish** I had communicated more clearly in that situation. (o vaxt daha aydın danışsaydım)
- Looking back, I **wish** I had started earlier.
- I **hope** to apply these lessons in future projects.

### Şikayət / ehtiyac

- I **wish** our meetings were shorter.
- I **hope** the team will improve the process.

## Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| I wish you pass the exam. | I **hope** you pass the exam. | real mümkün → hope |
| I hope I had more money. | I **wish** I had more money. | indi qeyri-real → wish |
| I wish I will win. | I **hope** I win. / I **wish** I could win. | gələcək real → hope |
| I wish you good luck. (düzgün) vs I hope you good luck. | ✓ I **wish** you good luck. | wish + obj + noun (formal) |
| I wish I have time. | I **wish** I **had** time. | wish + past simple |
| She wishes she would know. | She **wishes** she **knew**. | özü → past simple (would yox) |
| I wish I didn't do it. (keçmiş haqqında) | I **wish** I **hadn't done** it. | past perfect |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. wish / hope seçin

1. I ___ you a happy birthday!
2. I ___ you pass your driving test.
3. I ___ I had a bigger house.
4. I ___ it wasn't raining right now.
5. She ___ to become a doctor one day.
6. I ___ you would be more careful.
7. Let's ___ for the best.
8. I ___ I had studied harder last year.

**Cavablar:** 1-wish, 2-hope, 3-wish, 4-wish, 5-hopes, 6-wish, 7-hope, 8-wish

### 2. Düzgün formaya qoyun

1. I wish I ___ (have) more time.
2. I wish I ___ (can) speak Japanese.
3. I wish I ___ (study) harder last year.
4. I wish you ___ (stop) talking.
5. I hope he ___ (come) tomorrow.
6. I hope I ___ (get) the promotion next year.

**Cavablar:** 1-had, 2-could, 3-had studied, 4-would stop, 5-comes / will come, 6-get / will get

### 3. Tərcümə edin

1. Kaş daha yaxşı ingiliscə bilsəydim. → I **wish** I **spoke** better English.
2. Ümid edirəm sabah hava yaxşı olar. → I **hope** the weather **is** good tomorrow.
3. Kaş daha çox çalışmışdım. → I **wish** I **had worked** harder.
4. Səni görmək ümidindəyəm. → I **hope to see** you.
