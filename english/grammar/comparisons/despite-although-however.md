# Despite vs Although vs However — Kontrast İfadələri

## Səviyyə
B1-B2

---

## Niyə Çətindir?

Azərbaycancada hamısı "lakin", "baxmayaraq ki", "buna baxmayaraq" kimi ifadə edilir. İngilis dilindəki fərq **quruluşla** bağlıdır.

---

## Müqayisə Cədvəli

| Söz | Quruluş | Nümunə |
|-----|---------|--------|
| **although / even though / though** | + cümlə (S+V) | Although it was cold, she went out. |
| **despite / in spite of** | + isim / V-ing | Despite the cold, she went out. |
| **however / nevertheless / yet** | Cümləni birləşdirir (;) | It was cold; however, she went out. |
| **but** | + cümlə (koordinasiya) | It was cold, but she went out. |

---

## ALTHOUGH / EVEN THOUGH / THOUGH

**Quruluş:** although + Subject + Verb (tam cümlə)

- **Although** it was raining, we went for a walk.
- **Even though** she was tired, she finished the work.
- **Though** I don't like fish, I ate it.

### Fərqlər:
| | Güc | Register |
|--|-----|---------|
| although | orta | neytral |
| even though | güclü (vurğulayıcı) | neytral |
| though | zəif | informal |

- **Even though** he apologised, I was still angry. (baxmayaraq ki, üzr istədi — vurğulu)
- I enjoyed it, **though**. (Cümlənin sonunda — lakin yenə də bəyəndim)

---

## DESPITE / IN SPITE OF

**Quruluş:** despite/in spite of + **isim** / **V-ing** (tam cümlə DeyIL!)

```
despite + noun phrase
despite + V-ing
in spite of + noun phrase
in spite of + V-ing
```

- **Despite** the rain, we went for a walk. (the rain — isim)
- **Despite** being tired, she finished the work. (being tired — V-ing)
- **In spite of** his efforts, he failed. (his efforts — isim)
- **In spite of** living far away, she visits often. (living — V-ing)

### "Despite the fact that" + cümlə:
- **Despite the fact that** it was raining, we went out. (cümlə istifadə etmək lazım gəlirsə)

---

## Although vs Despite — Dönüşüm

| Although | Despite |
|---------|---------|
| **Although** it was cold... | **Despite** the cold... |
| **Although** he worked hard... | **Despite** working hard... |
| **Although** she was sick... | **Despite** being sick... / **Despite** her illness... |

---

## HOWEVER / NEVERTHELESS / NONETHELESS / YET

**Quruluş:** cümlə. **However**, + cümlə.  
(Adi kontrast əvəzliklərindən fərqli olaraq — virgül + yeni cümlə)

- It was very cold. **However**, she didn't wear a coat.
- The plan had many weaknesses. **Nevertheless**, we decided to proceed.
- He lied to us. **Nonetheless**, we forgave him.
- She was exhausted; **yet**, she continued.

### Mövqe:
- Cümlənin **əvvəlində**: However, she...
- Cümlənin **ortasında**: She, however, didn't...
- Cümlənin **sonunda**: She didn't, however.

---

## BUT — Sadə Kontrast

**Quruluş:** cümlə, **but** + cümlə (koordinasiya bağlayıcısı)

- It was cold, **but** she went out.
- He tried hard, **but** he failed.
- I like fish, **but** I don't eat it often.

> But — ən sadə, ən gündəlik. Formal yazıda çox istifadə etmək tövsiyyə edilmir.

---

## Tam Müqayisə — Eyni Fikri Müxtəlif Yollarla İfadə

**Fikir:** Yağış yağırdı, amma gəzdik.

| Quruluş | Cümlə |
|---------|-------|
| although + cümlə | **Although** it was raining, we went for a walk. |
| even though + cümlə | **Even though** it rained, we went for a walk. |
| despite + isim | **Despite** the rain, we went for a walk. |
| in spite of + isim | **In spite of** the rain, we went for a walk. |
| despite + V-ing | **Despite** it raining, we went for a walk. |
| however | It was raining. **However**, we went for a walk. |
| but | It was raining, **but** we went for a walk. |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|-------|
| Despite it was cold... | **Despite** the cold... / **Although** it was cold... | Despite + isim/V-ing (cümlə yox) |
| Although the cold... | **Although** it was cold... | Although + tam cümlə |
| However it was cold... | **However**, it was cold. / **Although** it was cold | However cümləni birləşdirmir, ikinci cümləni başladır |
| Despite of the rain... | **Despite** the rain... | "Despite of" yanlışdır! |
| In spite the rain... | **In spite of** the rain... | "In spite of" — of mütləqdir |

---

## Formal Yazı üçün Seçim

| Kontekst | Tövsiyyə |
|---------|---------|
| Gündəlik danışıq | but, though |
| Akademik/IELTS yazı | although, even though, despite, however, nevertheless |
| Güclü kontrast vurğulamaq | even though, nevertheless |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Cümləni tamamlayın
1. ___ his young age, he was very mature. (despite)
2. ___ she studied hard, she didn't pass. (although)
3. The film was long. ___, it was very entertaining. (however)
4. ___ working all night, she didn't finish. (despite)
5. ___ it was expensive, he bought it. (even though)

**Cavablar:**
1. **Despite** his young age...
2. **Although** she studied hard...
3. **However**, it was very entertaining.
4. **Despite** working all night...
5. **Even though** it was expensive...

### 2. Dönüşüm edin
1. Although the weather was bad, we enjoyed ourselves.
   → Despite ___

2. Despite being very tired, she continued working.
   → Although ___

**Cavablar:**
1. Despite the **bad weather**, we enjoyed ourselves.
2. Although she **was very tired**, she continued working.
