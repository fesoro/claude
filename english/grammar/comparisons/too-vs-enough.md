# Too vs Enough

## Səviyyə
A2-B1

---

## TOO — Artıq, Çox (Mənfi mənada!)

**Too** — bir şeyin lazım olandan **artıq** olduğunu bildirir. Mənfi vəziyyət, problem var.

```
too + adjective/adverb

The coffee is too hot. (Qəhvə çox istidir — içmək olmur)
He drives too fast. (Çox sürətli sürür — təhlükəlidir)
```

### Quruluş:
- too + adjective → too **hot**, too **expensive**, too **tired**
- too + adverb → too **quickly**, too **loudly**, too **often**

### "Too + adj + to + infinitive":
```
The coffee is too hot to drink. (Qəhvə içmək üçün çox istidir)
I'm too tired to work. (İşləmək üçün çox yorğunam)
She's too young to drive. (Sürmək üçün çox cavan)
```

### "Too + adj + for + someone":
```
This bag is too heavy for me. (Bu çanta mənim üçün çox ağırdır)
The task is too difficult for beginners. (Başlayanlar üçün çox çətindir)
```

---

## ENOUGH — Kifayət qədər

**Enough** — bir şeyin lazımi qədər **olub-olmadığını** bildirir.

### Müsbət: Yetərli var
- I have **enough** money. (Kifayət qədər pulum var)
- She is **old enough** to vote. (Səs vermək üçün kifayət qədər yaşlıdır)

### Mənfi: Yetərli yoxdur
- I don't have **enough** time. (Kifayət qədər vaxtım yoxdur)
- He isn't **tall enough** to reach the shelf.

---

## Enough-un Mövqeyi — Çox Vacib!

```
ENOUGH + noun  →  enough + isim (öndə)
adjective + ENOUGH  →  sifət + enough (arxada)
```

| Düzgün | Yanlış |
|--------|--------|
| **enough** money | money **enough** |
| **enough** time | time **enough** |
| old **enough** | **enough** old |
| warm **enough** | **enough** warm |
| fast **enough** | **enough** fast |

### "adj + enough + to + infinitive":
```
She is old enough to drive. (Sürmək üçün kifayət qədər yaşlıdır)
Is it warm enough to go swimming? (Üzmək üçün kifayət qədər isti?)
He wasn't fast enough to catch the bus. (Avtobusa çatmaq üçün kifayət qədər sürətli deyildi)
```

---

## TOO vs ENOUGH — Müqayisə

| Too (artıq — problem) | Enough (kifayət) | Not enough (az — problem) |
|-----------------------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| The soup is **too** hot. | The soup is warm **enough**. | The soup isn't warm **enough**. |
| I'm **too** tired to work. | I'm awake **enough** to work. | I'm not alert **enough** to work. |
| He's **too** young to drive. | He's old **enough** to drive. | He's not old **enough** to drive. |

---

## TOO vs VERY — Fərq!

**Very** — sadəcə gücləndirir (neytral)
**Too** — həddən artıq, problem var (mənfi)

| Very (neytral) | Too (problem) |
|----------------|---------------|
| The film was **very** long. (Çox uzundu — informasiya) | The film was **too** long. (Çox uzundu — bezikdirdi) |
| She works **very** hard. (Çox çalışır — yaxşıdır) | She works **too** hard. (Çox çalışır — sağlığı zərər çəkir) |
| It's **very** cold today. (Çox soyuqdur — informasiya) | It's **too** cold to go out. (Çıxmaq olmaz — problem) |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|-------|
| I have money enough. | I have **enough** money. | Enough + noun = öndə |
| She is enough old. | She is old **enough**. | Adj + enough = arxada |
| He is very tired to work. | He is **too** tired to work. | Problem/maneə = too |
| This is too much difficult. | This is **too** difficult. | Too + adj (much olmadan) |
| I don't have enough of money. | I don't have enough money. | Enough + noun (of olmadan) |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Too yoxsa Enough?
1. The coffee is ___ hot. I can't drink it.
2. She isn't ___ tall ___ to reach the top shelf.
3. I have ___ money to buy the ticket.
4. He was driving ___ fast.
5. Are you ___ comfortable? (rahat mısınız?)

**Cavablar:**
1. **too** hot
2. **tall enough**
3. **enough** money
4. **too** fast
5. comfortable **enough**

### 2. Cümləni birləşdirin (too...to ya enough...to)
1. The water is very cold. I can't swim.
2. She is very young. She can't drive.
3. He is very strong. He can lift it.
4. I'm not very tired. I can continue.

**Cavablar:**
1. The water is **too cold to** swim (in).
2. She is **too young to** drive.
3. He is strong **enough to** lift it.
4. I'm not tired **enough to** stop.
