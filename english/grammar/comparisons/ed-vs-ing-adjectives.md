# -ed vs -ing Adjectives — Darıxdırıcı vs Darıxmış

## Səviyyə
A2-B1 (azərbaycanlıların ən çox etdiyi səhv)

---

## Əsas Qayda

Bəzi feillərdən iki fərqli sifət düzəlir — **-ed** və **-ing** sonluqları ilə:

| Forma | Kimi / nəyi təsvir edir | Məna |
|-------|-------------------------|------|
| **-ing** | səbəb (causer) | hiss yaradır |
| **-ed** | hiss edən (feeler) | hiss edir |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **-ing** = o adamı / şeyi nə edir (səbəb)
> - **-ed** = mən nə hiss edirəm (hiss)

---

## 1. boring vs bored — Ən Klassik Nümunə

| Forma | Məna | Nümunə |
|-------|------|--------|
| **boring** | darıxdırıcı (səbəb) | The movie is **boring**. (film darıxdırır) |
| **bored** | darıxmış (hiss) | I am **bored**. (mən darıxmışam) |

### Vizual nümunə

- The film **is boring**. → it causes boredom
- I **am bored**. → I feel boredom

### Səhv nümunələri

- ✗ I am boring. (bu "mən darıxdırıcıyam" deməkdir — sən maraqsızsan!)
- ✓ I am **bored**. (darıxmışam)
- ✓ He is **boring**. (o, darıxdırıcıdır)

> ⚠ Azərbaycanlılar ən çox "I am boring" deyirlər — amma bu "mən cansıxıcı insanam" mənasındadır!

---

## 2. interesting vs interested

| Forma | Məna |
|-------|------|
| **interesting** | maraqlı (səbəb) |
| **interested** | maraqlanmış (hiss) |

- The book is **interesting**. (kitab maraqlıdır — səbəb)
- I am **interested** in the book. (mən maraqlandım — hiss)

### Struktur

- **interested** + **in** + noun/gerund
- I'm **interested in** learning Python.
- She's **interested in** the job.

## 4. İstifadə Nümunələri

### Tiring / Tired

- The work was **tiring**. (iş yorucu idi)
- I am **tired** after work. (mən yorğunam)

### Exciting / Excited

- The game was **exciting**. (oyun həyəcanlı idi)
- I am **excited** about the trip. (mən həyəcanlıyam)

### Confusing / Confused

- The instructions are **confusing**. (təlimat çaşqın salır)
- I am **confused** by the instructions. (mən başa düşmürəm)

### Surprising / Surprised

- The news was **surprising**. (xəbər təəccübləndiricidir)
- I was **surprised** by the news. (mən təəccübləndim)

### Shocking / Shocked

- The movie is **shocking**. (film şok edir)
- I was **shocked** by the ending. (şok oldum)

### Relaxing / Relaxed

- The music is **relaxing**. (musiqi rahatlaşdırıcıdır)
- I feel **relaxed** after yoga. (rahatlaşmışam)

### Amazing / Amazed

- The view is **amazing**. (mənzərə heyrətləndiricidir)
- I was **amazed** by the view. (mən heyran oldum)

## 5. Ümumi Struktur — "about", "by", "with"

### -ed adjectives + preposition

| Adjective | Preposition | Nümunə |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| interested | **in** | I'm **interested in** music. |
| worried | **about** | I'm **worried about** the deadline. |
| excited | **about** | I'm **excited about** the project. |
| tired | **of** | I'm **tired of** waiting. |
| bored | **with** / **of** | I'm **bored with** this game. |
| surprised | **by** / **at** | I was **surprised by** the news. |
| impressed | **by** / **with** | I'm **impressed by** her work. |
| annoyed | **with / at / by** | I'm **annoyed with** him. |
| disappointed | **with** / **in** / **by** | She's **disappointed in** me. |
| scared | **of** | I'm **scared of** spiders. |
| confused | **about** | I'm **confused about** the plan. |
| satisfied | **with** | I'm **satisfied with** the result. |
| fascinated | **by** / **with** | I'm **fascinated by** AI. |
| frightened | **of** | She's **frightened of** dogs. |

## 6. Praktik Test — Hansını seçəcəksən?

| Cümlə | -ing? | -ed? |
|-------|-------|------|
| The movie is ___ . (darıxdırır) | boring | |
| I'm ___ . (darıxmışam) | | bored |
| This book is ___ . (maraqlıdır) | interesting | |
| I'm ___ in this book. (maraqlanıram) | | interested |
| The work is ___ . (yorur) | tiring | |
| I'm ___ . (yorğunam) | | tired |

### Praktik test soruşun özünüzə:

> **"Kim hiss edir?"**
> - Obyekt (film, iş, xəbər) hiss etmir — o, hissi **yaradır** → **-ing**
> - Şəxs (mən, sən, o) hiss edir → **-ed**

---

## 7. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Müsbət

- I'm **excited about** this opportunity.
- This role is **challenging** — I enjoy it.
- I'm **impressed with** your team's approach.
- I'm **interested in** learning more about the position.
- The project was **rewarding**.
- I find the work very **fulfilling**.

### Neytral / təsviri

- The codebase is **confusing** — it needs refactoring.
- I'm **confused about** the requirements.
- The presentation was **informative**.

### Mənfi / çətin situasiya (STAR)

- At first, it was **frustrating** — we couldn't find the bug.
- I was **disappointed with** the outcome.
- The deadline was **stressful**.
- I felt **overwhelmed** by the workload.
- I was **worried about** the release.

## 8. Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| I am boring. | I am **bored**. | hiss → -ed (yoxsa "cansıxıcıyam") |
| The movie is bored. | The movie is **boring**. | film hissi yaradır → -ing |
| I'm interesting in Python. | I'm **interested** in Python. | mən maraqlıyam → -ed |
| The news are surprised. | The news is **surprising**. | news hissi yaradır → -ing |
| I'm tiring today. | I'm **tired** today. | hiss → -ed |
| He is very excited person. (sifətlə) | He is a very **enthusiastic** person. / He gets **excited** easily. | excited adətən hiss |
| The test was confused. | The test was **confusing**. | test çaşdırır → -ing |
| I am worrying about my job. | I am **worried** about my job. | hiss → worried (-ed) |

---

## 9. Xüsusi Hallar — "Worrying" istisna

Bəzən **worrying** sifət kimi istifadə olunur ("narahatedici"):

- It's a **worrying** situation. (narahatedicidir)
- That's **worrying** news.

Amma "I'm worrying" yox → "I'**m worried**".

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. -ing və ya -ed?

1. The news was (shocking / shocked).
2. I was (shocking / shocked) by the news.
3. This job is (tiring / tired).
4. I am (tiring / tired) after work.
5. The book is (interesting / interested).
6. I am (interesting / interested) in history.
7. Children get (exciting / excited) at Christmas.
8. The game was very (exciting / excited).
9. I am (confusing / confused) by these instructions.
10. These instructions are (confusing / confused).

**Cavablar:** 1-shocking, 2-shocked, 3-tiring, 4-tired, 5-interesting, 6-interested, 7-excited, 8-exciting, 9-confused, 10-confusing

### 2. Tərcümə edin

1. Film çox darıxdırıcıdır. → The movie is very **boring**.
2. Mən darıxmışam. → I am **bored**.
3. Bu iş maraqlıdır. → This job is **interesting**.
4. Maraqlanıram. → I'm **interested**.
5. O, çox yorucu bir gündür. → It was a very **tiring** day.
6. Yorulmuşam. → I am **tired**.
7. Deadline məni narahat edir. → The deadline is **worrying** me. / I'm **worried about** the deadline.
8. İşimdən məmnunam. → I'm **satisfied with** my job.

### 3. Preposition əlavə edin

1. I'm interested ___ machine learning.
2. She's worried ___ her exam.
3. He is scared ___ dogs.
4. I was surprised ___ the result.
5. They are tired ___ waiting.

**Cavablar:** 1-in, 2-about, 3-of, 4-by/at, 5-of
