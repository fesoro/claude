# Cancel / Postpone / Delay / Reschedule — Dəyişiklik Fellər

## Səviyyə
B1 (tech meeting / iş kontekstində çox vacib!)

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Söz | Nə edir? | Nəticə |
|-----|----------|--------|
| **cancel** | ləğv et | olmayacaq |
| **postpone** | təxirə sal (plan var) | sonra olacaq |
| **delay** | gecikdir | başladı amma gecikdi |
| **reschedule** | yeni vaxta keçir | yeni vaxt |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **cancel** = olmayacaq
> - **postpone** = sonra (yeni tarix gec)
> - **delay** = gecikmə (davam edir)
> - **reschedule** = yeni tarix (konkret)

---

## 1. Cancel — Ləğv Etmək

**Olmayacaq!** Tam iptal.

### Nümunələr

- The meeting is **canceled**.
- **Cancel** the subscription.
- **Cancel** my flight.
- They **canceled** the event.
- I'll **cancel** the appointment.

### Yazı

- AmE: **cancel → canceled**
- BrE: **cancel → cancelled** (iki "l")

### İsim

- **Cancellation** fee.
- Last-minute **cancellation**.

---

## 2. Postpone — Təxirə Salmaq

Sonraya saxlamaq. Yeni tarix gec (gələcəkdə).

### Nümunələr

- The meeting is **postponed** until next week.
- Let's **postpone** the release.
- **Postpone** the discussion.
- The launch was **postponed**.

### Struktur

- **postpone** + N
- **postpone** + V-ing
- **postpone** + until/till + date

- **Postpone** the release.
- **Postpone** starting.
- **Postpone** until Monday.

### Cancel vs Postpone

- **cancel** = olmayacaq
- **postpone** = sonra olacaq

- "The meeting is **canceled**." (yoxdur)
- "The meeting is **postponed**." (yeni tarix olacaq)

---

## 3. Delay — Gecikdirmək

Gözlənildiyindən sonra baş vermək.

### Fel kimi

- The flight was **delayed**.
- We're **delayed** by 2 hours.
- **Delay** the response.
- Don't **delay** the deployment.

### İsim kimi

- There's a **delay**.
- A 30-minute **delay**.
- Without **delay** = dərhal.

### Postpone vs Delay

- **postpone** = qərar verilib (yeni tarix)
- **delay** = gecikmə baş verir (istəmədən)

- "We **postponed** the meeting." (qəsdən)
- "The meeting was **delayed**." (gecikdi)

- "The release is **postponed**." (yeni tarix plan)
- "The release is **delayed**." (gecikdi, amma olacaq)

---

## 4. Reschedule — Yeni Vaxta Keçirmək

Konkret yeni vaxt təyin edirsən.

### Nümunələr

- Can we **reschedule** for Thursday?
- I'd like to **reschedule** our meeting.
- Please **reschedule** this.
- Let's **reschedule** to 3 PM.

### Struktur

- **reschedule** + N
- **reschedule** for/to + date/time

- **Reschedule** for Monday.
- **Reschedule** to 4 PM.

### Postpone vs Reschedule

- **postpone** = sonra (dəqiq tarix ola və ya olmaya bilər)
- **reschedule** = konkret yeni tarix/saat

- "We **postponed** the meeting." (sonra)
- "We **rescheduled** the meeting for next Friday at 2 PM." (dəqiq)

---

## Kritik Fərq (4 Söz)

### Cümlə: Original meeting was Monday 10 AM

#### Cancel

"The meeting is **canceled**."
→ Artıq meeting yoxdur.

#### Postpone

"The meeting is **postponed**."
→ Sonra olacaq (tarix dəqiq olmaya bilər).

#### Delay

"The meeting is **delayed** by 30 minutes."
→ Eyni gün, amma 10:30-da.

#### Reschedule

"The meeting is **rescheduled** for Tuesday at 2 PM."
→ Yeni konkret tarix + saat.

### Postpone

- The release is **postponed** to next sprint. ✓
- **Postpone** the refactor for now. ✓

### Delay

- The deploy is **delayed** by 30 mins. ✓
- Without further **delay**... ✓

### Reschedule

- Let's **reschedule** the retro. ✓
- **Reschedule** the 1:1 for tomorrow. ✓

## Email / Slack Etiquette

### Polite cancellation

- "I need to **cancel** tomorrow's meeting." (rəsmi)
- "Can we **postpone** — I'm blocked on a prod issue?"
- "Sorry for the late notice, can we **reschedule**?"

### Apologizing for delay

- "Apologies for the **delay** in response."
- "Sorry for the **delayed** reply."

---

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ Can we delay the meeting to Tuesday? (konkret yeni tarix → reschedule)
- ✓ Can we **reschedule** the meeting to Tuesday?

- ✗ The meeting is delayed until next week. (yeni tarix → postponed)
- ✓ The meeting is **postponed** until next week.

- ✗ I cancelled the meeting for next week. (yeni vaxt → rescheduled)
- ✓ I **rescheduled** the meeting for next week. (əgər yeni vaxt varsa)

## Related

### Put off (phrasal — postpone sinonim)

- Let's **put off** the meeting.
- **Put off** the decision.

### Call off (phrasal — cancel sinonim)

- **Call off** the event.
- The strike was **called off**.

### Push back (tech slang)

- **Push back** the deadline. (postpone / delay)

### Advance / Move up

- Bring forward / make earlier.
- **Move up** the meeting to 9 AM.

## Spectrum

```
cancel     → olmayacaq
postpone   → sonra (yeni tarix ola bilər)
reschedule → konkret yeni tarix
delay      → gecikdi (olacaq)
```
