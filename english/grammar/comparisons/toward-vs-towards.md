# Toward vs Towards — AmE vs BrE

## Səviyyə
B1 (asan, amma region fərqi)

---

## Əsas Fərq

| Söz | Region | Məna |
|-----|--------|------|
| **toward** | AmE | -ə doğru |
| **towards** | BrE | -ə doğru |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **Məna eynidir!** Yalnız yazılış fərqi.
> - AmE = **toward** (no "s")
> - BrE = **towards** (with "s")

---

## 1. Əsas İstifadə

İki məna:

### A. Fiziki istiqamət

- Walking **toward** the door.
- Running **towards** the bus.
- **Toward** the north.

### B. Abstract "nisbətən / -ə münasibət"

- Attitude **towards** work.
- Contribution **toward** the goal.
- Feelings **towards** her.

---

## 2. Region Fərqi

### American English

- **toward** (no "s") standart
- But **towards** also acceptable.

### British English

- **towards** (with "s") standart
- **toward** rare but OK.

### Yazıda

- Amerika → **toward**
- Britaniya → **towards**
- International → hər ikisi OK

---

## 3. Both Are Correct

**Diqqət:** İkisi də doğrudur!

- "Walking **toward** the station." ✓
- "Walking **towards** the station." ✓

Heç biri səhv deyil.

---

## 4. Consistency Qaydası

Bir mətndə consistent ol:

### ✓ Consistent

- "Walking **toward** the door. Then **toward** the elevator."

### ✗ Inconsistent

- "Walking **toward** the door. Then **towards** the elevator."

Bir yazıda bir forma seç!

---

## 5. Eyni Qayda: Forward / Forwards

Eyni patterni izləyir:

- **forward** (AmE) / **forwards** (BrE) = irəli.
- **backward** (AmE) / **backwards** (BrE) = geri.

### Nümunələr

- Step **forward** / **forwards**.
- Look **backward** / **backwards**.

---

## 6. Digər Eyni Pattern

### -ward / -wards

- **inward** / **inwards** (içəri)
- **outward** / **outwards** (çölə)
- **upward** / **upwards** (yuxarı)
- **downward** / **downwards** (aşağı)
- **eastward** / **eastwards** (şərqə)
- **onward** / **onwards** (irəli)

**AmE** adətən "no -s".
**BrE** adətən "with -s".

---

## 7. Abstract İstifadə

### Attitude towards

- Her attitude **towards** me changed.
- Positive attitude **toward** work.

### Progress toward

- Progress **toward** the goal.
- Working **towards** a solution.

### Feeling toward

- Mixed feelings **toward** the proposal.

---

## 8. Fixed Expressions (Both OK)

- "**Toward** the end of the day."
- "**Towards** the end of the year."
- "**Toward** the middle."
- "**Towards** the future."

Hər ikisi OK.

---

## Test

Toward / Towards?

1. He walked ______ the exit. (AmE preferred)
2. Her attitude ______ work is positive. (BrE context)
3. Progress ______ the goal. (either)
4. I feel negatively ______ the change. (either)
5. **Look forward** / forwards to seeing you. (BrE)

**Cavablar:**
1. toward (AmE) / towards (BrE)
2. towards (BrE)
3. toward / towards
4. toward / towards
5. "Look forward to" ✓ (this phrase is fixed — no "s"!)

---

## Exception: "Look Forward To"

**Look forward to** = fixed expression.

- ✗ Look forwards to seeing you.
- ✓ **Look forward to** seeing you. (no "s"!)

Bu xüsusi ifadədir — həmişə "forward to" (no "s").

## Tech / İş Kontekstində

### Daily

- "Progress **toward** the sprint goal." ✓ (AmE)
- "Working **towards** MVP." ✓ (BrE)

### Interview

- "I'm moving **toward** / **towards** senior role." ✓
- "My attitude **toward** / **towards** learning." ✓

### ✓ Both acceptable

İnternational tech teams accept both.

---

## Style Guides

### Publications

- US media (NYT, Wired): **toward**
- UK media (BBC, Guardian): **towards**

### Programming docs

- No strict rule; match project style.

---

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ Look forwards to meeting you. (fixed → forward!)
- ✓ **Look forward to** meeting you.

- ✗ Different usage in same text.
- ✓ Be **consistent**.
