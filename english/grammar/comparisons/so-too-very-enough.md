# So / Too / Very / Enough / Such — Gücləndirici Sözlər

## Səviyyə
A2-B1

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Söz | Məna | Struktur |
|-----|------|----------|
| **very** | çox (neytral) | very + adj/adv |
| **so** | belə, o qədər (emotional) | so + adj/adv |
| **too** | həddindən artıq (neqativ) | too + adj/adv |
| **enough** | kifayət qədər | adj/adv + enough |
| **such** | belə (isim qabağında) | such + (a) + (adj) + noun |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - **very** = neytral artırma ("çox")
> - **so** = emosional artırma ("belə")
> - **too** = həddindən artıq, problem
> - **enough** = kifayət qədər (OK)
> - **such** = isimlə birgə artırma

---

## 1. Very — Neytral Güclənmə

Sadəcə "çox" — ifadəni gücləndirir, neqativ / pozitiv ola bilər.

### Struktur

- **very** + adjective
- **very** + adverb

### Nümunələr

- She is **very** smart.
- He speaks **very** fast.
- It's **very** hot today.
- I'm **very** tired.
- This is **very** important.

### Xüsusilə

- ✓ very good / very bad / very nice
- ✗ very same / very unique (çox məhduddur — very'dən əvvəl absolute adjectives yox)

---

## 2. So — Emosional "O Qədər"

Vurğulu, emosional. Adətən sürpriz və ya reaksiya.

### Struktur

- **so** + adjective (no noun!)
- **so** + adverb
- **so** + adj/adv + **that** + clause

### Nümunələr

- I'm **so** happy!
- The movie was **so** boring.
- He speaks **so** quickly.
- It's **so** cold today.

### So... that... (konstruksiya)

- It was **so** hot **that** we stayed inside.
- She was **so** tired **that** she fell asleep.
- The book is **so** interesting **that** I can't stop reading.

### Very vs So — Fərq

| Very (neytral) | So (emosional) |
|----------------|-----------------|
| She is **very** nice. | She is **so** nice! (həyəcanlı) |
| It's **very** cold. | It's **so** cold! (şikayət) |
| Ordinary intensifier | Expressive, emotional |

---

## 3. Too — Həddindən Artıq (Neqativ)

"Həddindən artıq" — problem, məhdudiyyət var.

### Struktur

- **too** + adjective / adverb
- **too** + adj/adv + **to** + V1
- **too** + adj/adv + **for** + noun

### Nümunələr

- This coffee is **too** hot. (içə bilmirəm)
- He's **too** young to drive.
- It's **too** late to call.
- I'm **too** tired **to** go out.
- She walks **too** fast **for** me.

### Too vs Very — Əsas Fərq

| Very | Too |
|------|-----|
| neytral | neqativ (problem) |
| It's **very** cold. (təsviri) | It's **too** cold. (üşüyürəm, problem) |
| I'm **very** tired. (yorğunam) | I'm **too** tired to work. (işləyə bilmirəm) |

> **Diqqət:** `too` = problem var. `very` = sadəcə çox.

---

## 4. Enough — Kifayət Qədər

"Kifayətdir" — nə çox, nə az.

### Struktur — adjective / adverb ilə

- adj/adv + **enough** (sonra gəlir!)
- **enough** + noun (əvvəl gəlir!)

### Adjective ilə

- He's tall **enough** to reach it.
- She's smart **enough** for the job.
- The water is warm **enough**.

### Noun ilə — əvvəl gəlir

- I have **enough** money.
- We don't have **enough** time.
- There are **enough** chairs.

### Struktur: enough + to + V1

- He's experienced **enough** to lead.
- This is good **enough** **to** publish.

### Tam struktur

- adj/adv + **enough** + **(for sb)** + **(to V1)**
- **enough** + noun + **(to V1)**

Nümunələr:
- The water is hot **enough for me to drink**.
- I have **enough money to buy it**.

---

## 5. Too vs Enough — Əksinə Fərq

| Too (artıq, problem) | Enough (kifayət, OK) |
|-----------------------|----------------------|
| **too** + adj/adv + to V | adj/adv + **enough** + to V |
| The coffee is **too hot to** drink. | The coffee is **hot enough to** drink. (əksinə!) |
| He's **too young** to work. | He's **old enough** to work. |
| I'm **too tired** to go. | I'm **awake enough** to go. |

> **Diqqət:** "too + adj + to" = edə bilmirəm. "adj + enough + to" = edə bilirəm.

---

## 6. Such — İsim ilə Birgə

`such` = "belə, o qədər" — amma isim qabağında (so-dan fərqli).

### Struktur

- **such** + a/an + (adj) + singular noun
- **such** + (adj) + plural noun
- **such** + (adj) + uncountable noun

### Nümunələr

- He's **such a** nice guy.
- It was **such an** interesting book.
- They are **such** good friends.
- She has **such** patience.
- It was **such a** cold day!

### Such... that... (konstruksiya)

- It was **such a** good movie **that** I watched it twice.
- She has **such** patience **that** everyone admires her.
- He's **such a** fast runner **that** nobody can beat him.

### So vs Such — Fərq

| So (adjective / adverb) | Such (noun) |
|--------------------------|-------------|
| **so** good | **such a** good person |
| **so** cold | **such a** cold day |
| **so** boring | **such** boring movies |
| He's **so** nice. | He's **such a** nice guy. |

> **Qayda:**
> - **so** + adj → no noun
> - **such** + (a) + (adj) + noun

Ətraflı: [so-vs-such.md](so-vs-such.md)

## 7. Müqayisə Cədvəli

| Vəziyyət | Düzgün söz |
|----------|-----------|
| Sadəcə artırmaq, neytral | **very** |
| Emosional reaksiya, sürpriz | **so** |
| Problem, həddindən artıq | **too** |
| Kifayət qədər, OK | **enough** |
| İsimlə birlikdə güclənmə | **such** |

### Nümunələr

| Söz | Nümunə |
|-----|--------|
| very | She is **very** tall. (təsviri) |
| so | She is **so** tall! (heyran) |
| too | She is **too** tall (for the door). (problem) |
| enough | She is tall **enough** (to reach). (OK) |
| such | She is **such a** tall person. (isim ilə) |

---

## 8. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Very (neytral)

- It was a **very** challenging project.
- I'm **very** motivated to join this team.
- This is a **very** interesting opportunity.

### So (emosional — müsahibədə az)

- I'm **so** excited about this role.
- The team was **so** supportive.

### Too (problem)

- The deadline was **too** tight to deliver quality.
- I was **too** focused on details and missed the big picture. (weakness!)
- It was **too** complex **to** solve alone.

### Enough (pozitiv)

- I have **enough** experience **to** lead.
- I'm **skilled enough to** handle this role.
- Do I have **enough** time? Yes, **enough**.

### Such (isim)

- It was **such a** rewarding experience.
- I've learned **such** valuable lessons.

---

## 9. Qarışıq Strukturlar

### too + adj + to = edə bilməzsən

- He's **too young to drive**.
- It's **too cold to swim**.

### adj + enough + to = edə bilərsən

- He's **old enough to drive**.
- It's **warm enough to swim**.

### so + adj + that = nəticədə...

- He's **so tired that he fell asleep**.

### such + (a) + (adj) + noun + that = nəticədə...

- She's **such a good singer that everyone loves her**.

### too + adj + for + noun

- It's **too small for me**.

### adj + enough + for + noun

- It's **big enough for me**.

---

## 10. Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| I am very tired to work. | I am **too** tired to work. | nəticə → too |
| The coffee is too hot. I love it! (neqativ kontekst) | The coffee is **very** hot. | neqativ → too, neytral → very |
| He's smart enough for the job, isn't he? (enough adj-dən sonra!) | He's smart **enough**. (adj + enough) | enough adj-dən sonra |
| I have enough not money. | I don't have **enough** money. | negation yeri |
| She is so nice person. | She is **such a** nice person. | noun → such |
| It was a so interesting book. | It was **such an** interesting book. / It was **so** interesting. | noun → such, no noun → so |
| He is too very kind. | He is **very** kind. / He is **so** kind. | too + kind = həddindən artıq |
| I am so excited to see you! ✓ | ✓ düzgün | so + adj |

---

## 11. Xüsusi İfadələr

### Very ilə

- **very** much (çox)
- **very** well (çox yaxşı)
- **very** little (çox az)
- thank you **very** much

### So ilə

- so far (indiyə qədər)
- so what? (nə olsun?)
- so-so (orta)
- I think so / I don't think so
- so that (məqsəd)
- so as to (məqsəd)

### Too ilə

- me too (mən də)
- too bad (çox pis)
- too much / too many
- all too often (çox tez-tez, neqativ)

### Enough ilə

- sure enough (həqiqətən də)
- fair enough (razıyam)
- that's enough (bəsdir)
- enough is enough (bu artıq çoxdur)

### Such ilə

- such as (məsələn)
- as such (bu halda)
- no such thing (belə bir şey yoxdur)

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. very / so / too / enough / such seçin

1. The coffee is ___ hot — I can't drink it.
2. He's ___ smart! I'm amazed.
3. She is a ___ good teacher.
4. I have ___ money to buy a car.
5. The movie was ___ interesting.
6. He's ___ young to drive.
7. It was ___ a cold day!
8. I'm ___ tired that I can't continue.

**Cavablar:** 1-too, 2-so, 3-very, 4-enough, 5-very / so, 6-too, 7-such, 8-so

### 2. too / enough struktur qurun

1. He is ___ young ___ drive a car. (18 yaşı yoxdur)
2. He is old ___ drive a car. (18+)
3. The water is ___ cold ___ swim.
4. This room is ___ big ___ us.

**Cavablar:** 1-too, to, 2-enough to, 3-too, to, 4-big enough for

### 3. so / such seçin

1. It was ___ a long day.
2. I'm ___ tired!
3. She is ___ a great leader.
4. The weather is ___ cold today.
5. He has ___ patience.

**Cavablar:** 1-such, 2-so, 3-such, 4-so, 5-such

### 4. Tərcümə edin

1. Çox yorğunam. → I'm **very / so** tired.
2. İşləmək üçün həddindən artıq yorğunam. → I'm **too tired to** work.
3. Oxumaq üçün kifayət qədər vaxt var. → There's **enough time to** read.
4. Belə gözəl gündür! → What **a** beautiful day! / It's **such a** beautiful day!
5. Çox maraqlıdır. → It's **very / so** interesting.
