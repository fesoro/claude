# During vs For vs While — Zaman İfadələri

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Fərq

| | During | For | While |
|--|--------|-----|-------|
| **Quruluş** | + isim | + müddət | + cümlə (S+V) |
| **Mənası** | bir hadisə zamanı | nə qədər müddət | eyni anda iki şey |
| **Sual** | When? (nə zaman?) | How long? (nə qədər?) | What at the same time? |

---

## DURING — Bir Hadisənin Zamanında

**Quruluş:** during + **isim** (cümlə deyil!)

```
during + the film
during + the meeting
during + my holiday
during + the war
during + the night
```

- I fell asleep **during** the film. (Film zamanı yuxuma getdim)
- She got a phone call **during** the meeting. (Görüş zamanı telefon zəngi aldı)
- We visited Paris **during** our holiday. (Tətilimiz zamanı Parisi ziyarət etdik)
- Many buildings were destroyed **during** the war.
- It rained **during** the night. (Gecə ərzində yağış yağdı)

---

## FOR — Nə Qədər Davam Etdi

**Quruluş:** for + **müddət** (rəqəm/vaxt ifadəsi)

```
for + 2 hours
for + a week
for + three years
for + a long time
for + ages
```

- I waited **for** two hours. (İki saat gözlədim)
- She lived there **for** 5 years. (5 il orada yaşadı)
- He studied **for** the whole night. (Bütün gecə oxudu)
- We've been friends **for** 20 years. (20 ildir dostuyuq)

---

## WHILE — Eyni Anda Baş Verən İki Şey

**Quruluş:** while + **Subject + Verb** (tam cümlə!)

```
while + I was sleeping
while + she was cooking
while + he worked
```

- I fell asleep **while** I was watching TV. (TV izləyərkən yuxuma getdim)
- She called me **while** I was driving. (Mən sürrürkən mənə zəng etdi)
- **While** he was sleeping, she cooked dinner.
- **While** I agree with you, I have some concerns.

### While — Eyni Anda Davam Edən Hal
- **While** I was reading, the phone rang. (Oxuyarkən telefon çaldı)
- He was cooking **while** she was setting the table.

---

## During vs While — Fərq

Hər ikisi "zamanı" mənasını verir, lakin quruluş fərqlidir:

| During | While |
|--------|-------|
| during + **isim** | while + **cümlə** |
| during the film | while I was watching the film |
| during the meeting | while we were in the meeting |
| during her speech | while she was speaking |

**Eyni fikrin iki ifadəsi:**
- **During** the concert, she sang three songs.
- **While** the concert was on, she sang three songs.

## Müqayisə Cədvəli

| Sual | Cavab | Misal |
|------|-------|-------|
| Ne zaman? | during | **During** the war |
| Ne zamandan? (eyni anda) | while | **While** I was sleeping |
| Ne qədər müddət? | for | **For** 3 hours |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|-------|
| During I was sleeping... | **While** I was sleeping... | During + isim lazımdır |
| While the film... | **During** the film... | Cümlə yoxdur, isim var → during |
| I waited during 2 hours. | I waited **for** 2 hours. | Müddət → for |
| For the meeting she left. | **During** the meeting she left. | Hadisə zamanı → during |
| While three years... | **For** three years... | Müddət → for |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. During, for, yoxsa while?
1. She met her husband ___ she was studying at university.
2. I lived in London ___ three years.
3. He didn't say a word ___ the whole meeting.
4. ___ he was talking, I was taking notes.
5. The power went out ___ the storm.
6. We trained ___ six months before the competition.

**Cavablar:**
1. **while** (cümlə var)
2. **for** (müddət)
3. **during** (isim: the whole meeting)
4. **While** (cümlə var)
5. **during** (isim: the storm)
6. **for** (müddət)

### 2. Cümləni tamamlayın
1. I read a book ___ the flight. (uçuş zamanı)
2. She fell asleep ___ she was reading. (oxuyarkən)
3. He studied ___ five hours without a break.

**Cavablar:**
1. **during**
2. **while**
3. **for**
