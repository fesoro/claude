# Job / Work / Career / Profession / Occupation — İş və Peşə Sözləri

## Səviyyə
A2-B1 (müsahibə üçün kritik)

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Söz | Növü | Əsas məna | Nümunə |
|-----|------|-----------|--------|
| **job** | countable noun | konkret iş yeri / vəzifə | I have a **job** as a developer. |
| **work** | uncountable noun / verb | ümumi iş fəaliyyəti / işləmək | I have a lot of **work** to do. / I **work** at Zipmend. |
| **career** | countable noun | peşə yolu (uzun müddətli) | My **career** in IT started in 2018. |
| **profession** | countable noun | xüsusi təhsil / ixtisas peşəsi | Medicine is a respected **profession**. |
| **occupation** | countable noun | formal / rəsmi peşə | Occupation: Software Engineer |

---

## 1. Job — konkret iş yeri (sayıla bilən)

`job` bir **konkret vəzifə**, iş yeridir. Sayıla bilən (count noun) → a / the / my / two jobs.

- I have a good **job**.
- She got a new **job** last month.
- He lost his **job**.
- I'm looking for a **job**.
- What's your **job**?
- I have two part-time **jobs**.

### Collocations (söz birləşmələri)

- get / find / apply for / look for / lose / quit / leave / take / accept a **job**
- part-time / full-time / permanent / temporary **job**
- dream **job** / dead-end **job** / nine-to-five **job**
- **job** interview, **job** offer, **job** application, **job** description, **job** market

---

## 2. Work — uncountable (ümumi fəaliyyət) və ya verb (işləmək)

### Noun (isim) kimi — uncountable (sayılmaz)

- I have a lot of **work** to do.
- This **work** is important.
- I finish **work** at 6 PM.
- Thanks for all your hard **work**.

> ✗ a work, ✗ works (çox az istisna ilə), ✗ many works
> ✓ a lot of work, much work, some work

### Verb (fel) kimi — işləmək

- I **work** as a software engineer.
- She **works** at Google / for Google.
- We **work** from home on Fridays.
- He **works** in IT / in finance. (sahə)
- They **work** on a new project.

### Collocations

- hard **work**, good **work**, extra **work**, overtime **work**
- go to **work** / be at **work** / leave **work**
- after **work**, before **work**
- **work** experience, **work** ethic, **work**-life balance
- out of **work** (işsiz)

---

## 3. Career — peşə yolu (uzun müddətli)

`career` = müxtəlif iş yerləri arasında bir peşə yolu, illər üzrə inkişaf.

- I want to build a **career** in software engineering.
- She has had a successful **career** in marketing.
- He is at the peak of his **career**.
- I'm at an early stage of my **career**.
- A **career** change (peşə dəyişikliyi) is difficult.

### Collocations

- pursue / start / build / develop / advance / change **a career**
- successful / long / brilliant / promising **career**
- **career** goals, **career** path, **career** ladder, **career** development

> **Fərq:** `job` = bu gün harada işləyirsən. `career` = ümumi peşə yolun, illərlə inkişaf.

---

## 4. Profession — xüsusi təhsil tələb edən peşə

`profession` adətən **ali təhsil / peşəkar təlim** tələb edir (həkim, hüquqşünas, mühəndis, müəllim).

- Medicine is a noble **profession**.
- He is a doctor by **profession**.
- She entered the legal **profession**.
- Software engineering is becoming a highly respected **profession**.

### Tipik profession nümunələri

- doctor, lawyer, engineer, architect, teacher, accountant, dentist, scientist

### Collocations

- by **profession** (peşəsinə görə) — He is an engineer **by profession**.
- enter / join a **profession**
- medical / legal / teaching **profession**

---

## 5. Occupation — rəsmi / formal peşə (anket, formlar)

`occupation` ən rəsmi sözdür, əsasən **rəsmi formlarda, sənədlərdə** istifadə edilir.

- Occupation: Software Engineer (sənəd)
- Please state your **occupation** on the form.
- What is your **occupation**?

> Danışıqda `What do you do?` daha tez-tez işlənir. `Occupation` yazılı formalarda.

---

## Tez-tez Qarışdırılır

### Job vs Work

| Cümlə | Düzgün söz | İzah |
|-------|-----------|------|
| I have a ___. | **job** | Konkret iş yeri |
| I have a lot of ___ today. | **work** | Yerinə yetiriləcək tapşırıq |
| I go to ___ at 9. | **work** | artıklsız, fəaliyyət |
| I got a new ___. | **job** | Yeni vəzifə |
| Good ___! (afərin) | **work** | job demək olmaz |

### Job vs Career

- A **job** is what you do this year.
- A **career** is what you build over years.

### Profession vs Job

- A **job** can be anything (cashier, waiter, developer).
- A **profession** usually requires formal education.

---

## İş Müsahibəsi Cümlələri (B1)

### Özünü tanıtma

- I'm a software engineer **by profession**.
- I work **as a** backend developer **at** Zipmend.
- I've been working **in** IT **for** 6 years.
- My **career** started in 2018 at a startup.
- I'm looking for new **job** opportunities.
- I want to develop my **career** in distributed systems.
- My current **work** focuses on payment systems.

### Tipik müsahibə sualları

- What do you do? → I'm a developer.
- What's your job? → I'm a backend engineer.
- Where do you work? → I work at Zipmend.
- Tell me about your career. → I've been in IT for 6 years...
- Why did you choose this profession? → I've always loved problem-solving...

---

## Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| I have a work. | I have **a job**. / I have **work**. | work sayılmayandır |
| I have many works to do. | I have **a lot of work** to do. | work uncountable |
| My job is in Baku. | My **office** / **company** is in Baku. | job = vəzifə, yer deyil |
| I go to my work. | I go to **work**. | artiklsız |
| She's a doctor by job. | She's a doctor **by profession**. | by profession |
| I want to change my work. | I want to change my **job** / **career**. | konkret iş → job |
| What's your occupation? (dost söhbətində) | What do you do? | Occupation rəsmi |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Doğru sözü seçin

1. I have a new ___ as a designer.
2. I have too much ___ this week.
3. He's a lawyer by ___.
4. My ___ in IT has been very successful.
5. Please write your ___ on the form.
6. She lost her ___ last month.
7. I go to ___ at 9 AM.
8. Good ___! You did it perfectly.
9. I'm looking for a new ___.
10. Teaching is a noble ___.

**Cavablar:** 1-job, 2-work, 3-profession, 4-career, 5-occupation, 6-job, 7-work, 8-work, 9-job, 10-profession

### 2. Interview-style cavab qurun

Şablondan istifadə edin:
"I'm a ___ (profession). I have been working **at** ___ **as a** ___ **for** ___ years. My **career** started in ___."

Nümunə: I'm a software engineer. I have been working **at** Zipmend **as a** backend developer **for** 3 years. My **career** started in 2021.
