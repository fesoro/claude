# Because / Because of / Due to / Owing to / Since / As — Səbəb Bağlayıcıları

## Səviyyə
B1

---

## Əsas Cədvəl

| Bağlayıcı | Nə ilə | Formal? |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **because** | + cümlə (subj + verb) | neutral |
| **because of** | + noun / -ing | neutral |
| **due to** | + noun | formal |
| **owing to** | + noun | formal |
| **since** | + cümlə | neutral (zaman da) |
| **as** | + cümlə | neutral / formal |
| **thanks to** | + noun (pozitiv) | neutral |

> **Qısa qayda:**
> - Sonra **cümlə** gəlir? → **because / since / as**
> - Sonra **isim** və ya **-ing** gəlir? → **because of / due to / owing to**

---

## 1. Because — ən çox istifadə (because + clause)

`because` sonrası **tam cümlə** (subyekt + fel).

### Struktur: because + subject + verb

- I stayed home **because** I was sick.
- We cancelled the meeting **because** the CEO was away.
- She is happy **because** she got the job.
- I can't come **because** I have to work.

### Where to put it

- Ortada: I stayed home **because** I was sick.
- Əvvəldə: **Because** I was sick, I stayed home. (vergül lazımdır)

> **Diqqət:** Rəsmi yazıda cümlə `because` ilə başlamaqdan çəkinmək olar, amma qrammatik olaraq doğrudur.

---

## 2. Because of — because + isim

`because of` sonrası **isim** (və ya **-ing** forması).

### Struktur: because of + noun / -ing

- I stayed home **because of** my illness.
- We cancelled the meeting **because of** the CEO.
- She is happy **because of** her promotion.
- The flight was delayed **because of** the weather.
- I'm tired **because of** working too much.

---

## 3. Due to — formal (due to + noun)

`due to` = because of-un formal variantı. Rəsmi yazıda, rapport və məqalələrdə istifadə edilir.

### Struktur: due to + noun

- The project was delayed **due to** bad weather.
- The error occurred **due to** a network issue.
- She succeeded **due to** hard work.
- Revenue dropped **due to** the pandemic.

### Due to vs Because of — Fərq

| Due to (formal) | Because of (neutral) |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| Rəsmi yazı, raport | Gündəlik danışıq |
| Passive cümlədə üstünlük | Hər cür kontekstdə |
| The delay was **due to** traffic. | We were late **because of** traffic. |

> **Qrammatik nüans (tarixi):** Köhnə qrammatikada `due to` yalnız noun-u təsvir edirdi (adjective kimi). İndi əksər rəhbərlik `because of` kimi qəbul edir.

---

## 4. Owing to — daha da formal

`owing to` = `due to`-nun daha formal variantı. Əsasən yazılı mətndə.

### Struktur: owing to + noun

- **Owing to** the snow, schools were closed.
- The event was postponed **owing to** unforeseen circumstances.
- **Owing to** budget cuts, we reduced the team.

> Gündəlik danışıqda nadir, rəsmi yazıda istifadə edin.

---

## 5. Since / As — çünki (neytral)

`since` və `as` həm də "çünki" mənasında istifadə oluna bilər (because alternativləri).

### Struktur: since/as + subject + verb

- **Since** you're here, can you help me?
- **As** it's late, let's go home.
- **Since** we have no choice, we'll accept.
- **As** she didn't answer, I left a message.

### Diqqət:

- **since** həm də "nə vaxtdan" (zaman) mənasında işlənir: I've lived here **since** 2020.
- **as** həm də "kimi" (role) və "-dikcə" mənasında işlənir.

Konteksdən asılı olaraq aydın olur.

### Because vs Since vs As

- **because** — səbəb (əsas diqqət səbəbdədir)
- **since** / **as** — səbəb məlumatı (qəbul edilmiş, əlavə)

- I can't come **because** I'm working. (sebeb vacibdir)
- **Since** you know him, can you call him? (onsuz da bilirik — əlavə məlumat)

---

## 6. Thanks to — müsbət səbəb

`thanks to` = "sayəsində" (adətən **pozitiv** nəticə).

### Struktur: thanks to + noun

- **Thanks to** my mentor, I learned a lot.
- **Thanks to** the team's effort, we launched on time.
- **Thanks to** the new process, bugs decreased.

İroni ilə negativdə də işlənə bilər:
- **Thanks to** the rain, we got soaked. (sarkazm)

---

## Nəticə — "bunun üçün / ona görə"

Əvvəlki cümlədən nəticə çıxartmaq üçün:

| Bağlayıcı | Məna | Nümunə |
|-----------|------|--------|
| **so** | ona görə | It was late, **so** we left. |
| **therefore** | buna görə (formal) | The data is clear. **Therefore**, we must act. |
| **consequently** | nəticədə (formal) | Costs rose. **Consequently**, profits fell. |
| **that's why** | buna görə | I was sick, **that's why** I didn't come. |
| **as a result** | nəticədə | Sales dropped. **As a result**, we cut budgets. |

---

## Struktur Təlimatı — Tez Baxış

```
because + clause         →  I'm tired because I worked late.
because of + noun        →  I'm tired because of my work.
due to + noun            →  The delay was due to traffic.
owing to + noun          →  Closed owing to the storm.
since + clause           →  Since you're here, help me.
as + clause              →  As it's late, let's go.
thanks to + noun         →  Thanks to you, I finished.

clause + so + clause     →  It rained, so we stayed in.
therefore + clause       →  I was late; therefore, I apologized.
```

## İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Müsahibədə istifadə

- "I chose this job **because** it matches my skills."
- "The project was delayed **due to** unforeseen technical issues." (formal)
- "**Thanks to** my team, we completed the migration."
- "**Since** you asked about my weakness..."
- "I'm leaving my current role **because of** limited growth opportunities."
- "**As a result of** this experience, I developed strong leadership skills."

### Yazılı — email

- "The meeting has been postponed **due to** a scheduling conflict."
- "**Owing to** high demand, response times may be longer."
- "**Thanks to** everyone's hard work, we exceeded our goals."

## Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| Because the weather, we stayed. | **Because of** the weather... | because + clause, because of + noun |
| I'm late due to I missed the bus. | I'm late **because** I missed the bus. / I'm late **due to** missing the bus. | due to + noun/-ing |
| Due to it was raining, we went inside. | **Because** it was raining... / **Due to** the rain... | due to + noun |
| He is happy because of he got promoted. | He is happy **because** he got promoted. | because + clause |
| Thanks to we worked hard, we finished. | **Thanks to** our hard work... / **Because** we worked hard... | thanks to + noun |
| The delay was because of the traffic was heavy. | The delay was **because** the traffic was heavy. / **due to** the heavy traffic. | ikisi bir yerdə yox |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. because / because of / due to / since seçin

1. I was late ___ the traffic.
2. I was late ___ the traffic was heavy.
3. The flight was cancelled ___ bad weather. (formal)
4. ___ you're here, let's start.
5. She failed the test ___ she didn't study.
6. ___ the strike, trains are delayed.
7. We finished early ___ the team's hard work.
8. I can't help ___ I'm busy.

**Cavablar:** 1-because of, 2-because, 3-due to, 4-Since, 5-because, 6-Due to / Because of, 7-thanks to / because of, 8-because

### 2. Doğru formaya qoyun

1. because / because of — I'm tired ___ working all day.
2. because / because of — I'm tired ___ I worked all day.
3. due to / because — The error was ___ a bug.
4. since / because — ___ you asked, I'll tell you.
5. owing to / because — The event is cancelled ___ bad weather.

**Cavablar:** 1-because of, 2-because, 3-due to, 4-Since, 5-owing to

### 3. Tərcümə edin

1. Yağış səbəbindən gecikdim. → I was late **because of / due to** the rain.
2. Komanda sayəsində layihəni tamamladıq. → **Thanks to** the team, we finished the project.
3. Xəstə olduğum üçün evdə qaldım. → I stayed home **because** I was sick.
4. Büdcə kəsintiləri ilə əlaqədar işçiləri azaltdıq. → We reduced staff **due to / owing to** budget cuts.
