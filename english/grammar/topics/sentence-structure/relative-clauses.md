# Relative Clauses (Təyin Budaq Cümləsi)

## Səviyyə
B1-B2

## Nədir?
**Relative clause** — bir ismi daha ətraflı təsvir edən budaq cümlədir. Azərbaycancada "-an/-ən", "-dığı/-diyi" şəkilçiləri ilə ifadə edilən cümlə quruluşuna bənzəyir.

---

## Relative Pronouns

| Pronoun | İşlənmə |
|---------|---------|
| **who** | insanlar (subyekt/tamamlıq) |
| **whom** | insanlar (tamamlıq — formal) |
| **which** | əşyalar, heyvanlar |
| **that** | insanlar VƏ əşyalar (defining-də) |
| **whose** | mülkiyyət (kimin) |
| **where** | yer |
| **when** | vaxt |
| **why** | səbəb |

---

## 2 Növ Relative Clause

### 1. Defining (Müəyyənləşdirici) — vergülsüz

Hansı şeydən söhbət getdiyini dəqiqləşdirir. Olmasa cümlə mənasız olur.

- The woman **who lives next door** is a doctor. (Qonşumda yaşayan qadın — hansı qadın? mühümdür)
- I liked the book **that you recommended**. (Tövsiyyə etdiyiniz kitab)
- The house **where I grew up** was demolished. (Böyüdüyüm ev)

> **That** — defining-də who/which əvəzinə işlənə bilər.

### 2. Non-defining (Əlavə Məlumat) — vergüllü

Artıq hansı şeydən söhbət getdiyini bilirik — əlavə məlumat verir. Olmasa da cümlə tam olur.

- My sister, **who lives in London**, is a teacher. (Bacım haqqında əlavə — bacım birdir, artıq bilirik)
- The Eiffel Tower, **which was built in 1889**, attracts millions of tourists.
- My father, **whose car was stolen**, filed a report.

> **That** — non-defining-də işlənmir!
> - ❌ My sister, **that** lives in London,...
> - ✅ My sister, **who** lives in London,...

---

## WHO — İnsanlar

```
...the person WHO does/did something
```

- The man **who called you** is waiting outside. (Sizi zəng edən adam)
- She is the teacher **who taught me English**. (Mənə ingilis dili öyrədən müəllim)
- Do you know anyone **who can help**? (Kömək edə bilən biri)

---

## WHICH — Əşyalar, Heyvanlar

```
...the thing WHICH is/does something
```

- The car **which I bought** last year broke down. (Keçən il aldığım maşın)
- That's the book **which won the award**. (Mükafat qazanan kitab)
- The cat, **which had been missing for days**, finally returned.

---

## THAT — Defining-də Hər İkisi (İnsanlar + Əşyalar)

```
defining clause-da who/which əvəzinə istifadə oluna bilər
```

- The woman **that** I met was very kind. (= who)
- The book **that** I read was amazing. (= which)

> Non-defining-də **that işlənmir** — yalnız who/which.

---

## WHOSE — Mülkiyyət

```
...the person/thing WHOSE ... is/was
```

- The student **whose essay won** the prize is my friend. (Essesi qalib gələn tələbə)
- This is the writer **whose books** I love. (Kitablarını sevdiyim yazıçı)
- A country **whose economy is growing** attracts investors.

---

## WHERE / WHEN / WHY

```
WHERE → yer
  The city where I was born is Baku.

WHEN → vaxt
  I remember the day when we first met.

WHY → səbəb (the reason why...)
  That's the reason why I left.
  That's the reason I left. (why buraxıla bilər)
```

---

## Relative Pronoun Buraxıla Bilər?

Relative pronoun **tamamlıq** rolunda isə buraxıla bilər (defining-də):

```
The book (that) I read was great.       → "that" buraxıla bilər (I = subyekt, book = tamamlıq)
The man (who/that) I met was kind.      → buraxıla bilər
The woman who called me was polite.     → "who" buraxıla BILMƏZ (who = subyekt)
```

**Qayda:** Relative pronoun arxasında subyekt varsa → buraxıla bilər.

| | Buraxıla bilər? | Nümunə |
|--|----------------|--------|
| Tamamlıq (S+V arxada) | ✅ | The film (that) I saw |
| Subyekt | ❌ | The man **who** called me |

---

## Preposition + Relative Pronoun

**Gündəlik (informal):**
- The hotel **that** we stayed **in** was lovely.
- The person **who** I spoke **to** was helpful.

**Formal:**
- The hotel **in which** we stayed was lovely.
- The person **to whom** I spoke was helpful.

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|-------|
| The man, that I met, was kind. | The man, **who** I met, was kind. | Non-defining → that olmaz |
| She is the woman which helped me. | She is the woman **who** helped me. | İnsan → who |
| The car who I bought... | The car **which/that** I bought... | Əşya → which/that |
| My brother, who he lives in Baku... | My brother, **who** lives in Baku... | Əlavə subyekt (he) olmaz |
| The house where I live in it... | The house **where** I live... | Where artıq "in it" mənasını verir |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Düzgün relative pronoun seçin
1. The student ___ passed the exam will get a prize.
2. This is the town ___ I was born.
3. The book ___ I recommended is out of stock.
4. My neighbor, ___ dog barks all night, is very rude.
5. I remember the year ___ the earthquake happened.
6. That's the reason ___ I left.

**Cavablar:** 1-who, 2-where, 3-that/which, 4-whose, 5-when, 6-why

### 2. İki cümləni birləşdirin
1. I know a woman. She speaks 5 languages.
2. The hotel was great. We stayed there.
3. I met a man. His wife is a famous actor.
4. That was the day. Everything changed then.

**Cavablar:**
1. I know a woman **who speaks** 5 languages.
2. The hotel **where we stayed** was great.
3. I met a man **whose wife is** a famous actor.
4. That was the day **when everything changed**.

### 3. Defining yoxsa Non-defining?
1. My car, which is red, is parked outside. ___
2. The car which is parked outside is mine. ___

**Cavablar:** 1-Non-defining (hansı maşın artıq bəlli), 2-Defining (hansı maşın? — parking-dəki)
