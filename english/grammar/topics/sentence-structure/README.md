# Cümlə Quruluşu (Sentence Structure)

## Səviyyə
A1 (əsas) → C1 (irəliləmiş)

---

## İngilis Cümləsinin Əsas Quruluşu

**SVO: Subject + Verb + Object**

| Azərbaycanca | İngilis |
|--------------|---------|
| Mən alma yedim. | **I** (S) **ate** (V) **an apple** (O). |
| O kitabı oxuyur. | **She** (S) **reads** (V) **the book** (O). |

> **Azərbaycancada:** SOV (Subject-Object-Verb) — "Mən alma **yedim**" → fel sonda  
> **İngiliscədə:** SVO — "I **ate** an apple" → fel ortada

---

## Cümlə Növləri

### 1. Sadə Cümlə (Simple)
Bir müstəqil cümlə:
- She **works** at a hospital.
- I **love** coffee.

### 2. Mürəkkəb (Compound)
İki müstəqil cümlə + koordinasiya bağlayıcısı (and, but, so, or):
- I was tired, **but** I continued.
- She called, **and** I answered.

### 3. Tabeli Mürəkkəb (Complex)
Bir müstəqil + bir asılı cümlə:
- **Although** it was raining, we went out.
- She called me **when** she arrived.

### 4. Qarışıq (Compound-Complex)
Həm koordinasiya, həm subordinasiya:
- **Although** it was late, she finished the work **and** sent it.

---

## Sual Cümləsi Quruluşu

### Yes/No Questions
Köməkçi fel + subyekt + əsas fel:
- **Do** you like coffee?
- **Is** she coming?
- **Have** they arrived?
- **Can** you help me?

### Wh-Questions
Sual sözü + köməkçi fel + subyekt + əsas fel:
- **What** do you want?
- **Where** does she live?
- **When** did they arrive?
- **Who** called you? (who subyektdirsə, köməkçi lazım deyil)
- **Why** are you late?
- **How** did you do this?
- **How many/much** does it cost?

---

## Mənfi Cümlə

Köməkçi fel + not + əsas fel:
- I do **not** (don't) like fish.
- She is **not** (isn't) here.
- They have **not** (haven't) finished.
- He can**not** (can't) swim.

---

## Relative Clauses — Münasibət Budaq Cümlələri

| Əvəzlik | İşlənmə | Nümunə |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **who** | insan (subyekt/obyekt) | The man **who** called you is my uncle. |
| **which** | şey | The car **which** I bought is red. |
| **that** | insan və şey | The book **that** I read was great. |
| **whose** | sahib bildirmək | The girl **whose** bag was stolen is my friend. |
| **where** | yer | This is the city **where** I was born. |
| **when** | zaman | That was the day **when** everything changed. |

**Defining vs. Non-defining:**
- **Defining** (vergülsüz): The man **who** lives next door is a doctor. (hansı adam — vacib)
- **Non-defining** (vergüllü): My father**, who** is 60 years old, is retired. (əlavə məlumat)

## There is / There are

| Azərbaycanca | İngilis |
|--------------|---------|
| Masada bir kitab var. | **There is** a book on the table. |
| Parkda uşaqlar var. | **There are** children in the park. |
| Heç kim yoxdur. | **There is nobody** here. |

> ❌ Is a book on the table. → ✅ **There is** a book on the table.

---

## Inversion (B2-C1)

Neqativ ifadə cümləni başlayanda subyekt-köməkçi fel yeri dəyişir:

| Normal | Inversiya |
|--------|-----------|
| I have never seen such beauty. | **Never have I** seen such beauty. |
| I have not only failed, but... | **Not only have I** failed, but... |
| I had hardly arrived when... | **Hardly had I** arrived when... |
| She had no sooner left than... | **No sooner had she** left than... |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

- ❌ Where you live? → ✅ Where **do** you live?
- ❌ She not likes it. → ✅ She **doesn't** like it.
- ❌ Is a problem. → ✅ **There is** a problem.
- ❌ The man who he called me. → ✅ The man **who** called me. (who artıq he-dir, ikisi birlikdə olmaz)
