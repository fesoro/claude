# Ellipsis & Substitution (Buraxma və Əvəzetmə)

Ellipsis — artıq məlum olan sözləri buraxmaq.
Substitution — artıq işlənmiş sözləri əvəzedici sözlərlə (so, do, one, neither) əvəz etmək.

---

## 1. Ellipsis — buraxma

### a) Verb Phrase Ellipsis
Eyni feli təkrar etməmək üçün buraxılır:

| Full form | Ellipsis | Azerbaijani |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| She can play piano and he can play piano too. | She can play piano and he **can** too. | O da bacarır. |
| I want to leave but I don't want to leave yet. | I want to leave but I don't want to **yet**. | Amma hələ istəmirəm. |

### b) Subject + Auxiliary Ellipsis (informal)
| Full | Ellipsis | Context |
|------|----------|---------|
| Are you coming? | Coming? | Söhbətdə |
| I am going now. | Going now. | Qeyd |
| Have you finished? | Finished? | Sual |

### c) "To" infinitive ellipsis
| Full | Ellipsis |
|------|----------|
| She asked me to come, but I didn't want to come. | She asked me to come, but I didn't want **to**. |
| Can you help? I'd love to help. | Can you help? I'd love **to**. |

---

## 2. Substitution with "do so / do it / do that"

Felin əvəzinə işlənir:

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| He forgot to lock the door. I reminded him to **do so**. | Qapını bağlamağı unutdu. Xatırlatdım. |
| Please clean your room. I've already **done it**. | Artıq etdim. |
| Don't worry, I'll **do that** later. | Bunu sonra edəcəm. |

---

## 3. Substitution with "so"

"So" — müsbət cümlələrdə bütöv bir fikrin əvəzinə:

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| Will it rain? I think **so**. | Fikrimcə, bəli. |
| Is she coming? I hope **so**. | Ümid edirəm ki, bəli. |
| Did you win? I believe **so**. | İnanıram ki, bəli. |

**"Not"** — mənfi cavab üçün:
| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| Is he ready? I'm afraid **not**. | Qorxuram ki, xeyr. |
| Will they agree? I hope **not**. | Ümid edirəm ki, xeyr. |

**Verbs used with so/not:**
think, believe, hope, expect, suppose, imagine, assume, afraid, guess

---

## 4. "So do I / Neither do I" — Agreement

**Müsbət razılıq:** So + aux + I/he/she...

| Statement | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| I love pizza. | **So do I.** (Mən də.) |
| She has been to London. | **So have I.** |
| They were tired. | **So was he.** |

**Mənfi razılıq:** Neither/Nor + aux + I/he/she...

| Statement | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| I don't like cold weather. | **Neither do I.** (Mən də.) |
| She hasn't finished. | **Neither have I.** |
| He wasn't there. | **Nor was she.** |

---

## 5. Substitution with "one/ones"

Sayıla bilən isimlerin əvəzinə:

| Full | Substitution | Azerbaijani |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| I need a new pen. I lost my old pen. | I need a new pen. I lost my old **one**. | Köhnəsini itirdim. |
| These shoes are nice but those shoes are nicer. | These shoes are nice but those **ones** are nicer. | O birləri daha gözəldir. |

---

## Quick Reference

```
Ellipsis:       Sözü tamamilə burax
do so/it/that:  Fel ifadəsini əvəz et
so / not:       Bütöv fikri əvəz et (think so, hope not)
So do I:        Müsbət razılıq
Neither do I:   Mənfi razılıq
one / ones:     Sayıla bilən ismi əvəz et
```
