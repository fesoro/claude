# Cleft Sentences (Parçalanmış cümlələr)

Cleft sentences — bir cümləni iki hissəyə bölərək müəyyən bir məlumatı **vurğulamaq** üçün istifadə olunur.

---

## 1. "It is/was... that/who" Cleft

Ən çox işlənən növ. Subyekti, obyekti, vaxtı və ya yeri vurğulayır.

**Structure:** It is/was + **[vurğulanan hissə]** + that/who + qalan cümlə

| Normal cümlə | Cleft cümlə | Vurğulanan |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| John broke the window. | **It was John** who broke the window. | Subyekt |
| John broke the **window**. | **It was the window** that John broke. | Obyekt |
| She called **yesterday**. | **It was yesterday** that she called. | Vaxt |
| They met **in Paris**. | **It was in Paris** that they met. | Yer |

**Azerbaijani izah:**
- Adi: "John pəncərəni sındırdı."
- Cleft: "Pəncərəni sındıran **məhz John** idi."

---

## 2. "What... is/was" Cleft (Pseudo-cleft)

**Structure:** What + S + V + is/was + **[vurğulanan hissə]**

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| **What I need** is a good rest. | Ehtiyacım olan şey yaxşı istirahətdir. |
| **What she wants** is more time. | İstədiyi şey daha çox vaxtdır. |
| **What surprised me** was his reaction. | Məni təəccübləndirən onun reaksiyası idi. |
| **What I did** was (to) call the police. | Etdiyim şey polisi aramaq oldu. |

---

## 3. "All... is/was" — məhdudlaşdırma

**Structure:** All + S + V + is/was + **[hissə]**

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| **All I want** is peace and quiet. | İstədiyim tək şey sakit bir mühitdir. |
| **All she did** was (to) cry. | Etdiyi tək şey ağlamaq idi. |
| **All we need** is love. | Lazım olan tək şey sevgidir. |

---

## 4. "The reason... is that" — səbəbi vurğulamaq

**Structure:** The reason + S + V + is that + [səbəb]

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| **The reason I'm late** is that the traffic was bad. | Gecikməyimin səbəbi tıxac idi. |
| **The reason she left** is that she felt ignored. | Getməsinin səbəbi özünü görmədilmiş hiss etməsi idi. |

---

## 5. "It is/was... when/where/why"

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| It was **in 1990** that Azerbaijan gained independence. | Azərbaycan 1990-da müstəqillik qazandı (vurğu: vaxt). |
| It is **for this reason** that we invested. | Elə bu səbəbdən investisiya etdik. |

---

## Normal vs Cleft — Fərq

| Normal | Cleft (vurğu ilə) |
|--------|-------------------|
| I need help. | What I need is help. |
| He broke my trust. | It was my trust that he broke. |
| She called last night. | It was last night that she called. |
| Money motivates them. | What motivates them is money. |

---

## Quick Reference

```
It-cleft:       It is/was + [focus] + that/who + rest
What-cleft:     What + S + V + is/was + [focus]
All-cleft:      All + S + V + is/was + [focus]
Reason-cleft:   The reason + S + V + is that + [reason]
```
