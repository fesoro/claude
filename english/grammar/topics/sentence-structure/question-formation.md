# Question Formation — Sual Qurmaq

## Səviyyə
A2-B1

## Niyə Azərbaycan Danışanlar üçün Çətindir?

Azərbaycan dilində sual yalnız intonasiya və ya "-mı/-mi" şəkilçisi ilə qurulur:
- "Sən gedirsən?" = "Are you going?"
- "Evdəsən?" = "Are you at home?"

İngilis dilində isə **söz sırası dəyişir** və çox vaxt **auxiliary verb (yardımçı fel)** lazım olur. Bu ən çox səhv edilən sahədir.

---

## 1. Əsas Qayda — Auxiliary Verb Lazımdır

İngilis sualında cümlənin **əvvəlinə bir yardımçı fel** gəlir:

| Zaman | Auxiliary | Nümunə |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Present Simple | do / does | **Do** you work? / **Does** she speak English? |
| Past Simple | did | **Did** you go? |
| Present Continuous | am/is/are | **Are** you working? |
| Past Continuous | was/were | **Were** you sleeping? |
| Present Perfect | have/has | **Have** you seen it? |
| Past Perfect | had | **Had** you finished? |
| Future (will) | will | **Will** you come? |
| Modal verbs | can/could/should/must/may/might | **Can** you help? |

### "Be" feli xüsusidir — auxiliary lazım deyil
Əgər əsas fel **be** (am/is/are/was/were)-dirsə, sual be ilə başlayır:
- "You are tired." → **"Are** you tired?"
- "She was here." → **"Was** she here?"
- "They are students." → **"Are** they students?"

---

## 2. Yes/No Sualları

### Struktur:
**[Auxiliary] + [Subject] + [Main Verb] + [Rest]?**

### Nümunələr:

| Cümlə | Sual |
|-------|------|
| You like coffee. | **Do you like** coffee? |
| She speaks English. | **Does she speak** English? |
| They went home. | **Did they go** home? |
| He is sleeping. | **Is he sleeping**? |
| We have finished. | **Have we finished**? |
| You will come. | **Will you come**? |
| She can drive. | **Can she drive**? |

### Cavab:
- Yes + auxiliary / be: "**Yes, I do.**" / "**Yes, she is.**"
- No + auxiliary + not: "**No, I don't.**" / "**No, she isn't.**"

### Ən çox səhv:
- ❌ "You work here?" (auxiliary yox)
- ✅ "**Do** you work here?"

- ❌ "You speak English?" 
- ✅ "**Do** you speak English?"

- ❌ "She goes to school?"
- ✅ "**Does** she go to school?" (qeyd: "goes" → "go" — 3-cü şəxs -s auxiliary-də!)

---

## 3. WH Sualları (Question Words)

| WH Söz | Mənası | Nümunə |
|--------|--------|--------|
| What | Nə | **What** do you do? |
| Who | Kim | **Who** is she? |
| Where | Harada | **Where** do you live? |
| When | Nə vaxt | **When** did you arrive? |
| Why | Niyə | **Why** are you late? |
| How | Necə | **How** did you get here? |
| Which | Hansı | **Which** do you prefer? |
| Whose | Kimin | **Whose** bag is this? |

### Struktur:
**[WH Word] + [Auxiliary] + [Subject] + [Main Verb]?**

### Nümunələr:
- **What do** you want?
- **Where does** he live?
- **When did** she leave?
- **Why are** you crying?
- **How have** you been?
- **How many** books **do** you have?
- **How long have** you lived here?

### Xüsusi hal: WH **Subject** olduqda (auxiliary YOX)
Əgər WH söz cümlənin subyektidirsə, auxiliary işlədilmir:
- "**Who** wrote this book?" (not: *Who did write this book?*)
- "**What** happened?" (not: *What did happen?*)
- "**Which** bus goes to the city?" (not: *Which bus does go...*)

**Qayda:** Sualın cavabı cümlənin subyektidirsə, auxiliary işlədilmir.

## 4. Azərbaycan Danışanların Ən Çox Səhvləri

### ❌ Səhv 1: Auxiliary unutmaq
| ❌ | ✅ |
|---|---|
| You like pizza? | **Do you** like pizza? |
| Where you live? | **Where do you** live? |
| What she said? | **What did she** say? |
| Why he is sad? | **Why is he** sad? (burada be-li var — OK) |

### ❌ Səhv 2: İki fel işlətmək (auxiliary + əsas fel)
| ❌ | ✅ |
|---|---|
| Do you are a student? | **Are you** a student? |
| Does she is here? | **Is she** here? |

Qayda: **Be feli auxiliary kimi özü işləyir**, **do/does lazım deyil**.

### ❌ Səhv 3: Tense qarışdırmaq
| ❌ | ✅ |
|---|---|
| Did you went there? | **Did you go** there? (past-da "go" qalır, "did" artıq past verir) |
| Does she went? | **Did she go**? |

Qayda: Auxiliary (did) **tense bildirir**, əsas fel **V1 formasında** qalır.

### ❌ Səhv 4: Statement sırasını saxlamaq
| ❌ | ✅ |
|---|---|
| What you are doing? | **What are you** doing? |
| Where you went? | **Where did you** go? |

## 5. Müsahibədə Faydalı Sual Nümunələri

### Şirkət haqqında
- What does your company do?
- How big is the team?
- Who would I report to?
- What does a typical day look like?

### Rol haqqında
- What are the main responsibilities of this role?
- What are the biggest challenges?
- What does success look like in this role?
- How is performance measured?

### Mədəniyyət haqqında
- Can you tell me about the company culture?
- How often does the team meet?
- What do you like most about working here?

### Növbəti addımlar
- What are the next steps in the process?
- When can I expect to hear back?
- Is there anything else you need from me?

## 6. Indirect Questions (Dolayı Suallar — Nəzakətli Forma)

Nəzakətli olmaq üçün "Could you tell me...", "I was wondering..." əlavə et. Bu halda söz sırası **statement kimi** olur (auxiliary **çıxır**).

| Birbaşa | Dolayı (nəzakətli) |
|---------|---------------------|
| What time is it? | Could you tell me **what time it is**? |
| Where is the bathroom? | Do you know **where the bathroom is**? |
| When does the meeting start? | Can you tell me **when the meeting starts**? |
| Why is he late? | I wonder **why he is late**. |

### Ən çox səhv:
- ❌ "Could you tell me where is the office?"
- ✅ "Could you tell me **where the office is**?"

(Daxili sual statement kimi olmalıdır.)

---

## 7. Question Tags (Sual Quyruğu) — B1

Təsdiqləmə üçün cümlənin sonuna əlavə olunur:
- "You're from Baku, **aren't you**?"
- "She speaks English, **doesn't she**?"
- "They didn't come, **did they**?"

### Qayda:
- Müsbət cümlə → mənfi tag
- Mənfi cümlə → müsbət tag
- Tag-in auxiliary-si əsas auxiliary ilə eyni

---

## 8. Təcrübə — Sualları Tamamla

Hər cümlə üçün sual qur:

1. She works in Baku. → _______ she work in Baku?
2. They went to the cinema. → _______ they go to the cinema?
3. He is a developer. → _______ he a developer?
4. You have finished the project. → _______ you finished the project?
5. She can speak three languages. → _______ she speak three languages?
6. They are working from home. → _______ they working from home?
7. He wrote the email. → _______ he write the email?

### Cavablar:
1. **Does**
2. **Did**
3. **Is**
4. **Have**
5. **Can**
6. **Are**
7. **Did**

---

## 9. WH Sualı Qurma — Təcrübə

Cümlədəki altdan xətt çəkilmiş sözə uyğun sual qur:

1. I live in **Baku**. → Where ____________?
2. She works at **Google**. → Where ____________?
3. The meeting is at **3 pm**. → When ____________?
4. I drink **three** cups of coffee. → How many ____________?
5. He is **tired**. → Why is he tired? / How is he feeling?

### Cavablar:
1. **Where do you live?**
2. **Where does she work?**
3. **When is the meeting?** (be felidir, "is" auxiliary kimi işləyir)
4. **How many cups of coffee do you drink?**
5. **How is he?** / **How is he feeling?**

---

## 10. Sürətli Yoxlama — Sual Qurmağın 4 Addımı

1. **Tense nədir?** (Present, Past, Future, Perfect)
2. **Hansı auxiliary?** (do/does/did/is/are/was/were/have/has/had/will/can...)
3. **Auxiliary-ni birinci yerə qoy** (WH sözdən sonra)
4. **Əsas fel V1 formasında qalır** (auxiliary artıq tense bildirir)

### Nümunə addım-addım:
Cümlə: "She **went** to work yesterday."  
Sual: "Did she **go** to work yesterday?"

1. Tense: Past Simple
2. Auxiliary: **did**
3. Did (başda) + she (subyekt) + go (V1)
4. "Did she go to work yesterday?"

---

## 11. Kombinəedilmiş Sual (Yes/No + WH)

Bəzən bir cümlədə sual formasının iki elementi işlənə bilər:
- "Could you tell me **whether / if** you like coffee?"
- "I don't know **if** he will come."
- "Do you remember **what time** the meeting starts?"

## 12. B1 Səviyyə Test — Özünü Yoxla

Bu 10 cümləni ingiliscə suala çevir:

1. Sən Bakıda yaşayırsan?
2. O harada işləyir?
3. Sən nə vaxt gəldin?
4. Onlar niyə gecikdilər?
5. Sən bu kitabı oxudun?
6. Biz bunu edə bilərik?
7. O kimə zəng etdi?
8. Müsahibə nə qədər çəkdi?
9. Sən sabah harada olacaqsan?
10. Onlar iş tapıblar?

### Cavablar:
1. Do you live in Baku?
2. Where does he/she work?
3. When did you arrive / come?
4. Why were they late?
5. Did you read this book? / Have you read this book?
6. Can we do this?
7. Who did he/she call? (or: Who called him/her? — subyekt olaraq)
8. How long did the interview take?
9. Where will you be tomorrow?
10. Have they found a job?
