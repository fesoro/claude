# Dolayı Suallar (Indirect Questions)

## Səviyyə
B1-B2

## Nədir?
**Indirect questions** — bir sualı nəzakətli şəkildə ifadə etmək üçün istifadə edilir. Birbaşa sualdan daha formal və nəzakətlidir.

---

## Birbaşa vs Dolayı Sual

| Birbaşa (Direct) | Dolayı (Indirect) |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Where is the station? | Could you tell me **where the station is**? |
| What time does it start? | Do you know **what time it starts**? |
| How much does it cost? | Could you tell me **how much it costs**? |
| Is she coming? | Do you know **if she is coming**? |

---

## Əsas Fərq: Söz Sırası

Dolayı sualda **normal cümlə quruluşu** işlənir (sual quruluşu deyil!):

```
Birbaşa:  Where does he live?   (V + S)
Dolayı:   ...where he lives.    (S + V) ← Normal quruluş!
```

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| Where **does** she work? | Do you know where **she works**? |
| What **did** he say? | Can you tell me what **he said**? |
| How **long will** it take? | I wonder how long **it will take**. |
| When **is** the train? | Could you tell me when **the train is**? |

---

## Giriş Cümləcikləri

| Formal/Nəzakətli | Gündəlik |
|-----------------|---------|
| Could you tell me...? | Do you know...? |
| Would you mind telling me...? | Can you tell me...? |
| I was wondering if/whether... | I'd like to know... |
| Do you have any idea...? | I wonder... |
| I was hoping you could tell me... | — |

---

## Yes/No Suallar → if / whether

Birbaşa yes/no suallar dolayı şəkildə **if** yaxud **whether** ilə ifadə edilir:

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| Is she coming? | Do you know **if** she is coming? |
| Did he call? | Can you tell me **whether** he called? |
| Are there any seats? | I wonder **if** there are any seats. |
| Will it rain? | Do you know **whether** it will rain? |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|-------|
| Do you know where is the bank? | Do you know where **the bank is**? | S+V quruluşu (sual deyil) |
| Can you tell me what does he want? | Can you tell me what **he wants**? | do/does/did istifadə edilmir |
| I wonder where did she go? | I wonder where **she went**? | Keçmiş felin normal forması |
| Could you tell me is she here? | Could you tell me **if** she is here? | Yes/no → if/whether |

---

## Nəzakətli İfadə Üçün İstifadə

**Birbaşa (kobud ola bilər):**
- Where is the toilet?

**Dolayı (nəzakətli):**
- Could you tell me where the toilet is?
- I was wondering where the toilet is.
- Would you mind telling me where the toilet is?

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Dolayı suala çevirin
1. What time does the museum open?
   → Could you tell me...

2. Is there a pharmacy nearby?
   → Do you know...

3. How long has she been working here?
   → I wonder...

4. Where did he go last night?
   → Can you tell me...

**Cavablar:**
1. Could you tell me **what time the museum opens**?
2. Do you know **if there is a pharmacy nearby**?
3. I wonder **how long she has been working here**.
4. Can you tell me **where he went last night**?
