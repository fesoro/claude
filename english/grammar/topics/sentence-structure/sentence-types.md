# Sentence Types

## 1. Simple Sentence (Sadə cümlə)
One independent clause — one subject + one verb.

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| She reads. | O oxuyur. |
| The dog barked loudly. | İt ucadan hürüdü. |
| I love coffee in the morning. | Mən səhər qəhvəni sevirəm. |

---

## 2. Compound Sentence (Mürəkkəb bağlı cümlə)
Two or more independent clauses joined by **coordinating conjunctions** (FANBOYS: for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so) or a semicolon.

**Structure:** Independent clause + **conjunction** + Independent clause

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| I was tired, **but** I kept working. | Yorğun idim, **amma** işləməyə davam etdim. |
| She studied hard, **so** she passed. | O çox çalışdı, **ona görə** keçdi. |
| He likes tea, **and** she likes coffee. | O çay sevir, **o da** qəhvə sevir. |

---

## 3. Complex Sentence (Mürəkkəb tabe cümlə)
One independent clause + one or more **dependent (subordinate) clauses**, joined by subordinating conjunctions (because, although, when, if, since, unless...).

**Structure:** Independent clause + **subordinator** + Dependent clause
*(or: Dependent clause + **,** + Independent clause)*

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| I left **because** I was tired. | **Çünki** yorğun idim, getdim. |
| **Although** it rained, we went out. | **Yağış yağsa da**, çıxdıq. |
| **When** she arrived, everyone clapped. | **O gələndə**, hamı alqışladı. |
| I'll call you **if** I'm late. | Gecikirsəm, seni arayacam. |

---

## 4. Compound-Complex Sentence (Qarışıq mürəkkəb cümlə)
Two or more independent clauses **AND** at least one dependent clause.

| Example | Azerbaijani |
|---------|-------------|
| **Although** it was cold, we went hiking and we enjoyed it. | Soyuq olsa da, yürüşə getdik və zövq aldıq. |
| She passed the exam **because** she studied, so she celebrated. | Çalışdığı üçün imtahanı keçdi, buna görə qeyd etdi. |

---

## 5. Sentence Types by Purpose

| Type | Usage | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Declarative** | Fact / statement | The sun rises in the east. |
| **Interrogative** | Question | Where do you live? |
| **Imperative** | Command / request | Please close the door. |
| **Exclamatory** | Strong feeling | What a beautiful day! |

---

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct | Problem |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I was tired. But I continued. | I was tired, but I continued. | "But" cümləni tək başlamaz (informal istisna) |
| Although it was late, but I stayed. | Although it was late, I stayed. | "Although" + "but" birlikdə işlənmir |
| Because I was hungry. | I ate because I was hungry. | Dependent clause tək cümlə ola bilməz |

---

## Quick Reference

```
Simple:           [S + V + O]
Compound:         [S+V] , conj [S+V]
Complex:          [S+V] + sub [S+V]
Compound-Complex: [S+V] + sub [S+V] , conj [S+V]
```
