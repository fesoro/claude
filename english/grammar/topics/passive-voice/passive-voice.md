# Passiv Növ (Passive Voice)

## Səviyyə: B1-C1

## Nədir?
Aktiv növdə **edən** önəmlidir. Passiv növdə **edilən** önəmlidir.

- **Aktiv:** The company **fired** 50 workers. (şirkət — əsas)
- **Passiv:** 50 workers **were fired**. (işçilər — əsas)

---

## Nə Vaxt İşlənir?

1. **Kim etdiyini bilmirik:**
   - My wallet was stolen. (kim çaldı — bilinmir)

2. **Kim etdiyindən daha çox nə olduğu vacibdir:**
   - The Eiffel Tower was built in 1889. (kim tikdi — az vacib)

3. **Rəsmi/akademik üslub:**
   - The data was analysed using statistical software.

4. **Qəsdən subyek gizlədilir:**
   - Mistakes were made. (kim etdi — gizlədilir)

---

## Quruluş: BE + Past Participle

| Zaman | Aktiv | Passiv |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **Present Simple** | make / makes | am/is/are **made** |
| **Past Simple** | made | was/were **made** |
| **Present Continuous** | am/is/are making | am/is/are **being made** |
| **Past Continuous** | was/were making | was/were **being made** |
| **Present Perfect** | have/has made | have/has **been made** |
| **Past Perfect** | had made | had **been made** |
| **Future (will)** | will make | will **be made** |
| **Modal** | can/should/must make | can/should/must **be made** |
| **Going to** | am/is/are going to make | am/is/are going to **be made** |

---

## Nümunələr — Bütün Zamanlarda

| Zaman | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| Present Simple | This product **is made** in Germany. |
| Past Simple | The law **was passed** in 2020. |
| Present Continuous | The road **is being repaired** at the moment. |
| Past Continuous | The suspect **was being questioned** when we arrived. |
| Present Perfect | The report **has been submitted**. |
| Past Perfect | By the time we arrived, the decision **had been made**. |
| Future will | The results **will be announced** tomorrow. |
| Modal | The problem **should be solved** immediately. |

---

## Active → Passive Çevirmə

**4 addım:**
1. Aktivdəki **obyekti** passivin **subyekti** et
2. Feli **be + past participle** formasına gətir (zamana görə be seç)
3. Aktivdəki **subyekti** "**by** ___" ilə sona qoy (lazım gəlsə)
4. Gereksiz məlumatı at

**Nümunə:**
> Active: **The police** arrested **the suspect** yesterday.
> Passive: **The suspect** was arrested **(by the police)** yesterday.

---

## BY — Kim Tərəfindən

"By" — agenti (edəni) bildirmək üçün:
- This bridge **was designed** by a famous architect.
- "Romeo and Juliet" **was written** by Shakespeare.

"By" atılır — əgər agent bilinmirsə ya vacib deyilsə:
- My car **was stolen** last night. ✓
- The decision **was made** without consulting us. ✓

---

## İki Obyektli Cümlələr

Bəzi fellər iki obyekt alır — hər ikisi passiv subyekti ola bilər:

> Active: They **gave** her a prize.
> Passive 1: **She was given** a prize. ✓ (daha çox işlənir)
> Passive 2: **A prize was given to her.** ✓

---

## Passiv + İnfinitive (Formal Xəbərlər)

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| is said to | He **is said to be** very wealthy. |
| is believed to | The building **is believed to be** 500 years old. |
| is thought to | The virus **is thought to have** originated in... |
| is reported to | She **is reported to be** considering resignation. |
| is known to | He **is known to have** worked for the agency. |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | Səbəb |
|------|--------|-------|
| The book was written **from** Tolstoy. | written **by** Tolstoy | agent → "by" |
| It is being **build**. | It is being **built**. | build qeyri-müntəzəm: built |
| The work was **did**. | The work was **done**. | do qeyri-müntəzəm: done |
| It **is known** to **worked** there. | is known to **have worked** | passiv + perfect infinitive |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Active → Passive çevirin
1. They clean the office every day.
2. Someone broke the window last night.
3. They are building a new hospital.
4. The committee has approved the plan.
5. They will announce the results on Friday.
6. You should return the books immediately.

**Cavablar:**
1. The office **is cleaned** every day.
2. The window **was broken** last night.
3. A new hospital **is being built**.
4. The plan **has been approved**.
5. The results **will be announced** on Friday.
6. The books **should be returned** immediately.

### 2. Hansı cümlə daha yaxşıdır? Niyə?
a) Someone stole my laptop.
b) My laptop was stolen.

**Cavab:** b) — kim etdiyini bilmirik; "my laptop" vacibdir.
