# Durğu İşarələri (Punctuation Guide)

## Səviyyə: A2-C1

---

## 1. Nöqtə (Full Stop / Period) — .

Cümləni bitirmək üçün:
- I live in Baku**.**
- She is a teacher**.**

Abbreviaturalarda (British-də bəzən atılır):
- Mr. / Mrs. / Dr. (American) → Mr / Mrs / Dr (British)

---

## 2. Vergül (Comma) — ,

### a) Siyahıda
- I bought apples**,** oranges**,** and bananas.
- She is smart**,** hardworking**,** and kind.

### b) İki əsas cümləni bağlayıcıyla birləşdirəndə
- I was tired**,** but I kept working.
- It rained**,** so we stayed home.

### c) Budaq cümlə əvvəldə olanda
- **Although** it was cold**,** we went out.
- **If** you need help**,** call me.

### d) Non-defining relative clause
- My sister**,** who lives in London**,** is a lawyer.

### e) Xitab (Direct address)
- **John,** can you help me?
- Thank you**,** everyone.

### f) Ara cümlə (Parenthetical)
- This policy**,** however**,** has failed.
- The project**,** in fact**,** was never completed.

---

## 3. Nöqtəli Vergül (Semicolon) — ;

İki əlaqəli tam cümləni birləşdirir (bağlayıcı olmadan):
- I wanted to go**;** she preferred to stay.
- The results were positive**;** however, more research is needed.

---

## 4. İki Nöqtə (Colon) — :

### Siyahıdan əvvəl
- You need three things**:** patience, practice, and dedication.

### Açıqlamadan əvvəl
- There is one problem**:** we have no money.
- She had one goal**:** to win the championship.

### Sitatdan əvvəl (formal)
- As Einstein said**:** "Imagination is more important than knowledge."

---

## 5. Apostrof (Apostrophe) — '

### Qısaltma (Contraction)
- I **am** → I**'m**
- do **not** → don**'t**
- it **is** / it **has** → it**'s**
- they **are** → they**'re**
- **will** not → won**'t**

### Mülkiyyət (Possessive)
- John**'s** book (John-un kitabı)
- my sister**'s** car (bacımın maşını)
- the children**'s** toys (uşaqların oyuncaqları)
- the students**'** results (tələbələrin nəticələri — cəm)

> **Diqqət:** it**'s** = it is / it has
> its = mülkiyyət (apostrof yoxdur!)
> - The cat licked **its** paw. ✓
> - **It's** a beautiful day. ✓

---

## 6. Sual İşarəsi (Question Mark) — ?

- What is your name**?**
- Are you coming**?**

İfadə sual kimi görünmürsə işlənmir (indirect question):
- She asked where I lived. (nöqtə — sual işarəsi yox)
- I wonder whether he knows. (nöqtə)

---

## 7. Nida İşarəsi (Exclamation Mark) — !

- What a beautiful view**!**
- Stop**!** Don't move**!**

> Akademik yazıda çox az işlənir. İnformal yazıda işlənir.

---

## 8. Dırnaq İşarəsi (Quotation Marks) — " " / ' '

### Birbaşa söyləm (Direct Speech)
- She said**,** "I'll be there at 5."
- **"**Where are you going**?"** he asked.

### Başlıqlar / Xüsusi adlar
- I read an article called "The Future of AI."

> **British:** single quotes ' '
> **American:** double quotes " "

---

## 9. Tire (Dash & Hyphen)

### Defis (Hyphen) — -
Mürəkkəb sözlərdə:
- well-known, long-term, mother-in-law
- twenty-five, forty-three

### Em Dash — —
Vurğu, əlavə məlumat, dramatik pauza:
- The answer — if there is one — remains unclear.
- She opened the door — and froze.

---

## 10. Mötərizə (Brackets / Parentheses) — ( )

Əlavə məlumat:
- The meeting (which lasted three hours) was productive.
- See page 45 (Figure 3) for details.

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| its' | its | "its" (mülkiyyət) apostrofsuz |
| its | it's | "it is/has" — apostrof lazımdır |
| I went to London, and I saw Big Ben. | I went to London and saw Big Ben. | Eyni subyektdə vergülə ehtiyac yox |
| Although it was cold, but we went. | Although it was cold, we went. | although + but eyni vaxtda olmaz |
| She said, "come here". | She said, "Come here." | Dırnaq içi böyük hərf |

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Durğu işarələri əlavə edin
1. I need three things time money and patience
2. Although she was tired she kept working
3. Its a beautiful day isnt it
4. She said I will be late
5. My brother who lives in Paris is visiting us

**Cavablar:**
1. I need three things: time, money, and patience.
2. Although she was tired, she kept working.
3. It's a beautiful day, isn't it?
4. She said, "I will be late."
5. My brother, who lives in Paris, is visiting us.
