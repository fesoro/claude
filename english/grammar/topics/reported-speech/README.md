# Dolayı Nitq (Reported Speech)

## Səviyyə
B1 (əsas) → B2 (tam)

## Reported Speech Nədir?
Birinin dediyini başqasına ötürəndə zamanlar, əvəzliklər dəyişir. 

| Birbaşa Nitq | Dolayı Nitq |
|-------------|------------|
| She said: "I **am** tired." | She said (that) she **was** tired. |
| He said: "I **will** come." | He said (that) he **would** come. |

---

## Zaman Dəyişiklikləri (Backshift)

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| Present Simple | Past Simple |
| Present Continuous | Past Continuous |
| Present Perfect | Past Perfect |
| Past Simple | Past Perfect |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| must | had to |

**Nümunələr:**

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| "I **work** here." | She said she **worked** there. |
| "I **am working**." | She said she **was working**. |
| "I **have finished**." | She said she **had finished**. |
| "I **will help**." | She said she **would help**. |
| "I **can** do it." | She said she **could** do it. |

---

## Əvəzliklərin Dəyişməsi

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| I | he/she |
| my | his/her |
| we | they |
| you | I/he/she/they (kontekstdən asılı) |
| here | there |
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| last week | the week before |

---

## Say vs. Tell

| | Say | Tell |
|--|-----|------|
| **Quruluş** | say (that) | tell + şəxs + (that) |
| **Nümunə** | She **said** (that) she was tired. | She **told me** (that) she was tired. |

- ✅ She **said** she was coming.
- ✅ She **told me** she was coming.
- ❌ She **told** she was coming. (şəxs lazımdır!)
- ❌ She **said me** she was coming. (say + şəxs olmur!)

---

## Sualların Dolayı Nitqi

**Evet/Xeyr Sualları** → if / whether ilə:

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| "Are you coming?" | He asked **if** I was coming. |
| "Did she leave?" | She asked **whether** he had left. |

**Sual Sözlü Suallar** → sual sözü + normal cümlə quruluşu:

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| "Where do you live?" | He asked where I **lived**. |
| "What time will you come?" | She asked what time I **would come**. |

> **Qeyd:** Dolayı sualda sual quruluşu yox, **normal cümlə quruluşu** işlənir!  
> ❌ He asked where **did I live**.  
> ✅ He asked where I **lived**.

---

## Əmr Cümlələrinin Dolayı Nitqi

→ told + to + V / told + not to + V

| Birbaşa | Dolayı |
|---------|--------|
| "Come here!" | She **told** me **to come** there. |
| "Don't touch it!" | He **told** me **not to touch** it. |
| "Please sit down." | She **asked** me **to sit down**. |

---

## Məşq

Birbaşa nitqi dolayıya çevirin:
1. "I'm studying for the exam," she said.
2. "We will visit you tomorrow," they said.
3. "Did you finish your homework?" the teacher asked.
4. "Don't be late!" he told me.

**Cavablar:**
1. She said she was studying for the exam.
2. They said they would visit me the next day.
3. The teacher asked if I had finished my homework.
4. He told me not to be late.

---

## Xülasə Kartı

```
REPORTED SPEECH

Zamanlar bir addım geridə gedir (backshift)
Əvəzliklər, zaman ifadələri dəyişir

say → said (that)
tell → told + şəxs + (that)

Sual: if/whether (bəli/xeyr) / sual sözü + normal sıra
Əmr: told to + V / told not to + V
```
