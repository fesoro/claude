# Dolayı Nitq (Reported Speech)

## Səviyyə: B1-B2

## Nədir?
Birinin dediklərini **öz sözlərinlə** başqasına çatdırmaq.

- **Direct:** "I am tired," she said.
- **Reported:** She said (that) she **was** tired.

---

## Zaman Çevrilməsi

Reported speech-də zaman bir addım geriyə çəkilir:

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|---------------|----------------|
| Present Simple | Past Simple |
| Present Continuous | Past Continuous |
| Present Perfect | Past Perfect |
| Past Simple | Past Perfect |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| must | had to |
| shall | would |

**Nümunələr:**
- "I **work** here." → She said she **worked** there.
- "I **am working**." → He said he **was working**.
- "I **have finished**." → She said she **had finished**.
- "I **went** yesterday." → He said he **had gone** the day before.
- "I **will** come." → She said she **would** come.
- "I **can** swim." → He said he **could** swim.

---

## Say vs Tell

| | İşlənmə | Nümunə |
|---|---|---|
| **say** | say + (that) | She **said** (that) she was tired. |
| **tell** | tell + person + (that) | She **told me** (that) she was tired. |

> **Tez-tez edilən səhv:**
> - ✗ She said me she was tired.
> - ✓ She **told me** she was tired.
> - ✓ She **said** she was tired.

---

## Zaman və Yer İfadələrinin Dəyişməsi

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|---------|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| this week | that week |
| last week | the week before |
| next week | the following week |
| here | there |
| this | that |
| these | those |
| ago | before |

**Nümunə:**
- "I saw him **yesterday**." → She said she had seen him **the day before**.
- "I'll come **tomorrow**." → He said he would come **the next day**.
- "I live **here**." → She said she lived **there**.

---

## Sualların Reportlanması

### Wh- suallar
**Quruluş:** asked + wh- word + **S + V** (yardımçı fel öndə deyil!)

- "Where **do** you live?" → He asked where I **lived**. ✓
- "What **are** you doing?" → She asked what I **was doing**. ✓
- ✗ He asked where **did** I live. (yanlış sıra)

### Yes/No suallar
**Quruluş:** asked + **if/whether** + S + V

- "**Are** you coming?" → She asked **if** I **was** coming.
- "**Do** you like it?" → He asked **whether** I **liked** it.

---

## Əmrlərin Reportlanması (Reported Commands)

**Quruluş:** told/asked/ordered + person + **to** + base verb (ya da **not to**)

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|---------|
| "Close the door!" | He told me **to close** the door. |
| "Don't be late." | She told him **not to be** late. |
| "Please sit down." | She asked us **to sit** down. |
| "Stop talking!" | The teacher ordered them **to stop** talking. |

---

## Reporting Verbs — Müxtəlif Fellər

Hamısı "said" deyil — daha dəqiq fellər:

| Fel | İşlənmə | Nümunə |
|-----|---------|--------|
| **told** | tell + person | She **told** me she was leaving. |
| **asked** | ask + person | He **asked** me to help. |
| **warned** | warn + person | She **warned** me not to go. |
| **promised** | promise + to-inf | He **promised** to call. |
| **offered** | offer + to-inf | She **offered** to help. |
| **refused** | refuse + to-inf | He **refused** to answer. |
| **suggested** | suggest + -ing | She **suggested** going out. |
| **admitted** | admit + -ing | He **admitted** making a mistake. |
| **denied** | deny + -ing | She **denied** stealing it. |
| **explained** | explain + that | He **explained** that it was late. |
| **agreed** | agree + to-inf | She **agreed** to come. |
| **recommended** | recommend + -ing | He **recommended** trying it. |

---

## Zaman Dəyişməsi Olmayan Hallar

Əgər söylənilən **hələ doğrudursa**, zaman dəyişdirilməyə bilər:

- "The Earth **is** round." → He said the Earth **is** round. ✓ (elmi həqiqət)
- "I **live** in Baku." → She said she **lives** in Baku. ✓ (hələ doğrudur)

---

## Nümunə: Uzun Dialoqun Reportlanması

**Orijinal dialoq:**
> A: "I'm leaving tomorrow."
> B: "Why are you going so soon?"
> A: "I have an important meeting on Monday."
> B: "Will you come back next week?"
> A: "I'm not sure. I might stay longer."

**Reported:**
> A told B that he was leaving the following day. B asked why he was going so soon. A explained that he had an important meeting on Monday. B asked whether he would come back the following week. A replied that he was not sure and that he might stay longer.

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Reported speech-ə çevirin
1. "I am very busy today." → She said ___
2. "We went to the cinema yesterday." → They said ___
3. "I will call you tomorrow." → He told me ___
4. "I can't come to the party." → She said ___

**Cavablar:**
1. she was very busy that day.
2. they had gone to the cinema the day before.
3. he would call me the next day.
4. she couldn't come to the party.

### 2. Sualları reportlayın
1. "Where do you work?" (asked me) → He asked me ___
2. "Are you married?" (wanted to know) → She wanted to know ___
3. "What time does the train leave?" (asked) → She asked ___

**Cavablar:**
1. where I worked.
2. if/whether I was married.
3. what time the train left.
