# Keçimli və Keçimsiz Fellər (Transitive & Intransitive Verbs)

## Səviyyə
B1

## Nədir?

| Növ | İzah | Nümunə |
|-----|------|--------|
| **Transitive (keçimli)** | Tamamlıq tələb edir | She **reads** a book. |
| **Intransitive (keçimsiz)** | Tamamlıq tələb etmir | He **sleeps**. |

---

## Transitive Verbs (Keçimli Fellər)

Sualı: **WHAT?** (nəyi?) / **WHOM?** (kimi?)

- She **bought** a car. → What? a car ✓
- He **loves** her. → Whom? her ✓
- They **built** the house. → What? the house ✓

**Tamamlıq olmadan cümlə natamamdır:**
- ❌ She bought. (nəyi?)
- ❌ He loves. (kimi?)

---

## Intransitive Verbs (Keçimsiz Fellər)

Sonrasında tamamlıq gəlmir:

- He **sleeps** well.
- She **arrived** late.
- The sun **rises** every morning.
- They **laughed** loudly.
- The baby **cried** all night.

**Tamamlıq gələ bilməz:**
- ❌ He slept the bed. (yanlış)
- ✅ He slept **in** the bed. (prep + noun — mümkündür)

---

## Hər İki Növdə İşlənən Fellər

Bəzi fellər həm keçimli, həm keçimsiz ola bilər:

| Fel | Keçimli | Keçimsiz |
|-----|---------|---------|
| **run** | She runs **a business**. | She runs every day. |
| **read** | I read **the book**. | I read a lot. |
| **move** | He moved **the table**. | The crowd moved slowly. |
| **speak** | She speaks **English**. | He doesn't speak much. |
| **eat** | We ate **dinner**. | Let's eat! |
| **drive** | She drives **a car**. | He drives carefully. |
| **open** | Please open **the door**. | The door opened. |
| **grow** | They grow **vegetables**. | Children grow fast. |

---

## Passiv Cümlə ilə Əlaqə

Yalnız **keçimli** fellər passiv cümlədə işlənə bilər:

| Aktiv | Passiv |
|-------|--------|
| She **wrote** the letter. | The letter **was written** by her. ✓ |
| He **sleeps** here. | ❌ Here was slept by him. (mümkün deyil) |
| They **built** the bridge. | The bridge **was built** by them. ✓ |

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| She explained me. | She explained **to me** / She explained **it to me**. | "Explain" birbaşa şəxs tamamlığı almır |
| He reached to the station. | He reached **the station**. | "Reach" transitive — preposition olmadan |
| They discussed about the plan. | They discussed **the plan**. | "Discuss" transitive — about olmadan |
| Please listen me. | Please listen **to me**. | "Listen" intransitive — to tələb edir |
| She married with him. | She married **him**. | "Marry" transitive — with olmadan |

---

## Ən Çox Yanlış İşlənən Fellər

### Preposition olmadan işlənən keçimli fellər:
- **reach** → reach the top (not "reach to")
- **discuss** → discuss the problem (not "discuss about")
- **marry** → marry someone (not "marry with")
- **enter** → enter the room (not "enter into")
- **approach** → approach the building (not "approach to")
- **resemble** → resemble your father (not "resemble to")

### Preposition ilə işlənən keçimsiz fellər:
- **listen** → listen **to** music
- **look** → look **at** something
- **wait** → wait **for** someone
- **agree** → agree **with** someone
- **belong** → belong **to** someone
- **talk** → talk **to/with** someone

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Keçimli (T) yoxsa Keçimsiz (I)?
1. She sang a beautiful song. ___
2. The dog barked loudly. ___
3. He wrote a letter. ___
4. They arrived at noon. ___
5. She opened the window. ___
6. The baby smiled. ___

**Cavablar:** 1-T, 2-I, 3-T, 4-I, 5-T, 6-I

### 2. Yanlış preposition-u düzəldin
1. Please explain me the problem.
2. She reached to the top of the mountain.
3. They discussed about the new project.
4. He married with her last year.
5. Please listen me carefully.

**Cavablar:**
1. explain **the problem to me**
2. reached **the top**
3. discussed **the new project**
4. married **her**
5. listen **to me**
