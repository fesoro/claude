# Bağlayıcı Fellər (Linking Verbs)

## Səviyyə
A2-B1

## Nədir?
**Linking verbs** (bağlayıcı fellər) — subyekti təsvir edən sifət və ya isimə bağlayan fellərdir. Hərəkət bildirmir, **vəziyyət** bildirir.

```
Subject + Linking Verb + Adjective/Noun (Predicate)
She     + looks       + tired.
It      + is          + a problem.
```

---

## Ən Çox İşlənən Linking Verbs

### 1. BE (olmaq) — ən əsası
- She **is** happy.
- They **are** doctors.
- It **was** cold yesterday.

### 2. SEEM / APPEAR (görünmək)
- He **seems** tired.
- She **appears** nervous.
- It **seems** impossible.

### 3. BECOME / GET (olmaq, dəyişmək)
- He **became** angry.
- She **got** nervous before the exam.
- It **became** clear that he was lying.

### 4. Hiss orqanları ilə bağlı fellər

| Fel | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| **look** | görünmək | You **look** great! |
| **sound** | eşidilmək, səslənmək | That **sounds** interesting. |
| **smell** | iymək | This flower **smells** wonderful. |
| **taste** | dadı olmaq | The food **tastes** delicious. |
| **feel** | hiss etmək / hiss vermək | I **feel** sick. / The fabric **feels** soft. |

### 5. REMAIN / STAY / KEEP (qalmaq)
- She **remained** calm.
- He **stayed** quiet.
- Keep **still**! (Sakit dur!)

---

## Linking Verb vs Action Verb

**Diqqət:** Eyni fel həm linking, həm action ola bilər!

| Linking (sifət gəlir) | Action (zərf gəlir) |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| She **looks** beautiful. (O gözəl görünür) | She **looks** carefully. (O diqqətlə baxır) |
| The food **smells** good. | The dog **smelled** the bone. |
| He **feels** sick. | He **feels** the fabric. |
| The soup **tasted** great. | She **tasted** the soup. |

**Yoxlama üsulu:** Fel + **sifət** → linking / Fel + **zərf** → action

---

## Linking Verb Sonrasında Sifət (Adjective), Zərf Deyil!

❌ She looks **beautifully**.
✅ She looks **beautiful**. (sifət — subyekti təsvir edir)

❌ The music sounds **wonderfully**.
✅ The music sounds **wonderful**.

❌ I feel **badly** about it.
✅ I feel **bad** about it.

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | Səbəb |
|------|--------|-------|
| She looks beautifully. | She looks **beautiful**. | Linking verb + adjective (not adverb) |
| I feel badly today. | I feel **bad** today. | Linking verb + adjective |
| He became a doctor successfully. | He **became** a **successful** doctor. | Become + adjective/noun |
| The food smells well. | The food smells **good**. | "Well" adverb, lakin burada "good" (sifət) lazımdır |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Linking Verb (L) yoxsa Action Verb (A)?
1. She looks happy today. ___
2. She looked at the map carefully. ___
3. The cake smells amazing. ___
4. The dog smelled every corner. ___
5. He became a teacher. ___
6. He remained silent. ___

**Cavablar:** 1-L, 2-A, 3-L, 4-A, 5-L, 6-L

### 2. Sifət yoxsa Zərf?
1. You sound (angry / angrily).
2. She sings (beautiful / beautifully).
3. I feel (bad / badly) about the news.
4. He drives (careful / carefully).
5. The room looks (clean / cleanly).

**Cavablar:** 1-angry, 2-beautifully, 3-bad, 4-carefully, 5-clean
