# Stative Verbs — Vəziyyət Felləri

## Səviyyə
A2-B1

## Nədir?
Stative verbs vəziyyəti, hissi, düşüncəni bildirir. Bunlar adətən **Continuous (davam edən) formada işlənmir**.

## Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər

| ❌ Yanlış | ✅ Düzgün |
|-----------|-----------|
| I am knowing the answer. | I **know** the answer. |
| She is wanting coffee. | She **wants** coffee. |
| He is having a car. | He **has** a car. |
| I am believing you. | I **believe** you. |
| Are you understanding me? | **Do** you understand me? |

---

## İstisna: İkili Məna

Bəzi fellər həm stative, həm action ola bilər — məna dəyişir:

| Fel | Stative (sadə) | Action (davamlı) |
|-----|----------------|-----------------|
| **think** | I **think** it's right. (fikir bildirır) | I **am thinking** about you. (fikirləşir) |
| **have** | She **has** a car. (sahibdir) | I **am having** lunch. (yeyir) |
| **see** | I **see** a bird. (görür) | I **am seeing** a doctor. (görüşür) |
| **taste** | This **tastes** good. (dadı var) | He **is tasting** the soup. (dadına baxır) |
| **smell** | It **smells** nice. (iyi var) | She **is smelling** the flowers. (iy alır) |
| **feel** | I **feel** sick. (vəziyyət) | She **is feeling** the fabric. (əlilə yoxlayır) |
| **look** | She **looks** happy. (görünür) | She **is looking** at me. (baxır) |
| **appear** | He **appears** confident. (görünür) | She **is appearing** in a film. (iştirak edir) |

---

## Məşq

Düzgün formanı seçin:

1. I _______ (understand/am understanding) the lesson.
2. She _______ (is having/has) a meeting right now.
3. He _______ (thinks/is thinking) she is beautiful.
4. They _______ (are seeing/see) their parents every Sunday.

**Cavablar:** 1. understand 2. is having (action — meeting) 3. thinks 4. see
