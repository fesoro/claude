# Bağlayıcılar (Conjunctions)

## Səviyyə
A2-C1

---

## Növlər

### 1. Əlaqələndirici (Coordinating) — FANBOYS

| Bağlayıcı | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------------|--------|
| **F**or | çünki (rəsmi) | He was tired, **for** he had worked all day. |
| **A**nd | və | She bought bread **and** butter. |
| **N**or | nə də | He doesn't eat meat, **nor** does he eat fish. |
| **B**ut | amma | I wanted to go, **but** I was tired. |
| **O**r | ya, yaxud | Tea **or** coffee? |
| **Y**et | lakin, bununla belə | It was cold, **yet** she went out. |
| **S**o | ona görə | It was raining, **so** I stayed home. |

### 2. Tabe Edici (Subordinating)

| Bağlayıcı | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------------|--------|
| because | çünki | I stayed home **because** it rained. |
| although / though | baxmayaraq ki | **Although** it rained, I went out. |
| even though | həttə baxmayaraq ki | **Even though** he tried, he failed. |
| while | ikən, halbuki | **While** I was cooking, he called. |
| when | nə vaxt ki | Call me **when** you arrive. |
| if | əgər | **If** it rains, I'll stay. |
| unless | əgər...masaydı | **Unless** you hurry, you'll be late. |
| before | əvvəl | Wash your hands **before** eating. |
| after | sonra | **After** she left, we cleaned up. |
| since | -dan bəri, çünki | I've been here **since** morning. |
| until / till | -a qədər | Wait **until** I come back. |
| as soon as | dərhal ki | Call me **as soon as** you can. |
| as long as | -dığı müddətcə | Stay **as long as** you like. |
| so that | ki, -sın deyə | I studied hard **so that** I could pass. |
| in case | ehtimalı ilə | Take an umbrella **in case** it rains. |

### 3. Discourse Markers (B2-C1)

Rəsmi yazı və danışıq üçün:

| İfadə | Azərbaycanca | İşlənmə |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| However | lakin, bununla belə | Kontrast |
| Moreover / Furthermore | üstəlik, bundan əlavə | Əlavə məlumat |
| Therefore / Thus | buna görə | Nəticə |
| Nevertheless / Nonetheless | bununla belə | Kontrast (güclü) |
| In addition | əlavə olaraq | Əlavə məlumat |
| On the other hand | digər tərəfdən | Alternativ |
| In contrast | əksinə | Fərq |
| As a result | nəticə olaraq | Nəticə |
| For instance / For example | məsələn | Misal |
| In conclusion | nəticə olaraq | Yekun |

## Because vs. Because of

| | Quruluş | Nümunə |
|--|---------|--------|
| **because** | + cümlə | I stayed home **because it rained**. |
| **because of** | + isim/isim qrupu | I stayed home **because of the rain**. |

---

## Although vs. Despite / In spite of

| | Quruluş | Nümunə |
|--|---------|--------|
| **although** | + cümlə | **Although** it was cold, she went out. |
| **despite / in spite of** | + isim/-ing | **Despite the cold**, she went out. |

---

## Məşq

Uyğun bağlayıcını seçin:

1. She passed the exam _______ she didn't study much. (although/because)
2. I need to buy milk _______ cheese. (and/but)
3. He didn't come _______ he was sick. (although/because)
4. Take your jacket _______ it gets cold. (in case/so that)

**Cavablar:** 1. although 2. and 3. because 4. in case
