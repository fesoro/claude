# Tabe Edici Bağlayıcılar (Subordinating Conjunctions)

## Səviyyə: B1-B2

## Nədir?
Əsas cümləyə **budaq cümlə** (subordinate clause) bağlayan sözlər. Budaq cümlə tək başına mənalı deyil.

- **Main clause:** I stayed home.
- **Subordinate clause:** because it was raining.
- **Birlikdə:** I stayed home **because** it was raining.

---

## Kateqoriyalar

### Zaman (Time)
| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **when** | nə vaxt, -dığında | Call me **when** you arrive. |
| **while** | ikən | She called **while** I was cooking. |
| **before** | əvvəl | Wash your hands **before** you eat. |
| **after** | sonra | I'll call you **after** I finish. |
| **as soon as** | dərhal ki | I'll leave **as soon as** I'm ready. |
| **until / till** | -ənə qədər | Wait **until** I come back. |
| **since** | -dən bəri | I've lived here **since** I was a child. |
| **once** | bir dəfə ki, -dıqdan sonra | **Once** you try it, you'll love it. |
| **whenever** | hər dəfə ki | **Whenever** it rains, she stays home. |
| **by the time** | -ə qədər ki | **By the time** we arrived, it was over. |

### Səbəb (Reason)
| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **because** | çünki | I left **because** I was tired. |
| **since** | çünki, -dığından | **Since** it's late, we should go. |
| **as** | çünki (zəif) | **As** he was busy, she went alone. |
| **now that** | artıq ki | **Now that** you're here, let's start. |

### Güzəşt / Kontrast (Concession)
| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **although** | hərçənd | **Although** it was cold, we went out. |
| **even though** | -sə belə | I liked it **even though** it was long. |
| **though** | isə də (informal) | She passed, **though** it was difficult. |
| **whereas** | halbuki | I like coffee, **whereas** she prefers tea. |
| **while** | halbuki | **While** he is rich, he is unhappy. |

### Şərt (Condition)
| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **if** | əgər | I'll come **if** I have time. |
| **unless** | əgər...olmasaydı | I'll come **unless** it rains. |
| **as long as** | -dığı müddətcə | You can stay **as long as** you're quiet. |
| **provided (that)** | şərti ilə ki | I'll agree, **provided that** you help. |
| **in case** | ehtimala görə | Take an umbrella **in case** it rains. |
| **even if** | hətta olsa belə | I'll go **even if** it rains. |
| **only if** | yalnız əgər | I'll come **only if** you invite me. |

### Məqsəd (Purpose)
| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **so that** | ki, -sın deyə | She studied hard **so that** she could pass. |
| **in order that** | niyyətiylə (formal) | He left early **in order that** he could arrive. |

### Nəticə (Result)
| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **so...that** | o qədər...ki | It was **so** cold **that** the river froze. |
| **such...that** | elə bir...ki | It was **such** a cold day **that** I stayed in. |

---

## Mövqe — Cümlənin Əvvəli ya Sonu?

Budaq cümlə əvvəldə olarsa → **vergül** qoyulur:
- **Although** it was cold, we went. ✓ (vergül var)

Budaq cümlə sonda olarsa → **vergül yoxdur:**
- We went **although** it was cold. ✓ (vergül yox)

---

## Because vs Since vs As

Üçü də "çünki" mənasında — lakin güc fərqlidir:

| | Güc | İşlənmə |
|---|---|---|
| **because** | Güclü | Əsas səbəb üçün |
| **since** | Orta | Artıq bilinən səbəb |
| **as** | Zəif | Yan məlumat kimi |

- I stayed home **because** I was ill. (əsas səbəb)
- **Since** you're here, let's start. (artıq bilinir)
- **As** it was getting late, we left. (yan məlumat)

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | Səbəb |
|------|--------|-------|
| Although it was cold, but we went. | **Although** it was cold, we went. | although + but eyni vaxtda olmur |
| Because I was tired, so I slept. | **Because** I was tired, I slept. | because + so eyni vaxtda olmur |
| Unless you don't hurry, you'll miss it. | **Unless** you hurry, you'll miss it. | unless özü mənfidir |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Uyğun bağlayıcı seçin
1. ___ it was raining, we decided to stay inside. (Although/Because/While)
2. I'll help you ___ you ask nicely. (if/unless/while)
3. She left early ___ she could catch the train. (so that/although/because)
4. ___ I was cooking, the phone rang. (While/Before/Unless)
5. I won't go ___ you come with me. (unless/although/whereas)

**Cavablar:** 1-Although/Because (hər ikisi ola bilər, mənaya görə), 2-if, 3-so that, 4-While, 5-unless

### 2. İki cümləni birləşdirin
1. She was tired. She kept working. (although) → ___
2. It rained. We cancelled the trip. (because) → ___
3. You must hurry. You will miss the bus. (unless) → ___

**Cavablar:**
1. Although she was tired, she kept working.
2. We cancelled the trip because it rained.
3. Unless you hurry, you will miss the bus.
