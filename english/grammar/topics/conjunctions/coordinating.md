# Koordinasiya Bağlayıcıları (Coordinating Conjunctions)

## Səviyyə: A2-B1

## Nədir?
İki **bərabər** hissəni — iki cümləni, iki ismi, iki feli — birləşdirən sözlər.

**Qısa yaddaş:** **FANBOYS** — For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So

---

## 7 Koordinasiya Bağlayıcısı

| Bağlayıcı | Mənası | Nümunə |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **for** | çünki (formal) | She stayed inside, **for** it was raining. |
| **and** | və | I like tea **and** coffee. |
| **nor** | nə...nə (mənfi) | He didn't call, **nor** did he write. |
| **but** | amma, lakin | I'm tired **but** happy. |
| **or** | ya, yoxsa | Do you want tea **or** coffee? |
| **yet** | buna baxmayaraq | It's cold, **yet** she wears no coat. |
| **so** | buna görə, beləliklə | It was raining, **so** we stayed home. |

---

## Ətraflı İzah

### AND — Əlavə etmək
- İki şeyi birləşdirir
- **Nümunələr:**
  - She is smart **and** hardworking.
  - I went to the shop **and** bought some milk.
  - He speaks English, French, **and** Spanish.

> **Siyahıda:** Son elementdən əvvəl **and** işlənir: apples, oranges, **and** bananas.

### BUT — Ziddiyyət
- Gözlənilmədən fərqli bir şey bildirir
- **Nümunələr:**
  - I wanted to go, **but** I was too tired.
  - She's young **but** experienced.
  - It's expensive **but** worth it.

### OR — Seçim
- Alternativ bildirir
- **Nümunələr:**
  - Would you like tea **or** coffee?
  - You can stay **or** leave — it's up to you.
  - Is he coming today **or** tomorrow?

### SO — Nəticə
- Səbəb-nəticə: birinci cümlə → ikinci
- **Nümunələr:**
  - I was hungry, **so** I ate.
  - The shop was closed, **so** we went home.
  - She studied hard, **so** she passed.

### YET — Gözlənilməz ziddiyyət
- "But" kimi, lakin daha dramatik/rəsmi
- **Nümunələr:**
  - He is rich, **yet** unhappy.
  - The task was difficult, **yet** they succeeded.

### NOR — İkiqat mənfi
- "Neither...nor" ilə birlikdə, ya da ayrıca
- Nor-dan sonra yardımçı fel + subyekt sırası dəyişir!
- **Nümunələr:**
  - He doesn't drink, **nor** does he smoke.
  - She didn't call, **nor** did she send a message.

### FOR — Formal Səbəb
- "Because" kimi, lakin çox formal/ədəbi
- **Nümunə:**
  - He was exhausted, **for** he had worked all night.

---

## Vergül Qaydası

İki tam cümləni (subject + verb) birləşdirəndə **vergül** qoyulur:
- ✓ I was tired**,** but I continued.
- ✓ It rained**,** so we stayed home.

İki söz/ifadəni birləşdirəndə vergül **lazım deyil**:
- ✓ She is smart and kind. (vergül yox)
- ✓ I like tea or coffee. (vergül yox)

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | Səbəb |
|------|--------|-------|
| I went and I bought milk. | I went and **bought** milk. | Eyni subyektdə "I" təkrarlanmır |
| She is good but she is lazy. | She is good **but** lazy. | Qısa forma |
| It was cold, but yet we went. | It was cold, **yet** we went. | but + yet eyni vaxtda işlənmir |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Uyğun bağlayıcı seçin (and/but/or/so/yet)
1. I was tired, ___ I went to bed early.
2. She studied hard, ___ she failed the exam.
3. Would you like chicken ___ fish?
4. He is old ___ very active.
5. I was hungry, ___ I made a sandwich.

**Cavablar:** 1-so, 2-yet/but, 3-or, 4-but/yet, 5-so

### 2. İki cümləni birləşdirin
1. It was raining. We cancelled the picnic. → ___
2. She is talented. She is also very humble. → ___
3. You can take the bus. You can walk. → ___

**Cavablar:**
1. It was raining, so we cancelled the picnic.
2. She is talented and (also) very humble.
3. You can take the bus or walk.
