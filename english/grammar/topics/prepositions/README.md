# Qoşmalar (Prepositions)

## Səviyyə
A1-B2

## Qoşmalar Nədir?
Qoşmalar isim/əvəzliklə digər söz arasında əlaqəni göstərir: yer, zaman, istiqamət, üsul.

---

## Yer Qoşmaları (Prepositions of Place)

| Qoşma | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| in | içində | The cat is **in** the box. |
| on | üzərində | The book is **on** the table. |
| at | yanında, -da/-də | She's **at** the door. |
| under | altında | The dog is **under** the chair. |
| above / over | yuxarısında | The plane is **above** the clouds. |
| below / under | aşağısında | The fish is **below** the surface. |
| between | arasında (2 şey) | He sat **between** Tom and Sarah. |
| among | arasında (çoxlu) | She was **among** friends. |
| next to / beside | yanında | Sit **next to** me. |
| behind | arxasında | The car is **behind** the house. |
| in front of | qarşısında | She sat **in front of** me. |
| near / close to | yaxınında | The school is **near** the park. |
| opposite | qarşısında | The bank is **opposite** the post office. |
| inside | içərisindəki | **Inside** the house it was warm. |
| outside | xaricdəki | They waited **outside**. |

## Zaman Qoşmaları (Prepositions of Time)

### IN, ON, AT — ən vacib üçlük

| Qoşma | İşlənmə | Nümunə |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **in** | Ay, il, fəsil, günün hissəsi | in January, in 2020, in summer, in the morning |
| **on** | Gün, tarix, xüsusi gün | on Monday, on 5th March, on my birthday |
| **at** | Saat, xüsusi vaxt | at 8 pm, at noon, at midnight, at the weekend |

**Tez-tez edilən səhv:**
- ❌ in Monday → ✅ **on** Monday
- ❌ at January → ✅ **in** January
- ❌ on morning → ✅ **in** the morning

| Qoşma | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-------|--------------|--------|

| after | sonra | **After** dinner |
| since | -dan bəri | **Since** 2015 |

| during | ərzində | **During** the film |
| until / till | -a qədər | **Until** midnight |

## Tez-tez Qarışıq Düşən Qoşmalar

### in vs. at (yer üçün)

- **at** — konkret nöqtə: at the bus stop, at the corner, at the entrance
- **in** — içərisi, alan: in the room, in Baku, in the park

### for vs. since (zaman üçün)

- **for** — müddət: for 3 days, for a week, for years
- **since** — başlanğıc nöqtəsi: since Monday, since 2010

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| ❌ Yanlış | ✅ Düzgün | İzah |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| I am interested **for** music. | interested **in** music | Fixed expression |
| I arrived **to** Baku. | arrived **in** Baku | arrive in (şəhər) |
| I arrived **to** the airport. | arrived **at** the airport | arrive at (konkret yer) |
| She got married **with** him. | got married **to** him | get married to |
| I am good **in** English. | good **at** English | good at |
| He depends **from** others. | depends **on** | depend on |
| We discussed **about** it. | We discussed it. | discuss — qoşmasız |
