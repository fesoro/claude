# Infinitives (İnfinitivlər)

---

## Types of Infinitives

| Type | Form | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| **Full infinitive** | to + verb | I want **to go**. |
| **Bare infinitive** | verb only | She made me **wait**. |
| **Negative infinitive** | not to + verb | I decided **not to go**. |
| **Perfect infinitive** | to have + past participle | He seems **to have forgotten**. |
| **Passive infinitive** | to be + past participle | It needs **to be fixed**. |

---

## Verb + Object + Infinitive

| Structure | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **ask** sb to | She asked **me to help**. |
| **tell** sb to | He told **her to leave**. |
| **want** sb to | I want **you to understand**. |
| **expect** sb to | They expect **us to be** on time. |
| **allow** sb to | She allowed **him to stay**. |
| **persuade** sb to | He persuaded **me to go**. |
| **advise** sb to | I'd advise **you to rest**. |
| **remind** sb to | She reminded **me to lock** the door. |
| **warn** sb to | He warned **them not to touch** it. |
| **encourage** sb to | She encouraged **me to apply**. |
| **invite** sb to | They invited **us to join** them. |
| **force** sb to | He was forced **to confess**. |

## Bare Infinitive (without "to")

### After Modal Verbs:
- She **can** swim. / You **must** leave. / He **should** try.

### After Causative "make" and "let":
| Verb | Example |
|------|---------|
| **make** sb do | She **made** me **wait** an hour. |
| **let** sb do | He **let** me **use** his phone. |

> ⚠️ Passive "be made": She **was made to wait**. (infinitive geri qayıdır!)

### After Perception Verbs (see, hear, watch, feel, notice):
- I **saw** her **cross** the street. *(tamamlanmış hərəkət)*
- I **saw** her **crossing** the street. *(davam edən hərəkət)*

---

## Infinitive of Purpose

**"to"** məqsədi bildirir:
- I went to the shop **to buy** milk. (= in order to buy)
- She studied hard **to pass** the exam.

**Negative purpose:**
- I left early **so as not to** be late.
- He spoke quietly **in order not to** wake the baby.

---

## Adjective + Infinitive

| Structure | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| happy/glad/sorry **to** | I'm **glad to hear** that. |
| easy/difficult/hard **to** | It's **hard to explain**. |
| important/necessary **to** | It's **important to listen**. |
| first/last/next **to** | She was the **first to arrive**. |

---

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| She made him to wait. | She made him **wait**. |
| I want that you help me. | I want **you to help** me. |
| He decided going. | He decided **to go**. |
| She let me to use her phone. | She let me **use** her phone. |
| I saw her to cross the street. | I saw her **cross** the street. |
