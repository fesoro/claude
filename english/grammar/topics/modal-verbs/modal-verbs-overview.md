# Modal Fellər — Tam Müqayisəli Cədvəl

## Məna Üzrə Qruplaşdırma

### İmkan / Bacarıq
| Modal | Zaman | Nümunə |
|-------|-------|--------|
| can | İndiki/Gələcək | I can swim. |
| could | Keçmiş | I could run fast as a child. |
| be able to | Bütün zamanlar | I will be able to come. |

### İcazə
| Modal | Rəsmilik | Nümunə |
|-------|---------|--------|
| can | Qeyri-rəsmi | Can I use your phone? |
| could | Orta | Could I use your phone? |
| may | Rəsmi | May I come in? |

### Ehtimal
| Modal | Güc | Nümunə |
|-------|-----|--------|
| must | Çox güclü (~95%) | He must be tired. |
| should | Gözlənilən (~80%) | She should be here soon. |
| may | Orta (~50%) | It may rain. |
| might/could | Zəif (~30%) | It might rain. |
| can't | Mənfi əminlik | He can't be serious. |

### Zərurət / Məcburiyyət
| Modal | Mənbə | Nümunə |
|-------|-------|--------|
| must | Şəxsdən | I must call her. |
| have to | Xaricdən | I have to wear a uniform. |
| need to | Lazım | I need to study. |

### Qadağa / Lazımsızlıq
| Modal | Məna | Nümunə |
|-------|------|--------|
| mustn't | Qadağandır | You mustn't smoke here. |
| don't have to | Lazım deyil | You don't have to come. |
| needn't | Lazım deyil | You needn't worry. |

### Məsləhət
| Modal | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| should | You should rest. |
| ought to | You ought to see a doctor. |
| had better | You'd better hurry. |

---

## Qayda Xatırlatması

```
Modal + V (to-suz): can go, must eat, should call
İstisna: have to go, ought to rest, need to study

Keçmiş modal: modal + have + V3
  should have done, could have said, must have been
```
