| **Sıfır** | Present Simple | Present Simple | Həmişə doğru | If you heat ice, it melts. |
| **1-ci** | Present Simple | will + V | Real gələcək | If it rains, I'll stay. |
| **2-ci** | Past Simple | would + V | Xəyali indiki | If I had money, I'd travel. |
