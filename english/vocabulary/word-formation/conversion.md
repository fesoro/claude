# Conversion (Söz formasının dəyişməsi)

Conversion (Zero derivation) — sözün forması dəyişmədən fərqli nitq hissəsi kimi işlənməsi.

---

## Noun → Verb

Ən çox rast gəlinən növ. İsim fel kimi işlənir:

| Noun | Verb | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| email | to email | Can you **email** me the file? |
| text | to text | She **texted** me this morning. |
| google | to google | Just **google** it! |
| film | to film | They **filmed** the whole event. |
| book | to book | I **booked** a table for two. |
| water | to water | Don't forget to **water** the plants. |
| bottle | to bottle | The wine is **bottled** in France. |
| parent | to parent | **Parenting** is the hardest job. |
| highlight | to highlight | Please **highlight** the key points. |
| hand | to hand | **Hand** me that pen, please. |
| impact | to impact | How does this **impact** our plans? |
| access | to access | You can **access** the file online. |
| trial | to trial | The new system is being **trialled**. |
| chair | to chair | She **chaired** the meeting. |
| host | to host | Who is **hosting** the event? |
| pressure | to pressure | They **pressured** him to resign. |
| position | to position | The company **positioned** itself as eco-friendly. |

## Verb → Noun

Fel isim kimi işlənir:

| Verb | Noun | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| run | a run | I went for a **run** this morning. |
| walk | a walk | Let's go for a **walk**. |
| swim | a swim | She had a quick **swim** before work. |
| look | a look | Take a **look** at this. |
| try | a try | Give it a **try**! |
| call | a call | I'll give you a **call** later. |
| drink | a drink | Can I get you a **drink**? |
| laugh | a laugh | It was a real **laugh**. |
| cry | a cry | She had a good **cry**. |
| cut | a cut | He got a nasty **cut** on his hand. |
| reply | a reply | I'm waiting for a **reply**. |
| download | a download | The **download** took five minutes. |
| upgrade | an upgrade | I got a free **upgrade** to business class. |

## Adjective → Verb

| Adjective | Verb | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| dirty | to dirty | Don't **dirty** your clothes. |
| empty | to empty | Please **empty** the bin. |
| clean | to clean | Can you **clean** the table? |
| open | to open | **Open** the door, please. |
| close | to close | **Close** your eyes. |
| slow | to slow | **Slow** down — you're driving too fast. |
| calm | to calm | Try to **calm** down. |
| free | to free | The hostages were **freed**. |
| better | to better | She wants to **better** herself. |

## Adjective → Noun

| Adjective | Noun | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| daily | a daily | He reads a **daily** (newspaper). |
| final | a final | She passed her **finals**. |
| natural | a natural | She's a **natural** at singing. |
| classic | a classic | That film is a **classic**. |
| regular | a regular | He's one of our **regulars**. |
| local | a local | Ask a **local** for directions. |
| original | an original | Is this an **original** or a copy? |

## Same Word — Different Stress (Noun vs Verb)

Bəzi sözlər hər iki funksiyada işlənir, amma vurğu dəyişir:

| Word | As Noun | As Verb |
|------|---------|---------|
| **pre**sent | a **pre**sent (hədiyyə) | to pre**sent** (təqdim etmək) |
| **re**cord | a **re**cord (rekord) | to re**cord** (yazmaq) |
| **per**mit | a **per**mit (icazə) | to per**mit** (icazə vermək) |
| **ob**ject | an **ob**ject (obyekt) | to ob**ject** (etiraz etmək) |
| **pro**test | a **pro**test (etiraz) | to pro**test** (etiraz etmək) |
| **con**duct | **con**duct (davranış) | to con**duct** (idarə etmək) |
| **in**crease | an **in**crease | to in**crease** |
| **ex**port | an **ex**port | to ex**port** |

---

## Why Conversion Matters

1. **Lüğəti artırır** — bir sözü iki funksiyada istifadə edə bilərsən
2. **Danışıq dilindədir** — native speakers tez-tez istifadə edir
3. **Yeni texnologiya sözləri** — google, email, text, tweet hamısı bu yolla felə çevrilib
