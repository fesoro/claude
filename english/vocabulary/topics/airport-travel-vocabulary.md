## 12. Common Mistakes

### ✗ Azərbaycanca Səhvləri

- ✗ "I go to airport." (article → the)
- ✓ "I go to **the** airport."

- ✗ "Plane is delayed." (article)
- ✓ "**The** plane is delayed."

- ✗ "Book a fly." (noun form)
- ✓ "Book a **flight**."

- ✗ "Luggage**s**." (uncountable!)
- ✓ "**Luggage**." (no -s)

Hansı söz uyğundur?

2. I'd like an ______ seat, not window.
3. Please **fasten** your ______ .

2. aisle
3. seatbelt
4. baggage
5. declare
