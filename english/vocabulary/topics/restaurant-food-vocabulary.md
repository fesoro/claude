## 2. Arriving at Restaurant

### Greeting

- "Welcome. How many in your party?"
- "Table for **two**, please."
- "Do you have a **reservation**?"
- "Under the name **Smith**."

### Seating

- "Right this way."
- "Follow me."
- "Your table is ready."

### Preferences

- **Inside** / **outside** seating
- **Booth** (arxalıqlı yer)
- **Bar seat**
- **By the window**

### Wait times

- "How long is the wait?"
- "**20-minute wait**."
- "We'll text you when ready."
- "Put my name on the **waitlist**."

## 4. Ordering

### Polite requests

- "I'd like..."
- "Can I have...?"
- "Could I get...?"
- "I'll have..."
- "I'm going to go with..."

### Examples

- "I'd like the **steak**, please."
- "Can I have the **pasta**?"
- "I'll have a **burger** with fries."

### Specifics

- "**Medium rare**, please." (steak)
- "No **onions**."
- "**Gluten-free**, please."
- "**On the side**." (yanda)

### Drinks

- "**Water**, still or sparkling?"
- "I'll have a **beer**."
- "**Glass** of red wine."
- "**Tap water**, please." (pulsuz)

## 10. During the Meal

- "How is everything?"

### Your responses

- "Everything's great, thanks."
- "Could I get more water?"
- "Just the check, please."
- "I'm done, thanks."

### Problems

- "This is **cold** — could you warm it up?"
- "I didn't order this."
- "Can I send this back?"
- "This isn't what I ordered."

## 11. Paying

### Getting the bill

- "Check, please."
- "The bill, please." (BrE)
- "Could we have the check?"

### Splitting

- "Can we **split** the bill?"
- "**Separate checks**?"
- "One check, I'll cover."

### Payment

- "**Cash** or **card**?"
- "I'll pay with **card**."
- "Can I get a **receipt**?"
- "**Tap to pay**."

### Tipping (KRİTİK Amerika!)

- **AmE**: Tip **15-20%** standard
- **BrE**: Tip **10-12%**
- **Europe**: Tip **5-10%** or already included

### Tip phrases

- "Keep the **change**."
- "For **you**." (verirkən)
- "**Tip included**?"

## Test

Doldur:

1. "I'd like the ______ , **medium rare** please." (meat)
2. "Can I get the ______ , we're done." (bill)
3. "**Sparkling water**, please." (with gas)
4. "Is this ______ -free?" (gluten)
5. "I'll have a ______ ." (coffee drink)

**Sample answers:** 1. steak, 2. check, 3. ok, 4. gluten, 5. latte
