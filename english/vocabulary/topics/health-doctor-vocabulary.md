## 5. "I have a..."

Struktur: **I have a [symptom].**

- "I have a **headache**."
- "I have a **cough**."
- "I have a **sore throat**."
- "I have **fever**." (no article for fever — AmE)
- "I have **the flu**."
- "I have **a cold**."

### "I feel..."

- "I feel **sick**."
- "I feel **dizzy**."
- "I feel **nauseous**."
- "I feel **weak**."

## 6. At the Doctor

### Doctor asks

- "What's the problem?"
- "How can I help you today?"
- "What brings you in?"
- "Where does it hurt?"
- "When did it start?"
- "How long have you had this?"
- "Any other symptoms?"
- "Have you taken anything for it?"

### You answer

- "I've been feeling ..."
- "It started ..."
- "The pain is **sharp** / **dull** / **throbbing**."
- "On a scale of 1-10, it's a ..."

## 7. Examination

- "**Roll up your sleeve**." (check blood pressure)
- "**Open wide**." (mouth)

- **Temperature** = temperature

- **Height** = boy

### Tests

- **Urine test** = sidik
- **X-ray** = rəntgen

- "I'll **prescribe** you..."
- "You need **antibiotics**."
- "**Rest** and **drink fluids**."
- "Come back if it **gets worse**."

### Medicine types

- **Pills** / **Tablets** = həblər
- **Capsules** = kapsul
- **Liquid** = maye
- **Injection** / **Shot** = iynə
- **Ointment** / **Cream** = krem
- **Drops** = damla
- **Inhaler** = inhaler
- **Bandage** = sarğı

### Prescription vocab

- **Prescription** = reseptim
- **Over-the-counter (OTC)** = reseptsiz
- **Pharmacy** = aptek
- **Dosage** = doz
- **Side effects** = yan təsirlər

### Taking medicine

- "Take **twice a day**."
- "**With food**."
- "**On an empty stomach**."
- "For **7 days**."

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ I have illness.
- ✓ I'm **ill**. / I have **a cold** / **the flu**.

- ✗ I have pain in head.
- ✓ I have **a headache**.

- ✗ Doctor gave me treat.
- ✓ Doctor prescribed me **treatment**.
