# Confusing Words (Qarışdırılan Sözlər)

---

## affect vs effect

| Word | Type | Meaning | Example |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| **affect** | verb | təsir etmək | Stress **affects** your health. |
| **effect** | noun | nəticə, təsir | Stress has a negative **effect** on health. |

> Tip: **A**ffect = **A**ction (fel). **E**ffect = End result (isim).

## advice vs advise

| Word | Type | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| **advice** | noun | Can you give me some **advice**? |
| **advise** | verb | I **advise** you to rest. |

## accept vs except

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **accept** | qəbul etmək | I **accept** your offer. |
| **except** | ...istisna olmaqla | Everyone came **except** John. |

## lie vs lay

| Word | Meaning | Past | Example |
|------|---------|------|---------|
| **lie** (intransitive) | uzanmaq | lay | I need to **lie** down. / She **lay** on the sofa. |
| **lay** (transitive) | qoymaq | laid | Please **lay** the book on the table. |

## rise vs raise

| Word | Type | Meaning | Example |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| **rise** | intransitive | qalxmaq (öz-özünə) | The sun **rises** in the east. |
| **raise** | transitive | qaldırmaq (bir şeyi) | Please **raise** your hand. |

## lose vs loose

| Word | Type | Meaning | Example |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| **lose** | verb | itirmək, uduzamq | Don't **lose** your keys! |
| **loose** | adjective | boş, geniş | These trousers are too **loose**. |

## their / there / they're

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **their** | onların (mənsubiyyət) | **Their** house is big. |
| **there** | orada / var | She is **there**. / **There** is a problem. |
| **they're** | they are | **They're** coming soon. |

---

## its vs it's

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **its** | onun (mənsubiyyət) | The dog wagged **its** tail. |
| **it's** | it is / it has | **It's** raining. / **It's** been a long day. |

---

## your vs you're

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **your** | sənin (mənsubiyyət) | Is this **your** bag? |
| **you're** | you are | **You're** very kind. |

## than vs then

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **than** | müqayisə | She is taller **than** him. |
| **then** | sonra / o vaxt | We ate, **then** we left. |

---

## who vs whom

| Word | Usage | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| **who** | subyekt (o, onlar) | **Who** called you? |
| **whom** | obyekt (onu, ona) | **Whom** did you call? |

> Tip: Cavab "he/she" ise → who. Cavab "him/her" ise → whom.
