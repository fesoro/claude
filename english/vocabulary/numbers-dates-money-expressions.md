## 3. Tarixi Deyişmə (Saying Dates)

### Yazı formaları

- 15/04/2026 (BrE) → British: **day/month/year**
- 04/15/2026 (AmE) → American: **month/day/year**

### Söyləmə forması

**British (BrE):**
- 15 April 2026 → "the fifteenth of April, two thousand twenty-six"
- 1 May 2026 → "the first of May"

**American (AmE):**
- April 15, 2026 → "April fifteenth, twenty twenty-six"
- May 1, 2026 → "May first"

### İlləri Oxumaq

| İl | Oxunuşu |
|-----|---------|
| 1990 | nineteen ninety |
| 1999 | nineteen ninety-nine |
| 2000 | two thousand / the year two thousand |
| 2001 | two thousand (and) one |
| 2010 | twenty ten / two thousand (and) ten |
| 2020 | twenty twenty |
| 2026 | twenty twenty-six |

> **Qayda:** 2000-2009 → "two thousand + n". 2010+ → "twenty + n" üstünlük.

### Sual / Cavab

- **Q:** What's the date today?
- **A:** It's April 18th. / It's the 18th of April.

- **Q:** When were you born?
- **A:** I was born on March 5th, 1995.

### Prepositions ilə

- **on** 15 April
- **in** April
- **in** 2026
- **on** Monday, 15 April 2026
- **at** Christmas (bayram dövrü)
- **on** Christmas Day (gün)

## 5. Yaş İfadələri

### Yaşı soruşmaq

- How old are you?
- What's your age?

### Cavab

- I am 25 (years old).
- I am 25 **years old**.
- She is in her twenties / thirties / forties.
- He is **in his early 30s**. (30-un əvvəli)
- She is **in her mid-40s**. (40-ların ortası)
- He is **in his late 50s**. (50-lərin sonu)

### Doğum / yaşlanma

- I was born **in 1995**.
- I was born **on** March 5th, 1995.
- **at** the age of 25 (25 yaşında)

## 6. Pul İfadələri

### Valyutalar

| Ölkə | Valyuta | Simvol |

| UK | pound sterling | £ |
| USA | dollar | $ |
| EU | euro | € |
| Azerbaijan | manat | ₼ / AZN |
| Russia | ruble | ₽ / RUB |
| Japan | yen | ¥ |

### Qiymətləri Oxumaq

- $5 → "five dollars"
- $5.50 → "five (dollars) (and) fifty (cents)" / "five fifty"
- £10.25 → "ten pounds twenty-five" / "ten pounds (and) twenty-five (pence)"
- 500 ₼ → "five hundred manat(s)"
- €1,999.99 → "one thousand nine hundred ninety-nine euros, ninety-nine cents" / "nineteen ninety-nine, ninety-nine"

### Qiymət sualları

- How much is it?
- What's the price?
- How much does it cost?

### Qiymət cavabları

- It's **10 dollars**.
- It costs **about $50**.
- It's **around €100**.
- It's **approximately** $1,000.
- It's **under / less than** $20.
- It's **over / more than** $500.

## 9. Telefon Nömrələri və Kodlar

### Oxunuş

- 555-1234 → "five five five, one two three four"

## 10. İş / Müsahibə Kontekstində

### Müsahibədə saylar

- "I have **6 years** of experience."
- "I led a team of **12 engineers**."
- "We reduced costs by **25%**."
- "I was born in **1995**." → "in nineteen ninety-five"
- "I joined this company in **2020**." → "in twenty twenty"
- "Our user base grew to **2 million**."

### Maaş müzakirəsi

- "My expected salary is **$80,000** per year."
- "I'm looking for **around €50K**." ("fifty K")
- "The range is between **$60K and $75K**."

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Söz formasında yazın

1. 1,245 → ...
2. 3:45 → ...
3. 5/6 → ...
4. 1999 → ...
5. 21st April 2026 → ...

**Cavablar:**
1. one thousand, two hundred (and) forty-five
2. three forty-five / a quarter to four
3. five-sixths
4. nineteen ninety-nine
5. the twenty-first of April, twenty twenty-six (BrE)

### 2. Boşluqları doldurun

1. I was born ___ 1995 ___ March.
2. The book costs ___ 20 dollars.
3. I'm ___ my late 30s.
4. We meet ___ 3 PM ___ Fridays.

**Cavablar:** 1-in, in, 2-about/around, 3-in, 4-at, on

### 3. Tərcümə edin

1. 2026-cı ildə 31 yaşım olacaq. → In 2026, I'll be 31 years old.
2. Kitab təxminən 10 dollara başa gəlir. → The book costs about $10.
3. Həftədə üç dəfə idmana gedirəm. → I go to the gym **three times a week**.
4. Mən 1995-ci il mart ayının 5-də doğulmuşam. → I was born **on March 5th, 1995** / **on the 5th of March, 1995**.
