# Body Idioms (Bədən üzərə qurulmuş idiomlar)

---

## Head & Brain (Baş & Beyin)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| use your head | ağlını işlət | Just use your head — it's obvious! |
| off the top of my head | dərhal, hazırlıqsız | Off the top of my head, I'd say about 50. |
| keep your head | sakit qalmaq | She kept her head during the crisis. |
| lose your head | özünü itirmək | Don't lose your head — stay calm. |
| get your head around sth | anlamaq, qavramaq | I can't get my head around this maths. |
| bite someone's head off | kobud cavab vermək | She bit my head off when I asked. |
| head over heels | dərindən aşiq olmaq | He's head over heels in love with her. |
| put your heads together | birlikdə düşünmək | Let's put our heads together and find a solution. |

---

## Eyes (Gözlər)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| keep an eye on | nəzarətdə saxlamaq | Can you keep an eye on my bag? |
| see eye to eye | razılaşmaq | They don't see eye to eye on politics. |
| turn a blind eye | görmürsən göstərmək | The manager turned a blind eye to the problem. |
| catch someone's eye | diqqətini cəlb etmək | That painting really caught my eye. |
| in the blink of an eye | çox sürətlə | It all happened in the blink of an eye. |
| have eyes in the back of your head | hər şeydən xəbərdar olmaq | Teachers need eyes in the back of their head. |
| eye-opening | gözü açan, təəccüblü | The documentary was truly eye-opening. |

## Ears & Mouth (Qulaqlar & Ağız)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| lend an ear | diqqətlə dinləmək | Can you lend me an ear for a minute? |
| turn a deaf ear | eşitməzliyə vurmaq | He turned a deaf ear to their complaints. |
| be all ears | həvəslə dinləmək | Tell me what happened — I'm all ears! |
| bite your tongue | susmaq (söyləmək istəsən də) | I had to bite my tongue during that meeting. |
| let something slip | yanlışlıqla söyləmək | She let slip that she was pregnant. |
| keep your mouth shut | ağzını bağlamaq | Just keep your mouth shut about this. |
| by word of mouth | şifahi yolla, söz ilə | The news spread by word of mouth. |

## Hands & Arms (Əllər & Qollar)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| lend a hand | kömək etmək | Could you lend a hand with this? |
| have your hands full | çox məşğul olmaq | She has her hands full with three kids. |
| give someone a hand | alqışlamaq / kömək etmək | Let's give her a hand! / Can you give me a hand? |
| in good hands | etibarlı əllərdə | Don't worry — you're in good hands. |
| cost an arm and a leg | çox baha başa gəlmək | That holiday cost an arm and a leg. |
| twist someone's arm | zorla razı salmaq | You don't need to twist my arm — I'd love to come. |
| keep at arm's length | məsafə saxlamaq | He keeps his colleagues at arm's length. |
| hands down | şübhəsiz, asanlıqla | She's hands down the best student in the class. |

## Legs & Feet (Ayaqlar)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| break a leg | uğurlar (aktyorlar üçün) | Break a leg tonight! |
| get cold feet | son anda qorxmaq, geri çəkilmək | He got cold feet before the wedding. |
| put your foot in it | yersiz bir şey söyləmək | I really put my foot in it when I asked about her ex. |
| stand on your own two feet | müstəqil olmaq | It's time to stand on your own two feet. |
| drag your feet | ləng hərəkət etmək, tənbəllik etmək | Stop dragging your feet — we need a decision! |
| have a leg to stand on | arqumentinin əsası olmaq | Without evidence, you don't have a leg to stand on. |
| on its last legs | ölmək üzrə, bitmək üzrə | That old laptop is on its last legs. |
| put your best foot forward | ən yaxşı cəhdi göstərmək | Put your best foot forward in the interview. |

---

## Heart & Stomach (Ürək & Mədə)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| have a heart of gold | çox xeyirxah olmaq | She has a heart of gold — always helping. |
| know by heart | əzbər bilmək | She knows the poem by heart. |
| take something to heart | çox ciddi qəbul etmək | Don't take criticism too much to heart. |
| have the stomach for sth | cəsarəti / iradəsi olmaq | I don't have the stomach for horror films. |
| a gut feeling | içdən gələn hiss | I had a gut feeling something was wrong. |
| butterflies in your stomach | həyəcanlı olmaq | She had butterflies in her stomach before the exam. |
