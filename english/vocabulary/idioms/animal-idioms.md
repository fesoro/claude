## Niyə Öyrənməliyik?

Native speakers heyvan idiomlarını çox istifadə edir. İş yerində, gündəlik söhbətdə.

## 1. Cat Idioms

### Let the cat out of the bag

Sirr aç.
- Don't **let the cat out of the bag** about the surprise.

### Curiosity killed the cat

Çox sual verməmək məsləhət.
- Watch out — **curiosity killed the cat**.

### Cat got your tongue?

Niyə danışmırsan?
- What, **cat got your tongue**?

### Play cat and mouse

Taşlı oyun.
- They've been **playing cat and mouse** for weeks.

### When the cat's away, the mice will play

Boss yoxsa, işçi iş etmir.

### Cool cat

Sakit, serin adam.
- He's a **cool cat**.

### Catnap

Qısa yatmaq.
- I took a quick **catnap**.

---

## 2. Dog Idioms

### Dog-eat-dog world

Acı rəqabət.
- Tech is a **dog-eat-dog world**.

### Let sleeping dogs lie

Köhnə problemi gündəmə gətirmə.
- **Let sleeping dogs lie** on this issue.

### Every dog has its day

Hər kəsin növbəsi gələcək.
- **Every dog has its day** — your time will come.

### Barking up the wrong tree

Səhv yerə baxmaq.
- You're **barking up the wrong tree** with that approach.

### Dog-tired

Çox yorğun.
- I'm **dog-tired** after the release.

### In the doghouse

Problem.
- After the outage, the team was **in the doghouse**.

### A dog's life

Çətin həyat.
- Freelancing can be **a dog's life**.

### Raining cats and dogs

Çox yağış.
- It's **raining cats and dogs**!

→ [weather-idioms.md](weather-idioms.md)

### Top dog

Ən yaxşı / güclü.
- She's the **top dog** in the engineering team.

### Teach an old dog new tricks

Köhnəni öyrət (hard).
- You **can't teach an old dog new tricks** (sometimes true).

## 3. Bird Idioms

### Kill two birds with one stone

Bir zərbə ilə iki quş.
- This fix **kills two birds with one stone**.

### The early bird catches the worm

Erkən qalxan qazanır.
- **The early bird catches the worm** — ship fast!

### A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush

Əlindəki dəyərlidir.

### Birds of a feather flock together

Oxşar adamlar bir yerdə.
- **Birds of a feather flock together** — founders attract founders.

### Night owl

Gecə adamı.
- I'm a **night owl** — can't stand morning meetings.

### Eagle eye

İti görüş.
- The reviewer has an **eagle eye** for bugs.

### Chicken out

Qorxdum, etmədim.
- I was going to speak up, but I **chickened out**.

### Like a chicken with its head cut off

Qarışıq halda qaçmaq.
- Running around **like a chicken with its head cut off**.

### Free as a bird

Tam azad.
- **Free as a bird** after quitting my job.

## 4. Fish Idioms

### Big fish in a small pond

Kiçik yerdə böyük.
- He's a **big fish in a small pond**.

### Fish out of water

Yersiz.
- I felt like a **fish out of water** at the tech conference.

### Plenty of fish in the sea

Başqa variantlar var.

### Smells fishy

Şübhəli.
- Something **smells fishy** about this deal.

### Like shooting fish in a barrel

Çox asan.
- Hiring interns is like **shooting fish in a barrel**.

### Fish for compliments

Tərif dilən.
- She's **fishing for compliments**.

### Hook, line, and sinker

Tam inandı.
- He bought it **hook, line, and sinker**.

## 5. Horse Idioms

### Dark horse

Gözlənilməz.
- That startup is a **dark horse**.

### Hold your horses

Səbir!
- **Hold your horses** — let me check.

### Straight from the horse's mouth

Birbaşa mənbədən.
- **Straight from the horse's mouth**.

### A horse of a different color

Tam başqa məsələ.
- That's **a horse of a different color**.

### Get on your high horse

Üstün davranış.
- Don't **get on your high horse**.

### Put the cart before the horse

Səhv sıra.
- Shipping before testing is **putting the cart before the horse**.

### Work like a horse

Çox çalış.
- She **works like a horse**.

### Beat a dead horse

Faydasız təkrar.
- Stop **beating a dead horse** — the decision is made.

Xəyanətkar.
- Watch out — he's a **snake in the grass**.

### Crocodile tears

Saxta ağlama.
- She's shedding **crocodile tears**.

### Sly as a fox

Hiyləgər.
- The CEO is **sly as a fox**.

### Like a bull in a china shop

Kobud / diqqətsiz.
- He's **like a bull in a china shop**.

Hamı görür, heç kim danışmır.
- Let's address the **elephant in the room** — layoffs.

### Monkey business

Şübhəli iş.
- No **monkey business** in code review.

### Monkey see, monkey do

Təqlid.
- New devs — **monkey see, monkey do**.

### Get your monkey off my back

Üzərimdəki yük götür.

### Elephant memory

Güclü yaddaş.
- He has an **elephant memory**.

## 7. Bear / Lion Idioms

### Bear

#### Bear with me

Səbir et.
- **Bear with me** while I pull up the logs.

#### Bear the brunt

Zərbə al.
- The ops team **bore the brunt** of the outage.

#### Like a bear with a sore head

Əsəbli.
- He's **like a bear with a sore head**.

### Lion

#### Lion's share

Ən böyük pay.
- The founders took the **lion's share**.

#### Beard the lion in his den

Təhlükəli yerdə üzləş.

#### Throw to the lions

Təhlükəyə at.
- They **threw him to the lions** in the interview.

#### Heart of a lion

Cəsur.
- She has the **heart of a lion**.

Gəlir mənbəyi.

### Bull in a china shop

Kobud.

Problemi bilərəkdən həll et.

## Interview Usage

### ✓ Good use

- "I'd like to **address the elephant in the room**..." ✓
- "I often act as a **lone wolf**, but I love collaboration too." ✓
- "I have an **eagle eye** for bugs." ✓

### ✗ Avoid

- Too many in one interview
- Clichés without substance
