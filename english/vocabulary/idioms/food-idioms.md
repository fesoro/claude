# Food Idioms — Qida İdiomları

## Səviyyə
B1-B2

---

## Niyə Öyrənməliyik?

Native speakers qida idiomlarını çox işlədir. Gündəlik söhbətlərdə, iş yerində, hətta tech meeting-lərdə.

---

## 1. Apple İdiomları

### The apple of my eye

Gözümün qorası (ən sevdiyi).
- She's the **apple of my eye**.

### Apples to apples / apples to oranges

Oxşar / fərqli müqayisə.
- You can't compare **apples to oranges**.

### A bad apple

Pis adam qrupda.
- One **bad apple** ruins the team.

### An apple a day keeps the doctor away

Proverb — meyvə sağlamdır.

---

## 2. Piece of Cake / Cake

### Piece of cake

Çox asandır.
- That test was a **piece of cake**.

### Have your cake and eat it too

Həm özünün olsun, həm ye.
- You can't **have your cake and eat it too**.

### Take the cake

Ən yaxşısı (yaxud ən pisi).
- That **takes the cake**! (heyrətlidir)

### Icing on the cake

Əlavə bonus.
- Got a bonus — **icing on the cake**.

---

## 3. Cool as a Cucumber

Soyuq / sakit.
- She stayed **cool as a cucumber** during the interview.

---

## 4. Bread İdiomları

### Bread and butter

Əsas məhsul / gəlir mənbəyi.
- Enterprise sales are our **bread and butter**.

### Breadwinner

Ailəni dolandıran.
- He's the **breadwinner** of the family.

### The best thing since sliced bread

Möhtəşəm ixtira.
- This app is the **best thing since sliced bread**.

### Breadcrumbs

İz / yol (navigasiya).
- Follow the **breadcrumbs** in the URL.

### Tech: "breadcrumb navigation" = website struktur göstərici

---

## 5. Nut İdiomları

### In a nutshell

Qısaca.
- **In a nutshell**, we need more tests.

### Tough nut to crack

Çətin həll.
- That bug is a **tough nut to crack**.

### Go nuts

Dəli olmaq (pozitiv / neqativ).
- The crowd went **nuts**.
- Don't **go nuts** over it.

### Nuts and bolts

Əsas detallar.
- Let's discuss the **nuts and bolts** of the API.

---

## 6. Egg İdiomları

### Walking on eggshells

Çox diqqətli davranmaq.
- I was **walking on eggshells** around the CEO.

### Put all your eggs in one basket

Bütün umudu bir yerə qoy (risk).
- Don't **put all your eggs in one basket** — diversify.

### Egg on your face

Utancaq vəziyyət.
- I had **egg on my face** when the demo failed.

### Chicken and egg problem

Hansı birinci? Fikir dövriyyəsi.
- It's a **chicken and egg problem** — need users for features, features for users.

---

## 7. Tea / Coffee İdiomları

### Not my cup of tea

Mənim zövqümə deyil.
- Java isn't really **my cup of tea**.

### Spill the tea

Qeybət danış.
- **Spill the tea**! (slang)

### Wake up and smell the coffee

Həqiqətlə üzləş.
- **Wake up and smell the coffee** — the market is shifting.

---

## 8. Salt / Pepper

### Worth your salt

Layiqli.
- Any engineer **worth their salt** knows SOLID.

### Take with a grain of salt

Şübhə ilə qarşıla.
- Take online reviews **with a grain of salt**.

### Rub salt in the wound

Ağrıya əlavə acı.
- Telling me I failed — **rubbing salt in the wound**.

### Pepper someone with questions

Suallarla doldur.
- He **peppered me with questions** in the interview.

---

## 9. Pie İdiomları

### Piece of the pie

Pay almaq.
- Everyone wants a **piece of the pie**.

### Easy as pie

Çox asan.
- The migration was **easy as pie**.

### Pie in the sky

Gerçəkləşməyəcək xəyal.
- Full autonomy is **pie in the sky**.

### Humble pie

Təvazökar olma.
- Had to eat **humble pie** after my bug took down prod.

---

## 10. Meat / Beef

### Meat and potatoes

Əsas / vacib hissə.
- Let's get to the **meat and potatoes** of the issue.

### Have a beef with

Narazı olmaq.
- I have a **beef with** this code.

---

## 11. Sugar / Sweet

### Sugar-coat

Yumşaltmaq.
- Don't **sugar-coat** bad feedback.

### The icing on the cake

Əlavə bonus.

### Sweeten the deal

Təklifi yaxşılaşdır.
- They **sweetened the deal** with a sign-on bonus.

---

## 12. Fish / Meat

### A big fish in a small pond

Kiçik yerdə böyük.
- He's a **big fish in a small pond**.

### Fishy

Şübhəli.
- Something **fishy** about this log.

### Fish out of water

Yersiz hiss.
- I felt like a **fish out of water** at the startup event.

### Have bigger fish to fry

Daha vacib işlərin var.
- Stop debating this — we have **bigger fish to fry**.

---

## 13. Spice / Hot

### Spice things up

Maraq əlavə et.
- Let's **spice things up** with a demo.

### Too hot to handle

Çox çətin / risk.
- This project is **too hot to handle**.

### In hot water

Çətinlikdə.
- After the outage, I'm **in hot water**.

---

## 14. Milk / Cream

### Cream of the crop

Ən yaxşısı.
- She's the **cream of the crop**.

### Spilled milk

Keçmişə ağlamaq (faydasızdır).
- Don't cry over **spilled milk** — move on.

### Milk it

Çox faydalanmaq.
- He's **milking** his fame.

---

## 15. Cook / Bake

### Too many cooks spoil the broth

Çox aşpaz xörəyi korlayır (çox adam iş pis edər).
- This project has **too many cooks**.

### Cooking with gas

İşlər yaxşı gedir.
- Now we're **cooking with gas**!

### Half-baked

Yarımçıq.
- A **half-baked** solution.

### Bake in

Sistemə qur.
- We need to **bake in** security from day one.

---

## 16. Other Common Food Idioms

### Butter someone up

Yaltaqlan.
- He's **buttering up** the boss.

### Butter fingers

Əllərindən yıxanda olan.
- **Butter fingers** — dropped the phone!

### Couch potato

Evdə oturan.
- Don't be a **couch potato**.

### A tough cookie

Güclü adam.
- She's a **tough cookie**.

### Smart cookie

Ağıllı adam.
- He's a **smart cookie**.

### Full of beans

Enerjili.
- Kids are always **full of beans**.

### Spill the beans

Sirr açmaq.
- Don't **spill the beans** about the surprise.

### A lemon

Pis məhsul.
- This car is a **lemon**.

### Lemons / Lemonade

Problem → həll.
- When life gives you **lemons**, make **lemonade**.

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ Translate literally: "almam gözüm"
- ✓ "The **apple of my eye**." (idiomatic)

- ✗ Idiom olmadan işlət:
- ✓ "This is **a piece of cake**" (natural)
  vs "This is very easy" (literal — OK amma flat)
