## Niyə Vacibdir?

Sports idioms biznesdə, tech-də, gündəlik söhbətdə çox işlənir. Amerikanlar xüsusilə baseball / football idiomlarını daim istifadə edir.

## 1. Baseball Idioms (AmE!)

### Ballpark figure

Təxmini rəqəm.
- What's the **ballpark figure**?
- In the **ballpark** of $50k.

### Hit a home run

Böyük uğur.
- That product launch was a **home run**!

### Strike out

Uğursuz.
- My proposal **struck out**.

### Curveball

Gözlənilməz çətinlik.
- They threw me a **curveball** in the interview.

### Touch base

Əlaqə saxla.
- Let me **touch base** with you next week.

### Out of left field

Gözlənilməz.
- That question came **out of left field**.

### Right off the bat

Dərhal.
- **Right off the bat**, I noticed the issue.

### Step up to the plate

Cavab ver.
- Time to **step up to the plate** and lead.

### Cover all the bases

Hər detalı nəzərdən keç.
- We need to **cover all the bases**.

### In the same league

Eyni səviyyədə.
- Our product is **in the same league** as Stripe.

### Out of your league

Səviyyəndən üstün.
- That role is **out of my league**.

### Rain check

Təxir.
- Can I take a **rain check** on dinner?

## 2. Football Idioms

### American football

#### Monday morning quarterback

Sonradan ağıllı.
- Don't be a **Monday morning quarterback**.

#### Huddle

Qısa toplantı.
- Let's **huddle** quickly.

#### Game plan

Strategiya.
- What's our **game plan**?

#### Fumble

Səhv et.
- I **fumbled** the presentation.

#### Drop the ball

Səhv et, unut.
- I **dropped the ball** on that task.

#### Take one for the team

Tim üçün dəyəri ver.
- I'll **take one for the team** and work weekend.

#### Hail Mary

Son ümid.
- That was a **Hail Mary** attempt.

## 3. Boxing Idioms

### Down to the wire

Son ana kimi.
- The deal went **down to the wire**.

### Throw in the towel

Təslim ol.
- We had to **throw in the towel** on that feature.

### Roll with the punches

Çətinliyə dözmək.
- Good managers **roll with the punches**.

### Hit below the belt

Haqsız şəkildə vur.
- That comment was **below the belt**.

### On the ropes

Çətin vəziyyət.
- The company is **on the ropes**.

### Knockout

Böyük uğur / zərbə.
- That release was a **knockout**.

### Saved by the bell

Son anda xilas.
- **Saved by the bell**!

## 4. Horse Racing

### Neck and neck

Bərabər yarış.
- Our teams were **neck and neck**.

### Down to the wire

Son ana kimi (eyni boxing ilə).
- Vote was **down to the wire**.

### Dark horse

Gözlənilməz qalib.
- That startup is a **dark horse**.

### Get on your high horse

Özünü üstün tut.
- Don't **get on your high horse**.

### Hold your horses

Səbir et!
- **Hold your horses** — let me think.

### Straight from the horse's mouth

Birinci əldən mənbə.
- I heard it **straight from the horse's mouth**.

### Back the wrong horse

Yanlış seç.
- We **backed the wrong horse** with that vendor.

### By a nose

Çox az fərqlə.
- We won **by a nose**.

## 5. Chess / Card Games

### Chess

#### Checkmate

Son qələbə.
- **Checkmate**! They can't respond.

#### Pawn

Azacıq mövqe.
- Don't treat junior devs like **pawns**.

#### Gambit

Risk.
- It was a **gambit**, but it paid off.

#### Endgame

Son strategiya.
- What's your **endgame**?

### Card games

#### Ace up your sleeve

Gizli avantaj.
- I have an **ace up my sleeve**.

#### Poker face

Sir üzü.
- Keep a **poker face**.

#### Wild card

Gözlənilməz faktor.
- He's the **wild card**.

#### Play your cards right

Düzgün hərəkət et.
- If you **play your cards right**, you'll get promoted.

#### Hold all the aces

Bütün güc səndə.
- They **hold all the aces** in this negotiation.

#### Lay your cards on the table

Hər şeyi aç.
- Let me **lay my cards on the table**.

#### Close to the vest

Sirli.
- She plays it **close to the vest**.

#### Stack the deck

Haqsız üstünlük.
- The system **stacks the deck** against small teams.

## 9. General Sports Idioms

### Level playing field

Bərabər şərtlər.
- We need a **level playing field**.

### Play ball

İşbirliyi et.
- Is the client going to **play ball**?

### Team player

Tim üçün işləyən.
- We're looking for **team players**.

### MVP (Most Valuable Player)

Ən dəyərli.
- She's our team **MVP** this quarter.

### Warm up

İsinmə.
- **Warm up** to the idea.

### Cool down

Sərinləş.
- I need to **cool down** after that stressful meeting.

### Crunch time

Son dəqiqələr.
- It's **crunch time** before launch.

### Game changer

Oyun dəyişən.
- This tool is a **game changer**.

### On the bench

Oyundan kənar.
- He's been **on the bench** since the reorg.

### Benchmark

Standart.
- We set the **benchmark** for quality.
