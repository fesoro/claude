# Money Idioms — Pul İdiomları

## Səviyyə
B1-B2

---

## Niyə Öyrənməliyik?

Pul idiomları biznes söhbətlərində, negotiation-larda, interview-da lazım olur. Salary discussion-da xüsusilə!

---

## 1. Cost / Expense (Xərc)

### Cost an arm and a leg

Çox bahalı.
- That new phone **costs an arm and a leg**.

### Break the bank

Büdcəni aşmaq.
- We need to fix this without **breaking the bank**.

### Pay through the nose

Çox pul vermək.
- We **paid through the nose** for that software license.

### A fortune

Çox pul.
- The new office cost **a fortune**.

### Bleed money

Pul itirmək.
- The project is **bleeding money**.

### Cost a pretty penny

Çox bahalı.
- That car cost **a pretty penny**.

---

## 2. Saving / Earning

### Save up

Pul yığmaq.
- I'm **saving up** for a new laptop.

### Put aside

Kənara qoymaq.
- **Put aside** some money each month.

### Tighten your belt

Qənaət etmək.
- Times are tough — need to **tighten our belt**.

### Make ends meet

Dolanmaq.
- Hard to **make ends meet** on that salary.

### Live within your means

Qazandığın qədər xərclə.
- **Live within your means** to avoid debt.

### Nest egg

Yığılmış pul.
- Building a retirement **nest egg**.

---

## 3. Rich / Poor

### Be loaded

Pullu olmaq.
- The CEO is **loaded**.

### Rolling in it

Çox pullu.
- He's **rolling in it** since he sold his startup.

### Broke

Pulsuz.
- I'm **broke** until payday.

### Dirt poor

Çox pulsuz.
- Started **dirt poor**, now a millionaire.

### Flat broke

Tamamilə pulsuz.
- I'm **flat broke** this month.

### Money to burn

Çox əlavə pul.
- Startups with VC have **money to burn**.

---

## 4. Deal / Bargain

### Bang for your buck

Pul qarşılığı dəyər.
- Open source gives you **bang for your buck**.

### Bargain

Qazanılan alış.
- This laptop was **a bargain**.

### A steal

Çox ucuza alış.
- That was **a steal** at $500.

### Rip-off

Aldatma qiyməti.
- That restaurant was **a rip-off**.

### Pay top dollar

Ən yüksək qiyməti vermək.
- We **pay top dollar** for senior engineers.

### Value for money

Pulunun qarşılığı.
- Good **value for money**.

---

## 5. Money Management

### Money pit

Pul udan şey.
- That old house is **a money pit**.

### Throw money at

Pul ataraq problem həll etmək.
- You can't just **throw money at** the problem.

### Money doesn't grow on trees

Pul ağaçda bitmir.
- **Money doesn't grow on trees** — spend carefully.

### Easy money

Asan qazanılan pul.
- Affiliate marketing is not **easy money**.

### Hard-earned

Çətin qazanılmış.
- Spending my **hard-earned** money wisely.

### A penny saved is a penny earned

Qənaət qazancdır.

---

## 6. Debt / Loans

### In the red

Zərərdə.
- The company is **in the red** this quarter.

### In the black

Mənfəətdə.
- Finally **in the black** after 2 years.

### Up to your ears in debt

Çox borc.
- He's **up to his ears in debt**.

### Pay off

Borcu bitirmək.
- Finally **paid off** my student loans.

### Foot the bill

Hesabı ödəmək.
- Who'll **foot the bill** for this?

---

## 7. Career / Salary

### Make a killing

Çox pul qazanmaq.
- He **made a killing** on crypto.

### Bring home the bacon

Ailəyə pul qazanıb gətirmək.
- He **brings home the bacon**.

### Deep pockets

Pullu investor.
- We need investors with **deep pockets**.

### Shell out

Məcburi xərcləmək (çox).
- Had to **shell out** $2k for the conference.

### Pay raise / Pay cut

Maaş artımı / azalması.
- Got a 10% **pay raise**.
- Suffered a **pay cut** during layoffs.

### Bring in

Qazanmaq.
- She **brings in** $100k a year.

---

## 8. Business / Tech

### Money-maker

Qazanc gətirən məhsul.
- The enterprise version is our **money-maker**.

### Cash cow

Daimi gəlir mənbəyi.
- Apple's App Store is a **cash cow**.

### Burn rate

Pul xərcləmə sürəti (startup).
- High **burn rate** — need funding soon.

### Runway

Pul qalan müddət.
- 6 months of **runway** left.

### Bootstrap

Öz gücünə qurmaq.
- We **bootstrapped** the company.

### Break even

Mənfəət-zərər sıfır.
- We **broke even** last quarter.

---

## 9. Interview / Salary Negotiation

### Ballpark figure

Təxmini rəqəm.
- What's the **ballpark figure** for this role?

### In the ballpark

Təxmini yaxın.
- $80k is **in the ballpark**.

### Negotiate

Danış.
- Always **negotiate** your salary.

### Lowball

Aşağı təklif.
- They **lowballed** me at $50k.

### Counter-offer

Cavab təklif.
- I made a **counter-offer** of $75k.

### Sign-on bonus

Giriş bonusu.
- Got a $10k **sign-on bonus**.

### Total compensation

Ümumi paket.
- My **total comp** is $120k with stocks.

---

## 10. Spending Habits

### Tight-fisted / Stingy

Pinti.
- Don't be so **stingy** — split the bill.

### Big spender

Pul xərcləyən.
- He's a **big spender** on tech gadgets.

### Spend like water

Çox xərcləmək.
- Tech bros **spend money like water**.

### Frugal

Qənaətcil (pozitiv).
- She's **frugal** but not cheap.

### Live paycheck to paycheck

Maaşla dolanmaq.
- I'm tired of **living paycheck to paycheck**.

---

## 11. Investment / Risk

### Put your money where your mouth is

Sözü gəlsin əməli.
- If you believe in it, **put your money where your mouth is**.

### Money talks

Pul danışır.
- In negotiations, **money talks**.

### Bet on it

Ümid bağla.
- I'm **betting on** remote work.

### A safe bet

Risksiz investisiya.
- FAANG is a **safe bet** for experience.

### Play it safe

Ehtiyatlı davran.
- I'll **play it safe** with my savings.

---

## 12. Tips / Gratuity

### Leave a tip

Çay pulu qoy.
- Don't forget to **leave a tip**.

### Service charge

Xidmət haqqı (bill-ə əlavə).
- The restaurant adds a 10% **service charge**.

### Keep the change

Qalığı saxla.
- Here's $20, **keep the change**.

## İnterview Nümunələri

### Salary negotiation

- "What's the **ballpark figure** for this role?"
- "I'd like to **negotiate** the offer."
- "My expected **total compensation** is $X."
- "I'm looking for a **sign-on bonus** as well."

### Describing past work

- "We reduced costs — saved **an arm and a leg**."
- "The startup had a high **burn rate**."
- "We reached **break even** in Q3."
- "Our **cash cow** was the enterprise product."

## Azərbaycanlı Səhvləri

- ✗ "The product is very expensive" (sadə)
- ✓ "The product **costs an arm and a leg**." (idiom)

- ✗ "We don't have money"
- ✓ "We're **broke**." / "We're **tight on cash**."
