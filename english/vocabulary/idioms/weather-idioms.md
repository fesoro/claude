# Weather Idioms — Hava İdiomları

## Səviyyə
B1-B2

---

## Niyə Öyrənməliyik?

Weather idiomları:
- Small talk-da çox (havadan başlanır)
- İdeyalar göstərmək (stress → storm)
- Professional kontekstdə (financial / tech crisis)

---

## 1. Rain İdiomları

### Raining cats and dogs

Çox güclü yağış.
- It's **raining cats and dogs** outside!

### When it rains, it pours

Çətinliklər birdən gəlir.
- Server crashed, bug found, deadline missed — **when it rains, it pours**.

### Right as rain

Tamamilə yaxşı.
- After a week off, I feel **right as rain**.

### Save for a rainy day

Çətin gün üçün qənaət.
- **Save for a rainy day**.

### Take a rain check

Sonrakı vaxta təxir.
- I can't join dinner — can I **take a rain check**?

### Under the weather

Özünü pis hiss.
- I'm **under the weather** today — working from home.

### Weather the storm

Çətinliyə dözmək.
- The startup **weathered the storm** during COVID.

---

## 2. Storm / Thunder / Lightning

### A storm is brewing

Problem yaranır.
- With layoffs coming, **a storm is brewing**.

### Calm before the storm

Sakitlikdən əvvəl.
- It's quiet — **calm before the storm**.

### Stormy / turbulent

Qarışıq.
- **Stormy** relationship with the client.

### Take someone by storm

Tez uğur qazan.
- The app **took the market by storm**.

### Steal someone's thunder

Fikirin əldən almaq.
- Don't **steal my thunder** in the meeting.

### Lightning fast

Çox sürətli.
- The new server is **lightning fast**.

### Like lightning

Şimşək kimi.
- He works **like lightning**.

### Have a bolt of lightning

Ani fikir.
- I had a **bolt of lightning** moment.

---

## 3. Cloud İdiomları

### On cloud nine

Çox xoşbəxt.
- I'm **on cloud nine** after the promotion.

### Every cloud has a silver lining

Hər pis işdə yaxşı tərəf var.
- Lost the job, but learned a lot — **every cloud has a silver lining**.

### Cloud over

Qaralan, üzülən.
- His face **clouded over** at the news.

### Have your head in the clouds

Xəyallarda olmaq.
- Stop having your **head in the clouds** — focus!

### Under a cloud

Şübhədə.
- He's **under a cloud** after the accident.

---

## 4. Sun İdiomları

### A ray of sunshine

Xoş adam / xəbər.
- She's **a ray of sunshine** in the office.

### Make hay while the sun shines

Fürsətdən yararlan.
- **Make hay while the sun shines** — ship before competitors.

### Rise and shine

Oyan (səhər).
- **Rise and shine**!

### Under the sun

Mövcud olan hər şey.
- We tried every solution **under the sun**.

### Nothing new under the sun

Yenilik yoxdur.
- Honestly, **nothing new under the sun** in tech.

---

## 5. Wind İdiomları

### It's a breeze

Çox asandır.
- That task was **a breeze**.

### Get wind of

Xəbər tutmaq.
- She **got wind of** the layoffs.

### A second wind

Yeni enerji.
- I got my **second wind** after coffee.

### Gone with the wind

İtdi.
- All my progress — **gone with the wind**.

### Throw caution to the wind

Ehtiyatı unutmaq.
- He **threw caution to the wind** and joined the startup.

### Know which way the wind blows

Necə olduğunu bilmək.
- Good leaders **know which way the wind blows**.

### Wind down

Sakitləşmək.
- Let's **wind down** — long week.

---

## 6. Snow / Ice / Cold

### Snowed under

İşlə boğulmaq.
- I'm **snowed under** with PRs to review.

### Ice / Iced

Inkar etmək (slang) / bitir.
- The deal was **iced**.

### Break the ice

Söhbət aç.
- Let's **break the ice** at the standup.

### Skating on thin ice

Risk altında.
- You're **skating on thin ice** with that deadline.

### Cold feet

Qorxub çəkilmək.
- He got **cold feet** about the investment.

### Give someone the cold shoulder

İgnor etmək.
- She **gave me the cold shoulder** after the meeting.

### The tip of the iceberg

Görünən kiçik hissə.
- This bug is just **the tip of the iceberg**.

---

## 7. Fog / Mist

### In a fog

Qarışıq düşüncə.
- I'm **in a fog** after this long meeting.

### Lift the fog

Aydınlaşdırmaq.
- Explain it to **lift the fog**.

---

## 8. Hot / Heat

### Hot under the collar

Əsəbləşən.
- Getting **hot under the collar** about the bug.

### Feel the heat

Təzyiqi hiss et.
- Team is **feeling the heat** before release.

### Heat of the moment

Qızğın anda.
- I spoke in **the heat of the moment**.

### If you can't stand the heat, get out of the kitchen

Dözmürsənsə, çıx.

---

## 9. Freezing / Frozen

### Freeze up

Donmaq (stress).
- I **froze up** during the interview.

### In cold blood

Soyuq qanla.
- He planned it **in cold blood**.

### Leave out in the cold

Kənarda qoymaq.
- Don't **leave me out in the cold** on this decision.

---

## 10. Hail

### Hail from

Haradan olmaq.
- I **hail from** Baku.

### Hailed as

Təqdir edilmək.
- He was **hailed as** a hero.
