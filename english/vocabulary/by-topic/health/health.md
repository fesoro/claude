## Sağlıqlı Həyat (Healthy Lifestyle)

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| exercise regularly | I try to **exercise regularly**. |
| eat a balanced diet | It's important to **eat a balanced diet**. |
| get enough sleep | You should **get enough sleep**. |
| avoid stress | Try to **avoid stress** when possible. |
| stay hydrated | **Stay hydrated** by drinking water. |
| give up smoking | She decided to **give up smoking**. |
| cut down on sugar | He needs to **cut down on sugar**. |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Simptom → Xəstəlik
1. runny nose + cough + fever → ___
2. tooth hurts → ___
3. difficulty breathing, wheezing → ___

**Cavablar:** 1-flu/cold, 2-toothache, 3-asthma

### 2. Boşluqları doldurun
1. I need to ___ an appointment with the doctor. (make)
2. She ___ from migraines for years. (has suffered)
3. The doctor told him to ___ smoking immediately. (give up)
