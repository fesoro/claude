## B2-C1 Essay Lüğəti

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| a pressing issue | Inequality is **a pressing issue** in modern societies. |
| social cohesion | Multiculturalism can strengthen **social cohesion**. |
| cultural diversity | **Cultural diversity** enriches a nation. |
| generational divide | There is a growing **generational divide** in values. |
| social mobility | Education improves **social mobility**. |
| marginalised groups | **Marginalised groups** need stronger representation. |
| civic engagement | **Civic engagement** is vital for democracy. |
| vested interests | **Vested interests** often block reform. |
| grassroots movement | Change often starts as a **grassroots movement**. |

## Faydalı IELTS İfadələri

- In contemporary society, ...
- There is growing concern about...
- The relationship between X and Y is complex.
- Many argue that cultural identity is under threat due to...
- Proponents of globalisation claim that...
- Critics, however, contend that...
- Striking a balance between X and Y is crucial.

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Boşluqları doldurun
1. The government introduced a new ___ to tackle unemployment. (policy)
2. She campaigned for the ___ of minority languages. (preservation/protection)
3. There is a growing ___ between the rich and poor. (inequality/divide)
4. The village has a strong sense of ___. (community/identity)

### 2. Esse planı hazırlayın
**Mövzu:** "Globalisation destroys local cultures. To what extent do you agree?"

- Agree: ___
- Disagree: ___
- Your position: ___
