## 7. Collocations

| Verb | Noun | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| play | sport, football, tennis | I play tennis twice a week. |
| do | gymnastics, martial arts, athletics | She does gymnastics. |
| go | swimming, cycling, skiing | He goes running every morning. |
| win | a match, a trophy, a medal | They won the championship. |
| score | a goal, a point | He scored the winning goal. |
| break | a record | She broke the world record. |
| train | hard, daily | He trains six days a week. |

## 8. Sport Idioms (İdiomlara keçid)

| Idiom | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| on the ball | alert, aware | She's really on the ball today. |
| a level playing field | fair conditions | Everyone deserves a level playing field. |
| throw in the towel | give up | He threw in the towel after round 5. |
| move the goalposts | change the rules | They keep moving the goalposts. |
| hit below the belt | unfair attack | That comment was below the belt. |
| in full swing | at its peak activity | The season is in full swing. |
