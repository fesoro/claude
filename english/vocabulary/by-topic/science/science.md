## 7. Key Academic Phrases for Science Writing

| Expression | Usage |
|------------|-------|
| The results suggest that... | Nəticələr göstərir ki,... |
| Further research is needed to... | ...üçün əlavə tədqiqat lazımdır. |
| The data indicates a correlation between... | Məlumat ...arasında korrelyasiya göstərir. |
| This is consistent with previous findings. | Bu, əvvəlki nəticələrlə uyğundur. |
| The experiment was conducted under controlled conditions. | Eksperiment nəzarət altında aparıldı. |
| The sample size was insufficient to draw conclusions. | Nümunə həcmi nəticə çıxarmaq üçün kifayət deyildi. |

## 8. Collocations

| Verb | Noun |
|------|------|
| conduct | research, an experiment, a study |
| carry out | an experiment, a test, an investigation |
| make | a discovery, a breakthrough, an observation |
| reach | a conclusion, an agreement |
| publish | findings, results, a paper |
| develop | a theory, a vaccine, a prototype |
| test | a hypothesis, a theory, a drug |
