## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Kateqoriyaya aid edin
apple / chicken / milk / rice / coffee / carrot / salmon / cheese

- Fruits: ___
- Vegetables: ___
- Meat/Fish: ___
- Dairy: ___
- Grains: ___
- Drinks: ___

**Cavablar:** Fruits: apple / Vegetables: carrot / Meat: chicken, salmon / Dairy: milk, cheese / Grains: rice / Drinks: coffee

### 2. Boşluqları doldurun
1. I usually ___ breakfast at 7am. (have)
2. The steak was perfectly ___ on the grill. (grilled)
3. She is ___ to shellfish, so she can't eat shrimp. (allergic)
