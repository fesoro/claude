## Kollokasiyalar

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| protect the environment | We must **protect the environment**. |
| reduce carbon emissions | Companies should **reduce carbon emissions**. |
| raise awareness | The campaign aims to **raise awareness**. |
| face extinction | Many species are **facing extinction**. |
| go extinct | The dodo **went extinct** in the 17th century. |
| damage the ecosystem | Pollution **damages the ecosystem**. |
| preserve natural habitats | It's vital to **preserve natural habitats**. |
| combat climate change | World leaders agreed to **combat climate change**. |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Kateqoriyaya aid edin
mountain / dolphin / oak / flood / solar energy / eagle

- Landscape: ___
- Animal: ___
- Plant: ___
- Weather/Disaster: ___
- Environment: ___

**Cavablar:** mountain-landscape, dolphin+eagle-animal, oak-plant, flood-weather, solar energy-environment

### 2. Boşluqları doldurun
1. Many species are ___ due to deforestation. (endangered)
2. We need to ___ carbon emissions to slow climate change. (reduce)
3. The Amazon is the world's largest ___. (rainforest)
