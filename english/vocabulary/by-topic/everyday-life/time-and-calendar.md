### Saat Necə Deyilir?

| Saat | İngilis ifadəsi |
|------|----------------|
| 3:00 | three o'clock |
| 3:15 | quarter past three |
| 3:30 | half past three |
| 3:45 | quarter to four |

**Sual:** What time is it? / What's the time?

## Tarix Necə Deyilir?

**BritanIya:** day + month → **the 15th of March** / **15 March**
**Amerika:** month + day → **March 15th** / **March 15**

**Yazılış:** 15/03/2024 (UK) → 03/15/2024 (US)

**Oxunuşu:**
- 1st — the first
- 2nd — the second
- 3rd — the third
- 4th — the fourth
- 21st — the twenty-first

## Tezlik Zərfləri

| İngilis | % | Azərbaycanca |
|---------|---|--------------|
| always | 100% | həmişə |
| usually / generally | ~80% | adətən |
| often | ~70% | tez-tez |
| sometimes | ~50% | bəzən |
| occasionally | ~30% | ara-sıra |
| rarely / seldom | ~10% | nadir hallarda |
| never | 0% | heç vaxt |

> Tezlik zərfləri **be** fellərinin sonrasında, əsas fellərin önündə gəlir.
> - She **is** always late.
> - He **usually** walks to work.

## Prepositions of Time

| Prepositions | İşlənmə |
|-------------|---------|
| **at** | at 3 o'clock, at night, at the weekend |
| **on** | on Monday, on Friday, on 15 March |
| **in** | in January, in 2024, in the morning, in spring |
| **by** | by tomorrow (sabaha kimi) |
| **until / till** | until midnight (gecə yarısına kimi) |
| **for** | for 2 hours (2 saat ərzində) |
| **since** | since 2020 (2020-dan bəri) |
| **during** | during the meeting |

## Məşq

**1. Saatları yazın:**
1. 7:00 → ___
2. 11:30 → ___
3. 4:45 → ___
4. 9:15 → ___

**Cavablar:** 1-seven o'clock, 2-half past eleven, 3-quarter to five, 4-quarter past nine

**2. Boşluqları doldurun (at/on/in):**
1. I wake up ___ 7 o'clock.
2. She was born ___ March.
3. We have a meeting ___ Monday.
4. I go on holiday ___ summer.
5. He left ___ 2020.

**Cavablar:** 1-at, 2-in, 3-on, 4-in, 5-in
