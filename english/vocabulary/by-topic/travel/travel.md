## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Boşluqları doldurun
1. We arrived at the airport early to ___ (check in) for our flight.
2. My suitcase was too heavy — I had to pay for ___ baggage. (excess)
3. The flight was ___ by two hours due to bad weather. (delayed)
4. She lost her ___ pass and couldn't board the plane. (boarding)

### 2. Kollokasiya — uyğun fiil seçin
1. ___ a visa (get/make/do)
2. ___ the city (explore/visit/go)
3. ___ your bags (pack/carry/fill)
4. ___ a tour (take/do/make)

**Cavablar:** 1-get, 2-explore/visit, 3-pack, 4-take
