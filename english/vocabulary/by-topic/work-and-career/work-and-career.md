## Müsahibə Sualları və Cavabları

| Sual | Faydalı cavab strukturu |
|------|------------------------|
| Tell me about yourself. | Past experience → current skills → why this role |
| What are your strengths? | Strength + example + result |
| Why do you want this job? | Company + role + your goals |
| Where do you see yourself in 5 years? | Growth + contribution |
| Why did you leave your last job? | Positive framing — growth opportunity |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Uyğun söz seçin
1. She works ___ hours — 9am to 1pm only. (full-time / part-time)
2. After 30 years, he finally ___ and stopped working. (resigned / retired)
3. The company had to make 50 workers ___. (redundant / retired)
4. She got a ___ and became head of department. (promotion / contract)

**Cavablar:** 1-part-time, 2-retired, 3-redundant, 4-promotion

### 2. Müsahibə cavabı hazırlayın
"What is your greatest strength?"
- Strength: ___
- Example: ___
- Result: ___
