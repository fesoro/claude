## Emosiya İfadəsi — Cümlə Strukturları

| Struktur | Nümunə |
|---------|--------|
| feel + adjective | I **feel nervous** about the interview. |
| be + adjective | She **is excited** about the trip. |
| feel like + -ing | I **feel like crying**. |
| can't help + -ing | I **can't help worrying** about it. |
| be in a good/bad mood | He's **in a terrible mood** today. |
| cheer up | Try to **cheer up** — things will get better. |
| get over | It took months to **get over** the loss. |
| break down | She **broke down** when she heard the news. |

## Preposition + Emotion

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| afraid **of** | She is **afraid of** the dark. |
| anxious **about** | He is **anxious about** his results. |
| proud **of** | I'm so **proud of** you. |
| angry **at/with** | She was **angry at** me. |
| excited **about** | They are **excited about** the holiday. |
| disappointed **in/with** | He was **disappointed in** himself. |
| jealous **of** | Are you **jealous of** her success? |
| grateful **for** | I'm so **grateful for** your help. |
| embarrassed **about** | She was **embarrassed about** the mistake. |
| bored **with/of** | I'm **bored with** this routine. |

## IELTS Speaking-də Emosiya İfadəsi

Sadə cavablardan çəkinin:

**Zəif:** "I was happy."
**Güclü:** "I was absolutely **thrilled** — it was one of the proudest moments of my life."

**Zəif:** "I was sad."
**Güclü:** "I felt completely **devastated** — it took me months to come to terms with it."

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Daha dəqiq söz seçin
1. "I was very happy when I got the job." → **elated / overjoyed / thrilled**
2. "She was sad when her dog died." → **heartbroken / devastated / grief-stricken**
3. "He was angry about the unfair decision." → **furious / outraged**

### 2. Preposition əlavə edin
1. She is terrified ___ spiders.
2. He was disappointed ___ himself.
3. I'm really grateful ___ your support.
4. They were excited ___ the concert.

**Cavablar:** 1-of, 2-in/with, 3-for, 4-about
