## Texnologiya Kollokasiyaları

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| access the internet | She **accessed the internet** via her phone. |
| connect to Wi-Fi | Can I **connect to** your **Wi-Fi**? |
| run a program | The computer **runs** several programs simultaneously. |
| browse the web | I spent hours **browsing the web**. |
| go viral | The video **went viral** overnight. |
| lose connection | I **lost connection** during the call. |
| charge the battery | Don't forget to **charge** your **battery**. |
| store data | We **store** all **data** in the cloud. |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Boşluqları doldurun
1. I need to ___ my phone — the battery is dying. (charge)
2. The video ___ viral after just one hour. (went)
3. Many jobs are at risk due to ___ (automation)
4. He forgot his ___ and couldn't log in. (password)

### 2. Formal alternativ yazın
1. "The app crashed." → The application **experienced a malfunction**.
2. "They hacked the company." → The company's systems **were compromised**.
