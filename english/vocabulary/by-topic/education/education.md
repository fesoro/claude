## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Boşluqları doldurun
1. She ___ her final exams and graduated with distinction. (passed)
2. I forgot to ___ my assignment before the deadline. (submit)
3. He received a full ___ to study at Oxford. (scholarship)
4. The university has a large ___ with a library and sports facilities. (campus)

### 2. Kollokasiya düzəldin
1. make homework → **do** homework
2. give an exam → **take/sit** an exam
3. win a degree → **get/earn** a degree
