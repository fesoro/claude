# Fel + İsim Kollokasiyaları (Verb + Noun Collocations)

## Səviyyə

## Nədir?

## Tez-tez Yanlış İşlənənlər

| Yanlış | Düzgün |
|--------|--------|
| do a mistake | **make** a mistake |
| make homework | **do** homework |
| take a decision ✓ | make a decision ✓ (hər ikisi mümkündür) |
| do a photo | **take** a photo |
| make a shower | **have** a shower |
| give an exam | **take** / **sit** an exam |
| do fun | **have** fun |
| make research | **do** research |
| take a speech | **give** a speech |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Düzgün feli seçin (make/do/have/take/give)
1. She ___ a shower every morning.
2. He ___ a mistake in the report.
3. Can you ___ me a hand with this?
4. They ___ a meeting every Monday.
5. I need to ___ research on this topic.
6. She ___ a photo of the sunset.
7. He ___ a presentation to the board.
8. Let's ___ a break.

**Cavablar:**
1. has, 2. made, 3. give, 4. have, 5. do, 6. took, 7. gave, 8. take
