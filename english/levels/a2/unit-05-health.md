## Qrammatika: Should / Shouldn't

**Məsləhət vermək üçün:**

| Müsbət | Mənfi |
|--------|-------|
| You **should** rest. | You **shouldn't** work. |
| He **should** see a doctor. | She **shouldn't** eat that. |

**Nümunələr:**
- You have a cold. You **should** drink hot tea.
- You're tired. You **shouldn't** stay up late.
- I have a headache. **Should** I take aspirin?

---

## Nümunə Dialoq — Həkimdə

**Doctor:** What's the matter?
**Patient:** I don't feel well. I have a sore throat and a temperature.
**Doctor:** How long have you felt like this?
**Patient:** Since yesterday.
**Doctor:** Let me check. Open your mouth, please. Yes, you have an infection.
**Patient:** Is it serious?
**Doctor:** No, but you should rest and drink plenty of water. I'll give you some medicine.
**Patient:** Should I stay home from work?
**Doctor:** Yes, you shouldn't go to work for two days.

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Should/shouldn't ilə məsləhət verin
1. I have a headache. (rest / watch TV for hours)
2. She has a cold. (drink warm fluids / go swimming)
3. He has a bad back. (see a doctor / carry heavy things)

**Cavablar:**
1. You should rest. You shouldn't watch TV for hours.
2. She should drink warm fluids. She shouldn't go swimming.
3. He should see a doctor. He shouldn't carry heavy things.

### 2. Uyğun simptom seçin
1. My head ___. (hurts / aches → both OK: hurts)
2. I have a sore ___. (throat)
3. I feel ___. (dizzy / tired / sick)
