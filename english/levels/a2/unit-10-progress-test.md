# A2 Progress Test

## Bu Test Nəyi Yoxlayır?
A2 səviyyəsindəki bütün əsas mövzular:
- Past Simple
- Alış-veriş və Səyahət lüğəti
- Comparative / Superlative
- Hava və mövsümlər
- Sağlamlıq — should/shouldn't
- Hobbilər — like/love/enjoy + -ing
- Peşələr — a/an
- Gələcək — will / going to

---

## Bölmə 1: Qrammatika (20 bal)

### A. Past Simple — düzgün formanı yazın (5 bal)
1. She ___ (go) to the market yesterday.
2. They ___ (not/watch) the film last night.
3. ___ you ___ (enjoy) the concert?
4. He ___ (buy) a new phone last week.
5. We ___ (travel) to Istanbul two years ago.

**Cavablar:** 1-went, 2-didn't watch, 3-Did/enjoy, 4-bought, 5-travelled

### B. Will ya Going To? (5 bal)
1. Look at those clouds! It ___ (rain). [dəlilə əsaslanan]
2. I promise I ___ (call) you. [vəd]
3. We have a plan — we ___ (visit) Paris next summer.
4. The phone is ringing. I ___ (answer) it.
5. She ___ (study) medicine — she applied last month.

**Cavablar:** 1-is going to, 2-will, 3-are going to, 4-will, 5-is going to

### C. Comparative / Superlative (5 bal)
1. Mount Everest is ___ (high) mountain in the world.
2. A motorbike is ___ (fast) than a bicycle.
3. This film is ___ (boring) than the last one.
4. She is ___ (good) player on the team.
5. Gold is ___ (expensive) than silver.

**Cavablar:** 1-the highest, 2-faster, 3-more boring, 4-the best, 5-more expensive

### D. Should/Shouldn't (5 bal)
1. I have a headache. I ___ (take) some medicine.
2. You're tired. You ___ (stay up) late.
3. She has a cold. She ___ (see) a doctor.
4. He has a bad back. He ___ (lift) heavy things.
5. We're lost. We ___ (ask) for directions.

**Cavablar:** 1-should, 2-shouldn't, 3-should, 4-shouldn't, 5-should

## Bölmə 2: Lüğət (20 bal)

### A. Xəstəlik/simptom — İngilis sözü yazın (5 bal)
1. baş ağrısı → ___
2. öskürək → ___
3. boğaz ağrısı → ___
4. hərarət → ___
5. runny nose → ___ (Azərbaycancaya tərcümə et)

**Cavablar:** 1-headache, 2-cough, 3-sore throat, 4-fever/temperature, 5-axan burun

### B. Peşə — A or An? (5 bal)
1. She is ___ nurse.
2. He is ___ engineer.
3. I want to be ___ architect.
4. My father is ___ police officer.
5. She became ___ doctor.

**Cavablar:** 1-a, 2-an, 3-an, 4-a, 5-a

### C. Hobbiləri tamamlayın (5 bal)
1. I enjoy ___ (swim) in the sea.
2. She loves ___ (read) mystery novels.
3. He hates ___ (wake) up early.
4. We don't mind ___ (wait).
5. They can't stand ___ (argue).

**Cavablar:** 1-swimming, 2-reading, 3-waking, 4-waiting, 5-arguing

### D. Müqayisəli sifət — hava (5 bal)
1. Summer is ___ (hot) than spring.
2. Winter is ___ (cold) than autumn.
3. April is ___ (wet) than August.
4. May is ___ (pleasant) than January.
5. Spring is ___ (beautiful) than winter.

**Cavablar:** 1-hotter, 2-colder, 3-wetter, 4-more pleasant, 5-more beautiful

## Bölmə 3: Oxuma (10 bal)

**Mətni oxuyun:**

> Sarah is a 25-year-old graphic designer. She works for a creative company in Baku. She loves her job because she enjoys working with colours and technology. In her free time, she likes hiking and photography. Last summer, she went to Sheki and took hundreds of photos of the old caravanserai.
>
> Next month, Sarah is going to visit Georgia with her friends. They are going to spend five days in Tbilisi. She thinks Tbilisi is more interesting than any other city she has visited. She will probably take a lot of photos there too.

**Suallar:**
1. What is Sarah's job?
2. Where does she work?
3. What does she like doing in her free time?
4. Where did she go last summer?
5. What is she going to do next month?
6. What does she think about Tbilisi?

**Cavablar:**
1. She is a graphic designer.
2. She works for a creative company in Baku.
3. She likes hiking and photography.
4. She went to Sheki.
5. She is going to visit Georgia / Tbilisi.
6. She thinks Tbilisi is more interesting than any other city she has visited.
