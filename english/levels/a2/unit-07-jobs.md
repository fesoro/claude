## Qrammatika: A / An ilə Peşələr

Peşə bildirəndə **a / an** işlənir:

- I am **a** teacher. ✓
- She is **an** engineer. ✓
- He is **a** doctor. ✓

> Azərbaycanca "Mən müəllimim" deməklə yanaşı, ingilis dilində "a/an" **mütləq** lazımdır!
> - ✗ I am teacher.
> - ✓ I am **a** teacher.

## Nümunə Dialoq

**A:** What do you do?
**B:** I'm a nurse. I work in a city hospital.
**A:** That's an important job! Do you work full-time?
**B:** Yes, I work five days a week. Sometimes I work night shifts too.
**A:** That sounds tiring. What do you like about your job?
**B:** I love helping people. It's very rewarding. What about you?
**A:** I'm a graphic designer. I work for a marketing company.

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. A or An?
1. She is ___ architect.
2. He is ___ police officer.
3. I want to be ___ engineer.
4. My sister is ___ nurse.

**Cavablar:** 1-an, 2-a, 3-an, 4-a

### 2. Peşəni tapın
1. "I fly planes." → ___
2. "I teach children." → ___
3. "I design buildings." → ___

**Cavablar:** 1-pilot, 2-teacher, 3-architect
