# A2 Unit 08: Gələcək Planlar (Future Plans)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Will — spontan qərar, proqnoz
- Going to — plan, niyyət
- Present Continuous — yaxın gələcək planlar
- Gələcək zaman ifadələri

---

## Əsas İfadələr

| İngilis | Azərbaycanca |
|---------|--------------|
| What are you going to do? | Nə etmək niyyətindəsiniz? |
| I'm going to study medicine. | Tibb oxumağı planlaşdırıram. |
| I'll help you. | Sənə kömək edəcəyəm. |

## Qrammatika: Will vs Going To

| | **Will** | **Going To** |
|---|---|---|
| **İşlənmə** | Spontan qərar, proqnoz, vəd | Əvvəlcədən düşünülmüş plan |
| **Nümunə** | "The phone is ringing — I'll get it!" | "I'm going to visit my parents next week." |

### Will
**Quruluş:** Subject + will + base verb

| Müsbət | Mənfi | Sual |
|--------|-------|------|
| I **will** go. | I **won't** go. | **Will** you go? |
| She **will** help. | She **won't** help. | **Will** she help? |

**İşlənmə:**
- Spontan qərar: "It's cold. I'll close the window."
- Proqnoz (dəlilsiz): "I think technology will change everything."
- Vəd: "I promise I'll call you."

### Going To
**Quruluş:** Subject + am/is/are + going to + base verb

| Müsbət | Mənfi |
|--------|-------|
| I **am going to** study. | I **am not going to** study. |
| She **is going to** travel. | She **isn't going to** travel. |

**İşlənmə:**
- Plan/niyyət: "I'm going to learn Spanish this year."
- Dəlilə əsaslanan proqnoz: "Look at those clouds — it's going to rain!"

## Gələcək Zaman İfadələri

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| tomorrow | I'll call you **tomorrow**. |
| next week / month / year | She's going to travel **next month**. |
| in the future | **In the future**, robots will do our work. |
| soon | I'll finish **soon**. |
| this weekend | We're going to hike **this weekend**. |
| in 2030 | Many things **will** change **in 2030**. |

---

## Nümunə Dialoq

**A:** Have you got any plans for the summer?
**B:** Yes! I'm going to visit Istanbul with my family.
**A:** That sounds exciting! How long are you going to stay?
**B:** About two weeks. We'll visit the historical places.
**A:** I think you'll love it. The food is amazing there.
**B:** I'm sure I will! What about you?
**A:** I haven't decided yet. Maybe I'll go to the beach.

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Will ya Going to?
1. "The phone is ringing." — "I ___ answer it." (will)
2. "I have a plan." — "I ___ study in London next year." (am going to)
3. "Look at him run!" — "He ___ win the race." (is going to)
4. "It's your birthday!" — "I ___ buy you a cake." (will)

### 2. Cümlə qurun
Öz planlarınızı yazın:
- Next weekend I'm going to ___.
- In the future, I think I will ___.
- Tomorrow I'll ___.
