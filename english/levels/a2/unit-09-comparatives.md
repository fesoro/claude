# A2 Unit 09: Müqayisə (Comparatives & Superlatives)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Qısa sifətlərlə müqayisə: -er / -est
- Uzun sifətlərlə müqayisə: more / most
- Qeyri-müntəzəm formalar: good/bad/far
- "As...as" ilə bərabərlik

---

## Comparative (Müqayisə)

### Qısa sifətlər (1-2 heca): + **er**

| Sifət | Comparative | Nümunə |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| tall | taller | John is **taller than** Mike. |
| old | older | This building is **older than** that one. |
| fast | faster | A car is **faster than** a bike. |
| hot | hotter* | Summer is **hotter than** spring. |
| big | bigger* | A whale is **bigger than** a shark. |
| happy | happier* | She's **happier than** before. |

> *Qayda: son hərif qoşalanır (hot→hotter); -y, -ier olur (happy→happier)

### Uzun sifətlər (3+ heca): **more +** adj

| Sifət | Comparative | Nümunə |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| beautiful | more beautiful | Paris is **more beautiful than** London. |
| expensive | more expensive | Gold is **more expensive than** silver. |
| interesting | more interesting | History is **more interesting than** maths. |
| comfortable | more comfortable | This chair is **more comfortable**. |

---

## Superlative (Ən Yüksək Dərəcə)

### Qısa sifətlər: **the** + adj + **est**

| Sifət | Superlative | Nümunə |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| tall | the tallest | He is **the tallest** in the class. |
| old | the oldest | This is **the oldest** church in the city. |
| hot | the hottest | July is **the hottest** month. |

### Uzun sifətlər: **the most +** adj

| Sifət | Superlative | Nümunə |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| beautiful | the most beautiful | It's **the most beautiful** city I've seen. |
| expensive | the most expensive | This is **the most expensive** restaurant. |

---

## Qeyri-müntəzəm Formalar

| Sifət | Comparative | Superlative |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| good | **better** | **the best** |
| bad | **worse** | **the worst** |
| far | **further/farther** | **the furthest/farthest** |
| little | **less** | **the least** |
| much/many | **more** | **the most** |

---

## As...As — Bərabərlik

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| as + adj + as | She is **as tall as** her brother. |
| not as + adj + as | London is **not as hot as** Dubai. |

---

## Nümunə Dialoq

**A:** Which city is bigger, Baku or Tbilisi?
**B:** Baku is bigger than Tbilisi. It's also more modern.
**A:** Is it the biggest city in the Caucasus?
**B:** Yes, it's one of the most developed cities in the region.
**A:** What about the weather? Is it hotter than Tbilisi?
**B:** Baku is not as rainy as Tbilisi, but summers can be very hot.

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Düzgün formanı yazın
1. Mount Everest is ___ (high) mountain in the world.
2. A Porsche is ___ (fast) than a normal car.
3. This exercise is ___ (difficult) than the last one.
4. She is ___ (good) student in the class.

**Cavablar:**
1. the highest
2. faster
3. more difficult
4. the best

### 2. Cümlə qurun
1. gold / expensive / silver → Gold is more expensive than silver.
2. Baku / big / Ganja → Baku is bigger than Ganja.
3. elephant / heavy / horse → An elephant is heavier than a horse.
