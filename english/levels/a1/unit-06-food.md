## Nümunə Dialoq

**Waiter:** Good evening! A table for two?  
**A:** Yes, please.  
**Waiter:** Here's the menu. Are you ready to order?  
**A:** Not yet, thank you. (A few minutes later) Yes, I'd like the grilled chicken, please.  
**B:** And I'll have the fish with rice.  
**Waiter:** Anything to drink?  
**A:** I'd like a glass of water, please.  
**B:** A tea for me.  
**Waiter:** Of course. Enjoy your meal!
