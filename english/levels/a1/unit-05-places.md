## Yer Qoşmaları (Prepositions of Place)

| Qoşma | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| next to | yanında | The bank is **next to** the post office. |
| opposite | qarşısında | The school is **opposite** the park. |
| between | arasında | The café is **between** the bank and the hotel. |
| behind | arxasında | The car park is **behind** the supermarket. |
| in front of | qarşısında | There's a fountain **in front of** the museum. |
| near | yaxınında | Is there a pharmacy **near** here? |
| on the corner of | küncündə | The bank is **on the corner of** Main Street. |
| on the left / right | solda / sağda | The post office is **on the left**. |
| straight ahead | düz irəli | Go **straight ahead** for 200 metres. |

|---------|--------------|

## Yol Göstərmək

| İngilis | Azərbaycanca |
|---------|--------------|
| Turn left. | Sola dönün. |

| Go straight ahead. | Düz gedin. |

| It's on your left/right. | Solunuzda/sağınızdadır. |
| You can't miss it. | Görünür (çətin qaçırsınız). |
| It's about 5 minutes walk. | Yeriməklə 5 dəqiqə. |
| It's far. Take a taxi. | Uzaqdır. Taksi götürün. |

---

## Nümunə Dialoq

**A:** Excuse me, is there a bank near here?  
**B:** Yes, there is. Go straight ahead and turn left at the traffic lights. The bank is on your right, next to the post office.  
**A:** Is it far?  
**B:** No, it's about 5 minutes on foot.  
**A:** Thank you very much!  
**B:** You're welcome!

## Tapşırıqlar

### Tapşırıq 1 — Doğru Qoşmanı Seçin
1. The café is _______ the bank and the pharmacy. (between/next to)
2. The museum is _______ the park. (opposite/behind)
3. The supermarket is _______ the main road. (on/in)

### Tapşırıq 2 — Yazı
Yaşadığınız yerə yaxın olan 5 yerə (bank, məktəb və s.) aid 5 cümlə yazın.

### Cavablar

**Tapşırıq 1:** 1. between 2. opposite 3. on
