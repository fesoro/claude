# B2 Unit 01: İnversion və Vurğu Strukturları

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Inversion (ters söz sırası) — formal/ədəbi dil
- Cleft sentences (It is... that / What... is)
- Emphasis (vurğu) üçün strukturlar
- B2+ yazı dilini zənginləşdirmək

---

## Inversion Nədir?

Normal cümlə: **Subject + Verb**
Inversion: **Verb + Subject** (söz sırası dəyişir)

**Nə vaxt?** Mənfi/məhdudlaşdırıcı zərf cümləsinin əvvəlinə gəldiyi zaman:

---

## Mənfi Zərflərlə Inversion

| Zərf/İfadə | Nümunə (Inversion ilə) |
|------------|------------------------|
| Never | **Never have I** seen such beauty. |
| Rarely | **Rarely does she** make mistakes. |
| Seldom | **Seldom did we** agree. |
| Hardly / Barely | **Hardly had I** arrived when she called. |
| No sooner...than | **No sooner had he** left **than** she arrived. |
| Not only...but also | **Not only did he** apologise, but he also helped. |
| Only then | **Only then did I** understand. |
| Only when | **Only when I** saw it **did I** believe it. |
| Little | **Little did she** know what would happen. |

---

## Müqayisə

| Normal | Inversion |
|--------|----------|
| I have never seen this. | **Never have I** seen this. |
| She rarely makes mistakes. | **Rarely does she** make mistakes. |
| He had hardly arrived when it started. | **Hardly had he** arrived when it started. |
| I not only saw it but also heard it. | **Not only did I** see it, but I also heard it. |

> Inversion daha **formal** və **vurğulayıcıdır** — yazı, çıxış, IELTS/TOEFL-də işlənir.

---

## Cleft Sentences (Vurğu Strukturları)

### It is/was... that/who
Cümlənin müəyyən hissəsini vurğulamaq üçün:

- **It was John** who broke the window. (Pəncərəni sındıran Cohn idi)
- **It is hard work** that leads to success.
- **It was in 2020** that everything changed.

### What... is/was
- **What I need** is more time. (Lazım olan şey daha çox vaxtdır)
- **What surprised me** was his reaction.
- **What we need to do** is find a solution.

---

## So/Such vurğusu

```
So + adjective/adverb + that
Such + (a/an) + adjective + noun + that

It was **so** cold **that** we stayed inside.
It was **such a** cold day **that** we stayed inside.
```

---

## Do/Does/Did — Vurğu

Müsbət cümlədə yardımçı fel işlədərək vurğu:
- I **do** like your idea! (Həqiqətən xoşuma gəlir!)
- She **does** work hard. (O, həqiqətən çox çalışır)
- He **did** call you. (O, həqiqətən səni çağırdı)

---

## IELTS/Formal Yazı üçün İnversion Nümunələri

**Zəif:** I have never seen such a complicated problem.
**Güclü:** **Never have I** encountered such a complex issue.

**Zəif:** He not only lied but also stole.
**Güclü:** **Not only did he** lie, but he also resorted to theft.

**Zəif:** I understood only then.
**Güclü:** **Only then did I** come to realise the full extent of the problem.

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün |
|------|--------|
| Never I have seen... | **Never have I** seen... |
| Rarely she makes... | **Rarely does she** make... |
| Not only he lied but... | **Not only did he** lie, but... |
| It was John that **broke** it ✓ | — (bu doğrudur) |

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Inversion ilə yenidən yazın
1. I have never heard such a beautiful song.
2. She rarely eats meat.
3. He had hardly left when she arrived.
4. They not only apologised but also compensated.

**Cavablar:**
1. **Never have I** heard such a beautiful song.
2. **Rarely does she** eat meat.
3. **Hardly had he** left when she arrived.
4. **Not only did they** apologise, but they also compensated.

### 2. Cleft sentence ilə vurğulayın (qalın yazılanı)
1. **John** broke the vase (not Peter).
2. I need **more time** (not money).
3. She left **in anger** (not calmly).

**Cavablar:**
1. **It was John** who broke the vase.
2. **What I need** is more time.
3. **It was in anger** that she left.
