# B2 Unit 05: IELTS Writing Task 2

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Task 2 esse növləri
- Giriş (introduction) yazma
- Əsas paraqraflar (body paragraphs)
- Nəticə (conclusion)
- Band 6-7 strategiyaları

---

## Task 2 — Esse Növləri

| Növ | Sual nümunəsi | Struktur |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| **Opinion/Argumentative** | "Do you agree or disagree?" | Giriş + 2 əsas paraqraf + nəticə |
| **Discussion** | "Discuss both views and give your opinion." | Giriş + Görüş 1 + Görüş 2 + Fikrin + nəticə |
| **Problem-Solution** | "What are the causes? What are solutions?" | Giriş + Problemlər + Həllər + nəticə |
| **Advantages-Disadvantages** | "Discuss advantages and disadvantages." | Giriş + Üstünlüklər + Çatışmazlıqlar + nəticə |
| **Two-part Question** | "Why is this? What can be done?" | Hər suala bir paraqraf |

---

## Giriş Yazmaq (Introduction)

**2 hissə:**
1. **Paraphrase** — sualı öz sözlərinizlə yazın
2. **Thesis statement** — nə yazacağınızı bildirin

**Nümunə sual:**
> "Some people think that children should learn to be competitive. Others think that co-operation is more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion."

**Giriş:**
> In today's competitive world, the question of whether children should be encouraged to compete or cooperate with others is widely debated. While some argue that a competitive mindset prepares children for adult life, others believe that the ability to work together is a more valuable skill. This essay will examine both perspectives before presenting my own view.

---

## Əsas Paraqraf Strukturu (PEEL)

| Hissə | Azərbaycanca | Nümunə |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| **P**oint | Əsas fikir | Firstly, competition can motivate children to work harder. |
| **E**xplain | İzah et | When students compete for grades or prizes, they are encouraged to put in more effort. |
| **E**vidence/Example | Nümunə/Dəlil | For instance, academic competitions such as science fairs have been shown to increase engagement with learning. |
| **L**ink | Keçid/Nəticə | This suggests that a degree of healthy competition can be beneficial for children's development. |

---

## Nəticə Yazmaq (Conclusion)

**2 hissə:**
1. Əsas fikirləri qısaca xülasə et
2. Öz fikrini bir daha ifadə et (yeni fikir əlavə etmə!)

**Nümunə:**
> In conclusion, while competition undeniably has its merits in motivating individuals, the ability to collaborate effectively is arguably the more essential life skill. Therefore, education systems should strive to develop both qualities in equal measure, preparing children for the diverse challenges of modern society.

---

## Band 6-7 Checklist

**Task Achievement:**
- [ ] Sualın bütün hissələrinə cavab verdim
- [ ] Aydın pozisiya/fikir bildirdim
- [ ] Nümunə/dəlil istifadə etdim

**Coherence & Cohesion:**
- [ ] Discourse markers işlətdim (However, Furthermore, Therefore...)
- [ ] Paraqraflara düzgün bölmüşəm
- [ ] Məntiqli ardıcıllıq var

**Lexical Resource:**
- [ ] Formal lüğət işlətdim (obtain, demonstrate, significant...)
- [ ] Eyni sözü təkrar etmədim (sinonim işlətdim)
- [ ] Kollokasiyalar düzdür

**Grammar:**
- [ ] Müxtəlif cümlə strukturları işlətdim
- [ ] Modal verbs: could, should, may
- [ ] Passiv növ istifadə etdim
- [ ] Conditional cümlələr

**Uzunluq:** 250+ söz

## Faydalı Əsas Cümlə Şablonları

### Giriş üçün
- In recent years, there has been growing debate about...
- The question of whether...has become increasingly important.
- It is widely acknowledged that...

### Əsas paraqraf üçün
- One of the main arguments in favour of...is...
- A significant drawback of...is that...
- Proponents of this view argue that...
- Critics, however, point out that...

### Nəticə üçün
- In conclusion, while...it is clear that...
- Overall, it would appear that...the benefits outweigh the drawbacks.
- Taking all factors into consideration...

---

## Məşq Tapşırığı

**Esse yazın (250+ söz):**

> "The increasing use of technology in education has both advantages and disadvantages. Discuss."

**Plan:**
- Giriş: paraphrase + thesis (technology in education — two sides)
- Paraqraf 1: Üstünlüklər (access to information, interactive learning, self-paced)
- Paraqraf 2: Çatışmazlıqlar (distraction, screen time, reduced social skills)
- Nəticə: balanced view
