## Part 1 — Qısa Cavablar

**Strategiya:** Cavabı genişləndir — yalnız "Yes/No" deyil!

**Zəif cavab:** "Do you enjoy reading?" → "Yes."
**Güclü cavab:** "Yes, I really enjoy reading. I'm particularly fond of historical fiction — I find it's a great way to learn about different periods in history while being entertained at the same time."

**Formul: Cavab + Səbəb + Nümunə**

**Nümunə suallar və cavablar:**

*Q: Do you live in a house or an apartment?*
> I live in an apartment in the city centre. It's quite convenient because everything is within walking distance — shops, restaurants, the metro station. The only downside is that it's a bit noisy sometimes.

*Q: How do you usually spend your weekends?*
> Well, it depends on the weather, really. If it's sunny, I like to go cycling in the park or meet up with friends. If it's raining, I tend to stay home, maybe watch a film or do some reading.

## Part 2 — Cue Card

**Nümunə cue card:**
> Describe a book you have read that you found interesting.
> You should say:
> - What the book was about
> - When and why you read it
> - What you found interesting about it
> - And explain whether you would recommend it to others.

**1 dəqiqə ərzində not alın:**
- What: novel / topic
- When/Why: last year / recommended
- Interesting: characters / plot twist
- Recommend: yes — why

**Cavab şablonu:**
> I'd like to talk about a book called [title] by [author]. I read it about [time] ago, mainly because [reason].
>
> The book is about [brief plot summary]. What I found particularly interesting was [specific aspect] — for example, [detail].
>
> I'd definitely recommend this book to others, especially people who enjoy [genre], because [reason]. In fact, several of my friends have read it after I suggested it, and they all loved it too.

## Part 3 — Abstract Discussion

Part 3-də daha dərin, abstrakt suallar gəlir. Fikirləri **əsaslandırmaq** vacibdir.

**Nümunə suallar:**
- "Why do you think reading is becoming less popular?"
- "Do you think the internet has replaced books?"
- "How important is it for schools to encourage reading?"

**Güclü cavab strukturu:**
1. Fikir bil
2. Əsaslandır
3. Nümunə ver
4. Baxış açısı əlavə et (alternatively / on the other hand)

**Nümunə:**
> That's an interesting question. I think reading is indeed becoming less popular, particularly among younger generations. This is largely due to the rise of digital entertainment — streaming services, social media and video games all compete for people's attention. That said, I believe e-books and audiobooks have actually introduced a new form of reading to many people. So while traditional book reading may be declining, engagement with stories and information hasn't necessarily decreased.

## Band 6-7 Kriterlər

| Kriteriya | Band 6 | Band 7 |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Fluency** | Bir az duraksama | Az duraksama, rahat axır |
| **Vocabulary** | Uyğun lüğət, bəzi təkrar | Geniş, natural lüğət |
| **Grammar** | Əsas strukturlar düzgün | Mürəkkəb strukturlar cəhdləri |
| **Pronunciation** | Başa düşülür, aksent var | Aydın, intonasiya var |

## Faydalı İfadələr

### Vaxt qazanmaq
- That's a really interesting question...
- Let me think about that for a moment...
- Well, it's difficult to say, but...
- I've never really thought about this, but...

### Genişləndirmək
- What I mean is...
- To elaborate on that...
- A good example of this would be...
- This is particularly evident in...

### Fikir dəyişdirmək
- Then again...
- Having said that...
- On reflection...
- Actually, thinking about it more...
