# B2 Unit 02: Diskurs Markerlər (Discourse Markers)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Formal yazıda rabitə sözləri
- Müzakirədə fikir bildirmək
- Ziddiyyət, güzəşt, vurğu ifadələri
- Akademik üslub

---

## Əlavə Etmək (Addition)

| İfadə | Rəsmilik | Nümunə |
|-------|----------|--------|
| furthermore | yüksək | The plan is effective. **Furthermore**, it is affordable. |
| moreover | yüksək | She is talented. **Moreover**, she works hard. |
| in addition (to) | orta | **In addition to** cost, time is a factor. |
| what is more | orta | It's convenient. **What is more**, it's free. |
| also / too / as well | aşağı | I agree. I think so **too**. |
| not only...but also | orta | It is **not only** fast **but also** accurate. |

---

## Ziddiyyət (Contrast)

| İfadə | İşlənmə | Nümunə |
|-------|---------|--------|
| however | cümlə əvvəlində | **However**, the evidence is mixed. |
| nevertheless / nonetheless | güzəşt | It rained. **Nevertheless**, we continued. |
| on the other hand | iki tərəf | Cats are independent. **On the other hand**, dogs need attention. |
| in contrast | müqayisə | City life is busy. **In contrast**, villages are quiet. |
| whereas / while | eyni cümlədə | **Whereas** John is shy, Maria is outgoing. |
| despite / in spite of | +noun/-ing | **Despite** the rain, they went out. |
| although / even though | +clause | **Although** it was expensive, I bought it. |

---

## Güzəşt (Concession)

Rəqib fikri qəbul edib öz fikrini davam etdirmək:

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| Admittedly, ... | **Admittedly**, it costs more. However, the quality is better. |
| It is true that..., but... | **It is true that** smoking is a personal choice, but it harms others. |
| While I understand..., | **While I understand** your concern, I disagree. |
| granted, ... | **Granted**, there are risks. Nevertheless, the benefits outweigh them. |

---

## Səbəb və Nəticə (Cause & Effect)

| Səbəb | Nəticə |
|-------|--------|
| because / since / as | therefore / thus / hence |
| due to / owing to (+noun) | as a result / consequently |
| given that | this leads to / this results in |

**Nümunələr:**
- **Due to** rising costs, many companies have relocated.
- The road was icy; **consequently**, several accidents occurred.
- **Given that** resources are limited, priorities must be set.

---

## Vurğu (Emphasis)

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| indeed | It is **indeed** a serious problem. |
| in fact / as a matter of fact | **In fact**, the situation is worse than expected. |
| clearly / obviously | **Clearly**, more funding is needed. |
| it is worth noting that | **It is worth noting that** this trend is new. |
| above all | **Above all**, safety must be prioritised. |

---

## Nümunə: Akademik Paraqraf

> The impact of social media on mental health is a growing concern. **Furthermore**, recent studies suggest that excessive use is linked to anxiety and depression. **However**, it would be wrong to blame social media entirely. **In fact**, many users report positive experiences such as community support and connection. **Admittedly**, these platforms can be addictive by design. **Nevertheless**, with proper regulation and digital literacy education, the benefits could outweigh the harms. **Therefore**, rather than restricting social media, society should focus on responsible use.

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Uyğun marker seçin
1. ___ the cold weather, the match continued. (Despite/Although)
2. The product is innovative. ___, it is expensive. (However/Moreover)
3. She works full-time. ___, she is studying for a degree. (Furthermore/Despite)
4. The policy failed. ___, it was redesigned. (Consequently/Although)

**Cavablar:** 1-Despite, 2-However, 3-Furthermore, 4-Consequently

### 2. Cümləni tamamlayın
1. Although the film received good reviews, ___.
2. The city has grown rapidly. Consequently, ___.
3. Admittedly, fast food is convenient. Nevertheless, ___.
