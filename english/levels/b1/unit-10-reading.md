## Skimming — Sürətli Baxış

**Nə vaxt:** Mövzunu tez anlamaq lazım olanda.

**Necə:**
1. Başlığı oxu
2. Birinci abzası tam oxu
3. Hər abzasın yalnız ilk cümləsini oxu
4. Son abzası tam oxu

## Scanning — Spesifik Məlumat Axtarışı

**Nə vaxt:** Tarix, ad, rəqəm, xüsusi fakt lazım olanda.

**Necə:**
1. Nə axtardığını bil
2. Gözlərini mətn üzərindən sürüşdür
3. Böyük hərf, rəqəm, xüsusi sözlərə fokusla
4. Tapdığın yerdə dayanıb oxu

## Nümunə Mətn — B1 Səviyyəsi

**The Rise of Remote Work**

Over the past few years, remote work has become increasingly common around the world. Before the pandemic, only a small percentage of employees worked from home regularly. However, the situation changed dramatically when companies were forced to adapt quickly to new circumstances.

Many workers have discovered unexpected benefits of working remotely. First, they save time by not commuting to the office. A typical city worker might spend up to two hours travelling every day, which adds up to ten hours per week. Second, employees report feeling less stressed and more productive when working in a comfortable home environment.

Nevertheless, remote work also has its challenges. Some people struggle with isolation and miss the social aspect of office life. Others find it difficult to separate work from personal life when both happen in the same space. Additionally, not everyone has a suitable home environment for concentrated work.

Companies are now experimenting with hybrid models, where employees split their time between home and office. This approach seems to offer the best of both worlds — flexibility and social connection.

## Mətn Üzrə Suallar

**Skimming sualları (ümumi anlayış):**
1. What is the main topic of the text?
2. What are two benefits of remote work mentioned?
3. What are two challenges?

**Scanning sualları (xüsusi məlumat):**
1. How many hours per week does a typical commuter spend travelling?
2. What is a "hybrid model"?

**Cavablar:**
- Ümumi: Remote work, benefits and challenges
- 2 üstünlük: saving commute time, less stress
- 2 çətinlik: isolation, work-life balance
- Scanning: 10 hours per week; employees split time between home and office
