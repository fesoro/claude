# B1 Unit 02: Dolayı Nitq (Reported Speech)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Birinin sözlərini dolayı şəkildə bildirmək
- Zaman dəyişiklikləri (backshift)
- Say, reporting verbs
- Azərbaycanlıların ən çox etdiyi səhvlər

---

## Nədir?

**Direct speech:** Birinin dediyi söz bilavasitə:
> She said: **"I am tired."**

**Reported speech:** Onun sözlərini başqasına bildirmək:
> She said (that) **she was tired.**

---

## Zaman Dəyişikliyi (Backshift)

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|---------------|-----------------|
| am / is / are | was / were |
| was / were | had been |
| do / does | did |
| did | had done |
| have / has done | had done |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| must | had to |

**Nümunələr:**
- "I **live** in Baku." → He said he **lived** in Baku.
- "I **am working**." → She said she **was working**.
- "I **have finished**." → He said he **had finished**.
- "I **will come**." → She said she **would come**.
- "I **can help**." → He said he **could help**.

---

## Şəxs Əvəzliklərinin Dəyişməsi

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|---------|
| I | he / she |
| we | they |
| you | I / he / she / they |
| my | his / her |
| our | their |

---

## Say vs Tell

| Say | Tell |
|-----|------|
| say + (that) | tell + **person** + (that) |
| He **said** he was tired. | He **told me** he was tired. |
| She **said** "hello". | She **told** him to leave. |
| ❌ He said me... | ✅ He told me... |

---

## Sual Cümlələri (Reported Questions)

**Sual sözu olan suallar:**
- "**Where** do you live?" → He asked **where I lived**.
- "**What** are you doing?" → She asked **what I was doing**.

**Yes/No suallar (if/whether):**
- "**Do** you like music?" → He asked **if/whether I liked** music.
- "**Are** you coming?" → She asked **whether I was coming**.

> **Diqqət:** Reported question-da cümlə quruluşu birbaşa sual deyil!
> - ❌ He asked where **did I live**.
> - ✅ He asked where **I lived**.

---

## Əmr Cümlələri (Reported Commands)

```
tell/ask/order + object + to + infinitive

"Come here!" → He told me **to come** there.
"Don't smoke!" → She told him **not to smoke**.
"Please sit down." → He asked us **to sit down**.
```

---

## Azərbaycanlıların Tez-tez Etdiyi Səhvlər

| Səhv | Düzgün | İzah |
|------|--------|------|
| He said me that... | He **told me** that... | "say" şəxs tamamlığı almır |
| She asked where did he go. | She asked where **he went**. | Sual quruluşu deyil |
| He told that he was tired. | He told **me** that... / He **said** that... | "tell" şəxs tələb edir |
| She said to come. | She **told me** to come. | Əmr — tell + person + to |

---

## Nümunə Dialoq

**Direct:**
John: "I'm really stressed about the exam."
Mary: "Don't worry! You'll do well."
John: "I haven't studied enough."
Mary: "Can I help you?"

**Reported:**
John told Mary that he was really stressed about the exam. Mary told him not to worry and said that he would do well. John said he hadn't studied enough. Mary asked if she could help him.

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Reported Speech-ə çevirin
1. "I am very busy." — She said...
2. "We will be late." — They said...
3. "I can't come tomorrow." — He told me...
4. "Have you finished?" — She asked me...
5. "Close the door!" — He told me...

**Cavablar:**
1. She said (that) she **was** very busy.
2. They said (that) they **would** be late.
3. He told me (that) he **couldn't** come the next day.
4. She asked me if I **had finished**.
5. He told me **to close** the door.

### 2. Xəbər mövzusunda reported speech:
Bu gün eşitdiyiniz bir xəbəri reported speech ilə yazın (3-4 cümlə):
"The news said that..."
