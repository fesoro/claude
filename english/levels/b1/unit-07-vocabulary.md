## Söz Ailəsi — Word Families

Bir kök sözdən bir neçə forma:

| Kök | İsim | Fel | Sifət | Zərflik |
|-----|------|-----|-------|---------|
| create | creation / creator | create | creative | creatively |
| educate | education / educator | educate | educational | educationally |
| success | success | succeed | successful | successfully |
| decide | decision | decide | decisive | decisively |
| help | help / helper | help | helpful | helpfully |
| innovate | innovation | innovate | innovative | innovatively |

|--------|--------|--------|

## Şəkilçilər — Suffixes

### İsim düzəldən
| Suffix | Nümunə |
|--------|--------|
| **-tion/-sion** | education, decision, pollution |
| **-ment** | government, development, achievement |
| **-ness** | happiness, kindness, weakness |
| **-er/-or** | teacher, manager, director |
| **-ist** | artist, scientist, journalist |
| **-ity** | creativity, possibility, reality |

### Sifət düzəldən
| Suffix | Nümunə |
|--------|--------|
| **-ful** | careful, successful, beautiful |
| **-less** | careless, homeless, useless |
| **-able/-ible** | comfortable, responsible, flexible |
| **-ive** | creative, effective, innovative |
| **-ous** | famous, dangerous, serious |
| **-al** | educational, natural, cultural |

## Kontekstdən Məna Çıxarmaq

Yeni söz görəndə:
1. **Prefiks/suffix** varmı? — un**happy**, **re**build
2. **Kök söz** tanıdıqmı? — **education**al → education
3. **Cümlə konteksti** nə deyir?

**Nümunə:**
> "The government implemented several **anti-pollution** measures last year."
- "anti-" = əleyhinə → anti-pollution = çirklənməyə qarşı

## Faydalı Kollokasiyalar (B1)

### Make vs Do (ən çox yanlış)
| Make | Do |
|------|----|
| make a decision | do homework |
| make progress | do exercise |
| make a mistake | do research |
| make an effort | do business |
| make friends | do your best |

- take an exam / test
- take a break
- take part in
- take care of

### Have
- have a meeting
- have an argument

- have a good time
- have a look at

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Söz ailəsi — formanı tapın
1. success (verb) → ___
2. education (adjective) → ___
3. decide (noun) → ___
4. happy (noun) → ___

**Cavablar:** 1-succeed, 2-educational, 3-decision, 4-happiness

### 2. Prefixlə yeni söz düzəldin
1. He always ___ estimates the cost. (under)
2. I need to ___ read that chapter. (re)
3. The food was ___cooked — I couldn't eat it. (over)
4. I ___ understood the instructions. (mis)

**Cavablar:** 1-underestimates, 2-reread, 3-overcooked, 4-misunderstood
