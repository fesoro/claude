# B1 Unit 05: Passiv Növ (Passive Voice)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Passiv növ nədir və nə vaxt işlənir
- Present Simple, Past Simple Passive
- "By" ilə agent göstərmək
- Active → Passive çevirmə

---

## Passiv Növ Nədir?

**Aktiv:** The chef **cooked** the meal. (çörəkçi — əsas)
**Passiv:** The meal **was cooked** (by the chef). (yemək — əsas)

**Nə vaxt işlənir:**
- Kim etdiyindən daha çox nə olduğu vacibdir
- Kim etdiyini bilmirik
- Rəsmi/akademik üslubda

---

## Quruluş

**Be + Past Participle**

| Zaman | Aktiv | Passiv |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Present Simple | They **make** cars here. | Cars **are made** here. |
| Past Simple | Someone **stole** my bike. | My bike **was stolen**. |
| Present Continuous | They **are building** a new road. | A new road **is being built**. |
| Present Perfect | They **have cancelled** the flight. | The flight **has been cancelled**. |
| Future (will) | They **will repair** the bridge. | The bridge **will be repaired**. |
| Modal | They **should fix** it. | It **should be fixed**. |

---

## Active → Passive Çevirmə Qaydası

1. Aktivdəki **obyekt** → passivin **subyekti**
2. **Be** + **past participle** əlavə et
3. Aktivdəki **subyekti** "**by** ___" ilə sona köçür (lazım gəlsə)

**Nümunə:**
- Active: Shakespeare **wrote** Hamlet.
- Passive: Hamlet **was written** by Shakespeare.

---

## By — Kim Tərəfindən

"By" — kimin etdiyini bildirmək üçün:
- This bridge **was built by** engineers in 1920.
- The book **was written by** a young author.
- The meal **is prepared by** professional chefs.

Əgər kim etdiyini bilmirik ya da vacib deyilsə, "by" atılır:
- My car **was stolen** last night. (kim etdi — bilinmir)

---

## Nümunə Dialoq

**A:** Did you hear the news? A new hospital is being built in our district.
**B:** Really? When was the decision made?
**A:** Last month. The project was approved by the city council.
**B:** That's great. My old hospital was closed two years ago.
**A:** I know. Hopefully, the new one will be opened by next year.
**B:** I think it should be built faster — people need it now.

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Active → Passive çevirin
1. They produce wine in France.
2. Someone broke the window.
3. They are painting the walls.
4. The police have arrested a suspect.
5. They will announce the results tomorrow.

**Cavablar:**
1. Wine **is produced** in France.
2. The window **was broken**.
3. The walls **are being painted**.
4. A suspect **has been arrested**.
5. The results **will be announced** tomorrow.

### 2. By ilə ya onsuz?
1. The Eiffel Tower was built ___ Gustave Eiffel. (by)
2. My wallet was stolen ___. (by atılır — kim bilmir)
3. "Hamlet" was written ___ Shakespeare. (by)
