## Paraqraf Strukturu

Yaxşı paraqrafın üç hissəsi var:

```
1. Topic Sentence   — paraqrafın əsas fikri
2. Supporting Ideas — dəlil, izah, nümunə
3. Concluding Line  — nəticə ya keçid
```

**Nümunə:**
> **Topic:** Exercise is important for health.
> **Support:** Regular physical activity strengthens the heart, improves mood, and helps maintain a healthy weight.
> **Conclusion:** Therefore, experts recommend at least 30 minutes of exercise per day.

## Rabitə Sözləri (Connectors/Linking Words)

### Əlavə etmək
| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **and, also, too** | I like swimming. I also enjoy cycling. |
| **in addition, furthermore** | The hotel was cheap. Furthermore, it was clean. |
| **moreover** | The plan is efficient. Moreover, it saves money. |
| **not only...but also** | It is not only fast but also accurate. |

### Kontrast
| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **but, however** | I wanted to come. However, I was busy. |
| **although, even though** | Although it was cold, we went out. |
| **despite, in spite of** | Despite the rain, they played. |
| **on the other hand** | Cats are independent. On the other hand, dogs are loyal. |
| **nevertheless** | The task was hard. Nevertheless, they finished it. |

### Səbəb
| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **because, since, as** | I stayed home **because** it was raining. |
| **due to** | Delays were caused **due to** bad weather. |
| **therefore, so** | It rained; **therefore**, the match was cancelled. |

### Nəticə / Nümunə
| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| **for example, for instance** | Many cities, **for example** London, have congestion. |
| **such as** | Subjects **such as** maths require practice. |
| **in conclusion, to sum up** | **In conclusion**, technology has changed our lives. |

## Rəsmi vs Qeyri-rəsmi Email

| Rəsmi | Qeyri-rəsmi |
|-------|------------|
| Dear Mr/Ms Smith, | Hi Tom, / Hey! |
| I am writing to... | Just wanted to... |
| I would be grateful if... | Could you...? |
| Please do not hesitate to contact me. | Feel free to get in touch. |
| Yours sincerely / faithfully | Best / Take care / Cheers |

---

## Email Şablonu — Rəsmi Müraciət

```
Subject: Application for [Position]

Dear [Mr/Ms + Surname],

I am writing to apply for the position of [job title] advertised on [website].

I have [X] years of experience in [field] and I believe my skills in
[skill 1] and [skill 2] would make me a strong candidate for this role.

I have attached my CV for your consideration. I would welcome the
opportunity to discuss my application further.

Yours sincerely,
[Your name]
```

## Nümunə: Qeyri-rəsmi Email

**Subject:** Plans for the weekend

> Hi Sara,
>
> How are you? I hope you're doing well. I'm writing because I wanted to ask if you'd like to meet this Saturday.
>
> I was thinking we could go to the new café that opened near the park. Apparently, it's really good. Also, there's a film on at the cinema in the evening, so we could do both!
>
> Let me know what you think. I'm free from 2pm onwards.
>
> Looking forward to hearing from you!
>
> Best,
> Leyla

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Uyğun connector seçin
1. I was tired. ___, I went to bed early. (Therefore/However)
2. ___ the bad weather, we enjoyed the trip. (Despite/Because)
3. He studies hard. ___, he gets good grades. (Moreover/Therefore)
4. She is intelligent. ___, she is hardworking. (Furthermore/Despite)

**Cavablar:** 1-Therefore, 2-Despite, 3-Therefore, 4-Furthermore

### 2. Qısa email yazın
Dostunuza axşam görüşmək üçün email yazın (50-70 söz). Bunları daxil edin:
- Salamlama
- Görüşün məqsədi
- Vaxt və yer
- Xüsusi xahiş
