## Bölmə 1: Qrammatika (30 bal)

### A. Present Perfect vs Past Simple (5 bal)
1. I ___ (never/see) the ocean. [PP]
2. She ___ (visit) Paris three years ago. [PS]
3. ___ you ___ (finish) your homework yet? [PP]
4. We ___ (live) here since 2018. [PP]
5. He ___ (work) at that company from 2010 to 2015. [PS]

**Cavablar:** 1-have never seen, 2-visited, 3-Have/finished, 4-have lived, 5-worked

### B. Reported Speech (5 bal)
1. "I am tired." → She said she ___ tired.
2. "I will call you." → He said he ___ call me.
3. "We went to the cinema." → They said they ___ to the cinema.
4. "I can swim." → She said she ___ swim.
5. "Don't open the window." → He told me ___ open the window.

**Cavablar:** 1-was, 2-would, 3-had gone, 4-could, 5-not to

### C. Conditionals (5 bal)
1. If you ___ (heat) water to 100°C, it boils. [zero]
2. If she ___ (study), she will pass the exam. [first]
3. If I ___ (have) more time, I would travel more. [second]
4. Unless you ___ (hurry), you'll miss the bus. [first]
5. If he ___ (be) taller, he could play basketball professionally. [second]

**Cavablar:** 1-heat, 2-studies, 3-had, 4-hurry, 5-were/was

### D. Modal Verbs (5 bal)
1. You ___ (mustn't/don't have to) come — it's optional.
2. He ___ (must/should) have left — his coat is gone.
3. You ___ (should/must) see a doctor — you look pale.
4. She ___ (can't/mustn't) have said that — she was abroad.
5. We ___ (have to/should) wear uniforms — it's the school rule.

**Cavablar:** 1-don't have to, 2-must, 3-should, 4-can't, 5-have to

### E. Passive Voice (5 bal)
1. They built this bridge in 1950. → This bridge ___ in 1950.
2. Someone has stolen her bag. → Her bag ___.
3. They are testing the new medicine. → The new medicine ___.
4. The company will announce results tomorrow. → Results ___ tomorrow.
5. They should fix the road. → The road ___.

**Cavablar:**
1. was built
2. has been stolen
3. is being tested
4. will be announced
5. should be fixed

### F. Relative Clauses (5 bal)
1. The man ___ called me was my old teacher. (who/which)
2. The film ___ we watched last night was boring. (who/which/that)
3. The city ___ she was born is in the north. (where/when/who)
4. My sister, ___ works in London, is visiting us. (who/which)
5. The reason ___ I left early was a headache. (why/where)

**Cavablar:** 1-who, 2-which/that, 3-where, 4-who, 5-why

## Bölmə 3: Oxuma (10 bal)

**Mətni oxuyun:**

> Many psychologists believe that spending time in nature has significant benefits for mental health. Studies have shown that people who regularly visit parks or forests report lower levels of stress and anxiety. In one experiment, participants who walked in a natural environment for 90 minutes showed reduced activity in the part of the brain associated with depression.
>
> Furthermore, being outdoors encourages physical activity, which is also known to improve mood. However, researchers point out that the quality of nature matters — a polluted urban park may not provide the same benefits as a clean forest.
>
> If we spent more time in nature, our mental health would certainly improve. Governments should invest in creating more green spaces in cities. This would benefit millions of people who currently have limited access to natural environments.

**Suallar:**
1. What is the main claim of the text?
2. How long did participants walk in the experiment?
3. What part of the brain was affected?
4. What condition does the researcher mention about parks?
5. What does the writer suggest governments should do?
6. Find a word in paragraph 2 that means "to make better". (**improve**)

**Cavablar:**
1. Nature is beneficial for mental health.
2. 90 minutes.
3. The part associated with depression.
4. The park should be clean / not polluted.
5. Invest in more green spaces in cities.
6. improve
