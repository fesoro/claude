# B1 Unit 06: Əlaqəli Budaq Cümlələri (Relative Clauses)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Defining relative clauses (məhdudlaşdırıcı)
- Non-defining relative clauses (əlavə məlumat)
- Who, which, that, whose, where, when, why
- Relative pronoun-u ixtisar etmək

---

## Relative Pronouns — Ümumi Baxış

| Söz | Nə üçün | Nümunə |
|-----|---------|--------|
| **who** | insanlar üçün | The man **who** called me is here. |
| **which** | əşyalar/heyvanlar | The book **which** I read was great. |
| **that** | insanlar və əşyalar | The car **that** I bought is red. |
| **whose** | mülkiyyət (kimin) | The woman **whose** car was stolen reported it. |
| **where** | yer | The hotel **where** we stayed was nice. |
| **when** | zaman | The year **when** I graduated was 2020. |
| **why** | səbəb | The reason **why** I left is private. |

---

## Defining Relative Clauses — Məhdudlaşdırıcı

Hansı şəxs ya əşya olduğunu **müəyyənləşdirir** — vergülsüz yazılır.

- The woman **who lives next door** is a doctor.
  (Hansı qadın? — Qonşuda yaşayan)
- The phone **that I lost** was expensive.
  (Hansı telefon? — İtirdiyim)
- The city **where I was born** is beautiful.
  (Hansı şəhər? — Doğulduğum)

### Relative Pronoun-u ixtisar etmək

Relative pronoun **obyekt** rolundadırsa ixtisar edilə bilər:
- The book **(that) I read** was amazing. ✓ (I-nin oxuduğu kitab — "that" obyekt)
- The man **who called me** is here. ✗ (who-nu ixtisar etmək olmaz — subyektdir)

---

## Non-Defining Relative Clauses — Əlavə Məlumat

**Əlavə məlumat verir** — cümlə onsuz da mənasını qoruyur — **vergüllə** ayrılır.

- My brother, **who lives in London**, is a lawyer.
  (Qardaşım — yalnız bir qardaşım var, London məlumatı əlavədir)
- The Eiffel Tower, **which was built in 1889**, is in Paris.

> **Diqqət:** Non-defining-də **"that"** işlənmir!
> - ✗ My father, **that** is a doctor, lives here.
> - ✓ My father, **who** is a doctor, lives here.

---

## Fərq Cədvəli

| | Defining | Non-Defining |
|---|---|---|
| **Vergül** | Yoxdur | Var |
| **"That"** | İşlənə bilər | İşlənmir |
| **Atıla bilərmi?** | Budaq cümlə atılsa məna dəyişər | Atılsa məna qorunur |
| **Məqsəd** | Kim/nə olduğunu müəyyənləşdirir | Əlavə məlumat verir |

---

## Nümunə Dialoq

**A:** Have you met the new colleague who joined last week?
**B:** You mean Tom, who used to work in Berlin?
**A:** Yes! He's the one whose project won an award.
**B:** Right. The project that he presented was really impressive.
**A:** He's looking for a flat where he can work from home too.
**B:** I know a place where there's good internet and a quiet environment.

---

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Who, Which, Where, Whose — seçin
1. The film ___ we watched last night was excellent.
2. The woman ___ helped me was very kind.
3. The restaurant ___ we had dinner was expensive.
4. The student ___ phone rang had to leave.

**Cavablar:** 1-which/that, 2-who, 3-where, 4-whose

### 2. İki cümləni birləşdirin
1. She is a teacher. She teaches history. → She is a teacher **who teaches history**.
2. The book is on the table. I recommended it. → The book **that I recommended** is on the table.
3. Paris is a beautiful city. The Eiffel Tower is there. → Paris is a city **where the Eiffel Tower** is located.
