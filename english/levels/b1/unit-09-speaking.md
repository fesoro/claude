# B1 Unit 09: Danışıq Bacarıqları (Speaking Skills)

## Bu Vahiddə Nə Öyrənirsiniz?
- Fikir bildirmək və müzakirə etmək
- Razılaşmaq / razılaşmamaq
- Suallar vermək və cavab vermək
- Danışarkən vaxt qazanmaq (fillers)

---

## Fikir Bildirmək

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| I think / I believe | **I think** technology is important. |
| In my opinion / view | **In my opinion**, cities are too crowded. |
| As far as I'm concerned | **As far as I'm concerned**, it's a good idea. |
| It seems to me that | **It seems to me that** people work too much. |
| Personally, I... | **Personally, I** prefer living in the city. |

---

## Razılaşmaq

| Güclü razılıq | Zəif razılıq |
|---------------|-------------|
| Exactly! / Absolutely! | I suppose so. |
| I totally agree. | That's partly true. |
| You're absolutely right. | I see your point, but... |
| That's a good point. | To some extent, yes. |

---

## Razılaşmamaq (Nəzakətlə)

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| I'm not sure I agree. | I'm not sure I agree with that view. |
| I see it differently. | I see it slightly differently. |
| That may be true, but... | That may be true, but there's another side. |
| I take your point, however... | I take your point, however I think... |
| With respect, I disagree. | With respect, I disagree completely. |

---

## Vaxt Qazanmaq (Fillers)

Sual gəldikdə dərhal cavab verə bilmirsinizsə:

| İfadə | Nümunə |
|-------|--------|
| Well... | **Well**, that's an interesting question. |
| Let me think... | **Let me think** about that for a moment. |
| That's a good question. | **That's a good question**. I would say... |
| It depends... | **It depends** on the situation, really. |
| Actually... | **Actually**, I think the opposite is true. |
| To be honest... | **To be honest**, I've never thought about it. |

---

## Mövzuya Keçmək / Davam Etmək

| Məqsəd | İfadə |
|--------|-------|
| Yeni nöqtə əlavə etmək | Another thing is... / What's more... |
| Nümunə vermək | For example... / Take... for instance. |
| Nəticəyə gəlmək | So, to summarize... / Overall, I think... |
| Mövzu dəyişmək | Moving on to... / On a different note... |

---

## IELTS Speaking Part 1 — Nümunə

**Topic: Free Time**

**Q:** What do you enjoy doing in your free time?
**A:** Well, I enjoy a variety of activities. I'm quite keen on reading — mainly fiction and historical novels. I also love cycling at weekends when the weather is good. To be honest, I think having hobbies is really important for reducing stress.

**Q:** Do you think it's important to have hobbies?
**A:** Absolutely. In my opinion, hobbies help you relax and forget about work. They also help you meet new people with similar interests. For example, I've made several good friends through my cycling group.

## Məşq Tapşırıqları

### 1. Uyğun cavabı seçin
1. "I think social media is harmful for young people."
   a) "Exactly! I couldn't agree more."
   b) "I see it differently. I think it helps them connect."
   c) Both are valid responses.

2. "What's your opinion on working from home?"
   a) Start with a filler: "Well, it depends..."
   b) Give your view: "In my opinion..."
   c) Both are good strategies.

**Cavablar:** 1-c, 2-c

### 2. Sualları cavablandırın (1-2 cümlə)
1. What do you think is the most important invention?
2. Do you prefer living in a city or the countryside? Why?
3. How important is learning English in your country?
