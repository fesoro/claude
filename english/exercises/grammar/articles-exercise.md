---

## Exercise 3 — Short Paragraph

Bu abzasdakı boşluqlara a/an/the/— qoy:

> Yesterday, I visited (1)__ museum in (2)__ city centre. It was (3)__ fascinating place. I learned about (4)__ history of (5)__ city. (6)__ guide was very helpful. After (7)__ visit, I had (8)__ coffee at (9)__ café nearby. (10)__ coffee was excellent, so I ordered (11)__ second cup.

---

## Answers (Cavablar)

### Exercise 1:
1. an umbrella (ilk dəfə, qeyri-müəyyən)
2. an honest person (vowel sound)
3. The Nile / the longest river / the world
4. the piano (musiqi alətləri: the)
5. — work (institutional: no article)
6. a film / The film (ilk dəfə → ikinci dəfə known)
7. — Life (general abstract noun)
8. an engineer / a big company
9. — Mount Everest / the Himalayas (mountain range: the)
10. — breakfast / — school (institutional)
11. the salt (specific, on the table)
12. a degree / — economics (subject: no article)
13. — Dogs (general statement about all dogs)
14. The dog (specific dog)
15. the best / the team

### Exercise 2:
1. I have **a** headache.
2. She is **a** doctor.
3. **—** Gold is expensive.
4. I go to **—** bed at 10.
5. He plays **the** guitar.
6. **The** sun rises in the east.
7. It's **the** best movie I've seen. (superlative: the)
8. She has **—** brown hair.

### Exercise 3:
