# Conditionals Exercise (Şərt Cümlələri Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Identify the Type

Bu cümlələrin hansı şərt növü olduğunu müəyyən et (0, 1st, 2nd, 3rd, Mixed):

1. If you heat water to 100°C, it boils.
2. If it rains tomorrow, I'll take an umbrella.
3. If I won the lottery, I would travel the world.
4. If she had studied harder, she would have passed.
5. If I were you, I would apologise.
6. If he had saved money, he would be rich now.
7. If you freeze water, it becomes ice.
8. If they leave early, they'll catch the train.
9. If I lived in Paris, I would visit the Louvre every week.
10. If she hadn't missed the flight, she would be here now.

---

## Exercise 2 — Fill in the Blank

Doğru formu yazın:

1. If I __ (have) more time, I would learn a new language.
2. If it __ (not / rain) tomorrow, we'll go for a walk.
3. She __ (pass) the exam if she had revised more.
4. If water __ (reach) 0°C, it freezes.
5. If he __ (study) medicine, he would be a doctor now.
6. I __ (call) you if I hear any news.
7. If they __ (not / miss) the bus, they wouldn't be late.
8. She __ (be) healthier if she exercised regularly.
9. If I had known about the party, I __ (come).
10. If he __ (be) more careful, he wouldn't have had the accident.

---

## Exercise 3 — Error Correction

Xətanı tap:

1. If I will see her, I'll tell her.
2. If he studied, he would passed.
3. She would have come if she knew.
4. If I am you, I wouldn't do that.
5. If they had worked harder, they would be successful now. *(Bu düzgün ola bilər — yoxla)*
6. Unless he won't come, we'll cancel the event.
7. If she would have more money, she'd buy a new car.
8. I will help you if you would ask me.

---

## Exercise 4 — Sentence Completion

Öz sözlərinlə tamamla:

1. If I could live anywhere in the world, I would...
2. If I had more free time, I would...
3. If I had studied harder at school,...
4. My life would be different if...
5. If I could change one thing about myself,...

---

## Exercise 5 — Rewrite using Inversion (Formal)

"if" olmadan yenidən yaz:

1. If I had known, I would have helped.
2. If he were here, he would know what to do.
3. If this should happen, contact me immediately.
4. If she had arrived earlier, she would have met him.

---

## Answers

### Exercise 1:
1. Zero conditional
2. First conditional
3. Second conditional
4. Third conditional
5. Second conditional
6. Mixed conditional (3rd → 2nd: past cause, present result)
7. Zero conditional
8. First conditional
9. Second conditional
10. Mixed conditional (3rd → 2nd)

### Exercise 2:
1. had
2. doesn't rain
3. would have passed
4. reaches
5. had studied
6. will call / 'll call
7. hadn't missed
8. would be
9. would have come
10. had been

### Exercise 3:
1. If I **see** her (no future in if-clause)
2. he would **have passed** (3rd conditional)
3. if she **had known** (3rd conditional)
4. If I **were** you (2nd conditional — were, not am)
5. Correct — Mixed conditional
6. Unless he **comes** (unless = if not, no won't)
7. If she **had** more money (2nd conditional — no would in if-clause)
8. if you **ask** me (no would in if-clause)

### Exercise 5:
1. Had I known, I would have helped.
2. Were he here, he would know what to do.
3. Should this happen, contact me immediately.
4. Had she arrived earlier, she would have met him.
