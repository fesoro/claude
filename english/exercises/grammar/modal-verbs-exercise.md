# Modal Verbs Exercise (Modal Fellər Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Choose the Correct Modal

Uyğun modal feli seç:

1. You __ wear a seatbelt. It's the law. *(must / should / might)*
2. She __ speak three languages. *(can / must / should)*
3. It __ rain later — the sky looks dark. *(might / must / can)*
4. You __ smoke in this building. *(mustn't / don't have to / shouldn't)*
5. I __ finish this today — there's no deadline. *(don't have to / mustn't / can't)*
6. He __ be at home — his car is in the driveway. *(must / should / might)*
7. __ I open the window? It's hot in here. *(Could / Must / Should)*
8. You __ eat more vegetables. It's good for your health. *(should / must / can)*
9. She __ have forgotten — she never forgets anything. *(can't / mustn't / shouldn't)*
10. __ you help me carry this? *(Could / Must / Should)*

## Exercise 2 — Past Modal Forms

Doğru past modal formu yaz:

1. He didn't come. He __ (should / forget) the meeting.
2. She passed! She __ (must / study) very hard.
3. I __ (could / not / find) my keys this morning.
4. They __ (might / leave) already — the lights are off.
5. You __ (should / not / say) that. It was rude.
6. He looks upset. Something __ (must / happen).
7. She __ (could / become) a professional athlete — she was so talented.
8. I __ (need not / worry) — everything was fine in the end.

## Exercise 3 — Meaning Identification

Bu cümlələrdə modal feli nə ifadə edir? (ability / permission / obligation / prohibition / possibility / advice / deduction / past habit)

1. You must stop at a red light.
2. She could swim when she was four.
3. You can't park here.
4. He might be stuck in traffic.
5. You should get more sleep.
6. She must be tired — she's worked all day.
7. Can I use your phone?
8. He would always bring flowers when he visited.

## Exercise 4 — Error Correction

Xətanı tap:

1. She can to speak French.
2. You must to leave now.
3. He should studied more.
4. They might to arrive late.
5. She cans drive.
6. You mustn't to worry.
7. He could swam when he was young.
8. I should have went earlier.

## Exercise 5 — Rewrite with a Modal

Eyni mənayı modal feli ilə ifadə et:

1. "It's necessary for you to register." → You __ register.
2. "It's possible that she is busy." → She __ be busy.
3. "I'm sure he is at work." → He __ be at work.
4. "It's not allowed to use phones here." → You __ use phones here.
5. "It wasn't necessary to hurry." → You __ have hurried.
6. "I'm sure she didn't lie." → She __ have lied.
7. "It would be a good idea to apologise." → You __ apologise.

## Answers

### Exercise 1:
1. must (obligation/law)
2. can (ability)
3. might (possibility)
4. mustn't (prohibition)
5. don't have to (no obligation)
6. must (deduction)
7. Could (polite request)
8. should (advice)
9. can't (negative deduction)
10. Could (polite request)

### Exercise 2:
1. should have forgotten
2. must have studied
3. couldn't find
4. might have left
5. shouldn't have said
6. must have happened
7. could have become
8. needn't have worried

### Exercise 3:
1. obligation
2. ability (past)
3. prohibition
4. possibility
5. advice
6. deduction (present)
7. permission
8. past habit

### Exercise 4:
1. She **can** speak French. (no "to" after modal)
2. You **must** leave now. (no "to")
3. He should **have studied** more.
4. They **might** arrive late. (no "to")
5. She **can** drive. (no -s on modal)
6. You **mustn't** worry. (no "to")
7. He could **swim** when young. (base verb)
8. I should have **gone** earlier. (irregular past participle)

### Exercise 5:
1. must / have to
2. might / may / could
3. must
4. mustn't / can't
5. needn't / didn't need to
6. can't
7. should / ought to
