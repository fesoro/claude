# Passive Voice Exercise (Məchul Növ Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Active → Passive

Aktiv cümləni məchul növə çevir:

1. They build houses here every year.
2. Someone stole my wallet yesterday.
3. The company will announce the results tomorrow.
4. She has finished the report.
5. They were repairing the road when I drove past.
6. The government has introduced new regulations.
7. A famous architect designed this building.
8. They will have completed the project by December.
9. Someone needs to solve this problem.
10. People speak English in many countries.

---

## Exercise 2 — Passive → Active

Məchul cümləni aktiv növə çevir:

1. The book was written by Tolstoy.
2. The windows are cleaned every week.
3. A new law has been passed by the parliament.
4. The prize will be awarded by the committee.
5. The patients are being examined by the doctor.

---

## Exercise 3 — Fill in the Blank

Düzgün məchul formu yaz:

1. English __ (speak) all over the world.
2. The bridge __ (build) in 1890.
3. My car __ (steal) last night.
4. The results __ (announce) tomorrow.
5. The project __ (complete) by the time you return.
6. Several people __ (injure) in the accident.
7. The email __ (not / send) yet.
8. The new stadium __ (currently / build).
9. She __ (tell) the news an hour ago.
10. The documents __ (must / sign) before Friday.

---

## Exercise 4 — Causative Have / Get

"Have/Get something done" formasını tamamla:

1. I need to __ my car __ (service) — it hasn't been serviced in two years.
2. She __ her hair __ (cut) yesterday.
3. We're planning to __ the house __ (paint) next month.
4. He __ his watch __ (repair) at the jeweller's.
5. You should __ your eyes __ (test) once a year.

---

## Exercise 5 — Error Correction

Xətanı tap:

1. The letter was wrote by her assistant.
2. This car is been driven by my father.
3. A new hospital has been build in the city.
4. The thief was catched by the police.
5. The work should be did before Monday.
6. I was told to him by my manager.
7. The project was completed by them last week it. *(extra word)*
8. English is speak by millions of people.

---

## Exercise 6 — When to Use Passive

Hansı hallarda məchul növ daha uyğundur? Cümləni yenidən yaz:

1. Nobody knows who invented the wheel. → The wheel __.
2. They don't allow smoking in this area. → Smoking __.
3. Scientists have discovered a new species. → A new species __.
4. Somebody is following us. → We __.
5. They make these shoes in Italy. → These shoes __.

---

## Answers

### Exercise 1:
1. Houses are built here every year.
2. My wallet was stolen yesterday.
3. The results will be announced tomorrow.
4. The report has been finished.
5. The road was being repaired when I drove past.
6. New regulations have been introduced (by the government).
7. This building was designed by a famous architect.
8. The project will have been completed by December.
9. This problem needs to be solved.
10. English is spoken in many countries.

### Exercise 2:
1. Tolstoy wrote the book.
2. Someone cleans the windows every week.
3. The parliament has passed a new law.
4. The committee will award the prize.
5. The doctor is examining the patients.

### Exercise 3:
1. is spoken
2. was built
3. was stolen
4. will be announced
5. will have been completed
6. were injured
7. hasn't been sent
8. is currently being built
9. was told
10. must be signed

### Exercise 4:
1. have / serviced
2. had / cut
3. have / painted
4. had / repaired
5. have / tested

### Exercise 5:
1. was **written**
2. is **being** driven
3. has been **built**
4. was **caught**
5. should be **done**
6. I was told **by** my manager (not "to him")
7. The project was completed by them last week. (remove "it")
8. English is **spoken** by millions.

### Exercise 6:
1. The wheel was invented by nobody known. / The inventor of the wheel is unknown.
2. Smoking is not allowed in this area.
3. A new species has been discovered (by scientists).
4. We are being followed.
5. These shoes are made in Italy.
