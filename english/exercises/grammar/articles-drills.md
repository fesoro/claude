# Articles Drills — A/An/The Praktik Məşqləri

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

Articles (**a, an, the, zero article**) Azərbaycan danışanlar üçün **ən çətin** qrammatik mövzudur. Niyə? Çünki **Azərbaycan dilində article yoxdur**.

Bu faylda **100+ məşq** var — hər kateqoriya üçün cümlələr və cavablar. Nəzəri izahlar qısa saxlanıb, amma **çox praktika** var.

**Qayda:** Articles-ı **ayrı-ayrı oxumaq kifayət deyil** — onları konkret cümlələrdə təcrübə et.

---

## Əsas Qaydalar — Qısa Xülasə

### `a` / `an` (indefinite article — bilinməyən / ilk dəfə)
- **Tək hallarda** işlədilir
- İlk dəfə qeyd olanda
- `a` + samit: a book, a dog
- `an` + sait: an apple, an idea

### `the` (definite article — məlum olan)
- Oxucu/dinləyici hansını bilir
- İkinci dəfə qeyd olanda
- Unikal şeylər (the sun, the moon)
- Superlative (the best, the biggest)

### Zero Article (heç nə)
- Cəm isimlər ümumilikdə (Dogs bark.)
- Uncountable isimlər ümumilikdə (Water is liquid.)
- Xüsusi adlar (John, Baku, Google)
- Dil, idman adları (English, football)

## Drill 1 — A vs An (20 məşq)

Doğru variantı seç: **a** və ya **an**

1. I saw ___ elephant at the zoo.
2. She is ___ doctor.
3. He bought ___ new car.
4. We had ___ interesting conversation.
5. I need ___ umbrella.
6. That's ___ honest answer.
7. It was ___ hour ago.
8. She's ___ European citizen.
9. He's ___ engineer.
10. I ate ___ apple this morning.
11. He's ___ university student.
12. It's ___ easy question.
13. She wrote ___ article.
14. He made ___ MVP for the project.
15. I'll give you ___ example.
16. That's ___ unusual idea.
17. I have ___ idea.
18. He gave me ___ useful tip.
19. She's ___ one-year-old baby.
20. I saw ___ UFO yesterday.

### Cavablar:

1. **an** (elephant — sait)
2. **a** (doctor — samit)
3. **a** (new car — samit)
4. **an** (interesting — sait)
5. **an** (umbrella — sait)
6. **an** (honest — "h" səssiz, sait kimi başlayır)
7. **an** (hour — "h" səssiz)
8. **a** (European — "y" kimi başlayır, samit)
9. **an** (engineer — sait)
10. **an** (apple — sait)
11. **a** (university — "y" səsi, samit)
12. **an** (easy — sait)
13. **an** (article — sait)
14. **an** (MVP — "em" sait səsi ilə başlayır)
15. **an** (example — sait)
16. **an** (unusual — sait)
17. **an** (idea — sait)
18. **a** (useful — "y" səsi, samit)
19. **a** (one-year-old — "wan" samit)
20. **a** (UFO — "yu" səsi, samit)

### İzah: Vacib prinsip
**An/a** seçimi **yazılışa** deyil, **tələffüzə** əsaslanır.
- "university" /juːnɪvɜːsəti/ — y səsi → "a university"
- "honest" /ˈɒnɪst/ — h səssiz → "an honest"
- "MVP" — "em-vi-pi" — em sait ilə başlayır → "an MVP"

## Drill 2 — A/An vs The (20 məşq)

İlk boşluğa `a/an`, ikinciyə `the` qoy:

1. I bought ___ book yesterday. ___ book is very interesting.
2. She has ___ dog. ___ dog is friendly.
3. We saw ___ movie last night. ___ movie was terrible.
4. He told me ___ story. ___ story was about his childhood.
5. There's ___ man at the door. I don't know ___ man.
6. I met ___ woman at the cafe. ___ woman was very kind.
7. I need ___ new laptop. Is ___ laptop in this store good?
8. She has ___ idea. ___ idea is clever.
9. I live in ___ apartment. ___ apartment has two rooms.
10. We have ___ cat. ___ cat sleeps all day.

### Cavablar:

1. **a** book yesterday. **The** book is...
2. **a** dog. **The** dog is friendly.
3. **a** movie. **The** movie was terrible.
4. **a** story. **The** story was...
5. **a** man. I don't know **the** man.
6. **a** woman. **The** woman was...
7. **a** new laptop. Is **the** laptop in this store good?
8. **an** idea. **The** idea is clever.
9. **an** apartment. **The** apartment has...
10. **a** cat. **The** cat sleeps...

### İzah:
İlk dəfə qeyd = **a/an** (yeni məlumat)  
İkinci dəfə qeyd = **the** (oxucu bilir hansıdır)

## Drill 5 — Konkret Səhv-Yönəlmiş Məşqlər (15 məşq)

Bu cümlələrdə Azərbaycan danışanların sıx etdiyi səhvlər var. Düzəlt:

1. ❌ I go to the work at 9 am.
2. ❌ I am student.
3. ❌ I love music the classical.
4. ❌ She is engineer.
5. ❌ I took taxi to airport.
6. ❌ The people are friendly here.
7. ❌ I have dog.
8. ❌ I need the advice.
9. ❌ She plays the football.
10. ❌ I go to bed at 11 pm. (Bu cümləni yoxla — səhvdir?)
11. ❌ He speaks English and the French.
12. ❌ Tokyo is capital of Japan.
13. ❌ I went to the home.
14. ❌ He works at the Microsoft.
15. ❌ The breakfast is important meal.

### Cavablar:

1. ✅ I go to **—** work at 9 am. ("work" — funksiya, article yox)
2. ✅ I am **a** student. (tək isim → article lazım)
3. ✅ I love **—** classical music. (ümumi mövzu)
4. ✅ She is **an** engineer. (tək isim)
5. ✅ I took **a** taxi to **the** airport. (ilk qeyd / konkret)
6. ✅ **—** People are friendly here. ("people" ümumi)
7. ✅ I have **a** dog. (tək isim)
8. ✅ I need **—** advice. ("advice" uncountable)
9. ✅ She plays **—** football. (idman — zero article)
10. ✅ I go to **—** bed at 11 pm. (**düzgündür** — "to bed" = funksiya)
11. ✅ He speaks **—** English and **—** French. (dillər ümumi)
12. ✅ Tokyo is **the** capital of Japan. (unikal)
13. ✅ I went **—** home. ("home" — funksiya, article yox)
14. ✅ He works at **—** Microsoft. (şirkət adı — article yox)
15. ✅ **—** Breakfast is **an** important meal. (ümumi / ilk qeyd)

### İzah — "Functional" places
Bu sözlər, funksional mənada işlənəndə **article almır**:
- to work / at work
- to bed / in bed  
- to school / at school
- to church / at church
- to prison / in prison
- home / go home

4. ___ moon looks beautiful tonight.
5. Mt. Everest is ___ highest mountain in ___ world.
6. ___ internet has changed our lives.
7. ___ weather is nice today.
8. He's ___ only person who can help us.
9. ___ first time I flew was scary.
10. She lives on ___ fifth floor.

### Cavablar:

1. **The** earth revolves around **the** sun.
2. **the** best engineer.
3. **the** most difficult interview.
4. **The** moon looks beautiful.
5. **the** highest mountain in **the** world.
6. **The** internet has...
7. **The** weather is nice today.
8. **the** only person.
9. **The** first time I flew...

### İzah:

- Superlative → **the** (the best, the most)
- Ordinal (first, second...) → **the** (the first time, the fifth floor)
- Hava haqqında → **the** (the weather)

## Drill 11 — Müsahibə Cavabları (10 tam cümlə)

Bu cümlələr müsahibədə istifadə olunur. Articles-ı düzəlt:

1. "I am software engineer with 5 years of experience in backend development."
2. "Most important project I worked on was migration to microservices."
3. "I work in team of 8 developers at startup."

8. "I solved problem by adding proper locking mechanism."

### Cavablar:

1. "I am **a** software engineer with 5 years of experience in **—** backend development."

3. "I work in **a** team of 8 developers at **a** startup."
4. "I'm looking for **an** opportunity to work on **—** distributed systems."
5. "**The** best thing about my current job is **the** team."
6. "I have **a** strong background in **—** Python and **a** strong interest in **—** Go."
7. "**The** biggest challenge I faced was debugging **a** race condition in **—** production."
8. "I solved **the** problem by adding **a** proper locking mechanism."
9. "I would describe myself as **a** collaborative person."
10. "What attracted me to **—** this role is **the** opportunity to lead **—** projects."

### Cavablar və İzah:

1. **—** reading in **the** morning.  
   (həmin fel/hobbi ümumi; vaxt "in the morning" idiom)

2. **The** man I saw...  
   (konkret — "I saw" göstərir hansı)

3. **—** Men are from Mars.  
   (cəm ümumi)

4. **the** only one.  
   ("only" üçün "the" işləyir)

5. **an** old car.  
   ("old" sait ilə başlayır)

6. **an** hour late.  
   ("hour" h səssiz)

7. **The** world is changing.  
   (unikal — bir world)

8. **the** cinema.  
   (British idiom: "to the cinema" / "to the pictures")

9. **the** radio.  
   (idiom: "listen to the radio")

10. **the** piano.  
    (musiqi aləti + the)

11. **—** basketball.  
    (idman + zero)

12. **—** TV.  
    ("watch TV" — zero)

13. **—** church.  
    ("to church" — funksiya, ibadət üçün)

14. **—** hospital (British) / **the** hospital (American).  
    (British: funksiya; American: konkret bina)

15. **the** dentist.  
    ("to the dentist" — konkret mütəxəssis)

## Tez-tez Olan İdeal Qaydalar — Xülasə

### "The" Mütləq İşlədilən Hallar:
1. Unikal şeylər: the sun, the moon, the world, the internet
2. Superlatives: the best, the biggest, the first
3. Okean, dəniz, çay: the Atlantic, the Nile
4. Dağ silsilələri, ada qrupları: the Alps, the Bahamas
5. Qəzetlər: The Times, The Guardian
6. Musiqi alətləri: play the piano
7. "only" ilə: the only person

### "A/An" Mütləq İşlədilən Hallar:
1. İlk dəfə tək isim: I bought a car.
2. Peşə: She's a doctor. He's an engineer.
3. Ümumi sinif: A dog is a pet.
4. Bəzi müddət ifadələri: twice a week, once a month

### Zero Article Mütləq İşlədilən Hallar:
1. Xüsusi adlar: London, Google, John
2. Dil: English, Russian, Azerbaijani
3. İdman: football, chess
4. Yemək vaxtları: breakfast, lunch, dinner
5. Cəm ümumi: Cats are pets.
6. Uncountable ümumi: Water is liquid.
7. Funksiya yerləri: to work, to bed, to school
8. Aylar, günlər: in March, on Monday

## 5 Ən Çox Edilən Səhv

### ❌ Səhv 1: Peşə (article unutmaq)
- "I am engineer" → "I am **an** engineer"
- "She is doctor" → "She is **a** doctor"

### ❌ Səhv 2: "The music" ümumi mənada
- "I love the music" (ümumi) → "I love **—** music"
- "The music in this cafe" (konkret) ✅

### ❌ Səhv 3: Ölkə adları "the" olmaq
- "the Paris" → "**—** Paris"
- "the Germany" → "**—** Germany"

### ❌ Səhv 4: Uncountable + a/an
- "an information" → "**—** information"
- "an advice" → "**—** advice"

### ❌ Səhv 5: Funksiya yerləri
- "go to the work" → "go to **—** work"
- "in the bed" (yatanda) → "in **—** bed"
