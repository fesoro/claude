# Tenses Exercise (Zamanlar Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Fill in the Blank (Boşluğu doldur)

Düzgün zamanı seç:

1. She ________ (work) in a bank. She started 5 years ago and still works there.
2. I ________ (never / visit) New York. It's on my bucket list.
3. When I arrived, he ________ (already / leave).
4. Look at those clouds — it ________ (rain) soon.
5. She ________ (read) a book when I called her.
6. By 2030, scientists ________ (find) a cure for this disease.
7. I ________ (not / sleep) well lately. I'm exhausted.
8. Every morning, he ________ (drink) two cups of coffee.
9. They ________ (live) in that house for 20 years now.
10. I ________ (just / finish) my homework. I'm free now.

---

## Exercise 2 — Error Correction (Xətanı düzəlt)

Bu cümlələrdə bir xəta var. Tapın:

1. She is knowing the answer.
2. I have went to London last year.
3. When she will arrive, we will eat.
4. He has been working here since 3 years.
5. I didn't went to the party.
6. They are living in Baku for ten years.
7. She use to play tennis when she was young.
8. I am understanding English better now.
9. By the time he called, I have already left.
10. She will have finished by the time you arrived.

---

## Exercise 3 — Sentence Transformation

Cümləni verilmiş zamana çevir:

1. "I eat breakfast at 8." → Present Continuous (now)
2. "She goes to the gym." → Past Simple (yesterday)
3. "He watched TV." → Present Perfect
4. "They will arrive at 5." → Future Perfect (by 6)
5. "I study English." → Present Perfect Continuous (for 2 years)

---

## Answers (Cavablar)

### Exercise 1:
1. has worked / has been working
2. have never visited
3. had already left
4. is going to rain / will rain
5. was reading
6. will have found
7. haven't been sleeping
8. drinks
9. have been living / have lived
10. have just finished

### Exercise 2:
1. She **knows** the answer. (stative verb)
2. I **went** to London last year. (past time marker — not present perfect)
3. When she **arrives**, we will eat. (time clause — no future)
4. He has been working here **for** 3 years. (duration = for)
5. I didn't **go** to the party. (did + base verb)
6. They **have been living** in Baku for ten years. (still living = present perfect)
7. She **used** to play tennis. (used to — past form)
8. I **understand** English better now. (stative verb)
9. By the time he called, I **had already left**. (past perfect for earlier action)
10. She **will have finished** by the time you **arrive**. (future perfect)

### Exercise 3:
1. I am eating breakfast right now.
2. She went to the gym yesterday.
3. He has watched TV.
4. They will have arrived by 6.
5. I have been studying English for 2 years.
