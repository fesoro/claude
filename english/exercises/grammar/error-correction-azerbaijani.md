# Error Correction — Azərbaycan Danışanlar üçün

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

Azərbaycan dilini ana dil kimi bilən **bütün öyrənənlərin etdiyi ortaq 70+ səhv**. Hər səhv üçün:
- ❌ Yanlış forma (necə deyirik)
- ✅ Düzgün forma
- **İzah** (niyə)
- **Məşq cümləsi**

**Necə istifadə etmək:**
1. Əvvəl hər səhvi diqqətlə oxu
2. Öz ingiliscə yazdıqlarına bax — bu səhvlərdən hansını edirsən?
3. Hər gün 5 səhvə fokuslan, cümlə qur
4. Həftədə bir dəfə bütün səhvləri gözdən keçir

---

## KATEQORİYA 1: ARTİKLLƏR (The / a / an)

Azərbaycan dilində artikl yoxdur, ona görə ən çox səhv burada olur.

### 1. "The" unudulan
❌ "I am going to office."  
✅ "I am going to **the** office." *(konkret yer)*

❌ "I work in bank."  
✅ "I work in **a** bank." *(ilk dəfə qeyd)*

### 2. Zero artikl lazım olanda "the" qoymaq
❌ "I love the music." *(ümumi mənada)*  
✅ "I love music." *(musiqi ümumiyyətlə)*

❌ "The people are friendly here."  
✅ "People are friendly here." *(ümumi insanlar)*

### 3. Tək isim artiklsiz
❌ "I have car."  
✅ "I have **a** car."

❌ "He is engineer."  
✅ "He is **an** engineer."

### 4. Yerlər
❌ "I am at the home."  
✅ "I am at home." *(home, work, school — "the" yoxdur)*

❌ "I go to the work at 9."  
✅ "I go to work at 9."

**Qayda:** home, work, school, church, prison, bed + zero artikl (hərəkət / funksiya)

### Məşq:
1. I want to buy ___ new laptop.
2. I listen to ___ music every day.
3. She is ___ doctor.
4. Let's go to ___ cinema.
5. ___ sun is shining today.

**Cavablar:** 1. a, 2. — (yox), 3. a, 4. the, 5. The

## KATEQORİYA 2: ZAMAN (TENSES)

### 5. Present Perfect əvəzinə Past Simple
❌ "I work here **since** 2020."  
✅ "I **have worked** here **since** 2020."

❌ "I **live** in Baku **for** 10 years."  
✅ "I **have lived** in Baku for 10 years."

**Qayda:** "since / for" + indi davam edən hal → Present Perfect

### 6. Past Simple əvəzinə Present Perfect
❌ "I have been there yesterday."  
✅ "I **went** there yesterday." *(konkret keçmiş vaxt)*

❌ "I have seen him last week."  
✅ "I **saw** him last week."

**Qayda:** yesterday, last week, in 2020 = Past Simple

### 7. Present Continuous əvəzinə Present Simple (və ya əksi)
❌ "I am understanding you."  
✅ "I understand you." *(stative verbs — continuous istifadə etmirik)*

**Stative verbs:** know, understand, like, love, hate, want, need, believe, remember, own, belong, seem

### 8. "For" və "During" qarışdırmaq
❌ "I worked during 5 years."  
✅ "I worked **for** 5 years." *(müddət)*

❌ "I met her for my vacation."  
✅ "I met her **during** my vacation." *(dövr)*

**Qayda:** 
- **for** + müddət (rəqəmlə): for 3 hours, for 10 years
- **during** + dövr (hadisə): during the meeting, during summer

### 9. Past Simple zamanı "did" + V2 işlətmək
❌ "Did you went there?"  
✅ "Did you **go** there?" *("did" artıq past — fel V1 qalır)*

❌ "She didn't came."  
✅ "She didn't **come**."

### 10. Future üçün "will" vs "going to"
❌ "Look at the sky! It **will** rain."  
✅ "Look at the sky! It's **going to** rain." *(əlamət var)*

❌ "The phone is ringing. I **am going to** get it."  
✅ "The phone is ringing. I'**ll** get it." *(ani qərar)*

### Məşq:
1. I _____ (study) English for 3 years.
2. Yesterday, I _____ (see) a great movie.
3. I _____ (know) her since 2019.
4. While I _____ (walk), it started to rain.
5. _____ you _____ (finish) the report yet?

**Cavablar:** 1. have studied, 2. saw, 3. have known, 4. was walking, 5. Have ... finished

## KATEQORİYA 3: SUAL QURMAQ

### 11. Auxiliary unutmaq
❌ "You like pizza?"  
✅ "**Do** you like pizza?"

❌ "Where you live?"  
✅ "Where **do** you live?"

### 12. Do/does + be qarışdırmaq
❌ "Do you are ready?"  
✅ "**Are** you ready?" *(be + be, do lazım deyil)*

❌ "Does she is happy?"  
✅ "**Is** she happy?"

### 13. Dolayı sualda söz sırası
❌ "Could you tell me where is the bathroom?"  
✅ "Could you tell me where **the bathroom is**?" *(daxili sual = statement sırası)*

❌ "I don't know what does he want."  
✅ "I don't know what **he wants**."

## KATEQORİYA 4: PREPOSITIONS

### 14. "In" vs "on" vs "at"
❌ "I was born in 15 March."  
✅ "I was born **on** 15 March." *(konkret gün → on)*

❌ "The meeting is in 3 pm."  
✅ "The meeting is **at** 3 pm." *(konkret saat → at)*

❌ "I live on Baku."  
✅ "I live **in** Baku." *(şəhər → in)*

**Qayda:**
- **in** + ay, il, uzun dövr: in March, in 2026, in the morning
- **on** + gün, tarix: on Monday, on 15 March
- **at** + saat, konkret an: at 3 pm, at midnight, at the moment

### 15. "To" artıq lazımsız olanda
❌ "I will discuss about the problem."  
✅ "I will discuss the problem." *(discuss — direct object alır)*

**Discuss, enter, marry, reach** — "to/about" LAZIM DEYİL:
❌ "enter to the room" → ✅ "enter the room"
❌ "marry with her" → ✅ "marry her"
❌ "reach to the goal" → ✅ "reach the goal"

### 16. Fel + preposition
❌ "I am interested for music."  
✅ "I am interested **in** music."

❌ "She is good **on** math."  
✅ "She is good **at** math."

❌ "I depend of you."  
✅ "I depend **on** you."

### 17. "In" vs "into"
❌ "She went in the room."  
✅ "She went **into** the room." *(hərəkət)*

❌ "He is into the room."  
✅ "He is **in** the room." *(mövqe)*

### Məşq:
1. I usually wake up ___ 7 am.
2. My birthday is ___ June.
3. I live ___ a small apartment.
4. I'm interested ___ technology.
5. She is good ___ singing.

**Cavablar:** 1. at, 2. in, 3. in, 4. in, 5. at

## KATEQORİYA 5: COLLOCATIONS (Söz Birləşmələri)

### 18. "Make" vs "Do"
❌ "I do a mistake."  
✅ "I **make** a mistake."

❌ "I make homework."  
✅ "I **do** homework."

❌ "Take a decision."  
✅ "**Make** a decision." *(American English)*

### 19. "Say" vs "Tell"
❌ "Say me the truth."  
✅ "**Tell** me the truth." *(tell + şəxs)*

❌ "He told that he is tired."  
✅ "He **said** that he was tired." *(say + no person, tell + person)*

**Qayda:**
- **say** + sözlər: "He said, 'Hello.'"
- **tell** + şəxs + sözlər: "He told me a story."
- Xüsusi: tell a story, tell a lie, tell the truth, tell the time

### 20. "Have" collocations
❌ "I take a shower every morning."  
✅ Hər ikisi olur, amma daha təbii: "I **have** a shower" (British) / "take a shower" (American)

❌ "I have 25 years old."  
✅ "I **am** 25 years old." *(yaş — be)*

### Məşq:
1. He ___ a big mistake.
2. I ___ the truth.
3. We need to ___ a decision.
4. She ___ her homework every night.
5. I ___ 30 years old.

**Cavablar:** 1. made, 2. told, 3. make, 4. does, 5. am

## KATEQORİYA 6: FELİN FORMASI

### 21. "-ing" vs "-ed" sifətlər
❌ "I am boring."  *(= mən başqasını darıxdırıram!)*  
✅ "I am **bored**." *(mənim darıxdırılmışam)*

❌ "The movie is excited."  
✅ "The movie is **exciting**." *(film həyəcanlandırır)*

**Qayda:**
- **-ing** = başqasını təsir edən (interesting, tiring, boring)
- **-ed** = özün hiss edən (interested, tired, bored)

Tez-tez edilən:
- interesting / interested
- exciting / excited
- boring / bored
- tiring / tired
- confusing / confused
- surprising / surprised
- disappointing / disappointed
- relaxing / relaxed

### 22. "to" + bare infinitive
❌ "I want to going home."  
✅ "I want **to go** home."

❌ "I must to finish this."  
✅ "I must **finish** this." *(modal-dan sonra "to" yox)*

**Modals:** can, could, should, must, may, might, will, would + **V1** (to yox)

### 23. Gerund (-ing) vs Infinitive (to V)
❌ "I enjoy to play football."  
✅ "I enjoy **playing** football."

❌ "I decided going to the gym."  
✅ "I decided **to go** to the gym."

**Gerund sonra (V+ing):** enjoy, finish, stop, avoid, suggest, consider, mind, practice, keep

**Infinitive sonra (to + V):** want, decide, hope, plan, promise, agree, offer, need, learn, refuse

### 24. Two-verb səhvlər (if, when)
❌ "If I will see him, I will tell him."  
✅ "If I **see** him, I **will tell** him." *(if cümləsində will yox!)*

❌ "When I will finish, I will call you."  
✅ "When I **finish**, I will call you."

**Qayda:** **if / when / as soon as / until / before / after + present tense** (future cümlədə "will" işlənir)

### Məşq:
1. I am ___ (interest) in this topic.
2. The story was very ___ (bore).
3. I want ___ (go) home.
4. I enjoy ___ (read) books.
5. If it ___ (rain), we ___ (stay) home.

**Cavablar:** 1. interested, 2. boring, 3. to go, 4. reading, 5. rains, will stay

## KATEQORİYA 7: CÜMLƏ QURULUŞU

### 25. Subject-verb uzlaşma (3-cü şəxs -s)
❌ "She go to work."  
✅ "She **goes** to work."

❌ "He don't like it."  
✅ "He **doesn't** like it."

### 26. Çoxluq / Tək
❌ "I have many information."  
✅ "I have **a lot of / much** information." *(information uncountable!)*

**Uncountable nouns** (cəm yoxdur):
- information, advice, news, furniture, equipment, luggage, money, work, research, progress, traffic, weather, homework, feedback

❌ "I got many advices." → ✅ "I got a lot of **advice**."  
❌ "These are news." → ✅ "This is **news**."

### 27. "A lot of" vs "much/many"
| Forma | Countable | Uncountable | Neqativ/Sual |
|-------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| a lot of | ✅ | ✅ | - |
| many | ✅ | ❌ | usually ? |
| much | ❌ | ✅ | usually ? |
| few | ✅ | ❌ | - |
| little | ❌ | ✅ | - |

❌ "I have much books." → ✅ "I have **many** books." / "I have **a lot of** books."

### 28. Çift neqativ
❌ "I don't know nothing."  
✅ "I don't know **anything**." *(bir cümlədə bir neqativ)*

❌ "I haven't seen nobody."  
✅ "I haven't seen **anybody**."

### 29. İngiliscə ayrı söz sırası
❌ "I go always to the gym."  
✅ "I **always** go to the gym." *(adverb of frequency — main felin ƏVVƏLİ)*

**Qayda:** always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never → **be felindən sonra, main feldən əvvəl**
- ✅ "I **am always** happy."
- ✅ "I **always work** hard."

### Məşq:
1. He ___ (work) in Baku.
2. I have a lot of ___ (information / informations).
3. I ___ go to the gym on weekends. (always)
4. She ___ (not like) it.
5. I don't want ___ (nothing / anything).

**Cavablar:** 1. works, 2. information, 3. always, 4. doesn't like, 5. anything

## KATEQORİYA 8: WORD CHOICE (Söz Seçimi)

### 30. "Borrow" vs "Lend"
❌ "Can you borrow me $10?"  
✅ "Can you **lend** me $10?" / "Can I **borrow** $10 from you?"

- **lend** = başqasına vermək
- **borrow** = başqasından almaq

### 31. "Remember" vs "Remind"
❌ "Please remember me to call him."  
✅ "Please **remind** me to call him."

- **remember** = özün xatırlayırsan
- **remind** = başqasına xatırlatmaq

### 32. "Bring" vs "Take"
❌ "Take this to me." *(səhv kontekstdə)*  
✅ "Bring this to me."

- **bring** = burada gətir (danışanın tərəfinə)
- **take** = götürüb apar (danışandan uzağa)

### 33. "Fun" vs "Funny"
❌ "The party was very funny."  
✅ "The party was very **fun**." *(xoş)*

- **fun** = xoş, ləzzətli (zaman keçirilir)
- **funny** = gülüş doğuran (komik)

❌ "He is fun."  *(eyni mənada)*
✅ "He is **funny**." *(komikdir)*

### 34. "Economic" vs "Economical"
❌ "This car is economic."  
✅ "This car is **economical**." *(qənaətli)*

- **economic** = iqtisadi (iqtisadiyyat haqqında)
- **economical** = qənaətli

### 35. "Few" vs "A few"
❌ "I have few friends." *(= az dost, mənfi)*  
✅ "I have **a few** friends." *(= bir neçə, müsbət)*

- **few** = az (kifayət qədər deyil, mənfi ton)
- **a few** = bir neçə (müsbət ton)

### Məşq:
1. Can you ___ me your pen? I forgot mine.
2. Please ___ me about the meeting tomorrow.
3. The comedy was really ___.
4. We had ___ problems, but everything's fine now. (= bir neçə, müsbət)

**Cavablar:** 1. lend, 2. remind, 3. funny, 4. a few

## KATEQORİYA 9: TƏRZ (Translation Calques)

### 36. Birbaşa Azərbaycan tərcüməsi
❌ "I have 5 years experience of working." *(calque)*  
✅ "I have 5 years **of** experience working." / "I have 5 years' experience."

❌ "I do exam tomorrow."  
✅ "I **have/take** an exam tomorrow."

❌ "Open the television."  
✅ "**Turn on** the television." *(Azərbaycanca: aç, İngiliscə: turn on)*

❌ "Close the television."  
✅ "**Turn off** the television."

### 37. "Make a photo" — wrong
❌ "I made a photo of him."  
✅ "I **took** a photo of him."

### 38. "Drink a cigarette" (Azerbaycanca: siqaret çəkmək)
❌ "I drink cigarettes."  
✅ "I **smoke** cigarettes."

### 39. "Do a bath"
❌ "I do a bath every day."  
✅ "I **take/have** a bath every day."

### 40. Vaxt və maaş
❌ "I earn in a month 3000."  
✅ "I earn 3000 **per month**." / "I earn 3000 **a month**."

### 41. Gəlir vs Maaş
❌ "My salary is very good, it is 3000."  
✅ "My salary is **competitive** — it's 3000." / *(peşəkar daha az spesifik ol)*

### Məşq (tərcümə səhvləri):
1. Azərbaycanca "10 il təcrübəm var" nə kimi tərcümə olunur?
2. "Telefonu aç" ingiliscə necədir?
3. "Şəkil çək" ingiliscə?

**Cavablar:**
1. "I have 10 years of experience." (həmçinin: "I have been working for 10 years.")
2. "Turn on the phone." / "Pick up the phone" (zəng üçün)
3. "Take a photo." / "Take a picture."

## KATEQORİYA 10: BAĞLAYICILAR VƏ KONEKTORLAR

### 42. "But" iki dəfə
❌ "Although he was tired, but he kept working."  
✅ "Although he was tired, he kept working." *(although + but yoxdur)*  
✅ "He was tired, **but** he kept working."

### 43. "Because" cavab sualı kimi işlətmək
❌ "Why you are late? Because of traffic."  
✅ "Why are you late? **Because of** traffic." / "**Because** there was traffic."

- **because** + cümlə: Because I was tired.
- **because of** + isim: Because of the rain.

### 44. "So" sonunda
❌ "I didn't study so."  
✅ "I didn't study, so I failed." *(so — səbəb/nəticə bağlayıcısı, sonda olmur)*

## KATEQORİYA 11: İSTİNTAQ VƏ DƏYİŞƏN

### 45. Reflexive pronouns
❌ "I'm going to cut me."  
✅ "I'm going to cut **myself**." *(subject və object eyni şəxs)*

❌ "They enjoyed during the party."  
✅ "They enjoyed **themselves** during the party."

### 46. Possessive — "its" vs "it's"
❌ "The dog lost it's collar."  
✅ "The dog lost **its** collar." *(possessive — apostrof yox)*

- **its** = onun (possessive, apostrof yox)
- **it's** = it is / it has (apostrof var)

### 47. "Your" vs "You're"
❌ "Your welcome!"  
✅ "**You're** welcome!" *(you are)*

- **your** = sənin (possessive)
- **you're** = you are

### 48. "Their" vs "There" vs "They're"
- **their** = onların
- **there** = orada
- **they're** = they are

❌ "Their is a problem."  
✅ "**There** is a problem."

## KATEQORİYA 12: MODAL VERBS

### 49. "Must" past mənada
❌ "Yesterday I must go to the doctor."  
✅ "Yesterday I **had to** go to the doctor." *("must" past formu yox — "had to")*

### 50. "Could" past permission
❌ "When I was young, I can play football for hours."  
✅ "When I was young, I **could** play football for hours."

### 51. "Should have" regret
❌ "I should study yesterday."  
✅ "I **should have studied** yesterday." *(keçmiş peşmançılıq)*

### 52. "Can" vs "May"
❌ "Can I have a day off?" *(too informal in professional context)*  
✅ "**May I** have a day off?" / "**Could I** have a day off?" *(nəzakətli)*

### 53. "Money" uncountable
❌ "I have many moneys."  
✅ "I have **a lot of money**."

### 54. "Research" uncountable
❌ "I did many researches."  
✅ "I did **a lot of research**." / "I did **several research projects**."

### 55. "Advice" uncountable
❌ "Thanks for the advices."  
✅ "Thanks for the **advice**." / "Thanks for the piece**s** of advice."

### 56. "Progress" uncountable
❌ "I made many progresses."  
✅ "I made **a lot of progress**."

## KATEQORİYA 14: CÜZİ AMMA VACİB

### 57. "As well" vs "also"
❌ "I also want coffee too."  
✅ "I want coffee too." / "I also want coffee." *(ikisi birdən lazım deyil)*

### 58. "Near" vs "Near to"
❌ "I live near to the station."  
✅ "I live **near** the station." *(near preposition — to yoxdur)*

### 59. "Discuss" + about
❌ "We discussed about the project."  
✅ "We **discussed the project**."

### 60. "Mention" + about
❌ "She mentioned about the meeting."  
✅ "She **mentioned the meeting**."

### 61. "Meet" + with (bəzən)
❌ "I met with him yesterday." *(informal kontekstlərdə yanlış)*  
✅ "I **met** him yesterday." *(Native: "meet" + object, "with" ağırlıq verir)*

*Amma: "I had a meeting **with** him" — okay*

### 62. "Married to" not "married with"
❌ "She is married with John."  
✅ "She is married **to** John."

### 63. Time prepositions summary
- **at** night (BUT: **in** the morning/afternoon/evening)
- **on** the weekend (US) / **at** the weekend (UK)
- **in** time = vaxtında; **on** time = dəqiq vaxtda

## KATEQORİYA 15: FORMAL / INFORMAL

### 64. Email-də "Hi there"
❌ "Hi there, dear sir!" *(qarışıq ton)*  
✅ Formal: "Dear Mr. Smith," / İnformal: "Hi John,"

### 65. "Guys" professional kontekstdə
⚠️ "Hi guys" — OK team kontekstdə, AMMA formal müsahibədə "Hi everyone" daha təhlükəsizdir.

### 66. Softening (yumşaltma)
❌ "You are wrong."  
✅ "I **don't think** that's quite right." / "I **might** disagree."

## TƏCRÜBƏ TESTİ — Səhvləri Tap və Düzəlt

Hər cümlədə **1 səhv** var:

1. I have lived here since 10 years.
2. She go to work every day.
3. Did you went to the meeting?
4. I am agree with you.
5. Thanks for the advices.
6. I am interesting in music.
7. He don't like coffee.
8. I want to discussing about this.
9. She is married with a doctor.
10. Please remember me to call him.
11. I have much friends in Baku.
12. Where is the bathroom, can you tell me?
13. If I will see her, I will tell her.
14. I am boring in this meeting.
15. She earns 5000 in a month.

### Cavablar:
1. I have lived here **for 10 years**. *(since + point, for + duration)*
2. She **goes** to work every day. *(3rd person -s)*
3. Did you **go** to the meeting? *(did + V1)*
4. I **agree** with you. *(am yox)*
5. Thanks for the **advice**. *(uncountable)*
6. I am **interested** in music. *(-ed form)*
7. He **doesn't** like coffee. *(doesn't)*
8. I want to **discuss this**. *("discuss" + direct object, about yox)*
9. She is married **to** a doctor. *(married to)*
10. Please **remind** me to call him. *(remind)*
11. I have **a lot of / many** friends in Baku. *(many for countable)*
12. Can you tell me **where the bathroom is**? *(indirect question word order)*
13. If I **see** her, I will tell her. *(if + present, not will)*
14. I am **bored** in this meeting. *(-ed for feelings)*
15. She earns 5000 **per month / a month**. *("in a month" yox)*
