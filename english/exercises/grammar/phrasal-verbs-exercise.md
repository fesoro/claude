## Exercise 3 — Separable or Not?

Bu phrasal verblərin hansı ayrıla bilir (separable)? Cümləni yenidən yaz, əgər mümkündürsə:

1. She turned down the offer.
   → She turned __ offer down. / She turned it down.

2. He looked after the children.
   → He looked __ children after. *(possible?)*

3. They put off the meeting.
   → They put __ meeting off. / They put it off.

4. She came across an old photo.
   → She came __ old photo across. *(possible?)*

5. He gave up smoking.
   → He gave __ smoking up. *(possible?)*

6. Please fill in the form.
   → Please fill __ form in. / Please fill it in.

## Answers

### Exercise 1:

### Exercise 2:

2. look after

4. called off
5. gave away
6. set up
7. went through

### Exercise 3:
1. She turned the offer down. ✓ (separable)
2. NOT separable — "look after" stays together
3. They put the meeting off. ✓ (separable)
4. NOT separable — "come across" stays together
5. NOT usually separated with gerund — "give up smoking" stays
6. Please fill the form in. ✓ (separable)

### Exercise 4:
1. to
2. with
3. with
4. on
5. with
6. on
7. with / on
8. of

### Exercise 5:
1. She gave up **smoking**. (give up + gerund, not infinitive)
2. He looked after **the** baby. (no "to" after look after)
3. They called **the meeting** off. / They called it off. (pronoun between verb and particle)
4. She turned **the offer** down. / She turned **it** down.
5. He ran out **of** money.
6. She came across an interesting article. (no "to")
7. She took **her shoes** off. / She took **them** off. (not: put off shoes = postpone)
8. He turned down the job because **it** was too stressful.

### Exercise 6:
1. go out
2. broke up
3. fell out
4. made up
5. settle down
6. get back together
