# Reported Speech Exercise (Dolayı Nitq Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Direct → Reported Speech

Bu cümlələri dolayı nitqə çevir:

1. "I am tired," she said.
2. "We will call you tomorrow," they said.
3. "I have never been to Japan," he told me.
4. "She is working late tonight," he said.
5. "I don't like spicy food," she told him.
6. "We bought a new house last year," they said.
7. "I can swim," he said.
8. "They may cancel the event," she said.
9. "I was studying when you called," he said.
10. "We had already eaten before they arrived," she said.

---

## Exercise 2 — Reported Questions

Bu sualları dolayı nitqə çevir:

1. "Where do you live?" she asked me.
2. "Have you finished the report?" he asked her.
3. "What time does the meeting start?" she asked.
4. "Are you coming to the party?" he asked me.
5. "Why did you leave early?" the manager asked him.
6. "How long have you been working here?" she asked.
7. "Will you help me?" he asked her.
8. "Did you see the accident?" the police officer asked.

---

## Exercise 3 — Reported Commands & Requests

Bu əmr və xahişləri dolayı nitqə çevir:

1. "Close the door, please," she said to him.
2. "Don't be late!" the teacher told the students.
3. "Please send me the file," she asked him.
4. "Don't touch anything," the detective told everyone.
5. "Sit down and be quiet," he told the children.
6. "Please don't tell anyone," she asked me.

## Exercise 4 — Backshift (Zaman dəyişimi)

Hansı zaman dəyişimi baş verir? Cədvəli tamamla:

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|---------------|-----------------|
| "I **work** here." | She said she __ there. |
| "I **am working**." | He said he __ . |
| "I **worked** here." | She said she __ there. |
| "I **was working**." | He said he __ . |
| "I **have worked** here." | She said she __ there. |
| "I **will come**." | He said he __ . |
| "I **can help**." | She said she __ . |
| "I **may leave**." | He said he __ . |

## Exercise 5 — Reporting Verbs

"said" yerinə daha spesifik reporting verb işlət:
*(admitted, promised, suggested, warned, denied, insisted, complained, offered, refused, apologised for)*

1. "I'll definitely be there on time." → He __ to be there on time.
2. "I didn't steal the money." → She __ stealing the money.
3. "I'm sorry I was rude." → He __ being rude.
4. "Don't go near the edge — it's dangerous." → She __ them not to go near the edge.
5. "Why don't we try a different approach?" → He __ trying a different approach.
6. "Yes, I made a mistake." → She __ making a mistake.
7. "I will not change my mind." → He __ to change his mind.
8. "The service here is always slow." → She __ that the service was always slow.
9. "I can carry your bags for you." → He __ to carry her bags.
10. "It's absolutely necessary to finish today." → She __ that they finish that day.

## Exercise 6 — Error Correction

Xətanı tap:

1. She said me that she was tired.
2. He asked where did I live.
3. She told that she had finished.
4. He said he will come tomorrow.
5. She asked me that had I eaten.
6. He told to leave the room.
7. She said she can help us.
8. He asked what time is the meeting.

---

## Answers

### Exercise 1:
1. She said (that) she was tired.
2. They said (that) they would call me the next day.
3. He told me (that) he had never been to Japan.
4. He said (that) she was working late that night.
5. She told him (that) she didn't like spicy food.
6. They said (that) they had bought a new house the year before.
7. He said (that) he could swim.
8. She said (that) they might cancel the event.
9. He said (that) he had been studying when I called.
10. She said (that) they had already eaten before they arrived.

### Exercise 2:
1. She asked me where I lived.
2. He asked her if/whether she had finished the report.
3. She asked what time the meeting started.
4. He asked me if/whether I was coming to the party.
5. The manager asked him why he had left early.
6. She asked how long I had been working there.
7. He asked her if/whether she would help him.
8. The police officer asked if/whether I had seen the accident.

### Exercise 3:
1. She asked him to close the door.
2. The teacher told the students not to be late.
3. She asked him to send her the file.
4. The detective told everyone not to touch anything.
5. He told the children to sit down and be quiet.
6. She asked me not to tell anyone.

### Exercise 6:
1. She **told** me that she was tired. (tell + person / say + clause)
2. He asked where **I lived**. (no inversion in reported question)
3. She **said** that she had finished. (say that — no person object)
4. He said he **would** come the next day. (backshift: will → would)
5. She asked me **if/whether** I had eaten. (yes/no question: if/whether)
6. He told **them** to leave the room. (tell + person + to)
7. She said she **could** help us. (backshift: can → could)
8. He asked what time **the meeting was**. (no inversion)
