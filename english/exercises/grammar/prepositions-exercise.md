## Answers

### Exercise 1:
1. on (days: on Monday)
2. in (years: in 1995)
3. at (specific time: at 3)
4. on (floors: on the third floor)
5. at (arrival point: at the airport)
6. at (at night)
7. in (inside a place: in a hospital)
8. on (surface: on the table)
9. at a conference / in Paris / in June
10. in (in the morning)
11. since (point in time: since 2019)
12. at (at weekends)
13. at (at home)
14. at (at 7:45)
15. in (in the newspaper)

### Exercise 2:
1. at (good at)
2. in (interested in)
3. of (tired of)
4. at (arrive at)
5. to (married to)
6. for (applied for)
7. to (looking forward to)
8. about (worried about)
9. on (depends on)
10. for (apologised for)
11. with (agree with)
12. of (afraid of)
13. in (succeeded in)
14. of (proud of)
15. about (thinking about)

### Exercise 3:
1. since → **for** two hours (duration)
2. in → **at** the station
3. with → **to** (married to)
4. discussed **about** → discussed (no preposition)
5. entered **to** → entered (no preposition)
6. in → **at** (good at)
7. for → **in** (interested in)
8. to home → **home** (no preposition with home)
9. since 5 years → **for** 5 years
10. explained me → explained **to** me / told me

### Exercise 4:
1. across
2. up / down
3. into
4. through
5. along
6. out
7. towards / to
8. across
9. towards
10. up
