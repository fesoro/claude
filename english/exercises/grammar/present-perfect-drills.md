# Present Perfect Drills — Praktik Məşqlər

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

**Present Perfect** (have/has + V3) Azərbaycan danışanlar üçün **ən çətin** zamanlardan biridir. Niyə? Çünki Azərbaycan dilində "Mən yemişəm" cümləsi **Past Simple** kimi qəbul edilir, amma ingiliscə **Present Perfect** istifadə edilir.

Bu fayl sənə **90+ praktik məşq** ilə Present Perfect vs Past Simple fərqini **mükəmməl** öyrədir.

**Məqsəd:** 3 gün sonra bu ikisi arasında **avtomatik** seçim edə biləcəksən.

---

## Əsas Qaydalar — Qısa Xülasə

### Present Perfect (have/has + V3)
İstifadə edilir:
1. **İndiyə qədər tamamlanmış** hərəkətlər (konkret vaxt yoxdur)
2. **Müddət** indiyə qədər davam edir (since, for)
3. **Təcrübə** həyatda (ever, never)
4. **Hələ bitmədi** (just, already, yet)
5. **Nəticə indidir** (I've lost my keys = indi yoxdur)

### Past Simple (V2)
İstifadə edilir:
1. **Konkret keçmiş vaxt** (yesterday, last week, in 2020)
2. **Bitmiş hadisə** keçmişdə
3. **Ardıcıl hadisələr** hekayədə

---

## Açar Sözlər — Hansı Vaxt Hansını?

### Present Perfect Siqnalları:
- **ever** ("Have you **ever** been to...?")
- **never** ("I've **never** tried...")
- **just** ("I've **just** finished.")
- **already** ("She's **already** left.")
- **yet** ("Have you eaten **yet**?")
- **since** 2020 ("I've lived here **since** 2020.")
- **for** 5 years ("I've worked **for** 5 years.")
- **so far** ("We've finished 3 tasks **so far**.")
- **up to now**, **lately**, **recently**

### Past Simple Siqnalları:
- **yesterday**
- **last** week / year / Monday
- **ago** ("3 days **ago**")
- **in 2020, in March**
- **when** I was young
- **then, after that**

**Qayda:**
- **for** + müddət (3 hours, 5 years, a long time)
- **since** + vaxt nöqtəsi (2020, Monday, noon, I was a child)

**Hər ikisi Present Perfect ilə işlənir** (davam edən hallarda).

## Drill 3 — Təcrübə (Life Experience)

Present Perfect indiyə qədər həyat təcrübəsi üçün:

### "Ever" ilə suallar:
1. _____ you _____ (travel) to Europe?
2. _____ she _____ (meet) a celebrity?
3. _____ they _____ (try) Thai food?
4. _____ he _____ (give) a presentation?
5. _____ you _____ (fail) an exam?
6. _____ you _____ (lose) your wallet?
7. _____ she _____ (work) remotely?
8. _____ you _____ (break) a bone?
9. _____ they _____ (see) snow?
10. _____ he _____ (run) a marathon?

### Cavablar:
1. **Have you ever traveled** to Europe?
2. **Has she ever met** a celebrity?
3. **Have they ever tried** Thai food?
4. **Has he ever given** a presentation?
5. **Have you ever failed** an exam?
6. **Have you ever lost** your wallet?
7. **Has she ever worked** remotely?
8. **Have you ever broken** a bone?
9. **Have they ever seen** snow?
10. **Has he ever run** a marathon?

### Müsbət cavab + Past Simple (detallı)

Əgər "yes" deyirsənsə, detallar üçün **Past Simple** işlət:

> Q: "Have you ever traveled to Europe?"  
> A: "Yes, I **have**. I **went** to Italy in 2019. It **was** amazing."

### Məşq — Cavab yaz:
1. Have you ever worked with Python?  
2. Have you ever led a team?  
3. Have you ever failed a big project?  
4. Have you ever learned a new language?  
5. Have you ever quit a job?  

### Nümunə cavablar:
1. "Yes, I have. I've been using Python for about 4 years. I **started** when I was at university."
2. "Yes, I **led** a team of 3 engineers on my last project last year. It **was** challenging but rewarding."
3. "Unfortunately, yes. Two years ago, I **led** a project that **got** cancelled. I **learned** a lot from it."
4. "Yes, I have. I **started** learning English at school and still **work on** it every day."
5. "Yes, I **quit** my previous job in 2022. It **was** a difficult decision."

## Drill 4 — "Yet", "Already", "Just"

### Qaydalar:
- **yet** — sual və mənfi; sonda (Have you finished **yet**?)
- **already** — müsbət; have/has-dən sonra (I have **already** finished)
- **just** — yenidə; have/has-dən sonra (I have **just** arrived)

### Hər cümlə üçün "already", "yet", və "just"-dan birini qoy:

1. Have you seen the new movie _____?
2. I've _____ finished reading the report.
3. They haven't arrived _____.
4. She's _____ called me twice today.
5. We've _____ landed — the plane is still on the runway.
6. Have you had lunch _____?
7. I've _____ sent the email.
8. He hasn't called me back _____.
9. We've _____ started the project last week.
10. I've _____ woken up — give me 10 minutes.

### Cavablar:
1. Have you seen the new movie **yet**?
2. I've **already** finished reading the report.
3. They haven't arrived **yet**.
4. She's **already** called me twice today.
5. We've **just** landed — the plane is still on the runway.
6. Have you had lunch **yet**?
7. I've **already/just** sent the email. (ikisi də ola bilər)
8. He hasn't called me back **yet**.
9. We've **already** started the project last week.  
   **Düzəliş:** "last week" → Past Simple işlənməli: "We **started** the project last week."
10. I've **just** woken up.

## Drill 5 — Azərbaycan Danışanlar üçün Tipik Səhvlər

Aşağıdakı cümlələrdə qayda pozuntusu var. Düzəlt:

1. ❌ I work here since 2020.
2. ❌ She lives in Baku for 10 years.
3. ❌ I have seen him yesterday.
4. ❌ They have finished the project last week.
5. ❌ I know him for 5 years.
6. ❌ He has left home 2 hours ago.
7. ❌ Did you ever visit Paris?
8. ❌ I am working here since 2020.
9. ❌ When have you arrived?
10. ❌ She has been born in 1995.

### Cavablar:

1. ✅ I **have worked** here since 2020.  
   (since → Present Perfect)

2. ✅ She **has lived** in Baku for 10 years.

3. ✅ I **saw** him yesterday.  
   (yesterday → Past Simple)

4. ✅ They **finished** the project last week.  
   (last week → Past Simple)

5. ✅ I **have known** him for 5 years.

6. ✅ He **left** home 2 hours ago.  
   (ago → Past Simple)

7. ✅ **Have you ever visited** Paris?  
   (ever → Present Perfect)

8. ✅ I **have been working** here since 2020.  
   (Present Perfect Continuous daha təbii, amma Present Perfect də OK)

9. ✅ When **did** you **arrive**?  
   (konkret vaxt sualı → Past Simple)

10. ✅ She **was born** in 1995.  
    (doğum — həmişə Past Simple)

### Xüsusi qayda: "When" sualı
"When" sualları **adətən Past Simple**dir, çünki konkret vaxt soruşur:
- "When did you start?"
- "When did you leave?"

"When" Present Perfect ilə işləmir.

## Drill 8 — Müsahibə Sualları (Present Perfect pillələri)

Müsahibədə bu suallar tez-tez verilir. Doğru zamanla cavabla:

### Sual 1: "What have you been working on recently?"
**Cavab strukturu:** Present Perfect + detallar Past Simple-da

> "I **have been working** on a payment migration project for the past 3 months. Last week, we **shipped** phase 1, and this week we **started** phase 2."

### Sual 2: "Have you ever led a team?"
**Cavab:** Present Perfect + misal Past Simple

> "Yes, I have. In my last job, I **led** a team of 4 engineers on a major refactor. We **delivered** it on schedule."

### Sual 3: "How long have you been with your current company?"
**Cavab:** Present Perfect

> "I **have been** with them for 2 years. I **joined** in April 2024."

### Sual 4: "What's the most interesting project you've worked on?"
**Cavab:** Present Perfect + Past Simple

> "Probably the API redesign I **worked on** last year. We **had** some really interesting challenges around backward compatibility."

### Sual 5: "Have you worked with [X technology]?"
**Cavab:** Present Perfect / Past Simple

> "Yes, I **have**. I **used** it extensively on my last project. / I **have been using** it for 2 years."

### Məşq — Öz sualını cavabla (yaz və yüksək səslə de):
1. What have you been working on lately?
2. Have you ever managed a difficult stakeholder?
3. How long have you worked in this industry?
4. Have you ever had to make a tough decision?
5. What have you learned about yourself recently?

## Drill 10 — Present Perfect Continuous (bonus)

**Present Perfect** + **Continuous** = have been + V-ing  
Məsələn: "I **have been working**."

### İstifadə:
- Vəziyyət davam edir (still going)
- Müddətə fokus, hərəkət prosesinə
- "Recently, lately" ilə sıx işlənir

### Fərqlər:
| Present Perfect | Present Perfect Continuous |
|-----------------|-----------------------------|
| "I've written 3 emails." (nəticə) | "I've been writing emails all morning." (proses) |
| "She's lived here since 2020." (state) | "She's been working at a new job." (activity) |
| "I've known him for 10 years." (stative) | ❌ (stative verblər continuous-da olmaz) |

### Drill — Doğru formanı seç:

1. I _____ (work) on this bug for 3 hours.
2. She _____ (write) the report since morning.
3. We _____ (know) each other since 2015.
4. He _____ (learn) Python for 6 months.
5. They _____ (live) in Istanbul for 10 years.

### Cavablar:
1. **have been working** (aktivlik davam edir — continuous)
2. **has been writing** (aktivlik davam edir)
3. **have known** (know = stative, continuous yox)
4. **has been learning** (aktivlik)
5. **have lived** / **have been living** (ikisi də OK — state of being)

### Qayda:
- **Stative verblər** (know, have, love, want, believe) — **yalnız** Present Perfect, Continuous işləmir
- **Action verblər** (work, write, learn) — ikisi də ola bilər

## Drill 14 — Müsahibə Nümunələri (Tam Cavablar)

Aşağıdakı müsahibə suallarına cavab yaz, doğru zamanları istifadə et:

### Sual 1: "Tell me about your experience with Python."
**Sample answer:**
> "I **have been using** Python for about 4 years now. I **started** in 2021 when I **joined** my first company. Since then, I **have built** various applications — REST APIs, data pipelines, scripts. Most recently, I **have been working** on a microservices project using FastAPI."

### Sual 2: "What have you learned in your current job?"
**Sample answer:**
> "A lot, honestly. I **have learned** how to work in a large team — previously, I **was** in smaller teams. I **have also developed** my code review skills. Also, I **have gotten** much better at writing documentation."

### Sual 3: "Have you ever worked in a remote team?"
**Sample answer:**
> "Yes, I have. I **have been working** remotely for the past 2 years. Before that, I **worked** in an office setting. Remote work **has taught** me a lot about async communication."

### Sual 4: "What's your biggest achievement so far?"
**Sample answer:**
> "Probably the migration project I **led** last year. We **migrated** a monolithic app to microservices over 6 months. It **was** challenging, but we **shipped** it successfully. Since then, the team **has been able** to deploy much faster."

### Sual 5: "How has your approach to problem-solving changed over the years?"
**Sample answer:**
> "A lot. Early in my career, I **used to jump** into code immediately. Over time, I **have learned** the value of thinking first. Now, I **always spend** time understanding the problem. This **has saved** me countless hours."

## Tez-tez Edilən Səhvlər (Xüsusi Diqqət)

### Səhv 1: "Since" + Past Simple
- ❌ "I live here since 2020."
- ✅ "I **have lived** here since 2020."

### Səhv 2: "For" + Past Simple (davam edənlər üçün)
- ❌ "I work here for 5 years."
- ✅ "I **have worked** here for 5 years."

### Səhv 3: Konkret vaxtla Present Perfect
- ❌ "I have seen him yesterday."
- ✅ "I **saw** him yesterday."

### Səhv 4: "Ago" + Present Perfect
- ❌ "I have done this 2 hours ago."
- ✅ "I **did** this 2 hours ago."

### Səhv 5: Keçmişə aid konkret hadisə
- ❌ "I have been born in 1998."
- ✅ "I **was born** in 1998."

### Səhv 6: "When" sualları
- ❌ "When have you arrived?"
- ✅ "When **did** you arrive?"

Bu 20 cümlədə hansılar **Present Perfect**, hansılar **Past Simple**?

1. ___ you ever ___ (be) to Paris?
2. I ___ (see) him 10 minutes ago.
3. She ___ (live) in London since 2020.
4. We ___ (meet) at university in 2018.
5. He ___ (just, finish) his homework.
6. I ___ (not, eat) yet.
7. They ___ (travel) to Japan last year.
8. She ___ (work) here for 3 years.
9. I ___ (be) to India twice.
10. He ___ (write) three books already.
11. When ___ you ___ (start) your career?
12. I ___ (know) her since high school.
13. We ___ (have) dinner at 7 pm yesterday.
14. She ___ (not, call) me this morning.
15. ___ you ___ (finish) the report?
16. He ___ (graduate) from MIT in 2017.
17. I ___ (read) that book several times.
18. We ___ (move) to Berlin 5 years ago.
19. She ___ (lose) her keys — she can't find them.
20. They ___ (play) football for 2 hours yesterday.

### Cavablar:

1. **Have** you ever **been** (Present Perfect)
2. **saw** (Past Simple, ago)
3. **has lived** (PP, since)
4. **met** (PS, in 2018)
5. **has just finished** (PP, just)
6. **haven't eaten** (PP, yet)
7. **traveled** (PS, last year)
8. **has worked** (PP, for)
9. **have been** (PP, twice = təcrübə)
10. **has written** (PP, already)
11. **did** you **start** (PS, when)
12. **have known** (PP, since)
13. **had** (PS, at 7 pm yesterday)
14. **hasn't called** (PP, this morning — hələ morning-dir)
15. **Have** you **finished** (PP — sual)
16. **graduated** (PS, in 2017)
17. **have read** (PP, several times = təcrübə)
18. **moved** (PS, 5 years ago)
19. **has lost** (PP, nəticə indi — açar yoxdur)
20. **played** (PS, yesterday)

### Xal:
- 18-20 düz: **Present Perfect mükəmməlsən**
- 15-17 düz: **Yaxşı — kiçik boşluqlar**
- 10-14 düz: **Daha çox məşq lazımdır**
