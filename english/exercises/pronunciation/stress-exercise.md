1. present (isim: hədiyyə)

## Exercise 5 — Meaning Changes with Stress

Bu cümlədə 6 fərqli söz vurğulana bilər. Hər variant üçün Azərbaycanca mənanı yaz:

**"I didn't say she took the money."**

1. **I** didn't say she took the money. →
2. I **didn't** say she took the money. →
3. I didn't **say** she took the money. →
4. I didn't say **she** took the money. →
5. I didn't say she **took** the money. →
6. I didn't say she took **the money**. →

## Exercise 6 — Weak Forms in Connected Speech

Bu cümlələrdəki altıçizili sözlər güclü (strong) oxunurmu, yoxsa zəif (weak)? İşarələ:

1. "I'm going **to** the shop." — to: strong / weak
2. "I want **to** go." — to: strong / weak
3. "She works **for** a big company." — for: strong / weak
4. "What did you do it **for**?" — for: strong / weak
5. "He **can** swim." — can: strong / weak
6. "**Can** he swim?" — can: strong / weak

> **Qayda:** Cümlə sonunda, vurğulanan mövqedə, ya da tənha olanda → strong form.

1. **I** didn't — Başqası deyib, mən yox

5. I didn't say she **took** — Başqa bir şey etdi (aldı, götürdü deyil)
6. I didn't say she took **the money** — Başqa bir şeyi götürdü
