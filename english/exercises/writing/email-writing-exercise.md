# Email Writing Exercise (Email Yazma Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Formal or Informal?

Bu ifadələri Formal (F) ya da Informal (I) kimi təsnif et:

1. "I am writing to enquire about..." ( )
2. "Just wanted to check in about..." ( )
3. "Thanks so much for your help!" ( )
4. "I would be grateful if you could..." ( )
5. "Hey, can you send me that file?" ( )
6. "Please find attached the document." ( )
7. "I'm afraid I am unable to attend." ( )
8. "No worries, I'll sort it out." ( )
9. "I look forward to hearing from you." ( )
10. "Write back soon!" ( )

## Exercise 2 — Match Opening to Situation

Bu açılış cümlələrini uyğun vəziyyətlə uyğunlaşdır:

**Opening lines:**
1. "I hope this email finds you well."
2. "It was great to catch up with you last week!"
3. "I am writing in response to your job advertisement."
4. "I am writing to complain about..."
5. "Just a quick note to say thank you for..."
6. "Further to our telephone conversation this morning..."
7. "I'm so sorry I haven't been in touch for ages!"
8. "I am writing to request a refund for..."

**Situations:**
a. İş elanına müraciət
b. Uzun müddətdən sonra dosta yazmaq
c. Telefon söhbətinin davamı
d. Rəsmi email açılışı
e. Şikayət emaili
b. Yaxın dostla görüşdən sonra
g. Pulun geri qaytarılması tələbi

## Exercise 3 — Rewrite Formally

Bu qeyri-rəsmi cümlələri rəsmi dildə yenidən yaz:

1. "I want the money back." →
2. "You made a mistake in my bill." →
3. "Can you help me?" →
4. "I can't come to the meeting." →
5. "Send me more info about this." →
6. "I got your email." →
7. "Sorry for being late with this." →
8. "I think this is a bad idea." →

## Exercise 4 — Fill in the Gaps

Bu rəsmi emaildəki boşluqları doldur:
*(I am writing to / Please find / I would be grateful / I look forward / should you require / Kind regards)*

> **Subject:** Enquiry Regarding Training Course
>
> Dear Ms Thompson,
>
> (1)__ enquire about the Advanced Business English course advertised on your website.
>
> I am particularly interested in the intensive weekend programme. (2)__ if you could provide further details regarding the course dates, fees, and any prerequisites.
>
> (3)__ attached my CV for your reference.
>
> (4)__ to hearing from you at your earliest convenience. (5)__ any additional information, please do not hesitate to contact me.
>
> (6)__,
> David Chen

## Exercise 5 — Error Correction

Bu emaildeki 8 xətanı tap:

> Dear Mr. Smith
>
> I am write to you regarding the order I placed on 5th April. I am afraid that the product I received was damaged on arrival. The box was clearly broke and several items inside was missing.
>
> I would grateful if you could either send a replacement or provide a full refund. I have been a customer of your company since many years and have always been satisfy with your service until now.
>
> I look forward to hear from you.
>
> Your faithfully,
> Sarah Jones

## Exercise 6 — Write the Email

Bu vəziyyətlər üçün tam email yaz:

**Task A — Formal complaint:**
Siz onlayn mağazadan ayaqqabı sifariş etdiniz. Səhv ölçü gəldi. Emaildə: problemi izah et, həll tələb et, nəzakətli ol.

**Task B — Job application:**
Bir şirkətin vebsaytında marketing assistant vəzifəsi üçün elan gördünüz. Ərizə emaili yaz. Daxil et: niyə müraciət etdin, hansı keyfiyyətlərini var, CV əlavə etdiyini bildir.

**Task C — Informal — catch up:**
Uzun müddət görüşmədiyiniz bir dosta email yaz. Öz xəbərlərini danış, ondan xəbər al, görüşmək təklif et.

## Answers

### Exercise 3 (sample answers):
1. I would like to request a refund.
2. I believe there is an error on my invoice.
3. I would be grateful if you could assist me.
4. I am afraid I am unable to attend the meeting.
5. I would appreciate it if you could provide further information.
6. Thank you for your email.
7. I apologise for the delay in responding.
8. I have some concerns regarding this proposal.

### Exercise 4:
1. I am writing to
2. I would be grateful
3. Please find
4. I look forward
5. Should you require
6. Kind regards

### Exercise 5:
1. "Dear Mr. Smith" → **Dear Mr Smith,** (no period after title; comma after name)
2. "I am write" → **I am writing**
3. "was clearly broke" → **was clearly broken**
4. "several items inside was" → **were** missing
5. "I would grateful" → **I would be grateful**
6. "since many years" → **for many years**
7. "satisfy" → **satisfied**
8. "look forward to hear" → **look forward to hearing**
9. "Your faithfully" → **Yours faithfully** (when you don't know the name: Yours faithfully; when you do: Yours sincerely)
