# Collocations Exercise (Birlikdə İşlənən Sözlər Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Verb + Noun Collocations

Düzgün feli seç *(make / do / take / have / get / give)*:

1. __ a decision
2. __ homework
3. __ a mistake
4. __ a shower
5. __ a suggestion
6. __ a break
7. __ married
8. __ a phone call
9. __ exercise
10. __ a headache
11. __ a speech
12. __ an exam
13. __ a risk
14. __ someone a favour
15. __ progress

---

## Exercise 2 — Adjective + Noun Collocations

Uyğun sifəti seç *(strong / heavy / deep / high / close / bright / hard / long / wide / common)*:

1. __ coffee (qüvvətli qəhvə)
2. __ rain (güclü yağış)
3. __ future (parlaq gələcək)
4. __ friend (yaxın dost)
5. __ pressure (yüksək təzyiq)
6. __ sleep (dərin yuxu)
7. __ sense (adi düşüncə)
8. __ work (çətin iş)
9. __ term (uzun müddət)
10. __ variety (geniş çeşid)

---

## Exercise 3 — Wrong Collocation

Hansı söz bu kollokasiyaya uyğun deyil? Düzəlt:

1. do a mistake → __ a mistake
2. make homework → __ homework
3. take a decision → __ a decision
4. heavy snow ✓ / heavy sunshine → __ sunshine
5. strong tea ✓ / strong rain → __ rain
6. do a speech → __ a speech
7. get married ✓ / get a mistake → __ a mistake
8. have a risk → __ a risk

---

## Exercise 4 — Adverb + Adjective Collocations

Boşluğu doldur *(deeply, fully, highly, strongly, widely, bitterly, desperately, perfectly)*:

1. The new product is __ recommended by experts.
2. She was __ aware of the risks.
3. He was __ disappointed by the results.
4. The theory is __ accepted in scientific circles.
5. I __ disagree with that statement.
6. She is __ qualified for the position.
7. He was __ in need of help.
8. The instructions were __ clear.

---

## Exercise 5 — Business Collocations

Doğru söz birləşməsini tamamla:

1. __ a meeting (keçirmək: hold / do / make)
2. __ a contract (imzalamaq: write / sign / make)
3. __ a profit (əldə etmək: do / make / take)
4. __ a deadline (çatmaq: meet / do / hit)
5. __ a budget (hazırlamaq: set / put / make)
6. __ a deal (bağlamaq: close / make / do)
7. __ a project (idarə etmək: manage / do / make)
8. __ a presentation (vermək: give / do / make)
