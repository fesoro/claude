# Word Formation Exercise (Söz Yaratma Məşqi)

---

## Exercise 1 — Form the correct word

Mötərizədəki sözün düzgün formasını cümlədə işlət:

1. The ________ of the new policy was controversial. (INTRODUCE)
2. She is very ________ in her work. (CREATE)
3. We need to ________ our plans before the meeting. (FINAL)
4. The project was completed ________ on time. (SUCCESS)
5. There was a significant ________ in sales last year. (IMPROVE)
6. He showed great ________ in dealing with the crisis. (DETERMINE)
7. The new law was met with widespread ________. (OPPOSE)
8. She spoke ________ about her experience. (HONEST)
9. The ________ of the experiment surprised everyone. (CONCLUDE)
10. They made an ________ effort to finish on time. (REMARK)

## Exercise 3 — Suffixes: -ful / -less

Bu sözləri -ful (ilə dolu) ya da -less (olmadan) ilə tamamla:

1. hope + ? → The situation seemed ________. (without hope)
2. care + ? → Please be ________ with that glass! (with care)
3. use + ? → This old machine is completely ________. (without use)
4. power + ? → She gave a ________ speech. (full of power)
5. harm + ? → The medication is completely ________. (without harm)
6. thought + ? → That was such a ________ gift. (full of thought)
7. meaning + ? → His reply was ________. (without meaning)
8. wonder + ? → We had a ________ time. (full of wonder)
