# A2 Reading Comprehension — A2 Səviyyə Oxu Məşqləri

## Bu Fayl Haqqında

6 qısa mətn (hər biri 100-150 söz) + 5-6 anlama sualı + cavablar. Məqsəd: A2 səviyyəsində oxu sürətini və anlama bacarığını artırmaq.

**Necə işləmək:**
1. Mətni oxu (lüğətsiz — ilk oxuda)
2. Anlamadığın sözləri ÇƏKİNMƏ — kontekstdən təxmin et
3. Sualları cavablandır
4. Yalnız sonda lüğətə bax
5. Cavabları yoxla
6. Mətni yenidən yüksək səslə oxu

---

## Text 1: My New Job

I started a new job last month. I work at a software company in Baku. I am a junior developer and I write code in Python. I work from 9 am to 6 pm, Monday to Friday. My office is on the fifth floor. There are 20 people on my team, and my manager is very kind.

Every morning, I take the bus to work. It takes about 30 minutes. I always buy a coffee before I start work. At lunch time, I eat with my colleagues in the kitchen. Sometimes we go to a small restaurant near the office.

The job is interesting, but sometimes it is difficult. I learn new things every day. I am happy because I like my team and my work.

### Questions
1. What does the writer do?
2. How many people work on the team?
3. How does the writer get to work?
4. Where does the writer eat lunch?
5. Does the writer like the job? Why or why not?

### Answers
1. The writer is a junior developer (writes code in Python).
2. 20 people.
3. By bus (takes about 30 minutes).
4. In the kitchen with colleagues, or sometimes at a small restaurant near the office.
5. Yes, because he likes his team and his work. The job is interesting, though sometimes difficult.

### Key Vocabulary
- junior developer = təcrübəsiz proqramçı
- colleagues = iş yoldaşları
- take the bus = avtobusla getmək
- lunch time = nahar vaxtı

## Text 2: A Busy Weekend

Last Saturday, my family and I had a very busy day. We got up early at 7 am. My mother made breakfast for everyone. We had eggs, bread, and tea. After breakfast, we went to the supermarket. We bought food for the week.

In the afternoon, my brother came to visit. He lives in another city, so we don't see him often. We cooked lunch together. My brother makes very good kebab. After lunch, we watched a movie. It was a comedy — we laughed a lot.

In the evening, my friends came over. We played cards and talked about work. I was tired, but it was a great day. I love weekends when my family is all together.

### Questions
1. What time did the family wake up?
2. What did they have for breakfast?
3. Where did the brother live?
4. What kind of movie did they watch?
5. How did the writer feel at the end of the day?

### Answers
1. 7 am.
2. Eggs, bread, and tea.
3. In another city.
4. A comedy.
5. Tired but happy — "it was a great day."

### Key Vocabulary
- got up = oyandı, qalxdı
- supermarket = supermarket
- make good kebab = yaxşı kabab bişirmək
- come over = gəlmək (qonaq olaraq)

## Text 3: My Phone Problem

Yesterday morning, I had a problem with my phone. It didn't turn on. I pressed the button many times, but nothing happened. I was worried because I needed my phone for work.

I took the bus to a phone shop in the city center. The shop was busy, and I waited for 30 minutes. When it was my turn, the man looked at my phone. He said, "It's the battery. You need a new battery."

The new battery cost 40 manats. It was expensive, but I paid. The man changed the battery in 15 minutes. Now my phone works well. I was happy, but next time I will buy a cheaper phone!

### Questions
1. What was wrong with the phone?
2. How long did the writer wait at the shop?
3. How much did the new battery cost?
4. How long did it take to change the battery?
5. How did the writer feel at the end?

### Answers
1. It didn't turn on. (The battery was the problem.)
2. 30 minutes.
3. 40 manats.
4. 15 minutes.
5. Happy (but thinking about buying a cheaper phone next time).

### Key Vocabulary
- turn on = yandırmaq, işə salmaq
- press the button = düyməni basmaq
- battery = batareya
- cost = dəyər təşkil etmək (cost, cost, cost — irregular)

## Text 4: Learning English

My name is Leyla. I am 25 years old. I am learning English because I want to work at an international company. I started two years ago, and I still study every day.

I use many different ways to learn. In the morning, I listen to podcasts on the bus. I use an app on my phone to learn new words. I read articles in English every evening. At the weekend, I watch American TV shows with English subtitles.

The hardest thing for me is speaking. I know many words, but when I need to speak, I forget them. So I started talking to myself in English. I also have a language partner online. We talk for one hour every Friday.

Learning English is not easy, but I don't give up. I know it will help me in my life and my career.

### Questions
1. Why is Leyla learning English?
2. How long has she studied?
3. What does she do on the bus?
4. What is the most difficult skill for her?
5. How does she practice speaking?

### Answers
1. She wants to work at an international company.
2. Two years.
3. She listens to podcasts.
4. Speaking.
5. She talks to herself in English and has a language partner online (one hour every Friday).

### Key Vocabulary
- international company = beynəlxalq şirkət
- subtitles = altyazılar
- give up = imtina etmək
- language partner = dil partnyoru

## Text 5: My Office

I work in a small office in the center of Baku. There are about 30 people in my company. My desk is near the window, so I have a nice view of the city. I share my desk with a colleague named Nigar. She is very friendly.

Next to my desk, there is a coffee machine. I drink three cups of coffee every day! In the corner, there is a meeting room with a big table. We have team meetings every Monday at 10 am.

The kitchen is on the second floor. We eat lunch there. The company gives us free fruit and tea. On Fridays, sometimes my boss orders pizza for everyone.

I like my office because the people are nice and the work is interesting. But I want to work from home more often. Next week, I will ask my manager.

### Questions
1. How many people work at the company?
2. Who shares the desk with the writer?
3. How many coffees does the writer drink every day?
4. What day are team meetings?
5. What does the writer want to do?

### Answers
1. About 30 people.
2. Nigar (a friendly colleague).
3. Three cups.
4. Monday (at 10 am).
5. Work from home more often (plans to ask the manager next week).

### Key Vocabulary
- share a desk = masanı bölüşmək
- view = mənzərə
- meeting room = görüş otağı
- work from home = evdən işləmək

## Text 6: A Short Email

From: orkhan@example.com
To: sarah@example.com
Subject: Meeting tomorrow

Hi Sarah,

I hope you are well. I'm writing to confirm our meeting tomorrow at 2 pm. I'll be at your office, and we can discuss the new project.

I have some questions about the timeline and the budget. Can you send me the latest documents before the meeting? I want to read them tonight so I can prepare.

Also, is it okay if I bring my colleague, Elvin? He is a senior developer, and he can help answer technical questions.

Let me know if this works for you. If you need to change the time, please tell me today.

Thanks very much.

Best regards,
Orkhan

### Questions
1. Why is Orkhan writing the email?
2. What time is the meeting?
3. What does Orkhan want Sarah to send?
4. Why does Orkhan want to bring Elvin?
5. When does Orkhan need an answer?

### Answers
1. To confirm a meeting and ask some questions.
2. Tomorrow at 2 pm.
3. The latest documents (about the project — timeline and budget).
4. Elvin is a senior developer and can help answer technical questions.
5. Today, if the time needs to change.

### Key Vocabulary
- confirm = təsdiq etmək
- timeline = vaxt cədvəli
- budget = büdcə
- senior developer = təcrübəli proqramçı
- best regards = hörmətlə

## Strategiyalar

### İlk oxuma (skim)
- Mətnin **ümumi məzmununu** tap
- Başlığı və ilk cümləni oxu
- Detallara girmə

### İkinci oxuma (scan)
- Sualları oxu
- Hər sual üçün **açar söz** tap
- Mətnə qayıt və həmin sözü axtar

### Üçüncü oxuma (deep)
- Tam oxu
- Yeni sözləri yaz
- Yüksək səslə təkrar oxu

---

## Özünü Yoxla

- Hər mətn üçün vaxt tutur: **5 dəqiqə** mətn + sual
- Düzgün cavablar: **4/5 və ya daha çox** = A2 hazırsan
- **3/5 və ya az**: mətni yenidən oxu, kontekstə diqqət et
