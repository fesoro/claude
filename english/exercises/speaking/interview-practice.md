## Round 2 — Texniki Müsahibə üçün

**6. How do you stay up to date with your field?**
> "I follow [blogs/newsletters/communities]. I also try to build small projects to practice new things. Recently I've been learning [topic]."

**7. Describe your ideal working environment.**
> "I work best in a team where communication is open and people are comfortable sharing ideas and feedback. I'm happy working remotely or in an office — I just need clear goals and regular check-ins."

**8. Do you have any questions for us?**
*(Heç vaxt "No" demə — ən azı 2 sual hazırla)*

Yaxşı suallar:
- "What does success look like in this role after 6 months?"
- "How would you describe the team culture?"
- "What are the main challenges the team is currently facing?"
- "What does the onboarding process look like?"
